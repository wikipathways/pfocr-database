How to Cite

Pathway Figure OCR has been described in the following publications:

  • Shin M-G, et al. (2023) Using published pathway figures in enrichment analysis and machine learning. BMC Genomics.
  • Hanspers K, et al. (2020) Pathway information extracted from 25 years of pathway figures. Genome Biol.
  • Riutta A, et al. (2018) Identifying Genes in Published Pathway Figure Images. bioRxiv.

Mining the published literature for pathway figures and making their contents accessible for science.
