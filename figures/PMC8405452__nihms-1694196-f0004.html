Setting the stage for evolution of a new enzyme

Promiscuous enzyme activities recruited to serve new functions in laboratory evolution experiments in E. coli. (a) A four-step bypass pathway restores synthesis of PLP after deletion of pdxB []. (b) Recruitment of MetB to synthesize 2-ketobutyrate restores isoleucine synthesis after deletion of ilvA and tdcB []. Dashed lines indicate promiscuous reactions.
