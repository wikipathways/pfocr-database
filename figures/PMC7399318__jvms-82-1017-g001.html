Metabolic pathway of arachidonic acid (AA), icosapentaenoic acid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA)

Metabolic pathway of arachidonic acid (AA), icosapentaenoic acid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA). AA (A), EPA (B) and DHA (C) metabolic pathway classified by cyclooxygenase (COX), lipoxygenase (LOX), cytochrome P450 (CYP) or non-enzymatic oxidation (large black square). A black circle indicates analyzed lipid metabolite in urine of healthy cats. A black filled circle indicates detected metabolite. A black open circle indicates non-detected metabolite. A small black square indicates non-assessed metabolite.
