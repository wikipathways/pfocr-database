Transcriptional Memory in Taraxacum mongolicum in Response to Long-Term Different Grazing Intensities

The DEGs about photosynthesis pathway and photosynthesis-antenna proteins in two grazing treatments. Blue blocks mean down-regulated genes in light grazing treatment vs non-grazing treatment, dark blue blocks represent down-regulated genes in heavy grazing treatment vs non-grazing treatment, dark red blocks indicate up-regulated genes in heavy grazing treatment vs non-grazing treatment, red blocks mean up-regulated co-expression genes in light grazing treatment vs non-grazing treatment and heavy grazing treatment vs non-grazing treatment.
