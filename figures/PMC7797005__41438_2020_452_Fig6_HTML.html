Induced defense response in red mango fruit against Colletotrichum gloeosporioides

Presented are the phenylpropanoid-biosynthesis pathway and the heat map. Genes circled in red are significantly upregulated on the red side (RS) of the RF in response to C. gloeosporioides. Z-scores represent rescaled and normalized log2 fold-change values. Enzymes in each step: PAL, phenylalanine ammonia-lyase; TCMO, Trans-cinnamate 4-monooxygenase; CAD, cinnamyl alcohol dehydrogenase; PER, Peroxidase; COMT, caffeic acid O-methyltransferase; CHS, chalcone synthase; F3H, flavanone 3-hydroxylase; F3′H, flavanone 3′-hydroxylase; DRF, dihydroflavonol 4-reductase. GS, green side; GF, green fruit
