STRING analysis of habenular proteins with increased levels after (−)-menthol exposure

204 proteins were identified with increased levels after (−)-menthol exposure. Proteins were considered only if they were identified in all five biological replicates. Highlighted are the pathway or ontologies. Only interactions with interaction score of ≥0.9 are shown. The following select enriched pathways/ontologies are shown: Adaptive immune response (red), membrane trafficking (blue), and lysosome vesicle biogenesis.
