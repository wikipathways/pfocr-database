Identification of host genes leading to West Nile virus encephalitis in mice brain using RNA-seq analysis

Genes associated with death receptor signaling activated by (A) WNV NY99 and (B) WNV Eg101 are shown. Differentially expressed genes are highlighted in color. Color intensity indicates the degree of up-regulation (red) or down-regulation (green) relative to the mock-infected mice brains. Solid lines represent direct interactions and dashed lines indirect interactions.
