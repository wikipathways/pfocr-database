The enzyme modules incorporated into the RBC metabolic network

Schematic of the enzyme modules incorporated into the RBC metabolic network []. The ten modules constructed were primarily located in glycolysis and the pentose phosphate pathway. Other pathways were included as Q-linear kinetics approximations
