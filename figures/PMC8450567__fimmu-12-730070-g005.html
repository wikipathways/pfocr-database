Transcriptome Analysis of Immune Receptor Activation and Energy Metabolism Reduction as the Underlying Mechanisms in Interleukin-6-Induced Skeletal Muscle Atrophy

Down-regulated genes in the process of oxidative phosphorylation and the tricarboxylic acid cycle. (A) Genes involved in the oxidative phosphorylation pathway. (B) Genes involved in the citric acid cycle pathway. The newly discovered down-regulated genes after three days of interleukin-6 infusion are marked in green. ATP, adenosine triphosphate; TCA, tricarboxylic acid cycle.
