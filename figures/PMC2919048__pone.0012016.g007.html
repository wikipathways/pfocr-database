Diagram of TGFbeta signaling pathways from the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes , , showing pathway components that are considered to be present based on a p-value of detection <0.01. Factors present in green are present in a subset of the cell lines (the particular cell lines are indicated by letters adjacent to the box indicating the factor).
