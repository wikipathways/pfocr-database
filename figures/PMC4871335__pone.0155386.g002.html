High Phosphorus Diet Affects Lipid Metabolism in Rat Liver: A DNA Microarray Analysis

The HP diet induced decreased hepatic expression of enzymes involved in amino acid catabolism. Enzymes and factors whose mRNA expression is higher and lower in the livers of HP diet-fed rats are shown in red and blue, respectively, in comparison to their expression in the livers of control diet-fed rats. Also see for DEGs related to amino acid metabolism. Amino acids are represented by white letters and down-regulated pathways are represented by blue arrows. TCA, tricarboxylic acid.
