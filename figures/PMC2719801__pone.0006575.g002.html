Gene Expression in Skeletal Muscle Biopsies from People with Type 2 Diabetes and Relatives: Differential Regulation of Insulin Signaling Pathways

The insulin signaling pathways were found to be significantly downregulated on the gene expression level using the program GenMAPP/MAPPFinder. Analysis criteria were set as described in the section. Underneath each section of the pathway, genes found to have an increased expression are depicted in red, and genes found to have a decreased expression are depicted in green. Figure adapted from GenMAPP.
