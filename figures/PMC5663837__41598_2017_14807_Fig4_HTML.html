ErbB signaling pathway and its circRNA-related genes

ErbB signaling pathway and its circRNA-related genes. (A) The ErbB signaling pathway and its gene interactions; (B) Protein processing in endoplasmic reticulum and its gene interactions. The light green boxes represent circRNA-free genes among all single cells, the yellow boxes represent genes harboring circRNAs appearing in only a single cell and the orange boxes represent genes harboring circRNAs appearing in multi-samples.
