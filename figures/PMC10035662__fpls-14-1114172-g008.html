Comparative metabolomic profiling of Arabidopsis thaliana roots and leaves reveals complex response mechanisms induced by a seaweed extract

Pathway analysis of metabolites that altered in roots and leaves at day 0, day 3 and day 5 following SWE treatment. Out of 89 compounds of interest, 62 compounds were found in the KEGG pathway database, which was used to create this figure. The remaining 27 compounds are not shown in the figure. Log2FC data were used to represent the accumulation of the compounds (red color), or reduced accumulation (blue color), or no statistical change (white) of compounds.
