Ubiquitin Proteasome Pathway Transcriptome in Epithelial Ovarian Cancer

Ubiquitination and deubiquitination in the FA pathway: UBE2T (E2 conjugase), FANCL (E3 ligase), BRCA1 (E3 ligase), and SLX4 (E3 ligase adaptor). U, Ubiquitin; P, phosphate; ICL, interstrand crosslink. USP1 (deubiquitinase). Monoubiquitination of FANCD2 is required for DNA repair. Via UBE2T and FANCL, ubiquitin is transferred to FANCD2. Deubiquitination occurs after DNA repair is completed.
