ERK and AKT pathway on lymphangiogenesis

Overview of ERK and AKT pathway on lymphangiogenesis. Both pathways influence different aspects of lymphatic remodeling upon activation of VEGFR-3, leading to downstream phosphorylation of signaling proteins (). Crosstalk occurs across the pathways primarily as a means of negative regulation (–). Current therapies to inhibit these signaling pathways involve the sequestration of VEGFR and its ligands (–).
