High Phosphorus Diet Affects Lipid Metabolism in Rat Liver: A DNA Microarray Analysis

The HP diet induced alterations in the hepatic expression of enzymes involved in lipid catabolism and biosynthesis. Enzymes and factors whose mRNA expression is higher and lower in the livers of HP diet-fed rats are shown in red and blue, respectively, in comparison to their expression in the livers of control diet-fed rats. See for DEGs related to lipid metabolism. Up- and down-regulated pathways are represented with red and blue arrows, respectively. PUFA, polyunsaturated fatty acid; TCA, tricarboxylic acid.
