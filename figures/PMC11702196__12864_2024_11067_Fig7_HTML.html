Lipid Metabolism Regulatory Network Revealed by Multi-Omics Data Analysis. The figure presents a lipid metabolism regulatory network constructed through the integration of proteomic, transcriptomic, and metabolomic data. The nodes in the network diagram represent categories, including genes, proteins, metabolites, and pathways, while edges denote the interactions or regulatory relationships between these molecules. The color of the nodes indicates the biological functions in which the molecules are involved
