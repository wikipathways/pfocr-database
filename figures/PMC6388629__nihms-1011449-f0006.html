IPA network map depicting the DR signaling changes in HBEpCs after 6 h of infection with IAV H1N1

IPA network map depicting the DR signaling changes in HBEpCs after 6 h of infection with IAV H1N1. Up- and downregulated genes in red and green, respectively. This figure shows the direct (solid lines) and indirect (dashed lines) interactions reported for the DR pathway genes. Biological network analysis was performed by using IPA software and statistical significance with Fisher’s exact test.
