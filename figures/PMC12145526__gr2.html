The BMP pathway in the musculoskeletal system

Regulation of the BMP pathway in the musculoskeletal system. In bone, the BMP pathway facilitates bone formation by maintaining a homeostasis between osteoblasts and osteoclasts. In muscle, BMP enhances protein synthesis and prevents protein degradation through activation of the Smad 1/5/8 pathway, thereby contributing to muscle regeneration (Created with BioRender.com.)
