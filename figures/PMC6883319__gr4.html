MetaCore-generated pathway showing differentially expressed genes involved in sirtuin 6 regulation and function

MetaCore-generated pathway showing differentially expressed genes involved in sirtuin 6 regulation and function. Experimental data from all three GSE data sets are linked to and visualized on the maps as thermometer-like figures. Upward thermometers have red color and indicate upregulated signals and downward (blue) ones indicate downregulated expression levels of the genes. Annotations are listed in supplemental materials.
