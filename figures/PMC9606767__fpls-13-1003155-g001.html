Versatile roles of polyamines in improving abiotic stress tolerance of plants

Schematic presentation of polyamines biosynthetic pathway. There are three routes of putrescine synthesis from arginine (route 1), ornithine (route 2), and methionine (route 3) followed by multiple sequential steps, resultant putrescine converts into spermidine which in turn produce spermine.
