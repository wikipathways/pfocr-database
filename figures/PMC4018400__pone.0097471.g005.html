De Novo Characterization of the Spleen Transcriptome of the Large Yellow Croaker (Pseudosciaena crocea) and Analysis of the Immune Relevant Genes and Pathways Involved in the Antiviral Response

Total RNA was extracted from the spleens of large yellow croakers sampled at 0, 12 and 24(I:C) induction. Real-time PCR was used to validate gene expression changes in the pattern recognition receptors (A), signal transducers (B), interferons and interleukin (C), interferon-stimulated genes (D), JAK-STAT pathway (F), and T-cell receptor (TCR) signaling pathway. Increases and decreases in the relative levels of transcripts with respect to the control Î²-actin gene are shown.*P<0.05, **P<0.01.
