N-terminal tyrosine residues of SLP-76 on the canonical TCR signaling pathway

Effects of N-terminal tyrosine residues of SLP-76 on the canonical TCR signaling pathway. The canonical T-cell signaling pathway is represented with SILAC heatmap quantitation and the corresponding identified phosphorylation sites. Heatmaps were calculated from the average of five replicate experiments. White dots within a heatmap square indicate a statistically significant difference (Q value < 0.05) in the comparison between Y3F and SLP-76 reconstituted cells.
