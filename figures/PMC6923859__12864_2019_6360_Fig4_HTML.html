Regulation of immune response as the most relevant pathway in the infested susceptible (S) skin

Regulation of immune response as the most relevant pathway in the infested susceptible (S) skin. Regulation of immune response was predicted for the TopDEGs from S hosts after tick infestation, highlighted in cyan. Red circles represent over-expression: higher the value, stronger the intensity of red.
