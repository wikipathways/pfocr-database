Expanded representation of the KEGG pathway "colorectal cancer"

Expanded representation of the KEGG pathway "colorectal cancer". The current version of the KEGG pathway "colorectal cancer" was expanded from 40 to 70 genes using PESCADOR on a selection of 500 abstracts relevant to the topic. Green boxes: genes previously annotated. Cyan boxes: genes added by our analysis. Gene names in red font: mutated in colorectal cancer.
