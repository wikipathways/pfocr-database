Role of AMPK signalling pathway during compensatory growth in pigs

Plot of the biological network most significantly enriched by the list of genes differently expressed between animals subjected and not subjected to a feed restriction period: Carbohydrate Metabolism, Cardiovascular System Development and Function, Cellular Development. The shape of nodes indicates the functional classes of the gene products. The node color indicates the degree of expression: (red) up-regulated and (green) down-regulated in the RE group relative to the AL group
