Integrated genetic and epigenetic analysis of childhood acute lymphoblastic leukemia

(A) Schematic representation of the BCR signaling pathway, which was significantly differentially methylated in B-ALL versus T-ALL (DAVID P = 0.00064). Genes that were hypomethylated or overexpressed in B-ALL are highlighted red; genes that were hypermethylated or silenced in B-ALL are highlighted green. (B) Schematic representation of the TCR signaling pathway (DAVID P = 0.029). Genes that were hypomethylated or overexpressed in T-ALL are highlighted red; genes that were hypermethylated or silenced in T-ALL are highlighted green.
