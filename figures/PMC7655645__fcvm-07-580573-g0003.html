Protein interactions associated with signaling pathways enriched in diabetes mellitus-associated coronary heart disease

Protein interactions associated with signaling pathways enriched in diabetes mellitus-associated coronary heart disease. (A) Network diagram of protein-protein-KEGG pathway related to DMCAD. The ellipse for the module gene. The round rectangle for the KEGG pathway. The color of gene nodes represents MM, and the color at the edge of gene nodes represents modules. (B) CALM3, ZAP70, GNGT2, CACNA2D2, CTSF, and CTSW-related KEGG pathways. DMCHD, diabetes mellitus-induced coronary heart disease. DRAG, DM-related atherogenesis gene. MM, module membership.
