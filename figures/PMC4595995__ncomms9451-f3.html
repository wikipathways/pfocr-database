HIV–tuberculosis-associated immune reconstitution inflammatory syndrome is characterized by Toll-like receptor and inflammasome signalling

The TREM1, TLR and IL-1 signalling pathways are interconnected. Molecules in red were those found to be differentially abundant in the signatures of TB-IRIS patients at week 2 (normalization to median). Molecule activity prediction was performed using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis: molecules in orange were predicted to be upregulated and those in blue predicted to be downregulated.
