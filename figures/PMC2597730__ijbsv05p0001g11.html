Cascade between VEGFA, a direct target of BP1, and its downstream target genes in cell proliferation

Cascade between VEGFA, a direct target of BP1, and its downstream target genes in cell proliferation. VEGFA functional cascades were analyzed by the Pathway Studio 5.0. Genes in the network, red for upregulated and green for down-regulated ones, are the targets of VEGFA, which collectively promote cell proliferation.
