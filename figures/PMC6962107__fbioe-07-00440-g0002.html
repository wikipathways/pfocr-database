L-ornithine biosynthesis metabolic pathways in C

L-ornithine biosynthesis metabolic pathways in C. glutamicum and strategies to improve L-ornithine accumulation. The red× represented this pathway were inactivated. The blue font and arrows represented that pathways were attenuated. The green font and arrows indicated that pathways were overexpressed. The genes encoding enzymes involved in catalytically relevant reactions.
