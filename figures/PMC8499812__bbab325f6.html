DeepDRIM: a deep neural network to reconstruct cell-type-specific gene regulatory network using single-cell RNA-seq data

The unique GRNs of DETFs from the patients with severe COVID-19. A. GRNs related to response to a decreased oxygen level (GO:0036293); DNA damage response (GO:0030330); negative regulation of the mitotic cell cycle (GO:0045930); and the intrinsic apoptotic signaling pathway (GO:0097193). B. GRNs related to the microtubule organizing center (GO:0005851). The edges are shown if their absolute Pearson correlation coefficients are larger than 0.4. DETFs: differentially expressed transcription factors.
