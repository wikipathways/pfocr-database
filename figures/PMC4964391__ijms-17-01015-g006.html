Homogeneous Polysaccharide from Fructus Schisandra chinensis (Turz.) Baill Induces Mitochondrial Apoptosis through the Hsp90/AKT Signalling Pathway in HepG2 Cells

S. Chinensis polysaccharide-0-1 (SCP-0-1) induced molecular events of mitochondrial apoptosis rather than autophagy. Up-arrows and down-arrows denote the action of positive and negative regulation of specific proteins, respectively. Olid line arrows indicates pathways direction. Crosses and dash line arrows are represented as no appreciable effects on specific pathways. Double vertical lines refers to the induction of cell cycle G2/M arrest.
