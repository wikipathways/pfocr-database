Epigenomic profiling in visceral white adipose tissue of offspring of mice exposed to late gestational sleep fragmentation

Gene network corresponding to genes associated to DMRs exhibiting higher DNA methylation in LG-SF (Panel A) and LG-SC (Panel B) groups. Networks were built using a bioinformatic tool (Ingenuity Pathway Analysis) based on databases relying on experimental evidence of gene functions. Molecules with a reported function in lipid metabolism and immune response are highlighted. Canonical pathways (CP) related to those functions are indicated with labels. Molecules in main nodes are colored in red and blue (for higher methylation in LG-SF and LG-SC, respectively) and indicated by a colored circle.
