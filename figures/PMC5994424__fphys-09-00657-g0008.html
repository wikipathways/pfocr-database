ADM Scaffolds Generate a Pro-regenerative Microenvironment During Full-Thickness Cutaneous Wound Healing Through M2 Macrophage Polarization via Lamtor1

(A) In addition to the previously identified ADM scaffold-induced change from the default pro-inflammatory response to a pro-regenerative response via M2 macrophage polarization, (B) we found that Lamtor1, the amino acid-sensing pathway (v-ATPase, Lamtor1, and mTORC1)-associated lysosomal adaptor protein (Kimura et al., ), is indispensable for ADM scaffold-mediated induction of M2 macrophage polarization.
