Molecular Mechanisms of Chondrocyte Proliferation and Differentiation

The FGF signaling pathway in chondrocytes. FGF combines with FGFR to activate the downstream cascade (RAS-MAPK and PI3K-AKT), and then the expression of Sox9 and Runx2 is regulated. FGF can suppress BMP/Smad signaling via MAPK signaling in chondrocytes development.
