JMJD family proteins in cancer and inflammation

Representative diagram of the demethylating activities of JMJD family members in cancer and inflammation: JMJD3 demethylates tri-methylated Lys 27 on histone H3 and thus affects the transcription of inflammation-associated gene. JMJD6 is a 2OG oxygenase and catalyze methylarginine demethylation of histone H3/H4 residues, which regulates the transcription of cancer-related pathway genes such as MAPK signaling.
