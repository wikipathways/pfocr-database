VEGF and NOTCH signaling pathways

VEGF and NOTCH signaling pathways. Gene expression involved in the VEGF and NOTCH signaling pathways. Genes in red are over expressed in oocytes. Genes in green are over expressed in GCs. Relative expression of VEGF and FLT1 from RNAseq data. The Y axis corresponds to RNA-seq normalized read counts. A: Oocyte-GC communications in the NOTCH signaling pathway. B: GC-GC communications in the NOTCH signaling pathway. C: Oocyte-GC communications in the VEGF signaling pathway.
