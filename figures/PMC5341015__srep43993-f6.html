Eggshell membrane powder ameliorates intestinal inflammation by facilitating the restitution of epithelial injury and alleviating microbial dysbiosis

(a) TCA cycle, (b) urea cycle, (c) glycolysis pathway. The numbers in () are fold changes of the gene(g) expression, protein(p) and metabolite(m) levels, respectively, where red indicates an increase and green indicates a decrease in the two comparisons (DSS vs. CON and D-ESM8 vs. DSS). (d) Correlation diagram of each pathway. The colorful arrows represent promoted or increased (red) and suppressed or decreased (green) in the two comparisons.
