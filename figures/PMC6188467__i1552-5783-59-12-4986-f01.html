RNA-seq analysis revealed that LECs exhibit a highly perturbed cytokine–cytokine receptor pathway at 24 hours PCS

RNA-seq analysis revealed that LECs exhibit a highly perturbed cytokine–cytokine receptor pathway at 24 hours PCS. The cytokine–cytokine receptor pathway, as defined by the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG:04060), is annotated to highlight all pathway genes that are differentially expressed in LECs at 24 hours PCS. Blue: genes downregulated in LECs at 24 hours PCS; red: genes upregulated in LECs at 24 hours PCS.
