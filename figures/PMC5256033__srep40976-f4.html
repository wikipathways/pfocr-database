Genome and transcriptome analysis of surfactin biosynthesis in Bacillus amyloliquefaciens MT45

The metabolic network was reconstructed based on KEGG pathway analysis. Six modules were partitioned according to function. Transcripts of MT45 and DSM7T are shown near the pathway as a heat map, based on fold-changes in transcript levels relative to DSM7T after 12 h. Colour legend is shown at the top left of the map.
