Gene Expression Profiling of the Response to Interferon Beta in Epstein-Barr-Transformed and Primary B Cells of Patients with Multiple Sclerosis

The pathway depicted includes the 27 genes of the of the 'antimicrobial response' pathway that showed increased activation following IFN-β exposure of LCLs (activation score Z = 2.779) by Ingenuity analysis. In addition, genes outlined in blue were manually added to the pathway based on their known participation in the innate response in B cells or other leukocytes, and include TLR7 and TLR9 , TRIM21 and TRIM5 –. Only direct connections are featured, and subcellular location of gene products is as suggested by Ingenuity knowledge base. In green- down-regulated genes; in red- up-regulated genes, color intensity is proportional to the fold change in expression levels following IFN-β exposure.
