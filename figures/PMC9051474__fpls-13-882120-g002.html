Comparison of Metabolome and Functional Properties of Three Korean Cucumber Cultivars

Schematic representation of the flavonoid, carotenoid, and chlorophyll metabolic pathway with metabolite and related gene expression levels in the Chuichung and Mini cultivars. The pathway was modified from the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) database (http://www.genome.jp/kegg/). The colored squares (blue-to-red) represent the relative abundance. The blue characters represent metabolites detected through the mass spectrometry, and the relative abundance of these metabolites is shown in , .
