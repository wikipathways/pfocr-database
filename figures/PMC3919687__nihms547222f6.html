Kinetic changes in gene expression of selected components of Th2 responses

Kinetic changes in gene expression of selected components of Th2 responses. Pathways were generated by Ingenuity Pathway analysis. (A) Pathway analysis of Th2 differentiation. (B) IL-10 Signaling and IL-4Rα expression, and (C) IL-4 signaling and production. Adjacent to each step of the pathway are the gene profile plots of old and young CD4+ T cells.
