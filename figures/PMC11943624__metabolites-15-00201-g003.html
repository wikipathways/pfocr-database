The key metabolic pathway components and targets for nanomedicine-based targeting (rectangles represent components, rounded rectangles represent enzymes, and dashed lines and multiple arrows indicate omitted multistep reactions). The primary mechanisms of action of nanomedicines currently include blocking the intake of substances, depleting critical components, and targeting rate-limiting or key catalytic enzymes within metabolic pathways.
