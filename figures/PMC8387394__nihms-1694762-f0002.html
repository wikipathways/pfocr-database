Conservation of epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition process in neural crest cells and metastatic cancer

The Receptor Tyrosine Kinase (RTK) and BMP/TGFβ signaling pathways involved in NCC development and MC progression. The RTK signaling pathway is involved in EMT during (A) NCC and (B) MC development. Both systems share similar signaling ligands and downstream transcription factors, including STAT3, HIF-1α, LIF, PAX7, E2F2, AP2α, and SNAIL2. However, the receptor-coupled factors and intracellular cascades are less well known in NCC development compared to MC. The BMP/TGFb signaling pathway is involved in EMT during (C) NCC and (D) MC development. Both systems share similar signaling ligands and downstream transcription factors, including ZEB1, SLUG, SNAIL1, STAT3, SMAD1, SMAD2, cMYC, and LEF1.
