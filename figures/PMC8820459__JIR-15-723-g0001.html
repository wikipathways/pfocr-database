p38MAPK Signaling Pathway in Osteoarthritis: Pathological and Therapeutic Aspects

Basic mechanism of p38MAPK pathway. The P38MAPK signaling pathway involves three key kinases: MAPKKK (beige box), MAPKK (blue box), and MAPK (orange box). P38MAPK signaling pathway can be affected by extracellular stimuli (red box) and then enter the nucleus to active transcription factors (green box).
