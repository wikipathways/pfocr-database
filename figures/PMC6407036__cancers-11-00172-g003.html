Biomarkers in the Pathways in cancer

Biomarkers in the Pathways in cancer. (A) Various cancer pathways involve in different cancer initiation and progression. (B) CRC biomarkers for diagnosis, therapy and prognosis biomarkers in the CBD were mapped in different colours in Pathways in cancer. The CRC biomarkers have been associated with apoptosis, cell proliferation, VEGF signalling pathway and Ras signalling pathway in the Pathways in cancer. Red: diagnosis biomarker; Blue: treatment biomarker; Purple: prognosis biomarker; Orange: diagnosis & treatment biomarker; Yellow: treatment & prognosis biomarker; Pink: diagnosis & treatment & prognosis biomarker.
