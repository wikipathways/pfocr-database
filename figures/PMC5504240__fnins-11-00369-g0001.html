Circadian metabolites have pleiotropic mechanisms by which to maintain cellular rhythmicity

Circadian metabolites have pleiotropic mechanisms by which to maintain cellular rhythmicity. Metabolites are an essential part of the cellular circadian clock, and function intracellularly in a variety of ways, from direct metabolic pathway activation to mitochondrial and nuclear events.
