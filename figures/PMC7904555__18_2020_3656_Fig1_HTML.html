Pro-inflammatory signaling pathways of TLR4

Pro-inflammatory signaling pathways of TLR4. TLR4 activates the MyD88-signaling pathway at the plasma membrane and after a CD14-dependent endocytosis initiates the TRIF-dependent cascade. Via activated NF-κB TLR4 also contributes to the activation of the cytosolic NLRP3 inflammasome. See text for details
