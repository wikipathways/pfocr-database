Mechanisms of TFEB agonists

Schematic of mechanisms of TFEB agonists. The schematic shows the model of Ca2+ mediated regulation of TFEB, the model of lysosomal sensing and lysosome-to-nucleus signaling by TFEB and mTOR as well as the model of PP2A stimulating regulation of TFEB. AKT, loss of FLCN modulates the autophagy-lysosomal pathway via TFEB. PPAR-α-RXRα-PGC-1α complex and knocking down TDP-43 can promote the transcriptional activation of TFEB. Abbreviation: CaN, Calcineurin; AKT, protein kinase B.
