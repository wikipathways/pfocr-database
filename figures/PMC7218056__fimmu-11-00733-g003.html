Hippo pathways in lymphocytes

Hippo pathways in lymphocytes. Signaling pathways regulated by MST1/2 in lymphocytes. Compartmentalized areas colored light yellow and light green indicate the intracellular/cytoplasmic area and nucleus, respectively. The canonical Hippo pathway is depicted at the very left, whereas non-canonical/alternative pathways are depicted in the rest of the figure. MVB: multi-vesicular body.
