13-Gene Metabolic Prognostic Signature Is Associated With Clinical and Immune Features in Stomach Adenocarcinoma

The significant immune-related pathways of differentially expressed immune-related genes (DEIRGs) in stomach adenocarcinoma (STAD). (A) DEIRGs significantly enriched in cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction pathway. (B) DEIRGs significantly enriched in TGF-β signaling pathway. (C) DEIRGs significantly enriched in ErbB signaling pathway.
