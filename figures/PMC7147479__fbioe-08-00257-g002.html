Metabolic pathway heterologously expressed in recombinant bacteria (e.g., E. coli and H. bluephagenesis) for the synthesis of P4HB and copolymers from related and unrelated C sources. The deletion of endogenous sad and gabD both encoding succinate semialdehyde dehydrogenase reinforces the carbon flux toward 4HB-CoA. PhaA, 3-ketothiolase; PhaB, acetoacetyl-CoA reductase; OgdA, 2-oxo-glutarate dehydrogenase; SucD, succinate semialdehyde dehydrogenase; 4HbD, 4-hydroxybutyrate dehydrogenase; OrfZ, 4HB-CoA transferase; PhaC: PHA synthase.
