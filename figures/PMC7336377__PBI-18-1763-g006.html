Summary of key components contributing to RS formation in rice

Summary of key components contributing to RS formation in rice. Genes identified either in genome‐wide association studies or targeted association studies were highlighted in the pathway overview, its loci coloured in red. The central hubs identified from gene regulatory networks from differentially expressed genes (loci) were coloured in green. The central hub metabolites and DF components found in medium resistant starch and low resistant starch were highlighted as circles and square, respectively.
