Multi-omics reveals age-related differences in the diaphragm response to mechanical ventilation: a pilot study

“IL-17 signaling pathway” and involved DEGs and DEPs of the diaphragm in response to CMV in old rats. Casp, Cxcl1, Cxcl2, and Ccl2 were identified both as the CMV-induced DEGs and CMV-induced DEPs. Fold change information of each DEG is indicated in color from low (green) to high (red). Fold change information of each DEP is indicated in color from low (blue) to high (yellow)
