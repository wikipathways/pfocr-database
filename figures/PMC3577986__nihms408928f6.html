Gene Network Analysis of Small Molecules with Autoimmune Disease Associated Genes Predicts a Novel Strategy for Drug Efficacy

Various small molecules interact with shared ADs genes suggesting that SNPs in these genes could be modulated through these factors. This networking also demonstrates that current drugs those are used to treat various ADs interact with these newly associated genes. Susceptible gene with risk allele carrying patients could be better benefitted with the specific drug that interacts with that gene. Associated genes (dark blue), interconnecting genes (light blue), small molecules and miRNAs (red and orange respectively).
