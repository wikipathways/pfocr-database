Molecular pathways hypothesized to be involved in the pathogenesis of nonspecific orbital inflammation

Schematic representation of molecular pathways hypothesized to be involved in the pathogenesis of nonspecific orbital inflammation. Nonspecific orbital inflammation (NSOI) is known to be induced by dysregulation in the immune system, which is outlined in this figure. NSOI-induced dysregulations are represented in bold red and with red arrows.
