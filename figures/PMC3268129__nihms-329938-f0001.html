Amidoligases with ATP-grasp, glutamine synthetase-like and acetyltransferase-like domains: synthesis of novel metabolites and peptide modifications of proteins

Examples of previously known and predicted biosynthetic pathways containing amidoligases are shown. Previously characterized reactions are glutathione biosynthesis, protein glutamylation by the TTL family and cyanophycin biosynthesis. Also shown are various predicted reactions elucidated in part or entirely in this study: 2,3 diaminobutryrate synthesis in the friulimicin biosynthesis pathway; anticapsin biosynthesis in the bacilysin biosynthesis pathway; the transpeptidase reaction catalyzed by the BtrH-like peptidase; the biosynthesis of teichuronopeptide.
