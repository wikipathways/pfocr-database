Figure shows Selected subnetwork of the differential network for Aorta tissue in Diabetes

The figure shows Selected subnetwork of the differential network for Aorta tissue in Diabetes. In detail, starting from the differential network, we highlighted the genes involved in the pathways resulted from enriched analysis in different colours, whereas the genes not involved in pathways are identified in grey. In particular, the genes involved Toll-like receptor signaling pathway are coloured in blue, the genes involved in NOD-like receptor signaling pathway are coloured in yellow, the genes involved in Hepatitis B are coloured in pink, and the genes involved in Shigellosis are coloured in purple
