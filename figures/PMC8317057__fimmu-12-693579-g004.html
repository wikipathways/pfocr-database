Pathogenic Features of Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 (SARS-CoV-2): Possible Mechanisms for Immune Evasion?

SARS-like CoVs including SARS-CoV, MERS-CoV and SARS-CoV-2 inhibit host IFN signalling pathway. Proteins of SARS-CoV are in green, proteins of MERS-CoV in violet and proteins of SARS-CoV-2 in blue. The IFN upstream pathways are in the left side of the figure and the IFN downstream pathways are in the right side. The pattern diagram of SARS-CoV-2 is credited by Maria Voigt/RCSB PDB.
