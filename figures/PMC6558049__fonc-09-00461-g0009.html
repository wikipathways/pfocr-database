Possible mechanism of CAPE-pNO2 inhibits TNBC growth and metastasis

The possible mechanism of CAPE-pNO2 inhibits TNBC growth and metastasis. CAPE-pNO2 suppressed the expression of metastasis- and growth-associated proteins by restraining the EGFR/STAT3/Akt signal pathway, and EMT could be regulated by the signal pathway.
