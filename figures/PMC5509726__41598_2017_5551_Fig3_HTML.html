Protein-protein interaction networks of metabolism related pathway enriched genes

Protein-protein interaction networks of metabolism related pathway enriched genes. The node size was decided on the basis of the interaction degree value of each gene. Triangle node: FPKM < 10 in all intestinal regions; Circular node: 10 ≤ FPKM < 500 in at least one tissue with the highest expression; Square node: FPKM ≥ 500 in at least one intestinal tissue. Red: Higher expressed in the duodenum; Blue: Higher expressed in the large intestine; Green: Multiple transcript expression patterns are not consistent.
