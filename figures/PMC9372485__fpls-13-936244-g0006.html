Biosynthesis of menthane monoterpene and variations involving related genes and metabolites; (A) biosynthetic pathway of menthane monoterpene biosynthesis; (B) heatmap of menthane monoterpenes content; (C) heatmap of gene expression in menthane monoterpene biosynthesis.
