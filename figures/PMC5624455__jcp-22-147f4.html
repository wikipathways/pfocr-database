Leukocyte extravasation signaling pathway altered by a high-fat-diet, Taeumjowitang, and green tea in the epididymal white adipose tissue

Leukocyte extravasation signaling pathway altered by a high-fat-diet, Taeumjowitang, and green tea in the epididymal white adipose tissue. This pathway were obtained from software Ingenuity Pathway Analysis. HD, mice fed a high-fat-diet; ND, mice fed normal diet; TH, mice fed a high-fat-diet supplemented with Taeumjowitang; GH, mice fed a high-fat-diet supplemented with green tea.
