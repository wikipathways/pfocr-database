Mitochondrial generation of ROS

Mitochondrial generation of ROS. Complexes I, II, and III (complexes located on electron transport chain) play a pivotal role in the generation of ROS during the process of oxidative phosphorylation. The increased ROS regulated cancer progression, angiogenesis, metastasis, and survival.
