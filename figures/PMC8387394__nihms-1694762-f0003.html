Conservation of epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition process in neural crest cells and metastatic cancer

The Hippo and GPCR/Wnt signaling pathways involved in NCC development and MC progression. The Hippo signaling pathway is involved in the process of EMT in both (A) NCC and (B) MC development. Both systems share similar signaling ligands and the downstream transcription factor, YAP. The GPCR/Wnt signaling pathway is also involved in EMT in (C) NCC and (D) MC development. Both systems share similar signaling ligands and downstream transcription factors, including HIF-1α, GLI, and YAP.
