Role of HBXIP in regulating non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC)

Schematic model of the role of HBXIP in regulating non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC). HBXIP promotes NSCLC cell proliferation and tumorigenesis, possibly by activating the MAPK/ERK pathway, and HBXIP stabilizes MEK1 and prevents its degradation by the proteasome pathway.
