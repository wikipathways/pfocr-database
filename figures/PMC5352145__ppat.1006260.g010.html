Genetic basis for variation in resistance to infection in the Drosophila melanogaster genetic reference panel

Enriched cellular genetic pathway for candidate genes from all genome wide association analyses (squares), allowing one missing gene (white triangles) or compound (white circles). The border colors indicate the over-represented gene ontology categories (P<0.005): axon guidance (red), translation initiation (orange), protein phosphorylation (dark green), cell migration (magenta), phagocytosis and engulfment (yellow), arginine and proline metabolism (dark blue), purine metabolism (light blue), response to DNA damage (light green), ether lipid metabolism (gray), glycerolipid metabolism (teal), P450 related drug metabolism (brown).
