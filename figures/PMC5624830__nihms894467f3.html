NOD1 and NOD2: beyond peptidoglycan sensing

Induction of ER stress with the chemical inducers DTT and Thapsigargin or infection with Brucella abortus induce the IRE1 pathway of the unfolded protein response, which leads to activation of NF-κB via a process dependent on TRAF2, NOD1/2 and RIP2. IRE1 activation can also lead to activation of IRF3, via its ribonuclease function [].
