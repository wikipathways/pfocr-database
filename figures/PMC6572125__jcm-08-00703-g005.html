Enriched integrin signaling pathway in non-type subtype of bladder carcinoma cell lines

Schematic diagram of enriched integrin signaling pathway in non-type subtype of bladder carcinoma cell lines. Ingenuity pathway analysis lead to the identification of integrin signaling to be most enriched in the aggressive molecular subtype. The dysregulated proteins, the number of phosphopeptides, and the phosphosites identified are highlighted in the pathway.
