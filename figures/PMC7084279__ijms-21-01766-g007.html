Detailed analysis of the 13 out of the 23 screened genes in “monoamine GPCRs” pathway

Detailed analysis of the 13 out of the 23 screened genes in “monoamine GPCRs” pathway. The 13 genes are highlighted, and they are distributed in categories of adrenergic receptors, dopamine receptors, serotonin receptors, and muscarinic acetylcholine receptors, but not in histamine receptors. Arrows: blue, Gs pathway; orange, Gi/Go pathway; green, Gq/G11 pathway.
