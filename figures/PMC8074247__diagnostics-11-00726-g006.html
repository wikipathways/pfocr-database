Analysis of LAGEs Family Gene Signature and Prognostic Relevance in Breast Cancer

(A) In order to define the network of potential genes and related pathways affected by input genes, we exported LAGE2A co-expressed genes from TCGA and METABRIC breast cancer datasets to the MetaCore pathway analytical tool. (B) The result of MetaCore pathway analysis of “biology processes” illustrated that “development negative regulation of WNT/Beta catenin signaling” in the nucleus-related pathway was correlated with breast cancer development.
