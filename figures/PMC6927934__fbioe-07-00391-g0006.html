The cell cycle metaphase checkpoint pathway

(A) The cell cycle metaphase checkpoint pathway. (B) The APC cell cycle regulation pathway. The two top-scored regulated pathways were activated in the OC cell lines. The pathway images were generated by GeneGo Metacore™ enrichment analysis. Well-characterized proteins or protein complexes are shown as individual symbols within the image; experimental data from all the records are connected and depicted as thermometer-like figures on the maps. Upward-facing thermometers are shown in red and indicate upregulated gene transcripts. The linkage of proteins by arrows depicts the stimulatory and inhibitory effects or interaction of the encoded protein on the desired protein. The hub genes (protein families) that were involved in the canonical signaling pathways are marked in a circle (scarlet). Further explanations are provided at https://portal.genego.com/help/MC_legend.pdf.
