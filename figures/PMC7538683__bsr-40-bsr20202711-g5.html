Bioinformatics analysis and experimental validation of TTK as a biomarker for prognosis in non-small cell lung cancer

(A) Cell cycle signaling pathway is significantly enriched with BUB1B, BUB1, TTK, CDC45, CDC6, CHECK1, CCNB1, and CCNB2 that were involved in the cell cycle signaling pathway of NSCLC. (B) A p53 signaling pathway is significantly enriched with CCNB1, CCNB2, and RRM2 that were revealed in the p53 signaling pathway. The red star show the DEGs show up in cell cycle p53 signaling pathway. The red star indicates the enriched gene.
