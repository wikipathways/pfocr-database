Extracellular circulating miRNAs as stress-related signature to search and rescue dogs

Pathway and targets prediction. The figure shows the associations between let-7a and let-7f ecmiRNAs and their putative p53 signaling pathway (p-value 0.0027432, and 23 gene interactions). The server offers two-gene labeling levels: yellow (gene targeted by 1 selected miRNA, let-7a), orange (gene targeted by 2 selected ecmiRNAs, let-7a and let-7f). Green color indicates genes that are not targeted by let-7a and let-7f Pathways analysis was performed using DIANA-miRPath v3.
