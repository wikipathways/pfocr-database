Fatty acid biosynthesis and TAG assembly pathway reconstruction

Fatty acid biosynthesis and TAG assembly pathway reconstruction. The map shows proteins (uppercase) and metabolites (lowercase) involved in the metabolic pathway at flower stages (A) and at fruit stages (B). Relative abundance of proteins was averaged over three biological replicates (n = 3). ACC, acetyl-CoA carboxylase; MAT, malonyl-CoA ACP transacylase; OAS, 3-oxoacyl-ACP synthase; KAS, ketoacyl-ACP synthase; OAR, 3-oxoacyl-ACP reductase; KAR, ketoacyl-ACP reductase; HAD, hydroxyacyl-ACP dehydrase; EAR, enoyl-ACP reductase; SAD, stearoyl-ACP desaturase; FAS, fatty acid synthase; FAT, acyl-ACP thioesterase; FAD, fatty acid desaturase; LACS, long-chain acyl-CoA synthetase; GK, glycerol kinase; GPAT, glycerol-3-phosphate acyltransferase; AGPAT, acyl-glycerol-3-phosphate acyltransferase; PAP, phosphatidic acid phosphatase; DGAT, diacylglycerol acyltransferase; PDAT, phospholipid:diacylglycerol acyltransferase; MAGL, monoacylglycerol lipase; LPAP, phosphatidate phosphatase; DGK, diacylglycerol kinase; TAGL, triacylglycerol lipase.
