KEGG pathway of cardiac muscle contraction (a) and PI3K-Akt signaling pathway (b)

KEGG pathway of cardiac muscle contraction (a) and PI3K-Akt signaling pathway (b). Myofilament is activated by calcium and bind to troponin C. Troponin combines with tropomyosin and actin through the inhibitory protein troponin I without calcium.
