Identifying myasthenia gravis (MG) risk genes regulated by drugs and miRNAs in the hsa05200 pathway

Identifying myasthenia gravis (MG) risk genes regulated by drugs and miRNAs in the hsa05200 pathway. Orange represents the genes co-regulated by MG, MG risk miRNAs and drugs. Yellow represents the genes regulated by MG and MG risk miRNAs. Pink represents the genes regulated by MG risk miRNAs and drugs. Blue diamonds represent the drugs, whereas green triangles represent the miRNAs.
