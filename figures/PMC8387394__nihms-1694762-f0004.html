Conservation of epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition process in neural crest cells and metastatic cancer

The extracellular matrix (ECM) and Notch signaling pathways involved in NCC development and MC progression. The ECM signaling pathway is involved in the process of EMT in both (A) NCC and (B) MC development. Both systems share similar signaling ligands and downstream transcription factors. The Notch signaling pathway is also involved in the EMT process in (C) NCC and (D) MC development. Both systems share similar signaling ligands and downstream transcription factors, including SNAIL1, beta-CATENIN, SOX2.
