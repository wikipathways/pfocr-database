Molecular Mechanism of Yellow Mushroom (Floccularia luteovirens) Response to Strong Ultraviolet Radiation on the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau

KEGG annotation of DEGs involved in different metabolism pathways. (A) KEGG annotation of proteins encoded by DEGs involved in the phytochrome biosynthesis pathway. The genes whose products are highlighted in red are up-regulated during the FB stages. (B) KEGG annotation of DEGs involved in riboflavin metabolism pathways. Genes highlighted in red are up-regulated during the FB stages. (C) KEGG annotation of DEGs involved in the cell-cycle. DEGs between VM and FB are shown in the cell-cycle pathway at the S, G2, and M phases. Genes whose products are highlighted in red are up-regulated during the FB stages.
