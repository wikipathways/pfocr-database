Metabolomic Analysis Reveals the Mechanisms of Hepatotoxicity Induced by Aflatoxin M1 and Ochratoxin A

Metabolite integrated metabolic pathway analysis in serum of mice treated with individual and combined AFM1 and OTA. (A) DMSO vs. AFM1, (B) DMSO vs. OTA, (C) DMSO vs. AFM1+OTA and (D) the number of changed metabolites in different mycotoxins treatment. Red represents the upregulated metabolites and green represents the downregulated metabolites with criterion of FC > 1.50, and p < 0.05.
