Phosphopeptides from the MOE and FOE phosphoproteomes are represented in some distinct signaling pathways, including Rho signaling for MOE (A), GABA signaling for FOE (B) and amyloid processing for FOE (C)

Phosphopeptides from the MOE and FOE phosphoproteomes are represented in some distinct signaling pathways, including Rho signaling for MOE (A), GABA signaling for FOE (B) and amyloid processing for FOE (C). Within specific pathways blue shading indicates MOE phosphopeptides and orange shading indicates FOE phosphopeptides. The schematic for the amyloid pathway also indicates how some of the major pathways represented by the FOE phosphoproteome (indicated in yellow) are also linked to the amyloid processing.
