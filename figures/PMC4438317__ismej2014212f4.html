Metabolic abilities of Ferrovum-like FKB7 based on the expressed genes, which were predicted from its draft genome assembled from the metagenomic and metatranscriptomic data

Metabolic abilities of Ferrovum-like FKB7 based on the expressed genes, which were predicted from its draft genome assembled from the metagenomic and metatranscriptomic data. Those associated with carbon fixation, nitrogen metabolism, assimilatory sulfur reduction, amino-acid biosynthesis, energy metabolism, transporters, stress response and putative iron oxidation pathway are shown.
