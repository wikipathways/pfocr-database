Diagram of muscle‐growth signaling pathway and differentially expressed genes, thus summarizing the effect of thermal gradient on muscle growth

Diagram of muscle‐growth signaling pathway and differentially expressed genes, thus summarizing the effect of thermal gradient on muscle growth. Signaling pathway diagrams for (a) hyperplasic and (b) hypertrophic transcription events in skeletal muscle. IGF complexes mediating interactions with IGF‐IR at the plasma membrane trigger a signaling cascade that links mRNA activation/inhibition through phosphorylation/dephosphorization events
