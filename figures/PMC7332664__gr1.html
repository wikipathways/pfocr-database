Cores of the HIF pathway and the Hippo pathway

The cores of the HIF pathway and the Hippo pathway. (A) The oxidation states regulate the hydroxylation rate of HIFA, which dictates the fate of HIFA: degradation or transcriptional activation. The HIFA indicates HIF1A and/or HIF2A. (B) Hypoxia counteracts the inhibitory effects of Hippo core kinases on YAP/TAZ, which accelerates nuclear translocation and promotes the transcriptional activation of YAP/TAZ. YAP/TAZ indicates YAP and/or TAZ.
