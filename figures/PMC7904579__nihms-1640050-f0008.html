Applying Genome-wide CRISPR to Identify Known and Novel Genes and Pathways that Modulate Formaldehyde Toxicity

Gene and pathway analysis of mTOR signaling pathway. Data obtained from candidate genes in 150 µM formaldehyde treatment group at day 20 (FDR<0.01). A. List of genes involved in TOR signaling pathway. B. Annotations of all listed genes. C. Clustering analysis showing the interactions between top resistant genes. D. Kegg pathway showing the involvement of corresponding genes in the mTOR signaling pathway.
