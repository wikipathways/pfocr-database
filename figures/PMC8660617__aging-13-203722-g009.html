Prognostic and immune infiltration signatures of proteasome 26S subunit, non-ATPase (PSMD) family genes in breast cancer patients

Cell cycle-related networks correlated with the 26S proteasome delta subunit, non-ATPase 1 (PSMD1) family gene in breast cancer (BRCA). MetaCore pathway analysis of biological processes revealed that pathways related to "Cell cycle_Role of APC in cell cycle regulation" were correlated with BRCA development.
