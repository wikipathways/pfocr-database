Alzheimer pathway regulation within core border ROI. Pathway diagram of Alzheimer disease obtained from KEGG database with color added to reflect significant protein expression level changes specific to the core border ROI (a). Protein colored based on significant (Blue = significant increase; FDR < 0.05) fold change compared to contralateral ROI. The most significant changes center around amyloid processing. An inset of the pathway region where proteins are altered after ischemia is shown (b)
