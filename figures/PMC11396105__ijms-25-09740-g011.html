Schematic diagram reveals key genes and pathways involved in the heterosis formation of EFT

Schematic diagram reveals key genes and pathways involved in the heterosis formation of EFT. This regulatory network contained “Intake regulation”, “TCA cycle”, “Fatty acid synthesis”, “Fatty acid transport”, “Fatty acid oxidation”, “Lipolysis”, “EPA biosynthesis”, “Angiogenesis”, “Epidermal growth”, and “Myogenesis”. The up-regulated and down-regulated genes are shown in red and blue font, respectively. The star marks represented DNA-methylated regulation
