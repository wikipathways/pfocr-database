Research Advances in Multi-Omics on the Traditional Chinese Herb Dendrobium officinale

The biosynthetic pathway for terpenoid indole alkaloids in Dendrobium officinale. The alkaloid biosynthesis contains the precursor formation from the shikimate, MEP, and MVA pathways and the alkaloid pathway. 2P-CDP-MEP, 2-phospho-4-(cytidine 5′-diphospho)-2-C-methyl-D-erythritol;
