Gene expression levels of central carbon metabolic pathways

Gene expression levels of central carbon metabolic pathways. Tricarboxylic acid (TCA) cycle, glyoxylate pathway, gluconeogenesis, glycogenesis and pentose phosphate pathway (PPP) are presented. The comparative analysis includes the log2 fold-change (log2FC) xylose/glucose under carbon limitation conditions. The green label indicates overexpressed enzymes, “fbr” indicates feedback-resistant
