Scheme representing major factors of CWIP and the calcineurin signaling pathway

Scheme representing major factors of CWIP and the calcineurin signaling pathway. Impaired O-mannosylation results in severe growth defect in the absence of factors depicted in red (growth inhibition of 75% or more) and green (growth inhibition of 74% mkk2∆ and 73% rlm1∆). Details are described in . Our screening also revealed Ste20 and Tor1, involved in mating/filamentous growth/sterile vegetative growth signaling and in nutrient sensing, respectively ().
