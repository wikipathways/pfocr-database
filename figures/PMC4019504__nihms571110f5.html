Integrated Analysis of Genome-wide Copy Number Alterations and Gene Expression in MSS, CIMP-negative Colon Cancer

Wnt/β-catenin pathway genes that were significantly differentially expressed in the colon tumor tissue samples are indicated by red or green shading (red for up-regulated and green for down-regulated). The fold-change expression levels in tumor versus adjacent normal tissue are represented by the intensity of shading. Recurrent copy number gain was detected in genomic regions containing the WNT2 (7q31) and MYC (8q24) genes, corresponding to increased expression of the WNT2 and MYC gene.
