Relative adenine and thymine (%AT) DNA content of ergot alkaloid synthesis (EAS) loci

Relative adenine and thymine (%AT) DNA content of ergot alkaloid synthesis (EAS) loci. Gene name abbreviations are as follow: all eas genes = last letter, cloA = B and dmaW = W. Gene names are colored to represent the stage of the pathway for the encoded product (see ). Pseudogenes are represented by Ψ and white-filled arrows. The major pathway end product of each strain is indicated on the right in bold face (product produced) or regular type (product predicted but not yet tested), or in parentheses (product predicted but undetected). Arrows marked with * represent orthologues of C. purpurea AET79176 (GenBank). Cyan bars indicate repeats, and vertical black bars indicate miniature inverted-repeat transposable elements (MITEs). Where present, telomeres are positioned at left.
