Cellular pathways of prostaglandin (PG) metabolism

Cellular pathways of prostaglandin (PG) metabolism. A cell is depicted, showing enzymatic components (coloured boxes) involved in precursor prostaglandin synthesis, terminal prostaglandin synthesis, prostaglandin transport and prostaglandin inactivation, with reactions (thin arrows) and products (open circles).
