Interaction between zinc and selenium bio-fortification and toxic metals (loid) accumulation in food crops

Toxic effects of heavy metals on physiological, biochemical, and molecular properties of the plant. Heavy metals activate several signaling pathways in plant cells. Activation of the calcium signaling pathway and ROS and hormones and MAPKs cause the expression of genes responding to heavy metal stress. Phytohormone levels in the cell change with the production of ROS in the presence of heavy metals.
