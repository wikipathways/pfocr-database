Alternative NOTCH pathway representations

Alternative NOTCH pathway representations. NOTCH signalling pathways are shown that were either pre-defined through manual curation of literature (a – from KEGG) or generated dynamically through known genetic and physical interactions along with other molecular properties (b – from GeneMania). The interaction network defined by GeneMania was saved an imported into Cytoscape (c - ver.3.2.1) and T-ALL patient RPKM values were used to colour nodes based on high/medium/low (red/grey/black) expression level
