Secondary metabolite biosynthetic diversity in the fungal family Hypoxylaceae and Xylaria hypoxylon

Proposed biosynthetic pathway for the formation of cytochalasins in Hypoxylon fragiforme. Putative intermediates are shown in grey. Function of HffD and HffG have been elucidated in a previous study (). The biosynthetic gene cluster (MN477016.1) is shown at the bottom.
