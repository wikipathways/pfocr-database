MiRNAs affect the occurrence and development of EMT in liver cancer cells by activating the TGF-β signaling pathway and cross-talking multiple signaling pathways including PI3 K and MAPK. In addition, the activation of the TGF-β signaling pathway initiates the expression of EMT-TFs genes and induces EMT in liver cancer cells
