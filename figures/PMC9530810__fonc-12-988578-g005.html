Comprehensive analysis of m6A-modified circRNAs in peritoneal metastasis of high grade serious carcinoma of ovary

Pathway analysis of significantly altered m6A-modified circRNAs between the in situ and the peritoneal metastasis lesions of the HGSCO patients. (A) The up-regulated m6A-modified circRNAs involved in HIF-1 pathway. (B) The down-regulated m6A-modified circRNAs play important roles in MAPK signaling pathway.
