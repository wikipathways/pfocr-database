Simplified schematic of the kynurenine pathway

Simplified schematic of the kynurenine pathway. IDO, indoleamine 2,3‐dioxygenase; TDO, tryptophan 2,3‐dioxygenase; KAT, kynurenine amino transferase; KYNU, kynureninase; KMO, kynurenine 3‐monooxygenase; 3‐HAO, 3‐hydroxyanthranilic acid 3,4‐dioxygenase; ACMSD, α‐amino‐β‐carboxymuconate‐ε‐semialdehyde decarboxylase; QPRT, quinolinate phosphoribosyl transferase; NAD, nicotinamide dinucleotide. Metabolites shaded in blue are known to have neuroactive properties. Metabolites with abbreviations were analyzed in this study. [Color figure can be viewed at wileyonlinelibrary.com]
