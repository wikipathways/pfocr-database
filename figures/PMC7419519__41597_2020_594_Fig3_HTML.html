KEGG pathway mapping of CHO global spectral library

KEGG pathway mapping of CHO global spectral library. KEGG pathway analysis was applied to study the protein coverage in the (a) Protein processing in endoplasmic reticulum and (b) Cell cycle pathways. The proteins represented in the CHO global spectral library were boxed in yellow color while those confidently identified in the CHO-K1 SWATH-MS data set were highlighted in red color.
