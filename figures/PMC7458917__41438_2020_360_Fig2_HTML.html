IAA- and ABA-responsive transcription factor CgMYB58 upregulates lignin biosynthesis and triggers juice sac granulation in pummelo

Heatmap of transcript levels in FPKM for genes that are involved in the lignin biosynthetic pathway and are distinctly expressed during five developmental stages between three cultivars. S1 to S5 represent 55, 85, 115, 145, and 205 DPA, respectively. The darker red color indicates a higher expression of the gene. The pale color shows a lower or lack of expression
