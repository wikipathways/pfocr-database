Natural products that modulate specific proteins in the molecular pathway of inducing and inhibiting ferroptosis

Natural products that modulate specific proteins in the molecular pathway of inducing and inhibiting ferroptosis. This pathway is drawn by considering the information from many literature reports [173,174,175,176,177,178,179,180,181,182,183,184,185,186,187,188,189]. Natural products that target these specific proteins have been described in Table 1. The ferroptosis-inducing and inhibiting natural products are indicated in black and red, respectively. Although these natural products potentially target the ferroptosis signaling pathway, their potential impact on ferroptosis still warrants detailed investigation. The ferroptosis-inducing and inhibiting targets (as described in Section 1.3.1 and Section 1.3.2) are indicated in blue and red, respectively. Natural products that may induce the targets are indicated by an arrow line, while natural products that may inhibit the targets are indicated by a “T” line.
