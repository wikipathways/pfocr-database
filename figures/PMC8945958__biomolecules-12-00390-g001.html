Ferroptosis in Cardiovascular Diseases: Current Status, Challenges, and Future Perspectives

Regulatory pathways of ferroptosis. Ferroptosis is an iron-dependent, new kind of cell death. Regulatory pathways are complex, which can mainly be divided into several parts: iron metabolism; lipid peroxidation; Xc-GSH-GPX4 pathway; FSP1–CoQ10–NAD(P)H pathway; GCH1–BH4–DHFR pathway. Mitochondria and energy stress also play an important role.
