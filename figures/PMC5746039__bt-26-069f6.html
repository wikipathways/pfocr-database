Glycolysis and its branching pathways

Glycolysis and its branching pathways. The enzymes involved in the pathways are in bold. Glycolysis and its branching metabolic pathways are in italics. PPP: pentose phosphate pathway, FAS: fatty acid synthesis, TCA: tricarboxylic acid cycle, HK1/2: hexose kinase 1/2, PFK1: phophofructokinase 1, PKM1/2: pyruvate kinase 1/2, LDHA/B: lactate dehydrogenase A/B, Glucose-6P: glucose-6-phophate, Fructose-6P: fructose-6-phosphate, Fructose-1,6P2: fructose-1,6-bisphosphate, DHAP: dihydroxyacetone phosphate, Glycerol-3P: glycerol-3-phosphate, Glyceraldehyde-3P: glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate, and PEP: phosphoenolpyruvate.
