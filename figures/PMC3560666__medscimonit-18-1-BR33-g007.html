Pathway in MAS and differentially expressed genes: as shown in Figure, IGSF4, PTPRC (CD45) and CD3D belong to a family of cell surface adhesion molecules. The IGSF4 was down-regulated and CD45 and CD3D were up-regulated. In addition, the CD45 of hematopoietic cell lineage, CD3D (cAMP protein kinase inhibitor) and CSF2 belong to members of T cell receptor signaling pathway.
