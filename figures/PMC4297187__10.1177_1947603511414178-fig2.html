Schematic summary of the results obtained from the microarray analysis. Genes in red boxes negatively regulate the WNT signaling pathway, whereas the green boxes indicate a positive regulation of the network. A “±” sign after the gene name refers to a positive (higher gene expression in OA cartilage) or negative (lower gene expression in OA cartilage) fold change (FC) in the microarray analysis.
