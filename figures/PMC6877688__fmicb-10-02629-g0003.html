KEGG pathway enrichment analysis of photosynthesis related genes in S6, S72, and R (control) groups

KEGG pathway enrichment analysis of photosynthesis related genes in S6, S72, and R (control) groups. Down-regulated and up-regulated genes are indicated in green and red, respectively. Both up- and down-regulated genes are shown in yellow. S6 and S72, A. minutum cells exposed to nutrient deficient conditions for 6 and 72 h, respectively.
