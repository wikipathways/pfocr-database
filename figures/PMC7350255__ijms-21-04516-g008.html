Representation of the KEGG protein process in endoplasmic reticulum pathway

Representation of the KEGG protein process in endoplasmic reticulum pathway. Upregulated DEGs genes in response to Aβ-S8C stimulation in AD2-4 neuronal cultures are shown as red boxes, upregulated DEGs genes in response to Aβ-S8C stimulation in CON 8 and AD2-4 neuronal cultures are shown in yellow boxes and downregulated DEGs in AD2-4 neuronal cultures are shown as green boxes.
