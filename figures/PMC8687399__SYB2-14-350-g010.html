Petri Net modelling approach for analysing the behaviour of Wnt/ ‐catenin and Wnt/ Ca2+ signalling pathways in arrhythmogenic right ventricular cardiomyopathy

SPN model of Wnt/ for ARVC, this SPN shows the overload in cytoplasm and ER stress activates the apoptotic pathway by (IRE1, ASK, TRAF2, JNK to inhibit the Bcl2) which further leads to mitochondrial apoptosis through activation of caspases and mCalpain
