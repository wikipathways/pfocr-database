Calcium (or Ca2+) signaling pathway in T

The calcium (or Ca2+) signaling pathway in T. leucocephalus. The expression of genes in the calcium signaling pathway is indicated with different colors. Red denotes significant gene family expansion, and orange denotes nonsignificant gene family expansion. Green denotes expression in the signal of 12 transcriptome samples. White denotes no expression in the signal of 12 transcriptome samples
