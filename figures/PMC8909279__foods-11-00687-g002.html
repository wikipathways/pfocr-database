Nutrients against Glucocorticoid-Induced Muscle Atrophy

Anti-muscle atrophy signaling pathway regulated by multiple nutrients in a glucocorticoid-induced muscle atrophy model. The figure shows the major pathways that help maintain muscle protein turnover rate by inducing protein synthesis and inhibiting protein degradation.
