Major metabolic pathways upregulated in the cells utilizing 3-hydroxybenzoate, 4-hydroxybenzoate or hydroquinone as a sole carbon source

(A,C) The glyoxylate cycle, β-oxidation, and (B,D) modified β-oxidation pathway [] are depicted in a simplified form. The expression profiles obtained by the RNA-Seq (A,B; ) and LC-MS/MS analyses (C,D; ) are shown. The three squares illustrate the gene expression changes on different hydroxyaromatic substrates compared to galactose as indicated in the legend in the upper left corner on panels (A) and (C). Only the genes whose transcription was overexpressed on at least one hydroxyaromatic substrate are shown. Note that not all enzymes listed in and Figs are shown on the scheme.
