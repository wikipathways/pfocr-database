Beyond DNA repair and chromosome instability—Fanconi anaemia as a cellular senescence-associated syndrome

Schematic representation of FANC proteins in the three groups: proteins of group I associated with FAAP proteins constitute the FANC core complex, which allows monoubiquitylation of the FANCD2-FANCI complex (group II), which enables DNA incision, TLS (translesion synthesis), ICL elimination and replication rescue by homologous recombination, functions performed by group III FANC proteins.
