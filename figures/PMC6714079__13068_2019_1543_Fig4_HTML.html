Central carbon metabolism of T

Central carbon metabolism of T. cutaneum B3 for lipid synthesis. Up- and down-regulation of genes encoding key enzymes and transcriptional regulators are highlighted in red and blue colors, respectively. Dashed arrows indicate multiple enzymatic steps in the metabolic pathway.
