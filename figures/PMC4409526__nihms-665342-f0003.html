Diagram depicting altered mRNA expression of molecules involved in the regulation of the actin cytoskeleton by NMDA signaling pathways in schizophrenia

Diagram depicting altered mRNA expression of molecules involved in the regulation of the actin cytoskeleton by NMDA signaling pathways in schizophrenia. Green represents increased mRNA expression, red represents decreased expression and no color represents no significant expression change. The diagram was generated using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis using data from the qRT-PCR study (double circles, McLean 75 cohort) and microarray data obtained from the DLPFC of schizophrenia subjects in the McLean 66 cohort. Both PARD3 (par-3 family cell polarity regulator) and ARHGEF6 (Rac/Cdc42 guanine nucleotide exchange factor (GEF) 6) regulate dendritic spine formation (; ).
