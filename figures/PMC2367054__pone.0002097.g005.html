Uncovering Genes with Divergent mRNA-Protein Dynamics in Streptomyces coelicolor

Proteome data (red lines) and transcriptome data (blue lines) are plotted in the same graph. The horizontal axis of each plot represents time ranging from 7 h to 38 h. The fold-change ratios of genes in the vertical axis span a value of 6 in log2 scale, except for actinorhodin, undecylprodigiosin, type I polyketide biosynthesis and phosphate starvation response genes where the vertical axis spans a value of 8 in log2 scale. Each plot begins with log ratio  =  0 at 7 h. Single peptide protein identifications are indicated with an asterisk (⋆), and their MS/MS spectra are provided . Pathways and corresponding enzymes were identified from KEGG database . Only major reactions in each pathway are shown. Gene names and SCO numbers are provided in and .
