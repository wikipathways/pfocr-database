Compounds and hitting targets on ICH

Compounds and hitting targets on ICH. It showed an intuitional three-level structure that multi-compound hit groups of targets through multi-pathway to play the regulative role. All pathways were involved in compound control process. The nodes are pathway protein targets and the surrounding represent compounds of LP.
