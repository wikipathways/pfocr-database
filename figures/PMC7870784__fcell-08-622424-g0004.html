Depiction of MAPK signaling pathway dysregulation and miRNAs in the KEGG database

A depiction of MAPK signaling pathway dysregulation and miRNAs in the KEGG database. (A) The green-colored background and miRNAs are representative of miRNA target genes that are known to contain miRSNPs located within their 3′-UTR regions. (B) A model illustrating the potential underlying mechanisms of rs28457673 that likely played an important role in influencing the levels of expression of IGF1R via associated interactions with members of the miR-15/16/195/497 family.
