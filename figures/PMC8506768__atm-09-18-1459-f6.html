Bioinformatic analysis of differentially expressed genes and pathways in idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis

The 11 differentially expressed genes (DEGs) of the 50 DEGs associated with PD-L1 expression and the PD-1 checkpoint pathway in cancer, which is an enrichment in the non-small cell lung cancer (NSCLC) signaling pathway. The genes in the red boxes are up-regulated; the genes in the blue boxes are down-regulated.
