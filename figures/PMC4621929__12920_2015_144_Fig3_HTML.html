STRING network analysis reveals a database of known and predicted protein interactions

STRING network analysis reveals a database of known and predicted protein interactions. The interactions include direct (physical) and indirect (functional) associations. The network has been simplified for clear illustration of genes of interest. Stronger associations are represented by thicker lines. The significant genes were mainly TP53, AKT1, SRC, EGF, EGFR, PIK3CG, EP300 and FYN, and enriched functional categories included focal adhesion, cytokine–cytokine receptor interactions, the ERBB signaling pathway, the cell cycle, adherens junctions and the JAK–STAT signaling pathway
