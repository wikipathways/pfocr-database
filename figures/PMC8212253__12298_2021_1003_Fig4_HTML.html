Tolerance mechanisms in maize identified through phenotyping and transcriptome analysis in response to water deficit stress

Upregulated protein processing in endoplasmic reticulum pathway genes in the genotype SNJ201126. Genes indicated in green: Upregulated in response to water deficit stress only in SNJ201126. Genes indicated in blue: Upregulated in response to water deficit stress in both the genotypes. However, the upregulation was several folds higher in the genotype SNJ201126 (color figure online)
