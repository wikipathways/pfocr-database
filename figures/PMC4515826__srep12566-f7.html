Genome-wide mutational spectra analysis reveals significant cancer-specific heterogeneity

(a) large intestine cancer; (b) lung cancer; (c) endometrial cancer. Each gene pair with exclusive pattern implies that related genes tend to participate in the same cell signaling pathway (see text). The statistical analysis recapitulated the well-known gene network modules, such as the KRAS/BRAF in colon cancer, EGFR/KRAS/TP53 in lung cancer, and TP53/PTEN in endometrium. Refer to for the exclusive gene pairs detected in other cancers.
