De novo transcriptome analysis unravels tissue-specific expression of candidate genes involved in major secondary metabolite biosynthetic pathways of Plumbago zeylanica: implication for pharmacological potential

Differentially expressed unigenes (DEGs) identified in the “Ubiquinone and other Terpenoid-quinone Biosynthesis” pathway. The fully shaded red boxes represent gene transcripts with high expression in roots of P. zeylanica. The red colored column also indicates the biosynthesis of specific metabolic compounds at high level. This color-coded map corresponds to KEGG pathway: map00130
