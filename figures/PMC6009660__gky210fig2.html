Regulatory network generated by TGMI algorithm for the lignin biosynthesis pathway

Regulatory network generated by TGMI algorithm for the Arabidopsis thaliana lignin biosynthesis pathway using the microarray data generated from hypocotyledonous stem tissues under the short-day condition. Green nodes represent pathway genes. All other nodes are TFs regardless of what colors they are. Light coral nodes represent positive known pathway regulators in current knowledgebase with the literature evidence being provided.
