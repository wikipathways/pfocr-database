Hypothetical scheme of CA action in mammalian cells

Hypothetical scheme of CA action in mammalian cells. CA activates the Nrf2 pathway only at a low dose (≤20 μM) but activates both Nrf2 and ATF4 pathways at a high-dose (≥50 μM). Induced Nrf2 and ATF4 independently and cooperatively up-regulate CA-inducible gene expression (arrows pointing up) to exhibit physiological effects, such as stress response (bold white arrow). The arrows indicate activation and T-arrows show inhibition.
