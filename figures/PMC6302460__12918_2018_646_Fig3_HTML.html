Comparison of four TPs in breast cancer subtypes

Comparison of four TPs in breast cancer subtypes. The ranks of p53 signaling pathway (a), Focal adhesion (b), mTOR signaling pathway (c) and PI3K-Akt signaling pathway (d). The bar plot above describes the ranks of the TP in four subtypes and the below one shows the changed molecular and the hot member of the pathway
