Synthetic CO2 fixation pathways for acetyl-CoA synthesis

Synthetic CO2 fixation pathways for acetyl-CoA synthesis. (A) rGly pathway coupled FDH. (B) Formolase pathway coupled FDH. (C) SACA pathway coupled FDH and partial formolase path. (D) MCC pathway coupled FDH and partial formolase path. (E) MCG cycle coupled CBB cycle.
