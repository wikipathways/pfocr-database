Modular Nature of Dendritic Cell Responses to Commensal and Pathogenic Fungi

Following DC-ATLAS pathway structure, receptor/sensing modules, transduction modules and the outcome modules were represented for (A) Dectin-1 signaling and (B) DC-SIGN signaling. For the outcome modules the cytokines measured within the manuscript are indicated (bold). These graphs present both the vertical and horizontal resolution of DC-TLAS pathways. For the complete BCML pathways, please see and . Note: the TLP1-mediated signalosome upon DC-SIGN engagement recently described has not been drawn to simplify view of the modules.
