Ingenuity Pathway Analysis of Molecular Activation Based on Gene Expression Profiles(A) Gene expression data were analyzed by Ingenuity Pathway Analysis. Molecules that were activated by DSS treatment are displayed according to Z scores (extent of activation based on gene expression profile), which were determined by comparison of a non-DSS-treated mouse with a DSS+NC mouse on day 4 (left column). Note that sCA-miR-29a (center column) or sCA-miR-29b (right column) reversed the activation of the molecules to various extents. Many interferon (IFN) pathway-associated molecules were identified (green). (B) An intracellular signaling pathway map shows the production of inflammatory cytokines and IFN because of interactions of ligands with Toll-like receptors (TLRs). Genes activated by DSS treatment are shown in red. (C) Genes activated by DSS treatment are superimposed on known IFN signaling pathways. Orange indicates genes that are predicted to be activated by Ingenuity Pathway Analysis. Purple indicates upregulated genes according to mRNA array analysis.
