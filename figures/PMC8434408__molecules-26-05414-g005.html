Nanoparticle-Encapsulated Camptothecin: Epigenetic Modulation in DNA Repair Mechanisms in Colon Cancer Cells

Visualization of the GSEA-enriched genes from the leading-edge subset of the mismatch repair pathway in CPT-CEF-treated colon cancer cells. The pathway was extracted from the KEGG database, and the GSEA-enriched genes obtained from the data set were highlighted red. The underexpression of these genes is predicted to have an impact in the downstream signaling involved during mismatch repair.
