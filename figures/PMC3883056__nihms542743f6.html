Transcription Factors in Long-Term Memory and Synaptic Plasticity

A multitude of stimuli and environmental insults including cytokines, stress and growth factors can regulate a considerable number of intracellular processes that critically involve AP-1. The appropriate composition of subunits in the AP-1 dimer is determined by the nature of the extracellular stimulus and by the MAPK signaling pathway that is consequently activated [From Panomics © 2007 ()].
