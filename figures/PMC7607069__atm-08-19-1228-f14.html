PPI network of TP53 signaling pathway genes (GeneMANIA)

PPI network of TP53 signaling pathway genes (GeneMANIA). PPI network and functional analysis showing the enriched gene sets in the target network of TP53, TP53BP1, MDM2, MDM4, CKN2A, and CKN2B. Distinct colors of the network edge indicate the bioinformatics methods applied: physical interactions, co-expression, predicted, co-localization, pathway, genetic interactions, and shared protein domains. The distinct colors for the network nodes show the biological functions of the genes. PPI, protein-protein interaction; TP53, tumor protein 53.
