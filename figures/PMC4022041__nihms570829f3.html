Genes involved in the TGF beta signaling pathway after 5 days

Genes involved in the TGF beta signaling pathway after 5 days. After the overrepresented 5-day gene lists were submitted to the DAVID database (http://www.DAVID.org), 19 overrepresented genes were indicated as involved in the TGF-beta pathway as separate sets of data. Those 19 genes were labeled with red stars in this picture.
