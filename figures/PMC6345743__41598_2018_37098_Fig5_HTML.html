Comparing ECM-receptor interaction pathway for (a) DA and (b) non-DA neurons based on DEGs and DEmiR

Comparing ECM-receptor interaction pathway for (a) DA and (b) non-DA neurons based on DEGs and DEmiR. (a) For the DA neuron, the ECM-receptor interaction pathway was altered by DEGs (p < 0.05) and DEmiRs (p ≪ 0.001) with three possible miRNA regulatory points. (b) For the non-DA neuron, the pathway was similarly significant for DEGs (p < 0.05) and DEmiRs (p ≪ 0.001) with one miRNA targeting many genes. Purple nodes are significant DEmiRs, blue nodes are significant DEGs, and yellow nodes are non-significantly regulated genes.
