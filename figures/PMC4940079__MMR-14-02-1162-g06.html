Differentially expressed gene distribution in rno03320 pathway

Differentially expressed gene distribution in rno03320 pathway. Differentially expressed genes in the in the ginsenoside-Rg3 treatment group compared with the DN control group were shown to be associated with the PPAR signaling pathway. Bright green, upregulated genes; pale green, species specific genes. PPAR, peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor.
