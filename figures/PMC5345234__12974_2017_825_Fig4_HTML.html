CXCL5-centered gene network upregulated in astrocytes in response to Meth exposure

CXCL5-centered gene network upregulated in astrocytes in response to Meth exposure. Shades of red represent the level of upregulation of each gene, where light red is less upregulated and darker red is more upregulated. Genes that are connected by a green line are in the same pathway, whereas orange lines connect genes that are colocalized, yellow lines mean coexpression, gray lines mean genetic interactions, blue lines mean shared protein domains, and red lines mean physical interactions
