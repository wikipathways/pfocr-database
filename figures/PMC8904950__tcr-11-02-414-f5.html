Knockdown of TRIM44 inhibits the progression of ovarian cancer and is related to the FOXM1-EZH2 signaling pathway

The potential gene network interacting with TRIM44 was also analyzed using IPA (International Profession Certification Association), and FOXM1-EZH2 signaling is a downstream pathway regulated by TRIM44.
