M6A methylation profiles of immune cells in type 1 diabetes mellitus

Schematic overviews of T1DM-related signaling pathways enriched with hypomethylated mRNAs. (A) PI3K-Akt signaling pathway. (B) MAPK signaling pathway. The orange nodes represent hypomethylated mRNAs. The green nodes represent other mRNAs in the pathways that are not regulated by m6A.
