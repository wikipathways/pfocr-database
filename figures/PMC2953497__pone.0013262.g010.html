Tumor Necrosis FactorA Regulates Distinct Molecular Pathways and Gene Networks in Cultured Skeletal Muscle Cells

Schematic representation of gene network obtained from Ingenuity pathway analysis tool with differentially regulated genes by TNF-α with p-values ≤0.05 and ≥1.2-fold. The solid lines connecting molecules here represent a direct relation and dotted lines are representation of an indirect relation. The genes shown in red color boxes are those which are up-regulated whereas down-regulated genes are shown in green color boxes.
