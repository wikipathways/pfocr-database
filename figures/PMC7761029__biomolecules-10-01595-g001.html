Elucidated MIA biosynthetic pathway in Catharanthus roseus

The elucidated MIA biosynthetic pathway in Catharanthus roseus. Enzymes catalyzing pathways in the aerial parts of the plant are depicted in green, and enzymes in the roots are depicted in orange. MIA names highlighted in light blue circles have also been identified in Vinca minor. Depicted in the grey box is the rearrangement of the T30 epoxide product, forming an eburnamine-vincamine like skeleton, in cases where T3R is silenced as described in []. The different MIA scaffold classes are depicted by MIA names written in dark blue (iboga-type), light blue (strychnos-type), brown (corynanthe) and purple (aspidosperma). Uncharacterized reactions are depicted with dashed arrows.
