Sugar metabolism pathway components and corresponding heat-map expression profiles of sugar-related genes in 6-BA treated buds of ‘Nagafu No.2’ apple

a Sugar metabolism pathway components and corresponding heat-map expression profiles of sugar-related genes in 6-BA treated buds of ‘Nagafu No.2’ apple. b Changes in the levels of sucrose, fructose, glucose, and starch 6-BA treated and control buds of ‘Nagafu No. 2’ apple during floral transition. c Heat-map expression profiles of flowering DEGs in 6-BA treated buds of ‘Nagafu No.2’ apple. Bars represent the standard error (n = 3). * represented significant difference at 0.05 levels
