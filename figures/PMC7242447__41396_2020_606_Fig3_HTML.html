Genetic variation and evolutionary history of a mycorrhizal fungus regulate the currency of exchange in symbiosis with the food security crop cassava

Genes commonly upregulated with all fungal isolates are represented in between their products. Colored squares next to each gene represent the log2fold change obtained with Kallisto and DESeq2. The asterisks represent the significance of the change (* < 0.05, ** < 0.01, *** < 0.001). Numbers within each colored square represent the number of indicators of a significant phylogenetic signal (out of five indicators) between the fungal phylogeny and the transcription of a given cassava gene. Genes that were shown to play a role, or putatively play a role, in fatty acid biosynthesis are represented. For a description of each gene acronym see Data .
