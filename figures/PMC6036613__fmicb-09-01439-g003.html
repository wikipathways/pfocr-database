NFKB pathway induced by LaW37 in Caco-2 cells

Overview of differential gene expression within NF-κB pathway induced by LaW37 in Caco-2 cells. Effects of 6 h incubation of Lactobacillus acidophilus W37 (LaW37) (107 CFU/mL) on Caco-2 genes related NF-κB pathway signaling indicate a downregulation of this pathway. Red indicates upregulation and green indicates downregulation (p < 0.05).
