The network model was constructed to elucidate the biological interpretations of hub proteins in the core network. Functional protein–protein interaction (PPI) network of the 135 proteins between control and model group was constructed. Top 35 nodes ranked by MCC. The node fill colour bar represent up-regulated (red) or down-regulated (green). The node text colour bar represents the p-value.
