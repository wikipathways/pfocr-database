Autoimmune response signatures for ovarian cancer

Autoimmune response signatures for ovarian cancer. Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) for immunoglobulin (Ig)-bound proteins that had a case–control ratio > 1.2 in early stage ovarian cancer and for which the protein target was identified ovarian cancer cell lines (A). (B) and (C) represent the top 2 IPA-derived networks based on Ig-bound protein features described in . (D) represents IPA-derived network based on OVCAR8 MHC-II bound peptidome. TP53 and MYC are marked as red and blue, respectively.
