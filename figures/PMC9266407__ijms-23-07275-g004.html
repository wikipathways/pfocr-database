Evaluation of Adverse Effects of Resorbable Hyaluronic Acid Fillers: Determination of Macrophage Responses

Visualization of the gene expression changes for M0 macrophages derived from THP-1 cells exposed to Perfectha® filler extracts on the cell cycle pathway. Cells were exposed for 24 h to extracts of the various HA fillers. At 24 h, the cells were harvested and RNA isolated for cDNA preparation and microarray gene analysis.
