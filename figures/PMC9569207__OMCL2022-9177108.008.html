Therapeutic Roles of Cinnamaldehyde against Cardiovascular Diseases

CA displays inhibitory roles in cellular death via suppressing the mitochondrial-dependent apoptotic pathway. Oxidative stress and inflammatory cascades induce activities increase of p53 and NF-κB, both of which contribute to level elevation of Bax. While PI3K/Akt and Nrf2 signaling axis induce content increase of Bcl-2 and level reduction of Bax on the membrane, which weaken Cyt C release into the cytosol and then disrupt the activation of caspase-9 and caspase-3.
