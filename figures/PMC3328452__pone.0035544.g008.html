Identification of Protein Networks Involved in the Disease Course of Experimental Autoimmune Encephalomyelitis, an Animal Model of Multiple Sclerosis

The DLG4-KCNMA1 network (Panel A), APP-ACTB network (Panel B) and AGT-TP53 network (Panel C) are represented. These networks were obtained using the IPA-KB by linking proteins from the data-set (75 unique proteins) to the focus proteins. Nodes containing proteins identified in the dataset have a grey fill.
