Prognostic and immune infiltration signatures of proteasome 26S subunit, non-ATPase (PSMD) family genes in breast cancer patients

Cell cycle-related networks correlated with the 26S proteasome delta subunit, non-ATPase 14 (PSMD14) family gene in breast cancer (BRCA). MetaCore pathway analysis of biological processes revealed that pathways related to "DNA damage_ATMATR regulation of G2M checkpoint cytoplasmic signaling" were significantly associated with BRCA development.
