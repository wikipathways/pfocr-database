The formation of trans-prenyl diphosphates from and in these respective cellular compartments results in a spatially distinct pool of plant polyisoprenoids: Cytosolic is utilized for dolichol biosynthesis and the plastidial pool of is the precursor for polyprenol biosynthesis. Polyprenols, as well as other -derived isoprenoids, are incorporated into the thylakoid membranes and also into the envelope membranes of plastids.
