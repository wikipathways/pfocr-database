PPI network and signaling pathway analysis

The PPI network and signaling pathway analysis. (A) The PPI network, as shown in the interaction view, generated by the STRING database. Black squares indicate the protein clusters, and the red square the controlling hub, MAP3K4. (B) The computed predicted signaling pathways that are activated in RANKL-treated RAW 264.7 cells.
