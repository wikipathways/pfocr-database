Regulation and mechanism of YAP/TAZ in the mechanical microenvironment of stem cells

Signaling pathway by which stiffness regulates YAP/TAZ. RAP2 is a key intracellular mechanotransducer that relays extracellular mechanical signals for transcriptional regulation through the Hippo pathway. Extracellular matrix stiffness acts via RAP2 and its downstream Hippo kinase cascade to modulate a YAP/TAZ-mediated mechanoresponsive transcriptome.
