c-Rel Is a Myeloid Checkpoint for Cancer Immunotherapy

a, b, Results from ingenuity Pathway analysis of the RNA-seq data of bone marrorw-derived WT and Rel−/− MDSCs, showing upregulatated “infla,, atory response” genes(a, red) and downregulated Cebpb downstream genes (b, green) in Rel−/− MDSCs. Statistical significance was determined by calculated a right-tailed Fisher’s Exact Test. All data are pooled from 3 independent experiments.
