Interaction network of proteins underexpressed in in vitro matured cumulus complexes (interaction confidence: high (> 0.7), database matches n = 82)

Interaction network of proteins underexpressed in in vitro matured cumulus complexes (interaction confidence: high (> 0.7), database matches n = 82). Highly enriched KEGG pathway in the group of underrepresented proteins is the complement and coagulation cascade (red nodes, pathway ID 04610; n = 21; false discovery rate 1.3e− 32). The proteins of the complement cascade are represented by the red nodes within the yellow circle (n = 8), whereas the proteins of the coagulation cascade are within the blue circle (n = 13)
