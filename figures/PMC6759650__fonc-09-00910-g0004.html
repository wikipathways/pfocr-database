Mechanistic network analysis predicts multiple affected signaling networks

Mechanistic network analysis predicts multiple affected signaling networks. (A) Illustration of the ERBB2, (B) FOXM1, (C) ESR1, and (D) IGFBP2 mechanistic networks with predicted activated state of the network based on transcriptome data with subsequent predicted effects on downstream effector molecules. Figure legend illustrate the relationship between molecules within the network.
