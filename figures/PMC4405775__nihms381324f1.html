Dependence on signaling pathways based upon tumor suppressor status

Dependence on signaling pathways based upon tumor suppressor status. Tumors that are deficient in p16ink4a are more likely to use a reactive oxygen/rac/NFkB signaling pathway, while tumors that have mutant p53 are more likely to use a noncanonical NFkB signaling pathway and phosphoinositol-3 kinase dependent but Akt independent pathway.
