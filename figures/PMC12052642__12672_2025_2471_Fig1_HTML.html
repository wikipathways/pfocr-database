Schematic view of principal anti-tumor effects of resveratrol on different cancer-related mechanisms

A schematic view of principal anti-tumor effects of resveratrol on different cancer-related mechanisms. Resveratrol exerts anti-tumor effects by (1) inhibiting cancer cell invasion, metastasis, and stemness. (2) Suppressing cell proliferation and EMT. (3) Promoting oxidative stress and apoptosis (4) targeting key pathways like PI3K/AKT, Wnt/β-catenin, Hedgehog, and Hippo-YAP
