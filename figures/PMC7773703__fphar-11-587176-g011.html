Proposed Pathway of BJF for the Treatment of Skeletal Muscle Dysfunction in COPD

The Proposed Pathway of BJF for the Treatment of Skeletal Muscle Dysfunction in COPD. BJF improves skeletal muscle and mitochondrial function in COPD rats and L6 cells by promoting mitochondrial biogenesis and suppressing mitophagy via the AMPK pathway.
