Inflammation Stages in the Acute Lung Injury in Mice

The 3 major groupings of mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPkinase) pathways are activated rapidly after OA injection. Genes belonging to the extracellular signal-related kinases (ERK) (blue), the Jun amino-terminal kinases (pink) and the p38 proteins (green) are up-regulated early after OA injection. These genes are represented in bold.
