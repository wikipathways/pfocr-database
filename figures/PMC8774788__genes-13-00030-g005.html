Metformin Affects the Transcriptomic Profile of Chicken Ovarian Cancer Cells

Effect of metformin on epithelial–mesenchymal transition predicted by IPA. Up-stream analysis in IPA predicts the activation/inhibition of various transcription factors. (a,b) Transcription factors involved in EMT, NFKB, and STAT3 are predicted to be inhibited due to the downregulation of MMP7 and AICDA. (c) Canonical WNT signaling pathway involved in EMT was overlaid with dataset molecules that lead to a predicted inhibition of EMT acting downregulation of SNAIl-2 (SLUG). The key for the shape, color, direction, and molecule types in the above pathways is provided in .
