DISTINCT SPECTRUM OF COPY NUMBER ABERRATIONS IN PAEDIATRIC HIGH GRADE GLIOMAS

Signalling pathway heatmap of interactions defined by the TCGA (). Genes with amplifications are shown in red, genes with focal deletion are shown in blue. The overall frequency of copy number alteration in paediatric high grade glioma for each pathway is listed, and is significantly lower than in adults (25% RTK/PI3K, 19% p53 and 22% RB versus 59%, 70% and 66% for adult glioblastoma respectively).
