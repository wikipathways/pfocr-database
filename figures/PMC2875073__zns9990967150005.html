Albumin alters TGFB pathway gene expression

Albumin alters TGF-β pathway gene expression. TGF-β pathway map generated with GENMAPP software illustrating significant changes (>1.5 or <−1.5-fold change) in gene expression after albumin treatment compared with TGF-β1 treatment. Yellow-labeled genes represent genes up or downregulated after albumin treatment, blue-labeled genes represent genes up or downregulated after TGF-β1 treatment, and green-labeled genes represent genes up or downregulated after both treatments.
