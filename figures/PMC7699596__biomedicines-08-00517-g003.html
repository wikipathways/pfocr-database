Key autophagy pathway and its regulators (natural compounds) for treating diverse cancers

Key autophagy pathway and its regulators (natural compounds) for treating diverse cancers. This figure is modified from Wang et al. []. Natural compounds activate autophagy by several autophagic signaling targets and show a close relationship with cancer development and control. Multiple signaling pathways are activated or inhibited by natural compounds to modulate cancer cells.
