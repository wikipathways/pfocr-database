Metabolomic analysis of serum alpha-tocopherol among men in the Alpha-Tocopherol, Beta-Carotene Cancer Prevention (ATBC) Study

Gaussian graphical models of metabolites among associated chemical super-pathways and sub-pathways most related to serum alpha-tocopherol concentration in the study. Metabolites are drawn as hexagons, and the pairs with an absolute value of conditional correlation ≥0.2 are connected by a line. The colors represent the association directions of conditional correlations, with pink indicating positive conditional correlations, and blue indicating negative conditional correlations. Magnitudes of the conditional correlations are represented by line width (i.e., wider lines for stronger correlations). Metabolites with an “●” indicates an inverse association with serum α-tocopherol and “✶” indicates that the association met the Bonferroni correction threshold p<2.79 × 10−5. Enzyme on the pathway between metabolites are indicated by “*”.
