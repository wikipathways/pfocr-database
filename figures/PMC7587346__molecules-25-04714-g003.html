Proposed metabolic pathway under the lowest mineral supply for 21 days in Perilla frutescens

The proposed metabolic pathway under the lowest mineral supply for 21 days in Perilla frutescens. Significantly altered metabolites are represented by up and down arrows for each mineral-deficient condition depending on whether their levels increased or decreased, respectively. The pathway was modified from KEGG (http://www.genome.jp/kegg). N: nitrogen; K: potassium; Mg: magnesium.
