Transcriptome Analysis Reveals Novel Entry Mechanisms and a Central Role of SRC in Host Defense during High Multiplicity Mycobacterial Infection

Shown are AhR signaling, the NRF2-mediated oxidative stress response, and the NLRP3 inflammasome activation pathway regulated by ITGA5/ITGAV and SRC axis. Up-regulated genes downstream of ITGA5/ITGAV and SRC were linked to these three identified signaling pathways with data only displayed from BCG infection. For details on gene abbreviations and fold changes compared with control, see . The network is graphically represented as nodes (gene products) and edges (the biological relationship between nodes). Red-shaded nodes represent up-regulated genes.
