KEGG GO pathways of selected apple Full-length cDNA OvereXpressing (FOX) Arabidopsis mutant lines

KEGG GO pathways of selected apple Full-length cDNA OvereXpressing (FOX) Arabidopsis mutant lines. Pathways reflect known genes from Malus × domestica but are not necessarily specific to M. × domestica. Gene names in red indicate apple FOX inserts. (a) Plant hormone signaling pathways and MAPK/cold signaling pathway. (b) Plant pathogen and MAPK signaling pathways. (c) Plant circadian rhythm pathways. Adapted from KEGG [,].
