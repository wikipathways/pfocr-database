Colorectal cancer metastasis signaling pattern

Colorectal cancer metastasis signaling pattern. Colorectal cancer metastasis signaling is the the most commonly mutated canonical pathway, and includes Wnt, phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K)/AKT and transforming growth factor (TGF)-β/SMAD signaling. The key mutated genes detected in the present study are highlighted in red.
