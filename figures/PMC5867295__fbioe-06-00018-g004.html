MAP kinase and inflammatory signaling pathways

Overview of the canonical MAP kinase (MAPK) and inflammatory signaling pathways and newly acquired insights into their relation to the development of the osteoarthritic (OA) chondrocyte hypertrophic phenotype. The canonical pathway was updated with recent insights in the role of MAPK and inflammatory signaling in the hypertrophic switch occuring during OA development. In green pro-chondrogenic relations are shown, while pro-hypertrophic relations are depicted in red.
