Unique molecular profile of exosomes derived from primary human proximal tubular epithelial cells under diseased conditions

Eleven out of the 12 significantly differentially expressed exosomal proteins, derived from the inflammatory culture conditions compared to the normal culture conditions, associate with a cell to cell signalling/inflammatory response pathway identified by the Networks function of IPA. The colour intensity indicates amount of up-regulation within the network. Proteins highlighted in bold within the data table are the 11 significantly differentially expressed exosomal proteins.
