Crosstalk pathway

Crosstalk pathway. A. Circular layout of protein-protein interaction network representing interactions (lines) between over-expressed genes in adjacent mucosa (purple) and in tumor (orange). Nodes with a green border symbolize membrane proteins whereas red were used to represent secreted proteins B. Abstraction of the network in which only crosstalk interactions were drawn, using Cerebral view from Cytoscape.
