Hyperglycemia associated metabolic reprogramming in cancer cells and potential targets

Hyperglycemia associated metabolic reprogramming in cancer cells and potential targets. Pathways altered due to hyperglycemia leading to proliferation are indicated by green arrows. Inhibitors of various molecules are indicated in red. The inhibitors presented here and the corresponding clinical or research studies are mentioned in with their respective targets.
