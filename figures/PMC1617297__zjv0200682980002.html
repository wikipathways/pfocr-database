Cell-signaling networks induced by Pichinde virus infection as analyzed using the Ingenuity pathway analysis knowledge base

Cell-signaling networks induced by Pichinde virus infection as analyzed using the Ingenuity pathway analysis knowledge base. Significant pathways were merged to produce networks representing induction following infection with the attenuated P2 variant (A) and the lethal P18 variant (B). Nodes in red are up-regulated following virus infection; nodes in green are down-regulated. Reprinted with permission from Ingenuity Systems.
