Prognostic Biomarkers of OSBPL Family Genes in Patients with Pancreatic Ductal Adenocarcinoma

MetaCore pathway analysis of the co-expression gene network of oxysterol-binding protein-like 3 (OSBPL3) in pancreatic cancer patients. Downstream pathway analyses revealed that “Cytoskeleton remodeling_Regulation of actin cytoskeleton organization by the kinase effectors of Rho GTPases” might play an important role in pancreatic cancer development.
