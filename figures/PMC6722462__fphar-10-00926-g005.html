Metabolite pathway analysis with Pearson correlation coefficient only edges corresponding to correlations whose similarity was less than 0.5 are shown. (A) Metabolite pathway analysis between M3 and M20. (B) Metabolite pathway analysis between M20 and F20. The upward adjustment is red; the downward adjustment is green. The edges red solid line represents positive correlation, and the green solid line represents negative correlation. The line between metabolism–protein and protein–protein is indicated by a black dotted line.
