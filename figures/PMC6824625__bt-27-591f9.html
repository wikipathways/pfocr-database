Hypothetical schematic model for a novel oncogenic mechanism of G0S2

Hypothetical schematic model for a novel oncogenic mechanism of G0S2. G0S2-induced invasive phenotypes seem to be related to integrin-mediated FAK-Src and ERK1/2 signaling and inactivation of the Hippo pathway, subsequently leading to alteration in expressions of E-cadherin, β-catenin, MMPs, and cytokines.
