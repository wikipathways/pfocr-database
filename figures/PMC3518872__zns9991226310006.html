GPCR Canonical Signaling Pathways

GPCR Canonical Signaling Pathways. A–C, Schematic diagrams of three significantly altered GPCR Canonical Signaling Pathways identified using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis software (IPA; Ingenuity) as being affected by TGF-β1+LPS+IFNγ-treatment of astrocytes. As selected from , these pathways are α-Adrenergic Signaling (A), Endothelin-1 Signaling (B), and P2Y Signaling (C). Molecules are indicated by standard abbreviations. Relative changes in gene expression are depicted by gradated shades of color coding: red, up; green, down; white, no change. Direct and indirect interactions between molecules are depicted by solid and dotted lines, respectively.
