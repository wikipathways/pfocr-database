Stereoselective Binding of Trans-(±)-Kusunokinin and Cis-(±)-Kusunokinin Isomers to CSF1R

Top 12 candidate proteins of four kusunokinin isomers (red underlines) in the breast cancer progression pathway. Trans-(−)-kusunokinin showed best binding to CSF1R while trans-(+)-kusunokinin and the two cis-isomers possibly targeted AKR1B1.
