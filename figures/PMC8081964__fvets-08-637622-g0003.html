MicroRNA Expression Changes and Integrated Pathways Associated With Poor Outcome in Canine Osteosarcoma

Notch/HES1-associated miRNA–mRNA interactions. Dysregulated genes are shown as ovals or polygons, dysregulated miRNAs are shown in text boxes. In both cases, red indicates expression that is higher in tumors than in normal bone, blue indicates expression that is lower in tumors, and purple indicates that one probe in the Affymetrix array showed NFKB1 as upregulated and another as downregulated. Genes on the left are ligands or inhibitors of Notch; genes on the right are downstream targets of the Notch signaling pathway and/or specifically interact with HES1.
