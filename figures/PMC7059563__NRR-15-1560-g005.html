Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathway analysis.A KEGG pathway analysis revealed 15 substantially enriched pathways corresponding to transcripts dysregulated after spinal cord injury (SCI). The tumor necrosis factor (TNF) signaling pathway was one of the most enriched networks correlated with SCI rats. Genes in red boxes were differentially expressed genes. AP- 1: AP-1 transcription factor subunit; c/EBPβ: CCAAT/enhancer binding protein β; CcL2: C-C motif chemokine ligand 2; c-Jun: c-Jun proto- oncogene; CREB: cAMP-response element binding protein; Cx3cL1: C-X3-C motif chemokine ligand 1; CxcL: C-X-C motif chemokine ligand; Fos: Fos proto-oncogene; Icam1: intercelluar adhesion molecule-1; IkBα: I-kappa-B-α; Jun: Jun proto-oncogene; JunB: JunB proto-oncogene; Mmp4: metalloproteinase-4; Nfkbia: nuclear factor-kappa-B inhibitor alpha; Socs3: suppressor of cytokine signaling-3; TNFR1: tumor necrosis factor receptor 1; TNFR2: tumor necrosis factor receptor 2; Vcam1: vascular cell adhesion molecule-1.
