Mutations in key pathways controlling cell growth. (A) Representation of mutated genes in the JAK-STAT pathway, PI(3)K-AKT pathway, MAPK pathway and receptors. Mutated genes are shown in squares. Gene names in light grey color are non-mutated. (B) Representation of mutated genes involved in cell cycle.
