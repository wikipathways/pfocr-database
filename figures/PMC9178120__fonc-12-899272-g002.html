Contribution of the Tumor Microenvironment to Metabolic Changes Triggering Resistance of Multiple Myeloma to Proteasome Inhibitors

Contact independent interactions between MM cells and the TME. Cellular crosstalk between MM cells and other key players of the TME triggering the release of soluble factors (mainly cytokines, chemokines, and growth factors, but also extracellular vesicles). Upon binding to their cognate receptors, soluble factors trigger intracellular signaling pathways, which promote myeloma growth and survival as well as BM neovascularization, bone remodeling and immune evasion.
