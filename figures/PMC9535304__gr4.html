Molecular activity of cannabidiol in the regulation of Nrf2 system interacting with NFKB pathway under oxidative stress

Cannabidiol (CBD)-modulated proteins in the Nrf2 - NF-κB (p65 and p62) crosstalk. Nrf2 and p65 can negatively affect each other's transcriptional activities by decreasing their interactions with CBP/p300. On the other hand, Keap1 can interact with IκBβ and promote its proteasomal degradation. Moreover, p62, can promote Nrf2 activation and also NF-κB activity by stimulation of TRAF6, PKCζ, RIP1 and IKKγ. Regulatory feedback loop between p62 and Nrf2 B. The antagonistic effect between Nrf2 and p65.
