Inhibition of NFKB signaling retards eosinophilic dermatitis in SHARPIN-deficient mice

Pathway analysis of transcriptome data in skin using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis® software suggested activation of both classical and alterative NFKB pathways. Most of molecules in NFKB pathways were upregulated, such as p65/RelA and NFKB p52/RelB in the mutant group from the pooled expression data of 2-, 4-, 6-, 8-, and 10-week old mice. upregulation; downregulation; □ no significant change
