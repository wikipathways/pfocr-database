Five functional lncRNA-mRNA trans regulatory network

Five functional lncRNA-mRNA trans regulatory network. a TTR-lncRNAs-mRNA trans regulatory sub-network1: ubiquitin mediated proteolysis and cell cycle; b TTR-lncRNAs-mRNA trans regulatory sub-network2: DNA replication and cell cycle; c TTR-lncRNAs-mRNA trans regulatory sub-network3: mRNA splicing via spliceosome, RNA transport, protein translation and apoptosis mediated by MAPK and NFκB pathway; d TTR-lncRNAs-mRNA trans regulatory sub-network4: Oxidative phosphorylation regulation; e TTR-lncRNAs-mRNA trans regulatory sub-network5: transcriptional regulation and DNA repair
