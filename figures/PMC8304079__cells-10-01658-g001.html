CAMP in Beta Cell Stimulus–Secretion and Intercellular Coupling

Stimulus–secretion coupling in beta cells. The three interconnected intracellular signaling pathways in pancreatic beta cells are shown; the KATP dependent triggering pathways is indicated in dark yellow, the metabolic amplifying pathway in orange, and the neurohormonal amplifying pathway in red. Parts of a non-beta cell and a neighboring Cx36-connected beta cell are shown on the bottom, blood capillary and autonomic innervation are shown on top.
