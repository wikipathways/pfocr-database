Primary metabolic pathway of L

Primary metabolic pathway of L. plantarum NZ3410 (ΔctsR) compared to L. plantarum WCFS1 grown at 40°C. Green lines or triangles indicate downregulation, whereas red lines or triangles indicate upregulation, open rectangles indicate no change, and plus symbols indicate that expression of more than 3 genes is acquired for enzyme production. Abbreviations are addressed in the Additional file : Table S2, according to Teusink et al. [].
