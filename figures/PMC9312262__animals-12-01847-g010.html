Overexpression of DGAT2 Stimulates Lipid Droplet Formation and Triacylglycerol Accumulation in Bovine Satellite Cells

KEGG enrichment analysis of differentially expressed genes (DEGs) after interference of DGAT2. (A) KEGG enrichment factor map. (B) KEGG functional annotation of DEGs involved in the PPAR signaling pathway. Green rectangles indicate that DEGs were significantly downregulated (log fold change > 1, FDR < 0.001) in BSCs infected with sh-DGAT2 compared to control sh-NC.
