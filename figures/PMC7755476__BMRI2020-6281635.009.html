Protein-protein interaction network of E2F coexpression genes (GeneMANIA). Different colors of the network edge indicate the bioinformatics methods applied: coexpression, website prediction, pathway, physical interactions, and colocalization. The different colors for the network nodes indicate the biological functions of the set of enrichment genes.
