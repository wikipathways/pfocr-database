HMGB1/TLR4 and IL-1B/IL-1R1 Signaling Pathways in Epilepsy

Schematic of IL-1β/IL-1R1 pathway and epilepsy. This figure shows the activation and secretion of IL-1β and its epileptogenic mechanism in combination with IL-1R1. AMPA, aminohydroxymethyloxazole propionic acid; BBB: blood–brain barrier; DAMP, danger-associated molecular patterns; FCD, focal cortical dysplasia; GLU, glutamate; IL-1β, interleukin-1β; IL-1R, interleukin-1receptor; MyD88, myeloid differentiation factor 88; NMDAR, N-methyl-D-aspartic acid receptor; NF-κB, nuclear factors κB; PAMP, pathogen-associated molecular patterns; PRR, pattern recognition receptors; TSC, tuberous sclerosis complex.
