Haem synthetic pathway

Haem synthetic pathway. The eight enzymatic reactions needed to produce haem and the nonenzymatic side paths resulting in the synthesis of uroporphyrin I and uroporphyrin III pigments (chemical structures marked with a black box). Light blue arrows indicate nonenzymatic processes. Enzyme names are in red font, metabolite names are in black font. Enzyme Commission (EC) numbers provide a numerical classification scheme for enzymes, based on the chemical reactions they catalyze
