Putting human Tid-1 in context: an insight into its role in the cell and in different disease states

Overview of hTid-1 interactions in cellular signaling pathways. A hTid-1 interacts with the APC protein complex which further interacts with other proteins to regulate the Wnt signaling pathway. B hTid-1 interacts with Trk kinase which causes the phosphorylation of the tyrosine residues of hTid-1, facilitating Trk-dependent intracellular signaling. C hTid-1 interacts with the MuSK and Agrin proteins, which facilitates synaptic development via the Agrin signaling pathway
