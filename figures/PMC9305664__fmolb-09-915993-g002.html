Non-Coding RNAs in Primary Biliary Cholangitis

Signaling pathway of miRNAs in PBC. MiRNA are involved in regulating inflammation and autoimmunity. Some miRNAs are encapsulated in nanovesicles and exert critical effects on inflammatory and immune cells. miRNAs participate in PBC inflammation and autoimmune disorders primarily through NF-κB, TGF-β1, Th17, and so on.
