Tgfbr2 cKO CD34+ SCC cells upregulate genes implicated in invasive human cancers

Using ToppCluster, the genes upregulated in CD34+ SCC cells were compared to previously published datasets and a network of genes shared between Tgfbr2 deficient transitional epithelial CD34+ SCC cells and aggressive human cancers was generated. Genes colored in yellow (invasion and metastasis) and blue (RAC/RHO/RAS pathway) correspond to the genes selected for qRT-PCR validation in .DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.7554/eLife.22914.011
