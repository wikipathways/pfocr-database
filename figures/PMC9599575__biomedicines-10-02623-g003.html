Glycation-Associated Diabetic Nephropathy and the Role of Long Noncoding RNAs

AGEs cellular signaling pathway through its receptors. AGE-RAGE interaction promotes NADPH oxidase and ROS production as well as regulates many downstream signaling pathways. AGER complex helps in the detoxification and degradation of AGEs. Abbreviations: IGF-1, Insulin-like growth factor-1; PDGF, platelet-derived growth factor; ISRE, interferon-stimulated response element; ROS, reactive oxygen species; SIRT1, silent mating type information regulator 2 homolog 1.
