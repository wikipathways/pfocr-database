A high-fat diet (HFD) and chronic cannabidiol (CBD) administration on the sphingolipid metabolic pathway in rat myocytes

Effects of a high-fat diet (HFD) and chronic cannabidiol (CBD) administration on the sphingolipid metabolic pathway in rat myocytes. ↑, increase; ↓, decrease; red arrows indicate the effects of high-fat feeding; green arrows indicate the effects of CBD treatment; serine palmitoyltransferase, long chain base subunit 1 (SPTLC1); ceramide synthase 5 (LASS5); dihydroceramide desaturase (DES); neutral sphingomyelinase (NSmase); UDP-glucose ceramide glucosyltransferase (UGCG); ceramide synthase (CerS); acid ceramidase (ASAH1); sphingosine kinase 2 (SPHK2); phosphorylated protein kinase B (pAkt); phosphorylated glycogen synthase kinase 3 (pGSK-3).
