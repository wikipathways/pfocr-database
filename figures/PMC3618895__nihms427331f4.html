Significantly changing expression sub-networks in LMB hypothalamus

Pathway analysis of significantly changing expression sub-networks in LMB hypothalamus. The networks shown in the pathway are dopamine receptors DRD1 and DRD2, GABAA receptor, AR, and retinoic acid receptor expression targets. These signaling networks will have a significant impact on neurotransmitter systems that include GABA, catecholamines, and glutamate. Abbreviations are provided in the abbreviation list.
