Therapeutic Action of Autophagy in Gastric Cancer Managements: Novel Treatment Strategies and Pharmacological Interventions

MicroRNAs regulates autophagy-mediated cell proliferation and migration in gastric cancer. Overexpression of miR-375 inhibited the proliferation and migration of gastric cancer in vitro and xenograft nude mouse model. miRNA blocks autophagy via AKT/mTOR signaling pathway and regulating invasion as well as migration in epithelial-to-mesenchymal transition. In addition, most usually effective miRNAs control the transcriptional expression of upstream activators and inhibitors of autophagy in gastric cancer.
