Differential metabolic pathway and reaction analysis

Visualisation of the alteration in the reactions in pyruvate metabolism, glycolysis/gluconeogenesis metabolic pathway and glycosphingolipid biosynthesis-lacto and neolacto series pathway, transport reactions and enzyme activities. The red colour represents the upregulated or overactive metabolic pathways, and blue the downregulated or underactive ones. See for the full name of the reactions.
