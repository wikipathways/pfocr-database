Cellular processes altered by colonization by a heterologous symbiont: (A) oxidative stress response; (B) molecular signaling pathways; (C) sugar storage, transport, and signaling; and (D) lipid and protein metabolism and transport

Cellular processes altered by colonization by a heterologous symbiont: (A) oxidative stress response; (B) molecular signaling pathways; (C) sugar storage, transport, and signaling; and (D) lipid and protein metabolism and transport. Type color indicates up-regulated (red) and down-regulated (blue) expression in the heterologous S. trenchii-colonized host tissues. Entities in black type were not measured in this study. Arrow thickness indicates direction of pathway activity, and dashed lines indicate putative activity. Only genes and metabolites likely involved in the symbiosis are shown. Italic type represents genes, and roman type represents metabolites. Data are from , , and .
