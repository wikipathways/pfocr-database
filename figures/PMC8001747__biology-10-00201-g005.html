Pathway diagram of key drought-responsive metabolites from two cherry rootstocks

The pathway diagram of key drought-responsive metabolites from two cherry rootstocks. Solid arrows indicate direct reactions between metabolites. Dashed arrows indicate indirect reactions between metabolites. CCL and CSL represent control and drought treatment samples of CDR-1. GCL and GSL represent control and drought treatment samples of Gisela 5. Because the relative content of substances was detected by nontarget GC-MS, there are no units.
