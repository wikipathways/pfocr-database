Genes found altered in Central Carbon Metabolism (CCM) pathway in gingivo-buccal oral cancer

Genes found altered in Central Carbon Metabolism (CCM) pathway in gingivo-buccal oral cancer. EGFR, SLC7A5, NTRK3, the three key genes (marked in blue and appearing on the vertical lines to the left of the figure), were significantly dysregulated in the CCM pathway
