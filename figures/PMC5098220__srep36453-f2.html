Multi-label ℓ2-regularized logistic regression for predicting activation/inhibition relationships in human protein-protein interaction networks

Only the PPIs that are predicted with novel activation/inhibition relationships are illustrated, and the other PPIs in AR signalling pathway are omitted. The green line stands for activation and the red line stands for inhibition. The sinewave green line stands for the verified activations.
