Magnetic fields affects microtubules via EGFR and calcium pathways

Arrows with blunt ends indicate inhibition relation. Red arrows: the EGFR-PKC-MAPK/Erk pathway [–]; blue arrows: the LTCC/CaMKII-MAPK/Erk pathway [, ]; dark red arrows: influence of the EGFR-PKC-MAPK/Erk pathway on the LTCC/CaMKII-MAPK/Erk pathway []; dark blue arrows: influence of LTCC/CaMKII-MAPK/Erk pathway on the EGFR-PKC-MAPK/Erk pathway [–]. The focal adhesions are also under the regulation of the MAPK/Erk pathway [, ].
