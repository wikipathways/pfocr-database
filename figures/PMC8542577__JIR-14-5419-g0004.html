Atherosclerosis Pathways are Activated in Pericoronary Adipose Tissue of Patients with Coronary Artery Disease

Interactions between differentially expressed genes found by Ingenuity Pathway Analysis, forming the network involved in atherosclerosis progression. The upregulated and downregulated genes are marked red and green, respectively. Grey arrows indicate the direction of regulation.
