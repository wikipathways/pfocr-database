Insulin signaling pathway

Insulin signaling pathway. KEGG enrichment analysis identified that genes that were significantly downregulated in patients with leukoaraiosis compared with controls were enriched in the insulin signaling pathway. The yellow boxes indicate the main compounds in the insulin signaling pathway.
