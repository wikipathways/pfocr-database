Genes involved in metabolism (EMP pathway, TCA cycle, and inositol metabolism), rhizosphere adaptation, biocontrol, and plant growth promotion in SQR9 and their expression patterns in response to root exudates

Schematic of genes involved in metabolism (EMP pathway, TCA cycle, and inositol metabolism), rhizosphere adaptation, biocontrol, and plant growth promotion in SQR9 and their expression patterns in response to root exudates. Each significantly differentially expressed gene is marked with a triangle, for which the left and right directions represent 24 h and 48 h post-inoculation, respectively. Red indicates up-regulation and blue down-regulation
