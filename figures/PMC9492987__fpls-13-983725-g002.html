Limited water supply on metabolite composition in tomato fruits (Solanum lycopersicum L.) in two soils with different nutrient conditions

Schematic diagram of the metabolic pathway analysis and the relative changes in metabolites by water limitation in two different soils. The metabolic pathway was modified from the KEGG pathway database (https://www.genome.jp/kegg/pathway.html; URL accessed on 17 February 2022). The colored squares (blue to red) indicate fold changed normalized by water sufficient condition for each soil nutrient condition. #Significantly differed metabolites between NE-WS and NE-WL (p < 0.05, Student’s t-test).
