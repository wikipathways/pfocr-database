Synergistic effects of methyl 2-cyano-3,11-dioxo-18beta-olean-1,-12-dien-30-oate and erlotinib on erlotinib-resistant non-small cell lung cancer cells

DNA replication: ERL + CDODA-Me synergistically affected the DNA replication pathway at 24 h in HCC827R cells. (A) Assembly of pre-replicative complex; (B) activation of pre-replicative complex; (D) DNA replication imitation; (E) leading/lagging strand synthesis. Downregulated transcripts are denoted in solid red. CDC6: cell division cycle 6; CDT1: chromatin licensing and DNA replication factor 1; CDK2: cyclin dependent kinase 2; POLA2: DNA polymerase alpha 2, accessory subunit; PCNA: proliferating cell nuclear antigen.
