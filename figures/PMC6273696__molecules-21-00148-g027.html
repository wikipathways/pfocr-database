G2/M DNA damage check point signaling pathway was regulated by vancomycin in HK-2 cells

G2/M DNA damage check point signaling pathway was regulated by vancomycin in HK-2 cells. HK-2 cells were treated with 50 μg/mL vancomycin for 24 h and the protein samples were subject to quantitative proteomic analysis. Green indicates a down-regulation. The intensity of greencolor indicates the degree of down-regulation. Solid arrow indicates direct interaction.
