Oral Epithelial Cells Distinguish between Candida Species with High or Low Pathogenic Potential through MicroRNA Regulation

Pathway explorer results on HIF1-α signaling in Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA). Significantly down- or overexpressed genes in each condition were visualized within the canonical HIF1-α signal transduction network using pathway explorer and designer tools within IPA. The individual genes affected by each treatment were marked by the corresponding colors of each condition: blue for C. parapsilosis at an MOI of 1:1, purple for an MOI of 5:1, and red for C. albicans at an MOI of 1:1.
