Network of organismal abnormalities and cancer

The fifteen most up-regulated and down-regulated proteins produced by a 28-day exposure to 10 µM of the Rho-kinase inhibitor Y-27632 (a). An IPA® protein pathway analysis suggested a molecular network with the highest number of focus molecules (focus molecules 26, score 59) (b). This network was predicted to connect to organismal abnormalities and cancer. Error bars indicate 95% confidence intervals (n = 3).
