Transcriptome profiling of the subventricular zone and dentate gyrus in an animal model of Parkinson's disease

Synopsis of significant mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) signaling pathway in the subventricular zone (SVZ) of mice exposed to 1-methyl-4-phenyl-1,2,3,6-tetrahydropyridine (MPTP) according to the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) analysis. Specifically, related genes were upregulated (red) such as FBJ murine osteosarcoma viral oncogene homolog (Fos), and nuclear receptor subfamily 4, group A, member 1 (Nr4a1).
