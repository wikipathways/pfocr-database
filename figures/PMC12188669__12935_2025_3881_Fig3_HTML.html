Roles of YOD1 in Diseases

The Roles of YOD1 in Diseases. (A) YOD1 indirectly affects the stability of the core kinases MST and LATS in the Hippo pathway by regulating the ubiquitination of NEDD4 and ITCH in Liver Cancer (B) The expression of YOD1 is regulated by the transcription factor YY1 and the long non-coding RNA (LncRNA-TUG1) (C) YOD1 activates the TAK1-TAB protein complex by binding to NOD2 and RIPK2, which further activates the NF-κB and MAPK signaling pathways (D) The Role of YOD1 in Leukemia (E) The Role of YOD1 in Alzheimer’s Disease (F) The Role of YOD1 in Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC)
