The bone morphogenetic protein (BMP) signaling pathway and the regulation of miRNA levels

The bone morphogenetic protein (BMP) signaling pathway and the regulation of miRNA levels. BMP ligands induce the phosphorylation and translocation of Smads into the nucleus. The transcription of miRNAs or post-transcriptional miRNA processing is modulated by Smads. This regulation of miRNA levels is implicated in BMP signaling-mediated phenomena, such as vascular smooth muscle cell (VSMC) differentiation, osteogenesis and neurogenesis.
