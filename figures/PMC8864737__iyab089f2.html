WormPaths examples. (A) WormPaths map of glycolysis/gluconeogenesis. (C) An example of a web pop-up window from glycolysis/gluconeogenesis that shows the metabolite structure of beta-D-glucose 6-phosphate upon hovering the cursor over g6p-B.
