Estradiol supplementation on the brain transcriptome of old rhesus macaques maintained on an obesogenic diet

Differentially expressed genes within the GABA signaling pathway were downregulated in the amygdala. a GABA signaling pathway (Ingenuity Pathway Analysis) shows the differentially expressed genes (DEGs) between OvH and OvH-HT in the amygdala (AMG). Those genes that are upregulated are shown in pink and those that are downregulated in green with a pink circle. b A summary table of the DEGs showing the fold change (FC) and nominal and fdr p-values. c Boxplots representing the normalized counts for the DEGs between experimental groups, OvH (salmon) and OvH-HT (turquoise). The significance for each one of these genes is shown in b
