Major Downregulation of Circulating microRNAs in Zika Acutely Infected Patients: Potential Implications in Innate and Adaptive Immune Response Signaling Pathways

DM and molecule network related to senescence pathway regulation in ZIKV+. The network was built using IPA software. Each molecule was represented as a node, and the biological relationship between two nodes is represented as an edge (line). All edges are supported by at least one reference from the literature or canonical information stored in the Ingenuity Knowledge Base (IKB). The central nodes are connected to multiple other molecules. The ZIKV + network showed some molecules as central nodes: interferon-alpha, CXCL10, interferon-gamma (IFNG), interferon regulatory factor 1 (IRF1), miR-125a-5p and its experimentally validated target ELAV like RNA-binding protein 1 (ELAV1). The molecules are represented in a gradient of orange or blue based on the prediction status, activated or inhibited, respectively.
