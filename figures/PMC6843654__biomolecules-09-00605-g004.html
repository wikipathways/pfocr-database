The top three molecular networks identified by Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA): (a) Network 1. Protein Synthesis, Cardiovascular System Development and Function, Cellular Assembly and Organization; (b) Network 2. Cell Morphology, Cellular Assembly and Organization, Cellular Development and (c) Network 3. Endocrine System Disorders, Metabolic Disease, Organismal Injury and Abnormalities. Genes from our input-data that were prioritized based on pedigree segregation and PHRED-like CADD scores are shown in peach. Our top coding and non-coding candidates are highlighted in dark orange. Interactions of central genes of the network are highlighted in blue.
