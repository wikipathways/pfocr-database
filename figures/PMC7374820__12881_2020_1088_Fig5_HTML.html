Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) of network of eight DE genes

Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) of network of eight DE genes. The eight genes that were found by the intersection shown in Fig. were input into IPA to generate a network diagram with the most plausible connections to each other, and to additional genes in the IPA Knowledge Base. The eight genes of interest are highlighted in orange. A maximum of thirty-five genes are shown in the network for readability. The ERK/MAPK pathways genes found in this network are highlighted in cyan
