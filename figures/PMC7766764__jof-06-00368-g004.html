Regulatory network for fungal dimorphism in U

Overview of regulatory network for fungal dimorphism in U. maydis. The filamentous growth of U. maydis can be triggered through a few pathways: the cyclic-AMP/protein kinase A (cAMP/PKA) pathway, the mitogen-activated kinase (MAPK) pathway and the GTPase-mediated signaling pathway. These signal transductions trigger the alteration of transcriptional network that activates downstream effectors for morphogenesis. Details of molecular players in the figure can be found in .
