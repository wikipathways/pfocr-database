Integration networks of proteins associated with of fractionated seminal plasma (SP1 and SP2). (A) Regulation of HSF1-mediated heat shock response for SP1. (B) Major histocompatibility (MHC) class II antigen presentation for SP2. Blue nodes indicate the selected protein, while green nodes represent proteins from the neighboring pathway. Red and brown nodes represent interacting proteins of the enriched pathways of existing research.
