Proteasome pathway mapping of altered genes in DLD-1-miR-21 cells

Proteasome pathway mapping of altered genes in DLD-1-miR-21 cells. A Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathway enrichment analysis was performed as described in “Materials and Methods”. Differentially expressed genes (DEGs) in DLD-1-miR-21 cells were mapped to the KEGG proteasome pathway (hsa03050). The genes highlighted in red and green indicated the upregulated and downregulated genes in DLD-1-miR-21 cells. The original data are shown in .
