A portion of the human metabolic chart (online diagram: []), shown at its highest zoom level with all labels drawn, and displaying a reaction tooltip. Three highlighting operations have been performed. (1) A species-comparison operation highlights all reactions shared with Helicobacter pylori in red. (2) All genes whose names include the substring UPP are colored purple. (3) The compound uracil has been highlighted in orange.
