TSH−SPP1/TRB−TSH positive feedback loop mediates fat deposition of hepatocyte: Crosstalk between thyroid and liver

Expression changes of target genes of thyroid hormone signaling pathway in patients with non-alcoholic fatty liver disease (NAFLD) are mapped by colors. The color depth positively correlated with the degree value. Red represents increased while green represents decreased gene expression in NAFLD group compared with that in the health obese group.
