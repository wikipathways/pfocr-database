Regulation of the mucus production by MAPK signalling

Regulation of the mucus production by MAPK signalling. Multiple stimuli induce phosphorylation of MAPKs following activation of transcriptional factors and production of mucus. IL-13 increases MUC5AC expression by indirect mechanisms including STAT6 phosphorylation through FOXA2 suppression.
