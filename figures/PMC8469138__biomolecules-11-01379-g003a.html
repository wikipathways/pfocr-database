Molecular Biology Networks and Key Gene Regulators for Inflammatory Biomarkers Shared by Breast Cancer Development: Multi-Omics Systems Analysis

Adiposity-specific gene-regulatory networks of top KDs in meta-analysis of CRP and IL6. (CRP, C-reactive protein; eQTL, expression quantitative trait loci; IL6, interleukin-6; KD, key drivers. The bigger nodes with red outlines are the top KDs in the enriched pathway obtained from weighted KD analysis. The subnetworks of the KDs are indicated by different colors according to differences in their canonical functions.).
