Top two networks based on ingenuity pathway analysis (IPA) showing interactions between the controls and the case. (A) Cell-to-cell signaling and interaction (control vs. DEHP). (B) Cancer, cell death and survival (control vs. OA). The lines between the genes represent known interactions (solid lines represent direct interactions; dashed lines represent indirect interactions). Each gene is displayed using shapes that represent the functional class of the gene product as indicated in the legend. The intensity of the red and green colors indicates the degree of up- and downregulation, respectively. A total of 25 networks (p-scores > 18) are provided in .
