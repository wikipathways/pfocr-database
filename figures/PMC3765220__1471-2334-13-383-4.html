Pathway Studio analysis of the differentially expressed proteins of extremely severe HFMD serum samples. Proteins identified in Table  were imported into PathwayAssist and an interaction map was created. Each node represents either a protein entity or a control mechanism of the interaction. Shown are proteins that either bound directly to another identified protein or to another identified protein via one other protein. The legend of the interaction network is summarized on the right of the figure.
