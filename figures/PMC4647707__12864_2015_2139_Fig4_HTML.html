Differentially expressed genes in the cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction pathway (ko04060)

Differentially expressed genes in the cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction pathway (ko04060). Genes significantly up-regulated after inoculation of bacteria in both cycle stages are highlighted in green, genes only significantly up-regulated after inoculation in oestrus are highlighted in blue, those only significantly up-regulated in dioestrus in yellow and blue boxes around genes represent genes that were significantly higher in oestrus 3 h post-inoculation in comparison to dioestrus 3 h post inoculation. Genes in grey boxes were present in the horse transcriptome, but not differentially expressed, while genes in white boxes were not described as horse KO terms.
