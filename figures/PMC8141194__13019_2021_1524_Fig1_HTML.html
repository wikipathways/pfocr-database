Non-coding RNAs modulate autophagy in myocardial ischemia-reperfusion injury: a systematic review

A diagram showing regulation of autophagy pathway by ncRNAs after myocardial ischemia-reperfusion injury. The diagram exhibited the various ncRNAs and their targets that are involved in the modulation of autophagy during myocardial ischemia-reperfusion injury. The listed targets included LC3-II, p62, Beclin-1, ULK1, Atg3, Atg5, Atg7, Atg12, Atg13, PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. MiRNAs show as in black in the yellow box, and lncRNAs in green
