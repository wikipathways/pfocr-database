Hm-MyD88 and Hm-SARM: Two key regulators of the neuroimmune system and neural repair in the medicinal leech

The survey of H. medicinalis databases and our experiments pointed out that leech possesses (i) the main components of the canonical TLR4/LPS pathway and (ii) the molecules recruited by endosomal TLRs. “?” indicates that stimulation of Hm-TLR1 may activate an IRF or a NF-κB signaling.
