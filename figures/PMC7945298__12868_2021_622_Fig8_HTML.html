Subnetwork of PVT1 and HOTAIR

a Subnetwork of PVT1 and HOTAIR. The mRNA circles with dark outline are involved in PI3K-Akt signaling pathway. b Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-17 and CCND1 (by miRanda). c Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-17 and HIF1A (by miRanda). d Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-17 and E2F1 (by miRanda). e Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-17 and CDKN1A (by miRanda). f Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-193b and CCND1 (by miRanda). g Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-383 and VEGFA (by miRanda). h Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-143 and COL1A1 (by miRanda). i Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-143 and COL5A2 (by miRanda). j Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-17 and HOTAIR (by miRanda). k Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-193b, hsa-miR-143 and HOTAIR (by miRanda). l Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-761 and HOTAIR (by miRanda). m Predicted interaction of hsa-miR-152 and HOTAIR (by miRanda)
