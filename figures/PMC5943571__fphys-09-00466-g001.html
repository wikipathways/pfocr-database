Pathway for glycosphingolipids (GSLs) biosynthesis. Glc, glucose; Lac, lactosyl; Gal, galactose; GalNAc, N-acetylgalactosamine; GlcNAc, N-acetylglucosamine; Sia, sialic acid; b, globo-series; Lc3, lactotriaosylceramide; Lc4, neolactotetraosylceramide. The code names of gangliosides are according to . G refers to ganglioside, the second letter refers to the number of sialic acids residues (M, mono-; D, di-; T, tri-; Q, quad-; P, pent-), and the number (1, 2, 3, 4) refers to the order of migration of the ganglioside on thin-layer chromatography (e.g., GM4 > GM3 > GM2 > GM1).
