Tec kinase signaling and FAT10 pathway

Schematic diagram of Tec kinase signaling and FAT10 pathway. Schematic diagram of Tec kinase signaling activation by oxidative stress and multiple membrane receptors that are involved in a variety of downstream responses, including Ca2+ influx, proliferation, differentiation, and transformation. In alcoholic hepatitis, FAT 10 is the key molecule connected with TEC kinase pathway through STAT3 and NFκB modulations.
