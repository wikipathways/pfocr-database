IPA-generated gene network linking 88 of 445 genes shown by microarray analysis to be significantly (P < 0.01) up- (blue) or down- (red) regulated >twofold in bovine TCs in response to BMP6 treatment. Note the node centered on NR5A1 (SF-1) linking INSL3, INHA, CYP17A1, and several other components of the steroidogenic pathway that were heavily down-regulated by BMP6 (green highlight). To aid clarity dashed lines indicating indirect interactions have been excluded.
