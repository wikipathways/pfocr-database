Analysis of LAGEs Family Gene Signature and Prognostic Relevance in Breast Cancer

L-Antigen 2B (LAGE2B) is particularly overexpressed in breast cancer patients. (A) In order to discover prospective gene networks and pathways impacted by the chosen genes, we exported LAGE2B co-expressed genes from TCGA and METABRIC breast cancer datasets to the MetaCore pathway analytical tool. (B) The MetaCore pathway analysis of “biology processes” indicated that “immune response_IFN-alpha/beta signaling via PI3K and NF-κB”-related pathways were correlated with breast cancer development.
