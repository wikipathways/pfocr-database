Identification and Characterization of Key Differentially Expressed Genes Associated With Metronomic Dosing of Topotecan in Human Prostate Cancer

Proposed mechanisms underlying increased potency of METRO-TOPO dosing. METRO-TOPO treatment downregulates the key cancer pathway genes, which are responsible for cell proliferation, migration, invasion, differentiation, transformation, metastasis, and anti-angiogenesis.
