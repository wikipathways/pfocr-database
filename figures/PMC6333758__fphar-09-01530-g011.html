Schematic diagram of the metabolic pathway related to the effects of different concentrations of C-8-O-Beta-D-glu on L-02 cells

Schematic diagram of the metabolic pathway related to the effects of different concentrations of C-8-O-β-D-glu on L-02 cells. The metabolites in red represented the potential biomarkers identified in this research. The arrows next to the metabolites represented their change trends in each drug group. The substances in the green box represented the enzymes needed for the reaction.
