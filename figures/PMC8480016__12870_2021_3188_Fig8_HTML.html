Identification of miRNAs-mediated seed and stone-hardening regulatory networks and their signal pathway of GA-induced seedless berries in grapevine (V. vinifera L.)

A mode chart of miRNA-mediated regulatory network related to grape berry seed and stone development. Green rectangle represents diverse VvmiRNAs which might regulate seed and seed coat development, whereas yellow rectangle indicates their corresponding target genes. The blue ellipse donates the metabolism pathways and specific tissues of VvmiRNAs and their target gene mediated in grape seed and seed coat development
