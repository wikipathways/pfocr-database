Enhancement of Auranofin-Induced Apoptosis in MCF-7 Human Breast Cells by Selenocystine, a Synergistic Inhibitor of Thioredoxin Reductase

AF inhibits the Trx-reduced activity of TrxR, whereas SeC competes with Trx and causes ROS accumulation. AF and SeC synergistically induce DNA damage, AKT and ERK dephosphorylation and result in the activation of p53 pathway, which in turn trigger mitochondrial dysfunction to amplify the apoptotic signals.
