Canonical pathway “Osteoarthritis Signaling” for VA vs

The canonical pathway “Osteoarthritis Signaling” for VA vs. Ctr treatment group. In this instance, the comparison of expected node activities and actual measurements suggest a reduced activity of this canonical pathway (p-value: 5.5 × 10−12 z-score: −0.707). Noteworthy is the downregulation of NF-κB (expected activation) and inflammatory markers including IL-1β. Red fillings = “activated”, green fillings = “inhibited”, purple highlights = passed cut-offs, no highlight = did not pass cut-offs.
