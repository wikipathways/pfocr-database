Alterations in Kynurenine and NAD+ Salvage Pathways during the Successful Treatment of Inflammatory Bowel Disease Suggest HCAR3 and NNMT as Potential Drug Targets

Pathways of tryptophan metabolism. Linear regression of log2 fold-change of IDO1 gene expression ratios vs. other genes related to tryptophan metabolism along kynurenine pathway was calculated and marked with squares of varying size (denoting R2 values) and color (indicating slope).
