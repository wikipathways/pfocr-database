An integrative analysis of the InR/PI3K/Akt network identifies the dynamic response to Insulin signaling

(A) Flow chart showing the insulin network annotation framework. (B) Scatter plot showing the distinct dynamic protein complexes that associate or dissociate with the pathway in response to insulin stimulus. Enriched complexes were identified using the COMPLEAT tool () and the dynamics is computed based on the InsulinNet-PPI (unfiltered network) (see Experimental Procedures). (C) Selected examples of dynamically associating/dissociating protein complexes. (D) Protein complex view of the insulin network reconstructed using COMPLEAT and SignPredictor tools. The blue and red edges correspond to activation and inhibition relationships with the pathway, respectively. Green represents stable associations at all three time points.
