Alterations of Cardiac Protein Kinases in Cyclic Nucleotide-Dependent Signaling Pathways in Human Ischemic Heart Failure

Overrepresented signaling pathways in proteomics analysis. Dot size represents the number of protein encoding genes enriched in each pathway (IHD n = 6, Ctrl n = 5). (A) GO Biological processes. Adjusted p-value (p.adjust): red < purple < blue. (B) Top 10 Reactome pathways. (C) Enriched KEGG pathways. Red color represents upregulated protein levels. Purple and blue colors represent downregulated protein levels.
