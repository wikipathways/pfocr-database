Components and targets of pro-phenol oxidase, Toll-Dorsal/Dif and JAK/STAT pathways

Components and targets of pro-phenol oxidase, Toll-Dorsal/Dif and JAK/STAT pathways. Genes whose expression is modulated by L. boulardi-17 infection (up- or downregulated) are shown. The Toll-Dorsal/Dif and JAK/STAT pathway components that were tested in genetic experiments () for their requirement for a robust encapsulation response are also shown (genetic evidence).
