Reconstruction of insect hormone pathways in an aquatic firefly, Sclerotia aquatilis (Coleoptera: Lampyridae), using RNA-seq

Ecdysteroid pathway of S. aquatilis was reconstructed by mapping annotated proteins to KEGG pathway. Enzymes involved in S. aquatilis ecdysteroid biosynthetic pathway are shown in red and an enzyme involved in ecdysone degradation is shown in green.
