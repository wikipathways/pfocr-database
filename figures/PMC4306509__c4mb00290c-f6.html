Human disease mutations in short linear motifs

. Analysis of predicted SLiMs with motif-breaking mutations in the KEGG human prostate cancer pathway. (A) KEGG human prostate cancer pathway (KEGG id: hsa05215). Proteins highlighted with orange in the pathway contain at least one predicted SLiM that has a motif-breaking mutation (COSMIC). (B) Sequence features of retinoblastoma-associated protein-1 (RB1) (phosphorylation sites from Phospho.ELM, domain predictions from SMART and PFAM, and order–disorder profile predictions from GlobPlot and IUPred). A potentially functional SH2-binding motif (606pYLSP609) with a multiple sequence alignment of orthologs from representative model organisms is highlighted in black. The alignment is generated using ClustalW and visualized using JalView. .
