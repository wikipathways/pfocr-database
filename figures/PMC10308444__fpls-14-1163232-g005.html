Integrated transcriptome and microRNA sequencing analyses reveal gene responses in poplar leaves infected by the novel pathogen bean common mosaic virus (BCMV)

KEGG pathway analysis showed that BCMV infection significantly inhibited the expression of genes involved in photosynthesis and the photosynthetic apparatus in poplar mosaic disease. (A, B), KEGG enrichment results in the Photosynthesis pathway (ko00195); (C, D); KEGG enrichment results in the Photosynthetic apparatus pathway (ko00190). SD: short-period diseased poplar leaves; SD: long-period diseased poplar leaves. Genes in green represent downward adjustment when compared to the control, genes in blue represent both upward and downward adjustment, while no gene was upregulated in SD and LD leaves.
