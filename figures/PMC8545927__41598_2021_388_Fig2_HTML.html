Elucidate multidimensionality of type 1 diabetes mellitus heterogeneity by multifaceted information

The KEGG pathways in the PPI network of T1D. The nodes involved in Th17 cell differentiation (hsa04659), type 1 diabetes mellitus (hsa04940), Th1 and Th2 cell differentiation (hsa04658), NF-kappa B signaling pathway (hsa04064), apoptosis (hsa04210), and TNF signaling pathway (hsa04668) were colored in blue, red, green, yellow, pink, and orange, respectively. The KEGG information were from KEGG database,. The PPI network was generated using STRING v11.0 (https://string-db.org/).
