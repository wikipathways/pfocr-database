Development and implementation of the SUM breast cancer cell line functional genomics knowledge base

Results returned by the KEGG Pathway Engine for the essentialness of the Cell Cycle KEGG pathway in the SUM-149 cell line based on the screen hit data for that line. The KEGG Pathway Engine returns the pathway map with hit genes highlighted in red, and a table that shows for each hit gene, the rank in the screen, the expression level relative to normal cells as a Log2 ratio of the fold difference with respect to normal cells, and any targeted drugs associated with essential genes in the pathway.
