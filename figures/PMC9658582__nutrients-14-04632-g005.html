Tocotrienol-Rich Fraction and Levodopa Regulate Proteins Involved in Parkinson’s Disease-Associated Pathways in Differentiated Neuroblastoma Cells: Insights from Quantitative Proteomic Analysis

KEGG Parkinson disease pathway (hsa05012). The red boxes indicate the pathways involved by TRF and levodopa, green shaded boxes are proteins that were differentially regulated by (T) TRF and (L) levodopa in differ-neural cells. The proteins behind the KEGG symbol are as follows: UCHL1:UCHL1, CaM: CALM1, CALM2, CALM3, Bip: HSPA5, mPTP: SLC25A6, VDAC1, VDAC3, CxV: ATP5F1A, TUBA/TUBB: TUBA1B, TUBB, TUBB2B, TUBB3, TUBB4B, TUBB6 (Adapted from KEGG-Parkinson’s disease pathway- hsa05012 with permission from Ref. []. 2019. Kanehisa, M.).
