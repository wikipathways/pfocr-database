LncRNA/pseudogene–miRNA–mRNA ceRNA network in CRC

The lncRNA/pseudogene–miRNA–mRNA ceRNA network in CRC. The blue ellipses represent mRNAs, the purple rectangles represent miRNAs, the green diamonds represent lncRNAs, and and the green hexagon represents pseudogenes. There are 10 lncRNAs, 5 pseudogenes, 122 mRNAs, 39 miRNAs, and 433 interactions included in this ceRNA network. LncRNAs, pseudogenes, miRNAs, and mRNAs interact with each other through shared MREs
