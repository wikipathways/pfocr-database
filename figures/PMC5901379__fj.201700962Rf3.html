Signaling pathway analysis of splenic NK cells after brain ischemia

Signaling pathway analysis of splenic NK cells after brain ischemia. A) Pathway-act-network was generated according to the KEGG database. B) Splenic NK-cell gene-act-network. The KEGG database was used to build the network of genes from splenic NK cells according to the relationship among genes, proteins, and compounds in the database.
