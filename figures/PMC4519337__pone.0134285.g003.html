Transcriptome Profiling of Wild-Type and pga-Knockout Mutant Strains Reveal the Role of Exopolysaccharide in Aggregatibacter actinomycetemcomitans

A schematic presentation of metabolic pathways affected in A. actinomycetemcomitans (IDH781 and EA1002) strains. Red arrows indicate up-regulated genes in EA1002 while dashed black arrows indicate no change in expression. Green crosses on dashed black arrows refer to down-regulated genes whereas the blue arrows indicate intermediates feeding into a pathway.
