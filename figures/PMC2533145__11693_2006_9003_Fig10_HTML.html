Rap1 and glycolytic/TCA cycle reaction

Rap1 and glycolytic/TCA cycle reaction. Glycolysis leading to acetate and ethanol are shown. The gray box on the left contains a pathway overview of glycolysis, fermentation and the TCA cycle, where red connections are known and yellow are predicted. Rap1 can be seen to regulate key control points in glycolysis and the TCA cycle. Pathway map generated taken from the Pathway Tool Omics Viewer at SGD (Christie et al. )
