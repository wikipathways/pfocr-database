Pathway projection of metabolite and phytohormone profiles

Pathway projection of metabolite and phytohormone profiles. The log2-transformed fold change (log2FC) is visualized by color: red, increased in NIL-12 plants by PS treatment; blue, decreased by the treatment. PS, growth conditions by partial submergence treatment. Biological replicates, n = 3.
