The cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction pathway in IIM patients with/without anti-MDA5+ autoantibodies

Pathway analysis of the cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction pathway in IIM patients with/without anti-MDA5+ autoantibodies. The DEPs of the anti-MDA5- subgroup and anti-MDA5+ subgroup were marked in the cytokine-cytokine receptor interaction pathway. Proteins marked in red were upregulated against HCs, while those marked in green were downregulated
