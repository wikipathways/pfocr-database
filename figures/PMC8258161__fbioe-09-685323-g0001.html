Robustifying Experimental Tracer Design for13C-Metabolic Flux Analysis

Metabolic network model of the central carbon metabolism of S. clavuligerus (simplified view). The model comprises 48 intracellular metabolites (bold) and 89 reactions (italic), grouped into 8 pathways (color-coded): glycolysis (emp), pentose phosphate pathway (ppp), tricarboxylic acid cycle (tca), anaplerosis (ana), urea cycle (urea), clavam biosynthesis pathway (clavam), uptake (upt) and lumped biosynthesis reaction for biomass (bm).
