Dorsal root ganglion neurons regulate the transcriptional and translational programs of osteoblast differentiation in a microfluidic platform

DRG neurons promote the osteogenic differentiation potential of MSCs by regulating the canonical/β-catenin Wnt signaling pathway and expression of osteoblast-related genes/proteins in MSCs.
