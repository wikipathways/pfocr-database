USP22-pivot gene-module-pathway network suggesting USP22 could affect multiple pathways by up-regulating UBC

USP22-pivot gene-module-pathway network suggesting USP22 could affect multiple pathways by up-regulating UBC. (A) USP22 regulates the modules by regulating pivot genes in the development of LUAD. (B) Knocking down USP22 up-regulate UBC to result in affecting lysosome pathway. (C) Knocking down USP22 up-regulates UBC to affect cell cycle pathway. (D) Knocking down USP22 could up-regulate UBC to affect the proteolysis pathway mediated by ubiquitin. Blue indicates down-regulated expression, while red indicates up-regulated expression.
