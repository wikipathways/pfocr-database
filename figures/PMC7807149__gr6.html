Lycopene Production Pathway in Triple KOE

Lycopene Production Pathway in Triple KOE. coli. By isolating the upstream metabolites from lycopene with a yield >1%, NetFlow isolated the main carbon flow between glucose and lycopene (top). The net reaction for the production of lycopene from pyruvate and G3P was then generated from this subnetwork (bot). The full descriptions for all metabolites and reactions contained in this subnetwork are given in and , respectively.
