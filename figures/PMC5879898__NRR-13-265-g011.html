MAPK signaling pathway and the JAK-ATAT signaling pathway

KEGG pathway analysis of predicted target genes.(A) The predicted target genes that were annotated for the MAPK signaling pathway; (B) the predicted targets involved in the JAK-ATAT signaling pathway.
