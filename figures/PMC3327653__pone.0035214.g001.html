Recruiting a New Substrate for Triacylglycerol Synthesis in Plants: The Monoacylglycerol Acyltransferase Pathway

The substrates are G-3-P, glycerol-3-phosphate; LPA, lysophosphatidic acid; 2-LPA, sn2-lysophosphatidic acid; PA, phosphatidic acid; MAG, monoacylglycerol; DAG, diacylglycerol; TAG, triacylglycerol; PC, phosphatidylcholine. The enzymes are GPAT, glycerol-3-phosphate acyltransferase; GPAT4/6, denotes enzymes with both GPAT and phosphatase activity such as the A. thaliana GPAT4 and GPAT6; LPAAT, lysophosphatidic acid acyltransferase; PAP, phosphatidic acid phosphatase; MGAT, monoacylglycerol acyltransferase; DGAT, diacylglycerol acyltransferase; PDAT, phospholipid:diacylglycerol acyltransferase; CPT, CDP-choline:diacylglycerol cholinephosphotransferase; PDCT, phosphatidylcholine:diacylglycerol cholinephosphotransferase; PLC, phospholipase C; PLD-Z, phospholipase D Z-class; LPCAT, lysophosphatidylcholine acyltransferase; Pi, inorganic phosphate.
