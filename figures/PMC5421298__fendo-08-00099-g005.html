Gene expression changes in the Wnt/Beta-catenin signaling pathway

Gene expression changes in the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway. Shown is the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway with differential gene expression values plotted for genes with false-discovery rate <0.05. The color of each gene represents the direction of regulation (red = positive; gray = no change; green = negative). log2FC = log2 fold change. The intensity of the color is the value relative to the most highly regulated gene (log2FC = 2.6) in the entire dataset.
