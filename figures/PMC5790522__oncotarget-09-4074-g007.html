Transcriptome profiling identified differentially expressed genes and pathways associated with tamoxifen resistance in human breast cancer

DEGs (n=26) of patients and genes(n=98) involved in ER signal pathway were integrated using STRING website to explore the association between these DEGs with ER functional related genes. The result showed that three common DEGs including GFRA3, NPY1R and PTPRN2 were closely related to ER related pathway.
