The KEGG pathway maps [] represent the NF-ƙB signalling pathway in which DEG in (a) bovine macrophages infected with Nc-Spain1H and (b) Nc-Spain7 versus non-infected macrophages are highlighted. Upregulated genes are represented in red and downregulated genes are represented in green. The color intensity corresponds to the level of up- or downregulation. Results were obtained from three biological replicates for each condition
