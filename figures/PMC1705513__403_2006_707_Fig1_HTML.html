Inflammatory pathway in psoriasis

Inflammatory pathway in psoriasis []. Working model for immunopathogenesis of psoriasis. Multiple stages are proposed for trafficking patterns of immunocytes, involving signals in which symptomless skin is converted into a psoriatic plaque. Key inflammatory events include intraepidermal trafficking by CD8+ T cells and neutrophils. Reproduced with permission from J Clin Invest (2004, 113:1664–1675).
