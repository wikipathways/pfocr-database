Table lists miR-H1 altered genes involved in integrin signaling with fold change. (B) miR-K12-3-3p targets genes involved in the endocytic pathway. Pathway depicting miR-K12-3-3p downregulated genes involved in GPCR signaling assessed by IPA. Table lists genes involved in GPCR signaling with fold change.
