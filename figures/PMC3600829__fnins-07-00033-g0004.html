Functional analysis of the gene expression networks, performed using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA)

The functional analysis of the gene expression networks, performed using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA). This figure illustrates the activation of the cell-mediated immune response network. Genes in green were down-regulated and the in red were up-regulated with respect to the healthy control group. (Numbers are fold changes).
