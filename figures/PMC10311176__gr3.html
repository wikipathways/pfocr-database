Transcriptome analysis of CD4+ T cells from HIV-infected individuals receiving ART with LLV revealed novel transcription factors regulating HIV-1 promoter activity

Differentially expressed transcription factors in VS-HC and LLV-VS groups. A–B Relative expression levels of DEGs in NF-κB (A) and TNF signaling pathway (B) in HC, VS, and LLV individuals. C–D Betweenness-based networks formed by differentially expressed transcription factors in VS-HC (C) and LLV-VS groups (D). Betweenness scores are mapped by color intensity.
