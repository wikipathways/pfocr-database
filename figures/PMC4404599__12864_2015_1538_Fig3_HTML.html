Schematic overview of some of the most significant IPA-identified, canonical host response pathways. (A) Role of pattern recognition pathways and the interferon signaling pathway. (B) Antigen presentation pathways and pathways involved in leukocyte recruitment and IL-8 signaling. Red boxes are genes upregulated in SBVdelNSs compared to uninfected cells; white boxes are genes not differentially expressed.
