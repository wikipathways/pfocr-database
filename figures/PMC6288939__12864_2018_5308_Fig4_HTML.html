Expression differences in males and females in response to reduced insulin signaling for immune response genes

Expression differences in males and females in response to reduced insulin signaling for immune response genes. For genes in the Toll/Imd signaling pathway (KEGG: dme04624), the expression differences are indicated by color, with color gradations from − 1 or less, to 1 or greater, using KEGG graph and Pathview []. Female control vs female InRDN-expressing flies (a) and male control vs male InRDN-expressing flies (b) are shown. The data in this figure was not filtered by a statistical cut-off. Green indicates higher expression in InRDN-expressing flies and red indicates higher expression in control flies
