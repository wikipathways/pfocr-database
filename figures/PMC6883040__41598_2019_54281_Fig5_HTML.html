Analysis of the genes involved in the nitrogen metabolic and sulfur metabolic pathways

Analysis of the genes involved in the nitrogen metabolic and sulfur metabolic pathways. (a) Overview of the nitrogen metabolic pathway. (b) The relative abundances of the genes involved in the nitrogen metabolic pathway. (b) Overview of the sulfur metabolic pathway. (b) The relative abundances of the genes involved in the sulfur metabolic pathway. Red indicated up-regulated and green indicated down-regulated genes. The heatmap scale ranges from −3 to +3 on a log2 scale.
