Evasion and Interactions of the Humoral Innate Immune Response in Pathogen Invasion, Autoimmune Disease, and Cancer

Upon recognition of a foreign or damage cell surface, one or more complement pathway is activated. The classical and lectin pathways differ in their initiation. The alternative pathway is distinct from the other two pathways but all three pathways converge toward the formation of a C5 convertase, which is necessary for the MAC formation. The indicate a cleavage. Ag, antigen; MAC, Membrane Attack Complex.
