Cadmium Exposure on the Immune System and Immunoregulation

Effects of Cd exposure on innate immune and adaptive immune cells and their underlying mechanisms. Cd ions can enter cells through Ca channels of the L-type or react with surface structures of the cell. After entering the cell, Cd will affect the secretion of immune cytokines through activating oxidative stress, ERS, NF-κB, VDR/CREB, NLRP3, CYPs, and PI3K/Akt signaling pathway, which in turn decrease cell vitality and induce apoptosis.
