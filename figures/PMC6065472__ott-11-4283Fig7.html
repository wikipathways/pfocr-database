Enriched pathways for radix tetrastigma hemsleyani flavonoid treated lung cancer cells

Four main enriched signal pathways and their DE-miRNA target genes.Notes: (A) ErbB signaling pathway. (B) MAPK signaling pathway. (C) Lysosomes. (D) Endocytosis. The target genes of the known DE-miRNAs involved in each pathway are shown in red.Abbreviations: DE-miRNAs, differentially expressed microRNAs; MAPK, mitogen-activated protein kinase.
