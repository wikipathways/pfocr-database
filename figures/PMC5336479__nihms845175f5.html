Deep Coverage of Global Protein Expression and Phosphorylation in Breast Tumor Cell Lines using TMT 10-plex Isobaric Labeling

(A) A volcano plot of the log2 ratio of the averages of Her2 positive cell line- derived pY-IP enriched phosphopeptide intensities to the corresponding averages from Her2 negative cell lines plotted against the adjusted p value (probability that the ratio being different from 1 is due to random chance). Phosphosites with adjusted p value less than 0.005 were denoted in red. Selected phosphosites were labeled with their gene names. (B) Ingenuity pathway analysis suggested the ErbB signaling pathway was highly enriched. Proteins with a higher phosphorylation level at any site in this pathway were highlighted in red.
