Role of AhR in the pathology of atherosclerosis

Role of AhR in the pathology of atherosclerosis. Hypothesis 1: Signaling of downstream inflammatory factors such as vascular cell adhesion molecule 1 via the AhR/nuclear factor-κB signaling pathway leads to monocyte chemotaxis. Hypothesis 2: AhR promotes macrophage absorption of ox-LDL and the formation of foam cells by mediating endogenous and exogenous ligands such as ox-LDL, lipopolysaccharide and 2,3,7,8-tetrachlorodibenzo-p-dioxin. Hypothesis 3: Increased proliferation of vascular smooth muscle cells is implicated in the occurrence of vascular complications.
