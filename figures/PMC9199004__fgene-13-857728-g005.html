Major Downregulation of Circulating microRNAs in Zika Acutely Infected Patients: Potential Implications in Innate and Adaptive Immune Response Signaling Pathways

DM and molecule network related to oncostatin M pathway regulation in RECZIKV+. The network was built using IPA software. Each molecule was represented as a node, and the biological relationship between two nodes is represented as an edge (line). All edges are supported by at least one reference from the literature or canonical information stored in the Ingenuity Knowledge Base (IKB). The molecule network is directly or indirectly related to the oncostatin M signaling canonical pathway. MiR-30a-5p is a central node targeting some other node molecules like signal transducer and activator of transcription 1 (STAT1) and 3 (STAT3), activator protein 1 (JUN), and neuronal differentiation 1 (NEUROD1).
