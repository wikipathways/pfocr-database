Arachidonic acid metabolism pathway where the differential genes and metabolites of colorectal cancer were annotated

Arachidonic acid metabolism pathway where the differential genes and metabolites of colorectal cancer were annotated. Nodes near asterisk symbol belong to the key subpathway (path:00590_1) region identified by Subpathway-GM. The region contained the certain parts of three subsystems: COX, LOX and CYP450. Most of the differential genes involved in LOX5 belonged to the LOX subsystem.
