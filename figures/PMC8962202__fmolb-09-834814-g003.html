CIGB-300-Regulated Proteome Reveals Common and Tailored Response Patterns of AML Cells to CK2 Inhibition

Protein–protein interaction networks associated with the proteomic profiles differentially modulated in AML cells in response to treatment with 40 µM of CIGB-300. Proteins are shown as yellow circles and clusters identified with the MCODE algorithm are highlighted with different edge colors. For each cluster, the related biological process or pathway according to annotations gathered from Gene Ontology (GO), Reactome (R-HSA), or WikiPathways (WP) databases are indicated.
