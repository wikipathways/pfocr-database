RNA-Seq reveals different responses to drought in Neotropical trees from savannas and seasonally dry forests

Representative pathways containing up- (thick up arrows) and down-regulated (thick down arrows) genes. Blue arrows indicate Tabebuia aurea genes, pink H. impetiginosus and yellow H. serratifolius. a Autophagy pathway with DEG in H. serratifolius. b Galactose pathway with DEG in T. aurea, H. impetiginosus and H. serratifolius. c Calvin-Benson cycle showing DEG in H. impetiginosus and H. serratifolius. d Hormonal and signal transduction pathways with DEG in H. serratifolius.
