Layered network between muromonab and myasthenia gravis (MG)

Layered network between muromonab and myasthenia gravis (MG). Diamond indicates drug, circles indicate drug target genes, rectangles indicate the MG risk pathways, and hexagons indicate MG risk genes that regulate the MG risk pathway. Many MG risk genes carry validated miRNA targets.
