IPA Core analysis of the Epithelial Adherens Junctions Signaling in SW480 AHCY deficient cells with increased expression of LEF1 protein

IPA Core analysis of the Epithelial Adherens Junctions Signaling in SW480 AHCY deficient cells with increased expression of LEF1 protein. The accompanying Table 5 provides valuable insights into the transcriptional alterations observed in pivotal genes associated with epithelial adherens junctions signaling following differential expression analysis such as: CDH1, CDH2, TCF/LEF1. Notably, there are changes in gene expression observed in the TCF/LEF complex, mirroring the patterns seen in the Wnt signaling pathway and the regulation of the epithelial-mesenchymal transition pathway
