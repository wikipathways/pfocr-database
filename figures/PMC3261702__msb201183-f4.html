Structural coverage of the core vision pathway and its links to other functional modules

Structural coverage of the core vision pathway and its links to other functional modules. The published core pathway () was extended using evidence from our high-confidence network. Outputs to different functional cellular processes emanating from the proteins in the pathway are indicated, and the available structures are displayed by ribbon representation (see the main text and ). Proteins are colored according to their function.
