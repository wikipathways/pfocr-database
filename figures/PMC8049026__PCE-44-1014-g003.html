Simplified schematic overview of the signal transduction pathway of shade‐induced seed dormancy, early flowering, reduced branching, hyponasty and accelerated hypocotyl and petiole elongation. Interactions between important proteins (light green) and hormones (dark green) are shown and result in the different SAS responses. See main text for details. Arrows, positive effect; inhibitors, negative effect; solid lines, confirmed interactions/known signalling pathways; dotted lines, exact mechanisms to be elucidated.
