Tumor-derived extracellular vesicles as messengers of natural products in cancer treatment

Engineering methods of tumor-derived extracellular vesicles. A) Schematic illustration of the EVs modified with CD47 on membrane surface to achieve mononuclear phagocyte system blockade.
