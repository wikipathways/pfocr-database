Proposed metabolic routes associated with lipid and Beta-carotene biosynthetic pathways in ΔcarRP WJ11 in comparison with the WJ11 strain

The proposed metabolic routes associated with lipid and β-carotene biosynthetic pathways in ΔcarRP WJ11 in comparison with the WJ11 strain. The abbreviations of all metabolite names are in Supplementary Table S8. The abbreviations of all gene IDs are in Supplementary Table S3. A red metabolite name represents a reporter metabolite. Red EC numbers are associated with upregulated genes. The red arrows indicate upregulation routes associated with amino acid metabolism. The green arrows indicate downregulation routes associated with carotenoid and fatty acid biosynthesis. Green EC numbers are associated with downregulated genes
