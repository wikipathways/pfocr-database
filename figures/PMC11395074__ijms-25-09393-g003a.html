miRNAs play a pivotal role in maintaining the delicate balance between cell survival and death by modulating the activity of key pathways such as PI3K/AKT/mTOR, MAPK, TGF-β, Wnt/β-catenin, and JAK/STAT. Pro-apoptotic miRNAs (a): Some miRNAs act as pro-apoptotic regulators targeting anti-apoptotic genes in signaling pathways, promoting programmed cell death. Anti-apoptotic miRNAs (b): Conversely, certain miRNAs serve as anti-apoptotic regulators by targeting pro-apoptotic genes, inhibiting apoptosis and promoting cell survival.
