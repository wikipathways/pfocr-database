Genome-scale metabolic modeling and in silico analysis of opportunistic skin pathogen Cutibacterium acnes

C. acnes metabolic network of iCA843. C. acnes GEM includes central carbon metabolic pathways and amino acid biosynthesis pathways, in addition to characteristic pathways, such as the Wood-Werkman cycle, the SCFAs fermentation pathways, the porphyrin and vitamin B12 biosynthesis pathways, as well as skin condition related TAG lipase reaction and glycerol uptake pathway.
