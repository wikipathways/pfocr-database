Virus-Induced Tumorigenesis and IFN System

Signal transduction pathway network relevant for type I IFNs production and response to IFNs stimulus. Summary of the interfering oncoviruses proteins of different colors label is reported: HPV proteins (red), HTLV-1 (purple), HHV (pink), HCV (light blue), MCPyV (green), and HBV (orange). Faded pathways refer to non-canonical pathways involved in type I IFN signaling [].
