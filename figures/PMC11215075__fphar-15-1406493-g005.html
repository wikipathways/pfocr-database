Ezetimibe, Niemann-Pick C1 like 1 inhibitor, modulates hepatic phospholipid metabolism to alleviate fat accumulation

The proposed hepatic metabolic pathway and altered gene expression by treatment with ezetimibe (white arrow) in a rat model of obesity and type 2 diabetes.
