Identification of Therapeutic Targets for Amyotrophic Lateral Sclerosis Using PandaOmics – An AI-Enabled Biological Target Discovery Platform

Proposed targets in ALS-associated pathways. Several proposed targets, labeled in green, were presented in pathways related to ALS pathogenesis, including neuronal cell death, oxidative stress, neuroinflammation, and proteostasis dysfunction. Information of associated networks was retrieved from KEGG ALS pathway (map05014), Protein processing in endoplasmic reticulum (map04141), T cell receptor signaling pathway (map04660), Ubiquitin mediated proteolysis (map04120), and Pentose phosphate pathway (map00030).
