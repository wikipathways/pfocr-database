Visual representation of the JA/SA/ET biosynthesis and signaling pathways

Visual representation of the JA/SA/ET biosynthesis and signaling pathways. Dark green boxes, MAPK kinases leading from flagellin to defense genes; red boxes, genes within the SA biosynthesis pathway; purple boxes, MAPK kinases leading to repression of SA and induction of JA defense genes; yellow boxes, genes involved in JA biosynthesis; light blue boxes, genes involved in ET biosynthesis; pink boxes, genes involved in ET signaling.
