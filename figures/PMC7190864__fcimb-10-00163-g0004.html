Comparison of significant canonical pathways and upstream regulators in monocytes infected or non-infected with Mtb UT127 and UT205 isolates

Comparison of significant canonical pathways and upstream regulators in monocytes infected or non-infected with Mtb UT127 and UT205 isolates. Top enriched (A) pathways and (B) upstream regulators in MoTB and MoCT infected with M. tuberculosis. (C) Inhibition of TREM1 signaling pathway by MoTB in response to M. tuberculosis infection. Pathways are ranked according to the enrichment score (Fisher's exact test P ≤ 0.05), and the Z-score that predicts activation (≥2) or repression (≤ 2).
