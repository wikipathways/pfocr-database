Proteome analysis of Phytomonas serpens, a phytoparasite of medical interest

Dashed boxes show enzymes found in the present P. serpens proteome analysis but not in Phytomonas sp EM1 and HART1 genome []. Dashed arrows correspond to enzymes found in Phytomonas sp EM1 and HART1 genome but not in the present work. Solid arrows represent enzymes found in P. serpens proteome and in Phytomonas sp EM1 and HART1 genome.
