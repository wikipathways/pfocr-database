Bailing capsule (Cordyceps sinensis) ameliorates renal triglyceride accumulation through the PPARA pathway in diabetic rats

PPAR (peroxisome proliferator activated receptor) signaling pathway affected by Bailing Capsule in DN (diabetic nephropathy) rats. All the different expression genes of BC-H (high dosage of Bailing Capsule) group vs. DN group in PPAR pathway. Red represents up-regulated; green represents down-regulated, white represents no significant change. FC, fold change in BC-H vs. DN group.
