Comparison of the protein kinase/phosphatase signaling pathways in flies, worms, and humans (see text for description)

Comparison of the protein kinase/phosphatase signaling pathways in flies, worms, and humans (see text for description). Kinases are depicted as black rectangles, phosphatases are gray triangles, and other signaling components are in white. Shapes in dotted lines indicate mammalian proteins with no clear fly homologue; however, the function of these components in the pathway may be provided by other Drosophila proteins with related biochemical activities. Drosophila gene names are listed in parentheses.
