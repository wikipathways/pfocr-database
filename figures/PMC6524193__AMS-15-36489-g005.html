Functional prediction of key lncRNA-mRNAs network

Functional prediction of key lncRNA-mRNAs network. A – Hub lncRNA-mRNAs network 1 was involved in regulating Th1 and Th2 cell differentiation, Wnt signaling pathway, glycosaminoglycan biosynthesis and protein O-linked glycosylation. B – Hub lncRNA-mRNAs network 2 was involved in regulating cell cycle, cellular response to DNA damage stimulus, chromosome organization, and immune response
