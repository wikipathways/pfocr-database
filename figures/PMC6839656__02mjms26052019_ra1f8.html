Effect of andrographolide and Simvastatin on the mevalonate pathway in HL-60 leukaemia cells

Effect of andrographolide and Simvastatin on the mevalonate pathway in HL-60 leukaemia cells. Andrographolide inhibits the HMGCR activity and this affects the downstream pathway of cholesterol biosynthesis [image taken from Chen et al. ()]
