Key metabolic gene sets are differentially regulated in subcutaneous adipose tissue after 36 h fasting vs control study (horizontally) and in LBW vs NBW men (vertically)

Key metabolic gene sets are differentially regulated in subcutaneous adipose tissue after 36 h fasting vs control study (horizontally) and in LBW vs NBW men (vertically). KEGG Metabolism pathways are coloured by subgroup: Energy (dark blue), Carbohydrate (light blue), Lipid (red), Amino acid (purple), Other amino acids (purple), Nucleotide, Glycan biosynthesis and Terpenoids and polyketides (grey). Also coloured: Immune system (Organismal system; orange). Human diseases pathways are not included in the figure. #Pathway lower in LBW vs NBW after control study and higher in LBW vs NBW after fasting. xPathway regulated in the opposite direction in response to fasting in LBW and NBW men. SAT, subcutaneous adipose tissue; LBW, low birth weight; NBW, normal birth weight.
