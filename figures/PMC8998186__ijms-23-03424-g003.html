Recycling and Reshaping—E3 Ligases and DUBs in the Initiation of T Cell Receptor-Mediated Signaling and Response

Ubiquitination and deubiquitination in proximal T cell signaling. Overview of known ubiquitin E3 ligases (orange) and deubiquitinating enzymes (blue) involved in regulating the initiation of T cell receptor signaling (green). Details are described in the main text of the manuscript. The T cell signaling pathway is indicated by black arrows. Modifications that dampen T cell receptor signaling are indicated by red arrows. Ubiquitin modifications enhancing T cell signaling are indicated by blue arrows.
