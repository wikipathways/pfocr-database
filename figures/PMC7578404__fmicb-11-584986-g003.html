Summary of the effect of S

Summary of the effect of S. Enteritidis 45 min exposure to EWF (A,B) on selected metabolic pathways. Transporters are symbolized by gray ovals. Up-regulated, down-regulated, and non-regulated genes are represented in red, green or black, respectively. Genes with a ≥4-fold change in expression are in bold. The metabolites predicted to be increased are underlined. Up-regulated, down-regulated and non-regulated pathways are represented by thick, broken, or fine arrows, respectively. Genes with different expression levels between the incubation in EWMM (from ) and in EWF are boxed in pink. Metabolic pathway data were obtained from KEGG (www.genome.jp/kegg).
