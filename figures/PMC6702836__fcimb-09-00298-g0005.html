Partial pathway reconstruction of genes involved in amino acid and carbohydrate metabolism

Partial pathway reconstruction of genes involved in amino acid and carbohydrate metabolism. Only metabolic enzymes with significant differences in relative abundance (q < 0.05) between male and female ticks and KEGG annotation were included in this analysis. Continuous lines represent reactions catalyzed by the enzymes with significant differences in relative abundance between male and female ticks. Dashed lines represent pathway reconstruction based in KEGG.
