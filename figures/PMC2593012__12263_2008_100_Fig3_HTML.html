Gene expression data mapped on the mouse insulin signaling pathway

Gene expression data mapped on the mouse insulin signaling pathway. Fold changes are represented as a gradient and visualized per genebox on the left. Yellow boxes indicate a significance of P ≤ 0.05 and are visualized per genebox on the right. Light gray boxes indicate not significant and are also visualized on the right. The dark gray boxes represent the genes that could not be measured
