Regulation of intrinsic cancer metabolism by obesity-associated growth factors

Regulation of intrinsic cancer metabolism by obesity-associated growth factors. Insulin and insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1) activate the phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase (PI3K) pathway, which in turn upregulates glycolysis and subsidiary metabolic pathways to generate energy and fulfill the biosynthetic needs of proliferation.
