MicroRNA expression profiling of endocrine sensitive and resistant breast cancer cell lines

Network maps showing the miRNAs for which targets are over-represented in gene-sets related to cell adhesion, EMT, estrogen receptor pathway, metastasis, and the respective target genes. Blue: down-regulated miRNA/gene; red: up-regulated miRNA/gene; gray: gene involved in pathway. Gene-sets were pre-selected from the Molecular Signature Database and multiple Fisher Exact tests were performed as mentioned in methods. (For interpretation of the references to color in this figure legend, the reader is referred to the Web version of this article.)
