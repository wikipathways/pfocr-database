Hydrogenophaga b174 cell diagram representing selected catabolic and energy conservation pathways active during the study. This reconstruction is a composite of pathways that were active at any measured point during the 20-day study. Genes that were not expressed, or for which the gene was not identified in the metagenome and therefore no expression level could be assigned, are represented in the diagram as white and black, respectively. Protein complexes (e.g., ETC and hydrogenases) are represented in the diagram with a color gradient if not all subunit genes were expressed.
