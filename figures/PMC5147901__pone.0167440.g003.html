Modeling the Role of Lanthionine Synthetase C-Like 2 (LANCL2) in the Modulation of Immune Responses to Helicobacter pylori Infection

Systems Biologt Markup Language (SBML)-compliant network diagram displaying the three main pathways, CSF1R, CX3CR1, and IL-10R, controlling cytokine production and macrophage phenotype commitment. While interconnected, the three signaling receptors have dominant effects on arms within the network. The CSF1R/M-CSF pathway integrates DAP12 and NFAT activity and is closely associated with the inflammatory Traf/NF-kB pathway as well. The IL-10R pathway is modified by calcium signaling and the transcriptional activities of FOXP1 and NCOR2. The CX3CR1 pathway combines cAMP/PKA pathways with modification to Akt and inflammatory signaling.
