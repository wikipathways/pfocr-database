Comparative Transcriptome Profiling of an SV40-Transformed Human Fibroblast (MRC5CVI) and Its Untransformed Counterpart (MRC-5) in Response to UVB Irradiation

A literature search shows that the most discrepant genes are related to chromosome condensation, DNA repair, cell cycle arrest, and apoptosis (these studies are listed in ). The p53/CDKN1A/pRb pathway and BAX are included to help clarify the results.
