Transcriptomic data shows immunometabolic effects of vitamin D

WikiPathways representation of A) cell cycle; B) DNA replication pathways. Visualization of changes in gene expression after 1,25(OH)2D treatment in THP-1 cells. Log2 fold changes are shown as a gradient from blue (downregulated) to red (upregulated) over white (unchanged). Adjusted p-values < 0.05 are shown in green.
