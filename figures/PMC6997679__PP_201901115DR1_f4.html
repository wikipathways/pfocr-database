Transcriptional regulation of the PAO/phyllobilin pathway during dark-induced senescence is mainly affected in pao1

Transcriptional regulation of the PAO/phyllobilin pathway during dark-induced senescence is mainly affected in pao1. Heat maps represent log2 (FC) of gene expression in each of the four studied lines during dark-induced senescence, as indicated in the inset box. Genes/enzymes: ABF, ABA-responsive element binding factor; ABI5, ABA insensitive5; COI1, coronatine insensitive1; EEL, enhance em level; EIN, ethylene insensitive; ELF3, early flowering3; ERF17, ethylene response factor17; MYC, myelocytomastosis; NAC, NAM, ATAF1/2, and CUC2 domain protein; NAP, NAC-like, activated by PA3/PI; ORE1, oresara1; phyB, phytochrome B; PIF, phytochrome interacting factor; PYL9, pyrabactin resistance1-like9; SnRK2, Ser/Thr kinase2; SOC1, suppressor of overexpression of coi1. WT, wild type.
