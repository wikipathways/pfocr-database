Cancer Transcriptome Dataset Analysis: Comparing Methods of Pathway and Gene Regulatory Network-Based Cluster Identification

Several selected NCs from the GC datasets by PATHOME network-derived network clustering method. NCs A–D were derived from GSE36968, and NCs E–G were obtained from GSE27342. The red node colors indicate upregulated genes in GC tumors (compared to normal tissue), while the green node colors represent downregulated genes.
