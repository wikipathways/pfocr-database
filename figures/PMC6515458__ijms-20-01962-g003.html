Protein–protein interaction (PPI) network in muscle wasting in cancer cachexia

Protein–protein interaction (PPI) network in muscle wasting in cancer cachexia. Lines highlight PPI, with Stat3, Foxo1, Camk2b, Adipoq, Nr3c1, and Actc1 presenting the highest number of interactions. Colors highlight biological processes or molecular function of the circle network components. The larger the circle, the higher the number of interactions identified. STRING v10.5.1 was used to generate protein interactions, and the resulting network was visualized using Cytoscape v3.4.0.
