Genomic landscape of Mongolian hepatocellular carcinoma

Mutated genes and their activation/inhibition relationships to other genes, families, complexes, and cellular processes are displayed using curated pathway templates distilled from TCGA,. For each gene, the fraction of mutated samples within each molecular subclass, MO1-4, is shown. From the analysis of paired tumor-vs-nontumor gene expression ratios, the effect on each mutated gene (as activated or inactivated) within each molecular subclass is also provided. The bottom panel shows novel candidate driver genes in Mongolian hepatocellular carcinoma reported in this study.
