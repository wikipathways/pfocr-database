BDNF and MAP kinase pathways in combined portrait of depression

BDNF and MAP kinase pathways in combined portrait of depression. Genes enriched for BDNF signaling (blue circles) and MAP kinase pathway (red circles) were enriched among the top 1000 dysregulated genes in the combined portrait. Further, a subset of genes is in both pathways (yellow circles), including BDNF, DUSP1, DUSP4, DUSP5, FOS, MAP2K5, MAPK8, NR4A1, and two heatshock proteins. Plotting of gene interactions in STRING (version 11) highlights gene–gene interactions (lines).
