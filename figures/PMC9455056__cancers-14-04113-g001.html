MYCN and Metabolic Reprogramming in Neuroblastoma

Major metabolic pathways for supporting macromolecule biosynthesis in cancer cells. Increased central carbon metabolism provides building blocks for the production of proteins, nucleotides, and lipids to sustain the proliferation of cancer cells. 3-PG, 3-phosphoglycerate; 5,10-MTHF, 5,10-methylene-tetrahydrofolate; 10-formyl-THF, 10-formyl-tetrahydrofolate; α-KG, α-ketoglutarate; Glucose-6-P, glucose-6-phosphate; G6PD, glucose-6-phosphate dehydrogenase; HK2, hexokinase 2; LDH, lactate dehydrogenase; OAA, oxaloacetate; PHGDH, phosphoglycerate dehydrogenase; PPP, pentose phosphate pathway; THF, tetrahydrofolate. MYCN targets are highlighted in red.
