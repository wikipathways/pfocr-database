Differentially Active and Conserved Neural Enhancers Define Two Forms of Adaptive Noncoding Evolution in Humans

Genes modulating canonical Wnt signaling associated with selection and DE CREs. CREs were associated with genes if they occur within 500 kb of TSSs. Genes associated with selection CREs have square outlines. Genes associated with CREs that are DE between orthologs have red (higher in human) or blue (higher in chimpanzee) fill. Genes associated with CREs that are DE between cell types are marked with horizontal (higher in NPC) or vertical (higher in neuron) stripes, and stripe colors indicate if the human (stripes) or chimpanzee (stripes) CRE was DE. The selection and DE profiles of CREs associated with paralogous genes are combined for each gene in the pathway but are separately defined in . Genes with circular outlines and white color are not associated with CREs in our assay but are included to inform about relevant interactions.
