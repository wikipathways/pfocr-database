Mechanical stretch regulates the expression of specific miRNA in extracellular vesicles released from lung epithelial cells

Network of molecules identified by ingenuity pathway analysis (IPA) to be associated with miRNAs modified stretch. (A) Network was generated for the miRNA-EVs differentially regulated in response to 5% continuous stretch. (B) Network was generated for the miRNA-EVs differentially regulated in response to 10% cyclic stretch.
