Biosynthesis and metabolic pathway of BH4

The biosynthesis and metabolic pathway of BH4. BH4 is a cofactor of PAH, TH and TPH and participates in the synthesis of neurotransmitters. GTPCH I, GTP cyclohydrolase; PTPS, PTP synthase; SR, sepiapterin reductase; CR, carbonyl reductase; AR, aldose reductase; DHFR, dihydrofolate reductase; PCD, pterin-4α-carbinolamine dehydratase; DHPR, dihydrobiopterin reductase; TH, tyrosine hydroxylase; TPH, tryptophan hydroxylase; PAH, phenylalanine hydroxylase; AADC, aromatic l-amino acid decarboxylase.
