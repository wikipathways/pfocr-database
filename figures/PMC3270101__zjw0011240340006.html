Pathway map related to cisplatin-induced hepatotoxicity based on proteomic and genomic analysis

Pathway map related to cisplatin-induced hepatotoxicity based on proteomic and genomic analysis. Mapping of ratios of proteins and genes on the drug metabolism, oxidative stress, fatty acid metabolism, glycolysis and TCA cycle, and urea cycle and inflammation metabolism. Networks of inter-relationships of these genes and proteins were constructed using the KEGG pathway program. The rectangles represent proteins obtained from the MS data. The ovals represent genes obtained from the microarray data.
