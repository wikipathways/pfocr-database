Gene expression profile analysis of the rabbit retinal vein occlusion model

Each cell represents the log2 ratio gene expression data of the RVO group normalized to the control group. The coloration indicates upregulated (Red) and downregulated (Green) genes. Pathway maps of (A) HIF-1α, (B) VEGF, (C) TNFα, and (D) JAK-STAT signaling pathways. The analysis was performed using the R pathview package.
