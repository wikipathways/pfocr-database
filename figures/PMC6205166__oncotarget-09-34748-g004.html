Leukemia inhibitory factor via the Toll-like receptor 5 signaling pathway involves aggravation of cachexia induced by human gastric cancer-derived 85As2 cells in rats

The increased gene expression of the TLR signaling pathway in 85As2 cells compared to MKN45cl85 cells was demonstrated by MetaCore analysis of the DNA microarray. Red represents increased gene expression and blue represents decreased gene expression in 85As2 cells compared to MKN45cl85 cells.
