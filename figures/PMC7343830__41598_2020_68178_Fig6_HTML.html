Proposed regulatory mechanisms of blood circulation for hypoxic adaptation in Tibetan chickens

Proposed regulatory mechanisms of blood circulation for hypoxic adaptation in Tibetan chickens. These genes, marked with red color, are involved in HIF-1, VEGF, MAPK, mTOR, PI3K-Akt, Notch, Insulin, focal adhesion signaling pathway, Renin-angiotensin system, vascular smooth muscle contraction and oxygen binding. These pathways and genes indicate Tibetan chickens adapt to hypoxia by regulating angiogenesis and promoting blood circulation (green circles).
