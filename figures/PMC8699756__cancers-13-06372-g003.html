The human cell cycle pathway (WP179). The labels are colored according to the direction of the change in expression. Genes with a higher expression of TP53mut in comparison to HPV+ VSCCs are colored red. A thicker black border marks significantly (FDR ≤ 0.1) differentially expressed genes.
