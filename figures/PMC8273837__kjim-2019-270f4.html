Upregulation of receptor tyrosine kinase-like orphan receptor 2 in idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis

Correlation of idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis fibroblast gene expression between receptor tyrosine kinase-like orphan receptor 2 (ROR2) and Wnt signaling pathway genes.
