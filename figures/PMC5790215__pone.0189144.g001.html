Prediction of reaction knockouts to maximize succinate production by Actinobacillus succinogenes

NTP represents nucleotide triphosphate, where N = A, U, G, C. Part (a) shows the Glycolysis and the Oxidative Pentose Phosphate pathways, whereas part (b) shows the mixed acid fermentation pathway. The enzyme names are represented in red, whereas the pathway names are represented in blue.
