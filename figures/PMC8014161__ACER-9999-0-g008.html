Network Meta‐Analysis on the Mechanisms Underlying Alcohol Augmentation of COVID‐19 Pathologies

Ten of the 14 QCNE pathways that contain any of the key molecules consistently identified in the top 10 canonical pathways, upstream regulators, or networks regulated by predicted top upstream regulators in the IPA for core analysis of molecules affected by both EtOH and COVID‐19 via literature, RNA‐seq, and QCNE: HMGB1 signaling, IL‐1 signaling, IL‐6 signaling, IL‐2 signaling, SAPK/JNK signaling, PGDF signaling, PTEN signaling, CDK5 signaling, IGF‐1 signaling, and inhibition of ARE‐mediated mRNA degradation pathway.
