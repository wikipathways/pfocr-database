8-Hydroxydaidzein, an Isoflavone from Fermented Soybean, Induces Autophagy, Apoptosis, Differentiation, and Degradation of Oncoprotein BCR-ABL in K562 Cells

Signaling pathways and process networks associated with 8-OHD treatment. (a) KEGG MAPK signaling pathway (hsa04010) in K562 cells in response to 8-OHD (100 μM) treatment for 48 h. Expression changes of target genes are mapped by colors; red color: statistically significant increase in expression, yellow color: statistically significant decrease in expression, green color: expression statistically insignificant. (b) Gene Set Enrichment Analysis (GSEA) demonstrated that BioCarta MAPK pathway gene set was enriched in the DEGs of 8-OHD. (c) Top 15 significantly enriched pathways according to the MetaCore database in response to 8-OHD treatment. (d) Top 23 significantly enriched process networks according to the MetaCore database in response to 8-OHD treatment.
