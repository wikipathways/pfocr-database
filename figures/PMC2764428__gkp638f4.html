MiRNA/TF-mediated regulatory pathway associated with tumorigenesis

A miRNA/TF-mediated regulatory pathway associated with tumorigenesis. miRNAs (miR-34, miR-17, let-7 and miR-1) are shown as small pink circles. Oncogene TFs (MYC, MYB, E2F1, E2F3, E2F5, BCL6, ETS1 and STAT3) are shown as red circles. The tumor suppressor gene TP53 is shown as a green circle. Other miRNA-regulated TFs are shown as light blue circles. Red and green arrows indicate activation and inhibition, respectively. Predicted regulations are shown as dashed arrows, while validated regulations are shown as solid arrows.
