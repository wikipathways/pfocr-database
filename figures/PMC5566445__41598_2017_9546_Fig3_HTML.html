The KEGG photosynthesis pathway map for T

The KEGG photosynthesis pathway map for T. pseudonana during N depletion compared to N replete conditions (ND vs. NR). The KEGG photosynthesis pathway map can be found online at http://www.kegg.jp/pathway/map00195. The light green boxes indicate the genes were down-regulated and up-expressed (red boxes). The highlighted in red indicate the protein represent up-expressed and down-expressed (green word). The detail fold-change and annotation of these genes were summarized in Table  and Dataset .
