Chart of cell cycle genes

A chart of cell cycle genes. Green and red boxes highlight for higher expression (FDR < 0.1) in dormant and activated blastocysts, respectively. The genes, which showed higher expression with P ≤ 0.05 and FDR ≥ 0.1 in activated blastocysts, are highlighted as pink boxes. The lines with arrowheads or T heads represent stimulation or suppression, respectively. The pathway figure is based on the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes pathway database (), with slight modification. Based on published data (), the dashed lines from Brca1 represent increased expression of p21cip1/WAF1 and decreased expression of cyclin E in Brca1–/– embryos. +P, –P, and +U indicate phosphorylation, dephosphorylation, and ubiquitination, respectively.
