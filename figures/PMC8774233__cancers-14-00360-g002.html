Metastatic Progression of Osteosarcomas: A Review of Current Knowledge of Environmental versus Oncogenic Drivers

Effectors of the Hippo pathway, YAP/TAZ, are at the core of the epithelial–mesenchymal transition (EMT) regulation. Their nuclear translocation and transcriptional activities depend on microenvironment cues and the multiple signaling pathways that integrate these signals to induce cell adaptation. YAP/TAZ, in turn, modulate the expression of genes and activity of signaling pathways involved in the EMT.
