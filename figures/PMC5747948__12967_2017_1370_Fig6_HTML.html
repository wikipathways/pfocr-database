Comprehensive molecular biomarker identification in breast cancer brain metastases

The top three merged networks based on the 370 probe sets comprise molecules related to cell cycle, DNA replication, recombination, and repair, cellular assembly and organization, connective tissue and developmental disorders, gastrointestinal and inflammatory disease and inflammatory response (TableÂ ).
