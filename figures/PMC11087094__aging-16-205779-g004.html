Three greatly important metabolic pathways in digestive system tumors

Three greatly important metabolic pathways in digestive system tumors. The metabolic process of metabolites and the proteins involved in their regulation. Differentially expressed metabolites (red), unchanged metabolites (grey) regulated proteins (yellow), metabolic pathway names (blue).
