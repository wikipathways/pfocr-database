Enriched pathway maps

Enriched pathway maps. (A) Map of the Development Glucocorticoid receptor signaling. It is the top scored map (map with the lowest p-value) based on the enrichment distribution. Experimental data from all files is linked to and visualized on the maps as thermometer-like figures. Up-ward thermometers have red color and indicate up-regulated signals. (B) Map of the Glucocorticoids-mediated inhibition of pro-constrictory and pro-inflammatory signaling in airway smooth muscle cells. The second scored map (map with the second lowest p-value) based on the enrichment distribution sorted by “common” set. Experimental data from all files is linked to and visualized on the maps as thermometer-like figures. Up-ward thermometers have red color and indicate up-regulated signals. The details of the symbols used in this Figure are available here: https://portal.genego.com/legends/MetaCoreQuickReferenceGuide.pdf.
