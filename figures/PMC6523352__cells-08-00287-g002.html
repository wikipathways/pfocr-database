Heme activator proteins (HAP) complex

The heme activator proteins (HAP) complex. The master regulator of mitochondrial biogenesis in the yeast Saccharomyces cerevisiae. The four subunits constituting the complex are represented here, HAP2 (H2), HAP3 (H3), and HAP5 (H5) are the DNA-binding subunits and HAP4 is the activating subunit. Modified from [].
