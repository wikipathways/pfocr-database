The interaction effects of enriched KEGG pathway terms and putative target proteins identified in the present study

Schematic representation of the interaction effects of enriched KEGG pathway terms and putative target proteins identified in the present study. Green rectangles represent putative target proteins identified in this study. Carmine characters represent enriched KEGG pathway terms. The left panel shows potential MOAs that are directly related to Aβ synthesis, transport, degradation, and clearance. The right panel shows MOAs that are indirectly linked or irrelevant to Aβ.
