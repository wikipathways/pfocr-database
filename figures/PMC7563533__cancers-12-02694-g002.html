Key databases as primary resources for drug repositioning

Key databases as primary resources for drug repositioning. Databases (DBs) can be divided into five classes, including Drug Design DB, Specific DB, Target-based DB, Tool-based DB, and Raw DB. The content of each DB is shown with an edge to the subcategories. Refer to for a full description. DBs with omics and high-throughput datasets are colored in yellow.
