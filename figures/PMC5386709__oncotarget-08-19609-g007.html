Identification of downstream metastasis-associated target genes regulated by LSD1 in colon cancer cells

Gene expression for a subset of genes in the p53 signaling pathway is enhanced by lysine-specific demethylase1 (LSD1) depletion. p53 pathway is adapted from KEGG database. Upregulated genes are labeled in red, and downregulated genes are labeled in green.
