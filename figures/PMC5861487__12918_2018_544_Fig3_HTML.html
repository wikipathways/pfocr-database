TGFB pathway

KEGG protein distribution of TGF-β pathway proteins observed among the core proteins. The identified TGF-β signaling proteins were mapped to KEGG. These proteins act as the bridge in the TGF-β signaling pathway, and show significant association with multiple functions in the TGF-β pathway, including osteoblast differentiation, neurogenesis, ventral mesoderm specification, induction of apoptosis, regulation of G1 arrest, and left-right axis determination
