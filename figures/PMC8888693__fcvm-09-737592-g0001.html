Using a Systems Approach to Explore the Mechanisms of Interaction Between Severe Covid-19 and Its Coronary Heart Disease Complications

Existing model showing the mechanisms of coronary heart disease (–). The affective pathway of pharmaceuticals, blue boxes, is shown in Figure and salient serological biomarkers are indicated by the red tags (). The blunted blue arrows denote antagonize or inhibit and pointed blue arrows denote up-regulate or facilitate.
