Transcriptional Profiling Reveals Adaptive Response and Tolerance to Lactic Acid Stress in Pichia kudriavzevii

Pathways of P. kudriavzevii C-16 response to lactic acid stress. Red letters mean upregulated genes. Transcripts of P. kudriavzevii C-16 are shown near the pathway as a heat map, based on fold-changes in transcript levels relative to the type strain ATCC 24210. Colour legend is shown at the top right of the map.
