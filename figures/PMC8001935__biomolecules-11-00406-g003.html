PPI-specific gene-regulatory networks of top 5 KDs in IGF-I and IR (eQTL mapping)

PPI-specific gene-regulatory networks of top 5 KDs in IGF-I and IR (eQTL mapping). (eQTL, expression quantitative trait loci; IGF-I, insulin-growth factor-I; IR, insulin resistance; KD, key drivers; PPI, protein to protein interaction network; T2DM, type 2 diabetes; wKDA, weighted KD analysis). The bigger nodes with red outlines are top KDs in the enriched pathway obtained from wKDA. The subnetworks of the KDs are indicated by different colors according to their differences in canonical functions. (A) T2DM (module M19708)–specific KDs and subnetworks (from the meta-analysis of IGF-I and IR); (B) insulin signaling pathway (module M18155)–specific KDs and subnetworks (from IR eQTLs).
