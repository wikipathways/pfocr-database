Deep sequencing and in silico analyses identify MYB-regulated gene networks and signaling pathways in pancreatic cancer

MYB silencing in pancreatic cancer cells led to downregulation of pancreatic adenocarcinoma pathway in our IPA analysis (Z score = −0.333). The schematic depicts different signaling components of this pathway and highlights the down-regulated genes and associated signaling complexes in ‘green’, while the solitary up-regulated gene, PLD, is presented in ‘red’.
