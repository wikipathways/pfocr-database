Activation mechanisms of the Hippo kinase signaling cascade

The core Hippo pathway in Drosophila (A) and mammals (B). The core Hippo signaling events are shown. The same color scheme is used for the corresponding pathway components. Phosphorylation events are indicated by dark red arrows. See the text for further details.
