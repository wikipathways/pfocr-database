Differential Transcriptome Responses to Aflatoxin B1 in the Cecal Tonsil of Susceptible and Resistant Turkeys

Differential expression of genes in the IPA canonical pathway “GPCR-Mediated Integration of Enteroendocrine Signaling Exemplified by an L Cell.” Genes with significantly lower expression in domesticated turkeys relative to Eastern wild birds after AFB1 treatment are denoted in green. Genes tested by qRT-PCR are outlined in orange ().
