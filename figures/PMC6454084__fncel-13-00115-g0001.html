Putative palmitoylated proteins of the mechanistic target of rapamycin (mTOR) signaling pathway

Putative palmitoylated proteins of the mechanistic target of rapamycin (mTOR) signaling pathway. Palmitoyl-proteomic studies have identified a number of mTOR signaling components as potentially palmitoylated (outlined in yellow) including mTOR itself, subunits of the vacuolar ATPase, components of the regulatory GATOR2, Ragulator, and tuberous sclerosis complex (TSC) complexes, and other regulatory proteins. Additional mTOR signaling components are bioinformatically predicted to be palmitoylated (outlined in red).
