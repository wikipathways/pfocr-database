Network of miR-93 and miR-21 with putative target genes of important signaling pathways from KEGG enrichment analysis. The network was constructed by Cluepedia plugin in Cytoscape environment. Nodes reveal parallelogram for TGFβ signaling pathway, rectangle for MAPK signaling pathway, square for insulin signaling pathway, triangle for Wnt signaling pathway, diamond for neurotrophin signaling pathway, hexagon for miRNAs and edges for interactions.
