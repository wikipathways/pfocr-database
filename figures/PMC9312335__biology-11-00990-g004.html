High-Temperature Stress Effect on the Red Cusk-Eel (Geypterus chilensis) Liver: Transcriptional Modulation and Oxidative Stress Damage

Illustration of transcriptional changes in liver of G. chilensis in response to high-temperature stress using modified KEGG pathway map of protein processing in the endoplasmic reticulum. The red rectangles indicate down-regulated transcripts, while green rectangles indicates up-regulated transcripts between the control and high-temperature groups.
