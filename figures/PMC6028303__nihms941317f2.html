Revisiting antipsychotic drug actions through gene networks associated with schizophrenia

The figure shows two of the top ranked biological processes and pathways derived from Gene ontology and pathway enrichment analysis for the interconnected risk genes (n=79). a) Developmental biology pathway (477129, REACTOME, p=6.3e-9). b) Learning or memory (GO:0007611, p=1.87e-6) and Cognition (GO: 0050890, p=4.64e-6), which consisted of the same set of risk genes.
