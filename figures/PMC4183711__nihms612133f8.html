Diagram depicts a model in which Eya2 and Ebf3 are positioned in the gene regulatory pathway for the development of RGCs

Diagram depicts a model in which Eya2 and Ebf3 are positioned in the gene regulatory pathway for the development of RGCs. In this model, Eya2 acts to specify competent Atoh7-expressing RPCs to a RGC fate while Ebf3 acts at late stages of RGC differentiation.
