Prioritization of metabolic genes as novel therapeutic targets in estrogen-receptor negative breast tumors using multi-omics data and text mining

Top-ranked drug targets are highlighted with blue-border. Spheres are metabolites, and hexagons are enzymes. Yellow lines reflect KEGG RPAIR links whereas pink lines reflect reactant-enzyme links. Blue and red color represent significant (p < 0.05, ANOVA) decrease and increase in the levels in ERneg in comparison to ERpos tumors. Orange color reflects not significant changes in the levels. Size of the nodes reflects fold changes. ERneg tumors rely more on TCA anaplerosis, anabolic glycolysis, de-novo biosynthesis of amino acids and nucleotide salvage.
