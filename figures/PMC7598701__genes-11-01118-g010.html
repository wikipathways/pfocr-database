Differentially expressed genes involved in the p53 signaling pathway were annotated by KEGG

The differentially expressed genes involved in the p53 signaling pathway were annotated by KEGG. The DEGs included cdkn1a, fas, gadd45aa, gadd45ba, gadd45bb, casp3b, casp8l2, pmaip1, LOC100535166, serpine1, serpinel3 and sesn2. Red indicates upregulated genes.
