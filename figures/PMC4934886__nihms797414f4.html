Aortic valve stenosis–related biological processes

Integration of the genome-wide association studies (GWAS) and RNA-Seq results with known aortic valve stenosis (AS)–related biological processes. Integration of genes differentially expressed between calcified and normal valves and with/near single-nucleotide polymorphisms with P<1×10−4 in biological processes known to be implicated in AS development and progression.
