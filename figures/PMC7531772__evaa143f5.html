Genome of the Softshell Clam Mya arenaria and the Evolution of Apoptosis

The functional implications of the evolutionary dynamics of the apoptosis gene set. Top. The KEGG apoptosis human pathway is shown redrawn from (). Genes that are significantly depleted in ecdysozoans are highlighted in yellow. This analysis shows that the genes depleted in ecdysozoans function in every aspect of apoptosis including external signaling, mitochondrial disruption, and DNA fragmentation.
