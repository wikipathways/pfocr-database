TI2-induced subnetwork

The TI2-induced subnetwork was highlighted green in the PN model of . The subnetwork covers the formation of complex IIb (place CIIb indicated by a red arrow) and induction of apoptosis (transition Apoptosis indicated by a red arrow) via the activation of CASP3 (places CASP3 as logical places indicated by a red arrows) in the extrinsic pathway. Additional logical places that connected graphical subnetworks were Procaspase3 and TRADD:RIP1, each marked by a red arrow.
