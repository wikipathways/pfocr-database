AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK) signaling pathway

(A) Canonical pathway; and (B) heat map of the genes related to the AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK) signaling pathway in epididymal white adipose tissue (eWAT) of diet-induced obese C57BL/6J mice treated with green tea extract for 12 weeks. ND, normal diet, AIN-93G; HFD, high-fat diet, 60% kcal from fat; GT, green tea extract, 0.25% w/w. The significant pathways and functions were obtained via Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA).
