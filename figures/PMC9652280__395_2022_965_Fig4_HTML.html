Non-responsiveness to cardioprotection by ischaemic preconditioning in Ossabaw minipigs with genetic predisposition to, but without the phenotype of the metabolic syndrome

Protein–protein associations of variant protein coding sequences between the Ossabaw minipig and Göttingen minipig strains: The Search Tool for the Retrieval of Interacting Genes (STRING) identified networks for the topic mitochondrion, vitamin B12 metabolism, inflammation including the janus kinase (JAK)—signal transducer and activator of transcription (STAT) pathway, fat metabolism, mineralisation and ribosome. We assigned the cluster designation with reference of the functional enrichments in the STRING network analysis. The matching proteins within the respective networks are listed in the supplemental excel data sheet “STRING–protein–protein associations” column I “matching proteins in your network (labels)”
