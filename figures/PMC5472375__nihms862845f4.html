Based on GeneCodis3 analysis of Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathways, 23 significantly enriched pathways were revealed

Based on GeneCodis3 analysis of Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathways, 23 significantly enriched pathways were revealed. We combined these pathways and summarized them into three biologically relevant pathways: (A) insulin secretion, (B) stress/growth factor signaling, and (C) inflammatory pathway. Red color represents up-regulated genes and green color represents down-regulated genes.
