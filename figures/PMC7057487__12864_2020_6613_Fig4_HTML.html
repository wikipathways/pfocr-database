Interactive network for the 8 enriched signaling transduction pathways

The interactive network for the 8 enriched signaling transduction pathways. The network was constructed according to the interaction relationships annotated in KEGG database. Each oval represents a signaling transduction pathway. The ovals with pink fillings represent the significantly shared pathways in 5–7 domesticated species. The positively selected genes (PSGs) within the signaling pathways are labeled around the ovals with different colors for distinct species. The red color indicates PSGs identified in at least 2 species. GPCR: G protein-coupled receptor; RTK: Receptor tyrosine kinase
