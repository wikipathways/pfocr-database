Compound–target–pathway subnetwork of 12 compounds relating to AD

The compound–target–pathway subnetwork of 12 compounds relating to AD. Compound was denoted by blue triangle, target by magenta circle and pathway by green rectangular. Known compound–target interaction was represented as gray line, predicted compound–target interaction as salmon line and target–pathway association as blue line. This figure was plotted using Cytoscape 3.3.0.
