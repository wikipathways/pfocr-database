Schematic diagrams of SOS1 activation

Schematic diagrams of SOS1 activation. (A) The KRas4B activation cycles by SOS1 are shown. The dotted lines denote the rare activation cycle, SOS10D → SOS10T, in the absence of allosteric Ras. The thin solid lines represent the limited activation, SOS1D0 → SOS1DD → SOS1DT. The thick solid lines denote the accelerated activation cycle, SOS1T0 → SOS1TD → SOS1TT, with high level of Ras activation. (B) A schematic diagram illustrates SOS1 activity at the plasma membrane. SOS1 activates KRas4B, and active KRas4B leads to cell proliferation regulating through the MAPK pathway. To see this figure in color, go online.
