Activation of MAPK signaling pathway in BCSCs

Activation of MAPK signaling pathway in BCSCs. Cooperation between EGFR and HER2 promotes EMT, tumor growth, motility, and ALDH activity through activation of MNK and XIAP in a MAPK-dependent manner. In addition, chemotherapy promotes EMT and induces MAPK signaling pathway and expression of Nanog/KIF4 in a HIF1-dependent manner.
