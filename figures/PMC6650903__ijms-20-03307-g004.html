Schematic plot of significantly differentially expressed genes involved in GA metabolism and signaling under CK, CCC, and PBZ treatments in roots and shoots

Schematic plot of significantly differentially expressed genes involved in GA metabolism and signaling under CK, CCC, and PBZ treatments in roots and shoots. The plant growth retardants CCC and PBZ restricted the gene expression in the GA metabolism pathway both in the root and shoot in (A)–(D), and PBZ/CCC treatment raised the gene expression in the GA signaling pathway in roots in A and B, but decreased them in shoots in (C,D). The genes with red color indicated upregulation, and the green color genes were down regulated.
