Renal diseases

Renal diseases. (A) Networks display DEGs which is upregulated (red) and downregulated (green) genes involved in damage of kidney in T2DM based on the multiple corrections of the log P value. The deep orange arrow indicates the prediction of an increase of activation, yellow arrow indicates a decreased prediction of activation, and grey arrow could affect. IL6, ICAM1, GC, CFB, C4A/C4B, AGT, and TNF increase the prediction of kidney damage. (C), Causative and (CO), correlation relationships. (B) Network displays the role of acute inflammatory response pathway and cascade involving TNF and IL6 as an upstream regulator which affects the upstream transcription regulators; NFKB and NF-IL6 and kinases such as c-jun, and c-fos, which affects downstream targets related to damage of kidneys such as IL6, ICAM1, GC, CFB, C4A/C4B, AGT, and TNF which increase prediction of kidney damage.
