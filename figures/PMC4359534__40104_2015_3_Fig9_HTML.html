Lipid metabolism, molecular transport, small molecule biochemistry

Lipid metabolism, molecular transport, small molecule biochemistry. Pathway analysis of gene functions in broiler hypothalamus transcriptome in response to acute heat stress. Red color shows up-regulation and green color shows down-regulation (IPA). The intensity of green and red molecule colors indicates the degree of down or upregulation, respectively. White molecules are not differential expression, but are included to illustrate association with significantly up-regulated and down-regulated genes.
