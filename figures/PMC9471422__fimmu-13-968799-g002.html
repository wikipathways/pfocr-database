Contribution of the gut-liver axis to the immune signaling pathway of NAFLD

The destruction of the intestinal epithelial barrier caused by intestinal flora imbalance is an important condition for the development of NAFLD and nonalcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH). Intestinal inflammation and the production of metabolic toxins cause intestinal barrier dysfunction, exposing the liver to flora metabolites and promoting the development of NAFLD.
