Receptors and cellular signaling pathways implicated in the antitumor effect of ANP

Receptors and cellular signaling pathways implicated in the antitumor effect of ANP. NHE-1, Na+/H+ exchanger isoform 1; NPRs, Natriuretic peptide receptors; TKR, Tyrosine kinase receptor; VEGF, vascular endothelial growth factor; VEGFR, VEGF receptor. Details and main function/s of the different molecular components, and references supporting the role of each molecular component and pathway in ANP antitumor action are reported in (Table ).
