Pathway analysis using WikiPathways revealed that the factors related to the calcium signalling pathway (Kelder, T., Chichester, C., Hanspers, K.: Calcium Regulation in the Cardiac Cell (Danio rerio). http://www.wikipathways.org/instance/WP1365_r71502) were significantly downregulated (p < 0.0001) in z-usmg5 MO embryos compared to in wild-type embryos. Downregulated genes in z-usmg5 MO embryos with a fold change > 1.5 compared to wild-type embryos are highlighted in yellow boxes.
