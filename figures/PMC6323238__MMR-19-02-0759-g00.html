Regulatory system of ERK1/2 signaling

Regulatory system of ERK1/2 signaling. ERK1/2 has a number of substrates, including the MAPK cascade kinases. ERK1/2 may phosphorylate these proteins at numerous sites, which disrupts their normal binding to downstream substrates. Furthermore, ERK1/2 engages in crosstalk with the AKT signaling pathway, p53, DUSPs and Sprys, all of which form a massive regulatory network to tightly regulate ERK1/2 signaling. ERK, extracellular signal-regulated protein kinase; MAPK, mitogen-activated protein kinase; AKT, protein kinase B; DUSPs, dual-specificity phosphatases; Sprys, Sprouty proteins.
