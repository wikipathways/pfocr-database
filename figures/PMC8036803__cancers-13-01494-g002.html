Msi1 impact on cell cycle and division

Msi1 impact on cell cycle and division. (A) Network shows cell cycle and/or division genes downregulated in Msi1 KO GBM cells in comparison to controls. The network was built using String [] considering interaction (experimental evidence), text mining and co-occurrence. Different colors were used to indicate clusters of strongly related genes; Msi1 targets previously identified by CLIP or RIP [,,] are labeled in red. (B) The impact of Msi1 KO on cell cycle is shown using KEGG’s cell cycle pathway. Downregulated genes in U251 and U343 Msi1 KO cells in comparison to respective controls are in blue boxes and Msi1 targets previously identified by CLIP or RIP [,,] are labeled in red.
