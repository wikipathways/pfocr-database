Allelic origins of the carotenoid pathway genes

Allelic origins of the carotenoid pathway genes. The red and blue arrows indicate C. reticulata and C. maxima alleles, respectively. The yellow arrows indicate the C. medica allele. The black arrows correspond to alleles with undefined origin.
