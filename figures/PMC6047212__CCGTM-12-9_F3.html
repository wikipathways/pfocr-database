Cell cycle pathway and common checkpoints

Cell cycle pathway and common checkpoints. The downward arrows indicate decreased expression of Ink4 family of Cdk inhibitor (p16) and Cip/Kinase inhibitory protein (Kip) family inhibitor (p21/27) during hepatocarcinogenesis. Release of E2F transcription factor leads to G1 to S transition.
