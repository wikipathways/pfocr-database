NMR and Metabolomics—A Roadmap for the Future

Metabolic pathway summarizing the compound-induced changes in the C. reinhardtii metabolome identified by NMR and GC-MS (metabolites of interest). Metabolites that were only identified by NMR are colored blue. Metabolites that were only identified by GC-MS are colored red. Metabolites identified by both methods are colored black, and metabolites not identified are colored grey. The total numbers of metabolites of interest within these metabolic pathways that were identified by either NMR, GC-MS, or both techniques were 14, 16 and 17 metabolites, respectively.
