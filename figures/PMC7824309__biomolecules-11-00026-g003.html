Pathway enrichment analysis

Pathway enrichment analysis. (A) Schematic pancreatic adenocarcinoma signaling network generated from IPA analysis based on differentially miRNA-target genes in mice bearing pancreatic tumor and fed with ERSD versus normal nutriment. Upregulated genes, as revealed by fold-change analysis, are red colored. Color intensity indicates the score of upregulation. (B) IPA analysis depicting that either NF-kB or TGFβ pathways are activated in pancreatic cancer tissues of mice fed with control diet, leading to the stimulation of tumor growth and invasion, gene expression, and loss of growth inhibitory effects of TGFβ. Genes predicted to be activated (orange-colored) and inhibited (blue-colored) when comparing miRNA-target genes of mice fed with ERSD versus control diet are shown. Color intensity indicates higher absolute z-score.
