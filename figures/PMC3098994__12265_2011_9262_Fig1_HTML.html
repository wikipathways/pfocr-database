Genetics of Mechanosensation in the Heart

The figure summarises some of the postulated mechanosensory pathways active in cardiomyocytes, namely: (1) AT1 receptor-mediated pathway, (2) integrin-mediated pathway, (3) Z-disc-mediated pathway, (4) GPCR/N2B/FHL1/Erk 2 pathway, (5) MARP-mediated pathway, (6) titin kinase-mediated pathway. Interestingly, pathways 3–6 are sarcomere related. Abbreviations: AT1 angiotensin II receptor, ILK integrin-linked kinase, FAK focal adhesion kinase, GPCR G protein-coupled receptor, SRF serum response factor, MARP muscle ankyrin repeat protein
