Transcriptome analysis of S

Transcriptome analysis of S. tsukubaensis according to growth(A) Growth curve of S. tsukubaensis in R5(−) medium. RNA was extracted at early rapid growth (E), transition (T), late rapid growth (L), and stationary (S) phases. Data are represented as mean +/− standard deviation (SD). n = 3 biological replicates.(B) RNA fold-changes of differentially expressed genes. mRNA fold-change of 3,990 genes showing significant changes in expression at two or more growth phases (fold-change >2 and p-value <0.05) are represented in a heatmap. Expression pattern of genes clustered into 16 clusters using K-mean clustering. Leftmost numbers are the number of K-means clusters. Clusters with up- and downregulated tendency are represented as orange and navy lines, respectively.(C) Transcriptome profile of central carbon metabolism and the FK506 biosynthetic pathway. Schematic representation of RNA fold-changes involved in primary metabolism and the FK506 biosynthetic pathway. Orange colored genes are members of cluster 1 or 10, and red colored genes are FK506 BGC genes. Metabolites labeled with red boxes are precursors of FK506. DHCHC, 4,5-dihydroxycyclohex-1-enecarboxylic acid. T/E, fold-change between transition and early rapid growth phase; L/E, fold-change between late rapid growth and early rapid growth phase; and S/E, fold-change between stationary and early rapid growth phase. See also Method S2, Figures S3–S6, and S14, S15, and Table S2
