Dysfunction of miR‐802 in tumors

miR‐802 regulates the biological processes of cells by affecting signaling pathways. miR‐802 regulates the signal molecules in the Wnt/β‐catenin signaling pathway, the PI3K/AKT signaling pathway, Hedgehog (Hh) signaling pathway
