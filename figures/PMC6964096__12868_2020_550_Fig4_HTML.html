Wiki pathway analysis of the differentially expressed proteins (DEPs) during rhesus hippocampal aging

Wiki pathway analysis of the differentially expressed proteins (DEPs) during rhesus hippocampal aging. The DEPs predominantly enriched in electron transport chain (a), NRF2 pathway (b), cytoplasmic ribosome proteins (c), and focal adhesion PI3K–AKT–mTOR signaling pathway. The upregulated proteins are shown in red and the downregulated proteins are shown in green
