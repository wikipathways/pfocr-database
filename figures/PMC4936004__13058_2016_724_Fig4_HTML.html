LumA-R2 samples overexpress genes in the T cell receptor signaling pathway

LumA-R2 samples overexpress genes in the T cell receptor signaling pathway. The list of top 1000 genes differentially expressed in LumA-R1 and LumA-R2 samples was found to be significantly enriched for the pathway genes (p = 1.3e-07). Genes marked in red are overexpressed in LumA-R2 samples. Pathway and graphics were taken from the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) database
