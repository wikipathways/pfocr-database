Revised saxitoxin biosynthesis pathway in A

Revised saxitoxin biosynthesis pathway in A. minutum [cited by Kellmann et al. ()]. The sxt genes identified in this study are indicated in circles, and those not identified in this study are indicated in red letters. Sxt genes identified for the first time in dinoflagellates are labeled in red circles. Two types of sxtA genes are indicated in bold circles.
