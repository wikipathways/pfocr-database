Discovery of extremely halophilic, methyl-reducing euryarchaea provides insights into the evolutionary origin of methanogenesis

The main methyl-reducing pathway is shown by thick magenta arrows. Metabolically fixed low molecular weight compounds are shown in green. Either gene name or respective arCOG number is shown for each reaction and shown in red (details are available at ). Final biosynthetic products are shown as follows: light blue for amino acids, pale yellow for nucleotides, brown for lipid components, pink for cofactors. Abbreviations: MF, methanofuran; H4MPT, tetrahydromethanopterin; CoM, coenzyme M, CoB – coenzyme B, CoA – coenzyme A.
