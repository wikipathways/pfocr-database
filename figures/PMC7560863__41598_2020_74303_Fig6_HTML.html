Metabolite changes in flag leaves of wheat ‘Norin 61’ during 10 days of progressive drought stress (DT2, 4, 6, 8 and 10). The proposed metabolic pathways are based on KEGG database (https://www.genome.jp/kegg/) and the literature. Metabolites with significant (P ≤ 0.05) increase or decrease (fold change > 2.0 or fold change ≤ 0.5, respectively) and are shown in the pathway. Color bar indicates the log2 fold change.
