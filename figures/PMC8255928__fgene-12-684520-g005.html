Integrative Analysis of lncRNA-mRNA Profile Reveals Potential Predictors for SAPHO Syndrome

lncRNA-mRNA interaction network in a trans manner. Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) analysis indicated that the significantly (p < 0.05) enriched pathways in the lncRNA-targeted genes mainly included adipocytokine signaling pathway, ErbB signaling pathway, FoxO signaling pathway, etc. ().
