Phospholipid-binding tumor suppressor ANXA7 linked to the interrelated FGF8 and cyclin E pathways through Ca/phospholipid signaling in prostate cancer

Custom pathway of FGF-signaling with interrelated ANXA7, FGF8 and cyclin E links (all three highlighted in blue) was developed using the Ingenuity Pathways Analysis, IPA as described in ‘Materials and Methods’. Additional ANXA7 links are presented on a small image in the left corner. Overlay with gene expression profile (red – upregulated, green – downregulated) corresponds to the prostate cancer subtype.
