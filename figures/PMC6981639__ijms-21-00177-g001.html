The primary metabolic pathway of mammalian gangliosides

Schematic illustration of the primary metabolic pathway of mammalian gangliosides. Genes encoding proteins involved in ganglioside synthesis and degradation are shown in italics. Solid-line arrows indicate synthetic pathway. Dotted-line arrows indicate degradation pathway.
