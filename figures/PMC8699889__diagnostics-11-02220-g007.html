Prognostic and Genomic Analysis of Proteasome 20S Subunit Alpha (PSMA) Family Members in Breast Cancer

MetaCore pathway analysis of the coexpression gene network of proteasome 20S subunit alpha 3 (PSMA3) in breast cancer patients. We used the MetaCore platform to analyze genes coexpressed with PSMA3 from the associated METABRIC and TCGA datasets, and downstream pathway analyses revealed that “Immune response_Antigen presentation by MHC class I, classical pathway” participates in breast cancer development.
