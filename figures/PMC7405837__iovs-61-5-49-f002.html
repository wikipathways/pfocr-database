DEGs in RPE cells of the circadian rhythm pathway

DEGs in RPE cells of the circadian rhythm pathway. Genes that are differentially expressed over time are indicated by various colors, with an intensity scale of the standardized expression values ranging from low expression (dark blue) to high expression (dark red). ZT points are ranked from left to right for each differentially expressed gene, the most left color corresponding to ZT0 and the most right color corresponding to ZT19.
