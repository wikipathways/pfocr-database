Expression and regulatory roles of lncRNAs in G-CIMP-low vs G-CIMP-high Glioma: an in-silico analysis

Results from Ingenuity Pathway Analysis, a TP53 was identified as an upstream regulator of PANDAR and PVT1. b Differential lncRNAs associated with development of a carcinogenic activity. c This gene–gene network includes 6 differentially expressed lncRNAs (PVT1, PANDAR, FOXD2-AS1, CYTOR, CRNDE, and HOTAIRM1) and captures elements of cell growth, proliferation, survival and death
