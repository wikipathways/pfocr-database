Illustration of a proposed signaling pathway of innate and adaptive immune activation in the Nx model (A)

Illustration of a proposed signaling pathway of innate and adaptive immune activation in the Nx model (A). Illustrations of proposed maps of activation of TLRs, NF-κB and inflammasomes pathways at each point of the study are represented respectively in (B) Nx 15d, (C) Nx 60d and (D) Nx 120 days. The elements represented in black are overexpressed and those represented in bold are statistically upregulated.
