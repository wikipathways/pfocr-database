Regulation of the ZBP1-NLRP3 inflammasome and its implications in pyroptosis, apoptosis, and necroptosis (PANoptosis)

ZBP1 can be induced by type I interferons (IFNs) and induce PANoptosis in macrophages.Influenza virus (IAV) infection can be sensed by RIG-I, which will upregulate the expression of type I IFNs. The activated type I IFN signaling pathway can induce IRF1 expression, which acts as a transcription factor to control the expression of ZBP1. Influenza virus-produced Z-RNA can be sensed by ZBP1 in the nucleus and cytosol to induce the activation of PANoptosis.
