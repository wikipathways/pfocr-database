Manually curated version of RISN

The manually curated version of RISN. The over- and under-expressed genes are colored green and yellow, respectively. The cellular membrane and nuclear membrane are depicted by gray solid and dashed lines, respectively. Proteins are depicted by rounded rectangles, and DNA and small metabolite molecules are shown by circle. The phosphorylated P protein activated by AKT is denoted by P*, however, for the sake of simplicity, its process is not shown in the signaling pathway. Viral components are depicted by red shapes but with different caps to delineate activatory and inhibitory effects of RABV on RISN.
