Mutations causing susceptibility to intestinal inflammation

Schematic illustration of mutations causing susceptibility to intestinal inflammation. By screening mice with ENU-induced mutations with a low dose of DSS, we have identified a number of components necessary for protecting against intestinal inflammation. These include TLR signaling, epithelial growth factor receptor (EGFR) signaling, aquaporin 3 (Aqp3), the ER stress pathway that responds to unfolded proteins, and Yipf6, which is involved in formation and secretion of vesicles from Paneth and goblet cells. Allele names are in red; affected genes are in black.
