Using SEC-MX to provide molecular insights

Representation of selected proteins from the pathways mentioned in Fig. 7, based on published literature50,57–61. SEC-MX patterns are summarized on each protein: gHEK, gHCT, and gNS labels indicate assembly state abundance is higher in HEK293, HCT116, or not significantly changed, respectively. phHEK, phHCT, and phNS labels similarly indicate assembly state phosphorylation. gNA / phNA indicates a protein / phosphopeptide was measured but no peak was detected for the specific assembly state. Numbers in brackets indicate the phosphorylation site identified (S – Serine, T- Threonine, Y – Tyrosine), site NA indicates a phosphopeptide was measured but no site was identified with localization probability > 0.9 and therefore the specific site is not reported.
