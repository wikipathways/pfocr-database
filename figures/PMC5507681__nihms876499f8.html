Signaling pathways involved in keratinocyte growth and differentiation that are perturbed by SbIII and AsIII

Signaling pathways involved in keratinocyte growth and differentiation that are perturbed by SbIII and AsIII. Green arrows indicate positive signaling and red lines indicate repression of signaling. The two wavy lines represent nuclear DNA, and thick arrows show RNA transcripts from the indicated genes. Not indicated is the NRF2 pathway.
