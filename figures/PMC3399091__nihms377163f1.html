PAK1 Molecular Pathway

PAK1 Molecular Pathway. A, The molecular pathways influenced by PAK1. PAK1 is activated in response to extracellular stimuli and activated PAK1 in turn initiates cascades of pathways. PAK1 is established to impact the cytoskeleton remodeling and the cell survival and transformation pathways by phosphorylating various substrates and these substrates are shown. In the nucleus, PAK1 regulates cell cycle, chromatin and gene expression and the substrates involved in these pathways are also depicted. B, The current spectrum of various types of PAK1 inhibitors.
