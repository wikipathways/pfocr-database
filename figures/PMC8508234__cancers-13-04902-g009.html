Therapeutic and Prognostic Values of LSM Family Genes in Breast Cancer

MetaCore enrichment pathway analysis of LSM4 co-expressed gene list in a breast cancer database. The approach was to collect the top 10% of expression gene lists from both METABRIC (2000 genes) and Pan-Cancer (1800 genes), then overlap them to produce a final genes list of 463 total genes. The pathway list was ordered by the -log [p value] from the gene list extracted via TCGA Pan-Cancer atlas and METABRIC breast cancer patient databases. “Cell cycle Spindle assembly and chromosome separation” was at the top of the pathway list when performing the biological process analysis.
