Co-expression of genes involved in lignin formation across species

Co-expression of genes involved in lignin formation across species. (A) Consensus co-expression network of lignin formation across seven plant species obtained from the NetworkComparer-tool from PlaNet (http://aranet.mpimp-golm.mpg.de/aranet/NetworkComparer). Co-expression network of following genes/probeset IDs were used: Arabidopsis, At2g37040; rice, LOC_Os02g41650; Medicago, Medtr1g076720; barley, Contig1805_s_at; poplar, PtpAffx.1672.3.A1_a_at; wheat, TaAffx.45277.1.S1_x_at. (B) Monolignol biosynthesis pathway (adopted from ). Arabidopsis genes present in the co-expression network of AtPAL1 were mapped on the pathway. Question marks indicate that the function of the respective genes has not been shown yet. The main routes for monolignol synthesis in dicots are highlighted in gray.
