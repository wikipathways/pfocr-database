Affected Local Networks by RB1 and TP53 Inactivation Events

(A) Affected network for RB1. Recurrent nodes that were significantly changed in ≥ 5 cancer types were selected to plot on the graph. Node color is proportional to the number of cancer types in which the gene was changed. The nodes shaded in green mainly function in the cell-cycle pathway, and the nodes shaded in pink are mainly from the DNA repair pathway.(B) Affected network for TP53. Recurrent nodes that were significantly changed in ≥ 2 cancer types were selected to plot on the graph. The shaded areas were for signaling pathway (blue) and cell-cycle pathway (red), respectively.
