Didymin Ameliorates Liver Fibrosis by Alleviating Endoplasmic Reticulum Stress and Glycerophospholipid Metabolism: Based on Transcriptomics and Metabolomics

Effects of Didymin on metabolites. (A) The PCA was analyzed by the Omicshare database (https://www.omicshare.com/tools/). (B) The Heatmap was analyzed by the Omicshare database (https://www.omicshare.com/tools/); the red plots represent the high abundance of metabolite, and the blue plots mean low abundance. (C) The OPLS-DA of the model vs normal. (E) The VIP diagram of the model vs normal. (D) The OPLS-DA of the didymin vs model. (F) The VIP diagram of the didymin vs model. (G) The metabolic pathways regulated by didymin were analyzed by MetaboAnalyst 5.0. (H) The Glycerophospholipid metabolism pathway; C00157: Phosphatidylcholine; C00350: Phosphatidylethanolamine.
