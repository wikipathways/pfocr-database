Interaction network of differentially expressed miRNAs (DERs) and genes

The interaction network of differentially expressed miRNAs (DERs) and genes. The red diamond represents DERs in colorectal cancer (CRC) patients. The yellow circle represents the DER-target gene. The pink circle represents the gene that interacts with the target gene. The blue rectangle represents the annotated pathway of the target gene.
