Metabolic pathway of lignocellulose by integrating DEGs, differentially accumulated metabolites between ZY821 and YG689. Red marks indicate up-regulated genes or up-accumulated metabolites in the pathway of lignocellulose synthesis. Blue marks indicate down-regulated genes or down-accumulated metabolites in the pathway of lignocellulose synthesis.
