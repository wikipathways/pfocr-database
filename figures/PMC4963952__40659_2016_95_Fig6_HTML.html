Regulation of genes of cell cycle pathway during senescence induction in MRC-5 strains

Regulation of genes of cell cycle pathway during senescence induction in MRC-5 strains. Genes of the “cell cycle” pathway which are significantly up- (green) and down- (red) regulated (log2 fold change >1) during irradiation induced senescence (120 h after 20 Gy irradiation) in MRC-5 fibroblasts. Blue color signifies genes which are commonly down-regulated during both, irradiation induced and replicative senescence
