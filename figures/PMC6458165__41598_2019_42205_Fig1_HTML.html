Networks related to immune modulation and inflammatory response

Ingenuity pathway analysis networks related to immune modulation and inflammatory response are presented in this figure (A–D). Pathway analysis was performed using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) software (QIAGEN Inc., https://www.qiagenbioinformatics.com/products/ingenuitypathway-analysis) by loading 366 probe sets that were differentially expressed with HCA.
