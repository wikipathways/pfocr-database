7-Hydroxyflavone Alleviates Myocardial Ischemia/Reperfusion Injury in Rats by Regulating Inflammation

A schematic diagram summarizing the underlying mechanisms in this study. HF can protect the heart from myocardial ischemia/reperfusion injury. The potential mechanisms of this effect can be connected with the activation of the MAPK/NF-κB signaling pathway, reduction in the inflammatory cytokines (IL-1β, IL-6, and TNF-α) and apoptotic factor (caspase-3), and the enhancement of the antioxidant levels (SOD and MDA).
