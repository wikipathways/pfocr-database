Molecular interactions in Immunometabolism governing lipid metabolic disorders

Molecular interactions in Immunometabolism governing lipid metabolic disorders. Induction of Leptin receptor, GF receptors, TLR receptors and CD36 receptors lead to the activation of STAT3, mTORC1, XBP‐1 (through IRE‐1α) and HIF‐1α that steer to interference in lipid metabolism. Additionally, mitochondria also respond to high‐fat contents and divide the pyruvate pathway into lactate and OXPHOS that ultimately decide the inflammatory fate
