Signalling pathway in normal and hyperglycemia patients

Signalling pathway in normal and hyperglycemia patients. Diabetes can be corrected by metformin. The major target of metformin is highlighted in red i.e. Uncoupled eNOS, HSp90, GST, CDK2, neuraminidase
