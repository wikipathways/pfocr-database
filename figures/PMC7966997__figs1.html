EGFR pathway (Qiagen Pathway Central) shown as an interacting network for samples with maximal (top) or minimal (bottom) PAL in colorectal cancer (left) vs normal colon (right). Green/red arrows indicate activation/inhibition interactions, respectively. The color depth of transcript nodes reflects the extent of node activation (logarithms of the expression fold change for each node, the reference is the geometric average between expression levels in all samples in the respective groups).
