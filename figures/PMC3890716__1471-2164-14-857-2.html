Schematic for the putative biosynthetic pathways of three major classes of active compounds in I

Schematic for the putative biosynthetic pathways of three major classes of active compounds in I. indigotica. Terponoids, indole derivatives, and phenylpropanoid pathways are marked with background in yellow, red, and blue, respectively. Red characters indicate the non-detected genes. Dashed line in red indicate undetected pathway. Figures in panes indicate the number of unigenes corresponding to the catalytic gene in the pathway; U, unknown; N, Not annotated.
