Blocking the transforming growth factor B (TGFB) signalling pathway

Blocking the transforming growth factor β (TGF-β) signalling pathway. Blocking various aspects of the TGF-β signalling pathway is capable of reducing the invasive properties of breast cells in vivo. The embryonic zebrafish model provides an ideal system for using chemical inhibition, siRNA, or shRNA, to target the TGF-β signalling pathway. MMP, matrix metalloproteinase.
