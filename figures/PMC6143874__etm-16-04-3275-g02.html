JAK-STAT signaling in tandem with multiple cytokines and hormones participates in the regulation of Beta-cells

JAK-STAT signaling in tandem with multiple cytokines and hormones participates in the regulation of β-cells. Circles of the same color represent signals within the same pathway. Red and black lines indicate inhibition and promotion, respectively.
