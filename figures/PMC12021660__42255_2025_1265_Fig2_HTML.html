Fat-body metabolic pathway profiling

The relative expression (Yki versus Ctrl, average log2-transformed fold change) at days 5 (5D) and 8 (8D) of genes involved in glycolysis, gluconeogenesis, glycogenesis, and fatty acid (FA) synthesis. Shared enzymes of glycolysis and gluconeogenesis are shown in grey; upregulation is indicated by green, downregulation is indicated by magenta, and no significant change is indicated in grey (NoC). See also Extended Data Fig. 2
