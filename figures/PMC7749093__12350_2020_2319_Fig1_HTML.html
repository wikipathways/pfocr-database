Cardiovascular molecular imaging

Overview of cardiovascular molecular imaging. Pathogenetic processes targeted by current radiopharmaceuticals include inflammatory leukocytes, fibroblasts, and proteases involved in matrix reorganization and sympathetic neuronal signaling. Each pathway is thought to influence the others by means of cytokines or signal transduction cascades
