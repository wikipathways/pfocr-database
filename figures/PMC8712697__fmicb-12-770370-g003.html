Comparative Genomic Analysis of Labrenzia aggregata (Alphaproteobacteria) Strains Isolated From the Mariana Trench: Insights Into the Metabolic Potentials and Biogeochemical Functions

Metabolic reconstruction of L. aggregata. The gene locations and presence in the five strains are depicted as divided circles and pentagrams. G3P, glyceraldehyde 3-phosphate; F6P, fructose 6-phosphate; 6P-gluconate, 6-phospho-gluconate; TCA, tricarboxylic acid; PPP, pentose phosphate pathway; DMSP, dimethylsulfoniopropionate; DMS, dimethyl sulfide; OPGs, osmoregulated periplasmic glucans; and NAGGN, N-acetylglutaminylglutamine amide.
