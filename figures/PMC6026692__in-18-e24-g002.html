Dual Effect of Hepatic Macrophages on Liver Ischemia and Reperfusion Injury during Liver Transplantation

The response of activated KCs to reperfusion is bifunctional with the secretion of both pro-inflammatory and anti-inflammatory factors. TNF-α, IL-1β, IL-18, IFN-γ, and IL-12 secreted by KCs show a pro-inflammatory effect, in contrast, IL-10 and HO-1/Nfr2 pathway contribute to the anti-inflammatory regulation.JNK, c-Jun N-terminal kinase; PGC-1α, peroxisome proliferator-activated receptor-γ co-activator 1α; Mfn-2, mitofusion 2; VCAM-1, vascular adhesion molecule 1; MIP-2, macrophage inflammatory protein 2.
