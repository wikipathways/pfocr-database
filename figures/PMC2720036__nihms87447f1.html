Selective Raf Inhibition in Cancer Therapy

Components of the central activation cascade proceeding from ligand-bound EGFR through Raf to the nucleus are indicated in green or yellow boxes, connected by red arrows. Additional proteins regulating Raf are indicated in gray boxes. Additional Raf phosphorylated/regulated proteins beyond the central cascade are indicated in pink boxes: not all are shown (see text). After Ras stimulation, B-Raf heterodimerizes with c-Raf-1; mutationally activated B-Raf binds and activates C-Raf and MEK independent of Ras []. Therapeutics targeting B-Raf and c-Raf1 are the predominant topic of this review: agents targeting EGFR and MEK1 are also briefly discussed.
