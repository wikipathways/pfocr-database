Example KEGG pathways upregulated by 1/10 SAF Corexit 9527. (A) p53 signaling pathway; (B) Mismatch repair pathway; (C) Homologous recombination pathway; (D) Fanconi anemia pathway. Red and green colors indicate up or downregulation, respectively, of gene expression for the mice in a treatment group.
