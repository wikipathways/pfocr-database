Aconiti Lateralis Radix Praeparata as Potential Anticancer Herb: Bioactive Compounds and Molecular Mechanisms

The molecular mechanisms of Fuzi against cancer. 1) Fuzi induced cell autophagy and conducted apoptosis by targeting mitochondrial-mediated pathway and death receptor-mediated pathway. 2) Fuzi inhibited cell proliferation, migration, and invasion via regulating p38 MAPK, AKT, EMT, β3GnT8 and MMPs. The blue and brown solid arrows indicated direct targets of Fuzi alkaloids and FPS respectively. Correspondingly, the blue and brown dotted ones indicated downstream effects induced by Fuzi alkaloids and FPS.
