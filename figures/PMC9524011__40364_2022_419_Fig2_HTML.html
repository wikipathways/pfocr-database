Dysfunction and ceRNA network of the tumor suppressor miR-637 in cancer development and prognosis

miR-637 is involved in the regulation of multiple signaling pathways. miR-637 plays an important role in Jak/STAT3 signaling pathway, ERK signaling pathway, Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway, and PI3K/AKT signaling pathway, thereby regulating cell growth, apoptosis, cell cycle, invasion, and metastasis.
