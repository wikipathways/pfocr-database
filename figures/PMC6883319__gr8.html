MetaCore-generated pathway differentially expressed genes involved in antigen presentation by major histocompatibility complex (MHC) class I: cross-presentation

MetaCore-generated pathway differentially expressed genes involved in antigen presentation by major histocompatibility complex (MHC) class I: cross-presentation. Experimental data from all three GSE data sets are linked to and visualized on the maps as thermometer-like figures. Upward thermometers have red color and indicate upregulated signals and downward (blue) ones indicate downregulated expression levels of the genes. Annotations are listed in supplemental materials.
