Signaling pathways for melanin inhibition by NSC-CM

Signaling pathways for melanin inhibition by NSC-CM. The expression of Dickkopf-1 (DKK1) gene may directly or indirectly be induced by NSC-CM in melanocytes (A), which decreased the melanin synthesis via the inhibition of Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway and subsequent downregulation of the expression of MITF and melanogenic enzymes (B). The thunder symbol indicates DKK1 gene may directly or indirectly be induced by NSC-CM. TCF/LEF, T-cell factor/lymphoid enhancer factor; MITF, Microphthalmia-associated transcription factor; DVL, Dishevelled; GSK3β, Glycogen synthase kinase 3β; LRP5/6, Lipoprotein receptor-relased protein 5/6; Ub, Ubiquitination.
