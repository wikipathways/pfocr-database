Inhibition of the BRCA1 DNA damage response pathway indicated by a down modulation (green; up-modulation is indicated in red) of atm, brip1, cdkn1a, e2f3, fancd2, fancl, mdc1, rad51, and rpa1 in male X. maculatus skin exposed to 4,100 K FL. Down modulation of BRCA1 leads to inhibition of DNA repair and cell cycle arrest at the G1/S phase transition.
