Comparison of Transcriptomic Profiles of MiaPaCa-2 Pancreatic Cancer Cells Treated with Different Statins

RAS signaling pathway and the changes induced by statin treatment in the expression of the genes that code for members of the pathway. The color fill of the nodes indicates the base-2 logarithm of the fold change in gene expression upon treatment by a statin. Different statins are shown in the distinct position of the node as indicated in the key.
