Kinetic Scheme for the 5-HT1AR and 5-HT2AR mediated MAP kinase activation pathway

Kinetic Scheme for the 5-HT1AR and 5-HT2AR mediated MAP kinase activation pathway. See for the corresponding reaction rates and constants. Note that the area in light gray represents the activation of PLC by 5-HT1AR activity, which is cell-type specific and not clearly demonstrated in HEK 293 cells.
