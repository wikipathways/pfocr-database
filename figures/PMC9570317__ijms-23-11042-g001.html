Advances in Anti-Cancer Activities of Flavonoids in Scutellariae radix: Perspectives on Mechanism

Mechanism of baicalein, wogonin, along with their glycosides (baicalin and wogonoside) in inducing cell cycle arrest and regulating the canonical tumor-associated signaling pathway. Baicalin and wogonin induced cancer cell cycle arrest by regulating cyclin proteins (such as cyclin A, cyclin B1 and cyclin D1). Meanwhile, baicalein and wogonin promote cancer cells apoptosis and inhibit proliferation via PI3K-AKT and AMPK-mTOR pathways, respectively.
