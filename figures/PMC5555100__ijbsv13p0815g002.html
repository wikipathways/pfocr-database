In the presence of insulin/growth factor, all the growth factor binds with the receptor and activates the PI3K/AKT/SGK pathway. AKT and SGK phosphorylates the FOXO at different sites, and upon phosphorylation, it binds with 14-3-3 protein. The binding of 14-3-3 protein with phosphoryated FOXO helps FOXO translocate form nucleus to cytoplasm.
