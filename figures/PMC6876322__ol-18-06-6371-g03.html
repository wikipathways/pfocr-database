Gene Ontology and Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes pathway analysis of the target genes of CTD-2357A8.3 in HNSCC tissues

Gene Ontology and Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes pathway analysis of the target genes of CTD-2357A8.3 in HNSCC tissues. Analysis of the 213 mRNAs was performed using the Database for Annotation, Visualization, and Integrated Discovery and visualized using Cytoscape. ‘Signaling pathways regulating pluripotency of stem cells’ (hsa04550, P=0.003), ‘proteoglycans in cancer’ (hsa05205, P=0.02), ‘transcriptional misregulation in cancer’ (hsa05202, P=0.033) and ‘chemokine signaling pathway’ (hsa04062, P=0.049) were the top four significantly enriched gene sets.
