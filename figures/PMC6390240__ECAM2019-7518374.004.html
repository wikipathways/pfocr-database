Modules of core PPI networks of baicalein against HCC

Modules of core PPI networks of baicalein against HCC. (a) One module was related to beta-catenin-TCF complex assembly, response to unfolded protein, and ERBB2 signaling pathway. (b) The other module was related to regulation of signal transduction by p53, regulation of cell death, and intrinsic apoptotic signaling pathway.
