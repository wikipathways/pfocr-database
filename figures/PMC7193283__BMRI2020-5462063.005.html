Mechanism of RES on improving NAFLD

Schematic illustration of mechanism of RES on improving NAFLD. RES promoted autophagy to reduce the accumulation of lipid droplets and inhibited β-oxidation of fatty acids by activating the AMPK signaling pathway. RES alleviated the symptom caused by inflammation, liver fibrosis, and oxidative stress and improved the expression of LXR and FXR genes.
