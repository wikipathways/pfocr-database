Differentially expressed genes in BA, JA and UA groups mapped on the TNFR signaling pathway (1 denotes JA, 2 denotes UA, and 3 denotes BA)

Differentially expressed genes in BA, JA and UA groups mapped on the TNFR signaling pathway (1 denotes JA, 2 denotes UA, and 3 denotes BA). The thermometer represents differentially expressed level (red indicates up-regulation and blue indicates down-regulation). Significant common nodes are encircled in red. Unique nodes for BA are encircled in blue square, and unique nodes for UA are encircled in red square. (A color version of this figure is available in the online journal.)BA: baicalin; JA: jasminoidin; UA: ursodeoxycholic acid
