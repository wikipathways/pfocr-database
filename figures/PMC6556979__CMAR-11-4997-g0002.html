Chronic inflammation activates multiple signaling cascades

Chronic inflammation activates multiple signaling cascades. The signaling pathway of the mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) and the signaling pathway regulated by the epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR) are some of the cellular signaling pathways involved in the response to asbestos inhalation.Abbreviations: PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase; AKT, AKT serine/threonine kinase; mTOR, mechanistic target of rapamycin kinase; AP-1, activator protein-1; NFκB, nuclear transcription factor kappa-B; ROS, reactive oxygen species; RNS, reactive nitrogen species; CytC, cytochrome C; Apaf-1, apoptotic peptidase activating factor 1.
