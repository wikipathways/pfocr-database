Molecular Correlates of Early Onset of Diabetic Cardiomyopathy: Possible Therapeutic Targets

(a) Analysis of hub genes from protein-protein interaction (PPI) network for the candidate pathways. Nodes encircled in pink, red, blue, yellow, green, and brown indicate significant genes in pathways involved oxidative phosphorylation, cardiac muscle contraction, calcium signalling, fatty acid metabolism, insulin resistance, apoptosis, and TNF pathway, respectively. (b) Validation of key genes in ventricular tissue of rats in control, low and high STZ treatment groups using RT-PCR. ∗P < 0.05, ∗∗P < 0.01, ∗∗∗P < 0.001. Compared with control. n = 6 − 7 per group.
