Kinetic changes in gene expression of selected components of the Wnt/Beta-catenin signaling pathway in aging CD8+ T cells

Kinetic changes in gene expression of selected components of the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway in aging CD8+ T cells. Wnt/β-catenin pathway was generated by Ingenuity Pathway analysis. Adjacent to each step of the pathway are the gene profile plots of old and young CD8+ T cells. Axis labels are the same as in . Red indicates up-regulated genes, blue down-regulated genes.
