Proposed pathway for inhibition of increases in GSTpi-mediated monolayer permeability via Src/VE-cadherin signaling

Proposed pathway for inhibition of increases in GSTpi-mediated monolayer permeability via Src/VE-cadherin signaling. GSTpi inhibited TNF-α-induced endothelial permeability by S-glutathionylation of Src kinase, thereby decreasing downstream phospho-VE-cadherin, phospho-p120, and phospho-β-Catenin levels and inhibiting vascular permeability.
