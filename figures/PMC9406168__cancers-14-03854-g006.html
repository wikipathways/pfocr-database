Integrative, In Silico and Comparative Analysis of Breast Cancer Secretome Highlights Invasive-Ductal-Carcinoma-Grade Progression Biomarkers

Proposed mechanism by which hierarchical secretion of identified secretome proteins may lead to the progression from grade I and II to grade III breast cancer Proteins in bold belong to the overlapping protein cluster as shown in . Blue cell (grade I and II, Cluster 1) interactions: LAMB1-3, LAMC1, COL7A, and AGRN interactions with ITGB1 [,]. Downstream activation of JNK and ERK cascades through ITGB1 [,]. Parallel activation of the ERK cascade by EGF-EGFR interaction []. FBN1 interaction with VEGFR1 and downstream STAT2 activation []. BMP interaction with BMPR and subsequent activation of SMAD 1/5/8 []. Downstream effectors of JNK and ERK cascade []. Brown cell (grade III, cluster 2) interactions: HSPG2 and LOXL2 interaction with IGFB3 [,]. Laminin protein family interaction with MET and ITGB1 [,]. ACTG1 interaction with THBS1, LTBP1, fibronectin, and LOXL to drive SDC4 phosphorylation and subsequent WNT pathway activation [,,,,]. LDLR laminin interaction and internalization leads to PCSK9 activation and tumor microenvironment promotion []. VEGFA-VEGFR2 interaction promotes the ERK cascade activation []. IGFBP-3, fibrillin, and ERK cascade leads activation of TGF-beta [,]. Integrin-beta1-dependent activation of MET []. mTOR downstream effectors []. TGFβ genetic targets []. Wnt and b-catenin downstream genetic targets []. Image created with BioRender.com. Protein name abbreviations stated in .
