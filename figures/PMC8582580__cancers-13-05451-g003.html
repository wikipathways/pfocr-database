New Look of EBV LMP1 Signaling Landscape

An updated LMP1 signaling pathway. The involvement of LUBAC, LIMD1 and p62 in the LMP1 signal transduction is shown. LIMD1 expression is induced by NFκB and IRF4, and p62 expression is induced by NFκB and NRF2. The induction of p62-mediated selective autophagy and the activation of NRF2-mediated antioxidative defense by LMP1 signaling are shown.
