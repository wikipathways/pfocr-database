Differential expression and bioinformatics analysis of circRNA in osteosarcoma

The signalling pathways of ‘phosphatidylinositol signalling system’ and ‘inositol phosphate metabolism’ indicate the modulation of tumour markers and are related to the outcomes of osteosarcoma.
