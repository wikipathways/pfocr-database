Intersection of stem cell biology and engineering towards next generation in vitro models of human fibrosis

Potential Therapeutic strategies for fibrosis. (A) Anti-fibrotic therapeutic strategies through inhibition of TGF-β pathway. (B) Therapeutic strategies through inhibition of RAS system. (C) Targeted anti-fibrotic therapeutic approach through biomaterial-based delivery. (D) Stem cell therapy with mesenchymal stromal cells for the treatment of fibrotic disease. (E) CAR T-cell therapy using engineered T cells for the treatment of fibrotic disease. (F) Gene therapy through mediating miRNA as a novel approach for anti-fibrosis treatment.
