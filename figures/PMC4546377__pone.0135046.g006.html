Candidate Resistant Genes of Sand Pear (Pyrus pyrifolia Nakai) to Alternaria alternata Revealed by Transcriptome Sequencing

Frame numbers (instead of the enzyme number) indicate the corresponding genes associated with this enzyme. The pathway includes a number of different enzymes formed by complex biochemical reactions. Genes associated with this pathway are marked with different colored boxes: the red box is up-regulated genes, the green box is down-regulated genes, and the blue box is both up-regulated and down-regulated genes.
