GWSS host support of nutritional metabolisms in Sulcia-GWSS and Baumannia

a Inferred nutrition pathways in Sulcia-GWSS and Baumannia containing bacteriocytes. b The GS/GOGAT (glutamine synthetase/glutamate synthase) cycle and glutamate dehydrogenase (GDH) pathway for recycling NH3. More highly expressed host genes in bacteriocytes relative to body tissues are colored based on the log2 fold change ratio. See inset legend for additional details and explanation of metabolite abbreviations. See Table  for full names of gene products (color figure online).
