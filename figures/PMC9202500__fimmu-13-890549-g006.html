Functions of Viroporins in the Viral Life Cycle and Their Regulation of Host Cell Responses

Viroporins regulate host immune responses. viroporins modulate host cell immune responses by interfering with PRRs recognition, interfering with bridging molecules, kinases, and downstream effectors in the innate immune signaling pathway, and interfering with IFN-mediated signaling.
