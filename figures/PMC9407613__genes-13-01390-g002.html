New Insights into the Functions of MicroRNAs in Cardiac Fibrosis: From Mechanisms to Therapeutic Strategies

MiRNAs in the regulation of Smad-dependent (canonical) signaling pathway. In the canonical TGF-β signaling pathway, TGF-β1 interacts with TGFβRI/TGFβRII, leading to the phosphorylation of Smad2/3. This figure shows miRNAs that regulate the expression of the Smad-dependent (canonical) signaling pathway. The symbol of “— —>” means positive effect, and the symbol of “— —|” means negative effect.
