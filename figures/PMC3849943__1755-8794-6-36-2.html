Network of the interactions of the miRNAs and their target genes (experimental observed or highly predicted)

A network of the interactions of the miRNAs and their target genes (experimental observed or highly predicted). Ingenuity Pathway Analysis® tool was used to generate miRNA–mRNA interactions of miR-1, miR-133, miR-140-3p, miR-143-3p, miR-145-5p and, miR-23a-3p, miR-28-3p, miR-28-5p, miR-29b-3p and their targets in all selected functions. Red and green color represents the molecules to be upregulated and down-regulated respectively.
