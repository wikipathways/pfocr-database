Biosynthetic pathways in different kinds of edible berries

Schematic diagram of the biosynthetic pathway and relative content of metabolites in 6 different kinds of edible berries. The relative contents are presented as fold-changes normalized using the average of all values. The biosynthetic pathway was modified from the KEGG database (http://www.genome.jp/kegg/). L, honeyberry; V, blueberry; C, mandarin melonberry; M, mulberry; A, chokeberry; R, Korean black raspberry. Numbers on the biosynthetic pathway correspond to the metabolic numbers in Table .
