Schematic representation the regulatory networks of lncRNAs in major biology activities during brain developmental process

Schematic representation the regulatory networks of lncRNAs in major biology activities during brain developmental process. It was depicted in details that lncRNAs contribute to NSCs self-renewal, neurogenesis/neuronal differentiation, neurodevelopmental disorders and gliogenesis major emerging as sponging of miRNAs, crucial regulators in signaling pathway and targeting proximal gene expression
