Summarized scheme for the regulation of spexin on bile acid pathway

Summarized scheme for the regulation of spexin on bile acid pathway. Changes of the metabolites and genes were highlighted (Red, increased; Green, decreased). Spexin downregulates bile acid synthesis by suppressing CYP7A1 expression, resulting in a decrease of total bile acid pool in the enterohepatic circulation.
