Emerging role of BAD and DAD1 as potential targets and biomarkers in cancer

Feasible interaction between BAD and DAD1 and sequential apoptosis. Our hypothesis is highlighted with a dashed outline. By exerting a transcription factor-like function, the combination between BAD and the promoter region of the DAD1 gene results in DAD1 knockdown. Loss of DAD1 function triggers ER stress and activates the Perk-Atf4 signaling pathway, which sequentially activates the JNK signaling pathway via MEKK1, together with Wnd, and eventually initiates apoptosis (). The apoptosis signaling pathways in were created with reference to signaling pathway figures on the Cell Signaling Technology, Inc. website (–). ER stress, endoplasmic reticulum stress.
