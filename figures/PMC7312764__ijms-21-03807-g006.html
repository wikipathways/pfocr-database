Analysis of the major constituents in SHED-CM and the plausible mechanisms of SHED-CM-mediated neuroprotective effects

Analysis of the major constituents in SHED-CM and the plausible mechanisms of SHED-CM-mediated neuroprotective effects. (A) ELISA array showing the percentage of each constituent in the SHED-CM. (B) RNA-sequencing (RNA-seq) analysis and Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA) indicate the possible mechanisms for the neuroprotective effects mediated by SHED-CM. (C) A simplified network depicting the possible neuroprotective mechanisms mediated by the major constituents in SHED-CM. Abbreviations: IGFBP-6, insulin-like growth factor binding protein 6; TIMP-2, tissue inhibitor of metalloproteinase 2; TIMP-1, tissue inhibitor of metalloproteinase 1; TGF-β1, transforming growth factor-beta 1, IGFBP-2, insulin-like growth factor binding protein 2; IGFBP-4, insulin-like growth factor binding protein 4; BMP-5, bone morphogenetic protein 5.
