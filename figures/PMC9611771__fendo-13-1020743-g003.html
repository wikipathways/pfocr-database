Identification of differentially expressed genes in mouse paraspinal muscle in response to microgravity

Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathway enrichment analysis. (A) The 23 overlapping differentially expressed genes (DEGs) between the BG–BF group and the FC–BF group were selected using Venn analysis. (B, C) KEGG pathway enrichment analysis of the 23 DEGs. (D) KEGG analysis of the HIF signaling pathway. (E) KEGG analysis of the p53 signaling pathway. (F) KEGG analysis of the JAK-STAT signaling pathway. (G) KEGG analysis of the FOXO signaling pathway.
