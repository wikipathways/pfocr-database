Selected microRNA-target (cell cycle pathways) interaction data

Selected microRNA-target (cell cycle pathways) interaction data. Four microRNAs, namely miR-23b, miR-92b, miR-381 and miR-382, that levels were significantly decreased upon SFN stimulation were subjected to target analysis based on KEGG PATHWAY database and iBioGuide (https://www.advaitabio.com/ibioguide.html). Red circles indicate miR-23b targets, blue circles indicate miR-92b targets, yellow circles indicate miR-381 targets and gray circles indicate miR-382 targets. TGF-β was recognized as a target of miR-23b, miR-92b and miR-381.
