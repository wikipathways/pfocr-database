Differential mechanisms of CK2.3 in osteoblastogenesis and osteoclastogenesis

Differential mechanisms of CK2.3 in osteoblastogenesis and osteoclastogenesis. CK2.3 activates SMAD, AKT, and ERK, resulting in the upregulation of the osteoblastic master transcription factor RUNX2, which promotes osteoblastogenesis. On the other hand, CK2.3 activates ERK, resulting in the downregulation of the master transcription factor NFATc1, which inhibits osteoclastogenesis.
