Frequent genetic alterations in genes from angiogenesis pathway in UBC

Frequent genetic alterations in genes from angiogenesis pathway in UBC. Alterations are defined as SNVs/indels and SVs (leading to the target gene amplification or deletion). Alteration frequencies are expressed as a percentage of analyzed cases (65 cases for SV; 164 cases for SNV/indel). SNV/indel is indicated in brown font, amplification in magenta font, and deletion in blue font. The middle insets show the high level of microvessel density within UBC tumors
