Caveolin-1 promotes invasion and metastasis by upregulating Pofut1 expression in mouse hepatocellular carcinoma

Caveolin-1 upregulates Pofut1 by increasing phosphorylation of CREB, Sp1, HNF4A, and c-Myc via the mitogen-activated protein kinase pathway and activates Notch signaling to enhance invasion and metastasis in mouse hepatocellular carcinoma cells
