Human Disease Phenome construction

Human Disease Phenome construction. A bipartite graph was constructed, linking diseases to pathways. Each CUI consists of multiple genes, which are enriched in KEGG pathways (p < = 0.0001). One CUI can link to multiple pathways, and one pathway can be linked to multiple CUIs
