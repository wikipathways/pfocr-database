E-selectin ligand-1 controls circulating prostate cancer cell rolling/adhesion and metastasis

PCa cells express high level of E-selectin ligand, ESL-1, which mediates the rolling/adhesion of PCa cells on endothelial surface through the RAS-ERK- MAP kinase pathway.
