Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA)-generated network of isobaric tag for relative and absolute quantitation (iTRAQ)-identified proteins and their interacting partners in Alzheimer's disease (AD) parietal cortex (pCx)

The Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA)-generated network of isobaric tag for relative and absolute quantitation (iTRAQ)-identified proteins and their interacting partners in Alzheimer's disease (AD) parietal cortex (pCx). The IPA construct networks based on the differentially regulated proteins and their potential link with other known proteins in human AD pCx. Solid and dashed connecting lines indicate the presence of direct and indirect interactions, respectively. Modulatory roles of proteins on the expression of other proteins are indicated by arrows.
