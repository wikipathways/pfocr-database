Simulating ATO Mechanism and EGFR Signaling with Fuzzy Logic and Petri Net

Signaling pathway of different concentrations of Arsenic trioxide (ATO). A) High concentration of ATO lead to P21 expression and induce cellular apoptosis. B) Low concentration of ATO lead to cell proliferation and angiogenesis.
