Genetic and transcriptome analyses reveal the candidate genes and pathways involved in the inactive shade-avoidance response enabling high-density planting of soybean

Mapping of the most significantly enriched KEGG pathways of the four sets of specifically expressed genes. (A) Expression patterns of the genes that are specifically expressed the stems of the iSAS samples (iSAS-stem) that are involved in the significantly enriched pathway “proteasome.” (B) Expression patterns of the genes that are specifically expressed in the roots of the iSAS samples (iSAS-root) that are involved in the significantly enriched pathway “plant hormone transduction.” (C) Expression patterns of the genes that are specifically expressed in the stems of the strong-SAS samples (sSAS-stem) and that are involved in the significantly enriched pathway “aminoacyl-tRNA biosynthesis.” (D) Expression patterns of the genes that are specifically expressed in the roots of the strong-SAS samples (sSAS-root) and that are involved in the significantly enriched pathway “ribosome.”
