Acute Simian Varicella Virus Infection Causes Robust and Sustained Changes in Gene Expression in the Sensory Ganglia

DEGs at 10 dpi and 14 dpi are associated with neuronal development. (A) Network image of DEGs downregulated at 10 dpi that mapped to the GO process “multicellular organismal development” and directly interacted. (B) Heat map of the 20 most downregulated genes at 10 dpi mapping to the GO process “negative regulation of biological process.” (C) Network image of DEGs downregulated at 14 dpi that mapped to the GO process “phosphorus metabolic process” and directly interacted. (D) Heat map of DEGs downregulated at 14 dpi mapping to the GO process “ERBB signaling pathway.”
