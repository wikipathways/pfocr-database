Cancer metabolism and dietary interventions

Metabolic remodeling of cancer cells and the cancer microenvironment. Nutrients and/or intermediate metabolites assimilated from the diet or derived from extracellular matrix molecules or stromal cells in the tumor microenvironment, such as carbohydrates, amino acids, and lipids, are involved in rewiring cancer metabolism to meet energy and biomass synthesis requirements, and support cancer development.
