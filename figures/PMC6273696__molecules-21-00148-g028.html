Apoptosis signaling pathway was regulated by vancomycin in HK-2 cells

Apoptosis signaling pathway was regulated by vancomycin in HK-2 cells. HK-2 cells were treated with 50 μg/mL vancomycin for 24 h and the protein samples were subject to quantitative proteomic analysis. Red indicates an up-regulation; green indicates a down-regulation. The intensity of green and redcolors indicates the degree of down- or up-regulation. Solid arrow indicates direct interaction and dashed arrow indicates indirect interaction.
