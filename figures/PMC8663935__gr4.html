Autophagy-targeted therapy to modulate age-related diseases: Success, pitfalls, and new directions

Overview of the PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway and drug targets. Drugs that positively (AKT, PI3K, PDK1, and Rheb) or negatively (AMPK, PTEN, TSC1/2 complex) regulate the mTORC1 signaling process are highlighted. Depending on upstream and downstream regulators of these networks' autophagy may be activated or inhibited. Figure created with BioRender.com.
