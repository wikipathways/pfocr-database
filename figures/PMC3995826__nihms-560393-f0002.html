Pathways for (A) Maturity Onset Diabetes of the Young (hsa04950), (B) type 2 diabetes (hsa04930), and (C) Notch signaling pathway (hsa04330); adapted from Kanehisa, et al []. Genes in the pathways which are hypermethylated in ODM compared with ONDM are shown in red boxes, while hypomethylated genes are shown in blue boxes. Genes that did not show differential methylation are in gray boxes. Boxes for INS and CTBP2 are marked with both red and blue indicating that two separate regions with different methylation status overlapped with the promoter of these two genes.
