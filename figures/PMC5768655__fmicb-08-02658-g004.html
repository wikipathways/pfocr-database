Saprolegnia parasitica carbon metabolism

Saprolegnia parasitica carbon metabolism. Shown are metabolites and enzymes of the carbon metabolism covering glycolysis, the pentose-phosphate pathway, and citrate cycle. Boxes represent metabolites, arrows indicate enzyme reactions.
