Latent Pro-Survival Function for the Mir-290-295 Cluster in Mouse Embryonic Stem Cells

IPA was performed for validated targets of miR-295 from this and prior studies , , , and identified the network, “Cell Death, Cell Cycle, Cellular Function and Maintenance,” which centers around p53. Solid lines indicate direct interactions and dashed lines indicate indirect interactions. Validated targets of miR-295 are shown in red and computationally predicted targets are shown in orange.
