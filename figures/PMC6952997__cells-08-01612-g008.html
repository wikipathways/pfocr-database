Influence of ethylene on amount of compounds and expression of genes in lipid pathway in peach

Influence of ethylene on amount of compounds and expression of genes in lipid pathway in peach. The pathway includes four parts as fatty acid biosynthesis (a), phospholipid metabolism (b), galactolipid metabolism (c), and sphingolipid metabolism (d). The red arrows indicate promotive effect of ethylene on transcript levels; the green arrows indicate the inhibitive effect of ethylene on transcript levels. Solid arrows indicate biosynthetic steps and dashed ones indicate catabolic steps. The red box indicates lipid with content significantly higher in ethylene treated fruit; the green box indicates lipid with content significantly lower in ethylene treated fruit (p < 0.05) according to Duncan’s multiple range test.
