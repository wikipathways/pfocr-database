Fluxes of glycolysis, pentose phosphate, and TCA cycle pathways in hMφ treated with DETA‐NO and CK

Fluxes of glycolysis, pentose phosphate, and TCA cycle pathways in hMφ treated with DETA‐NO and CK. Upregulated and downregulated reactions are indicated for Glycolysis, Pentose phosphate, and TCA cycle pathways. Reaction colors correspond to the average flux values obtained from the sampling procedure (units: 1884 pmol/(h·cell)). The thickness and color of the arrows are based on the flux values. Fluxes with values higher than 1.5 are assigned the maximum scale color.
