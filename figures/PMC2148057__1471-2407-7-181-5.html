GenMAPP view of energy production pathway in MCF-7 and ZR75-1 cells

A GenMAPP view of energy production pathway in MCF-7 and ZR75-1 cells. Common genes are denoted by an* and significant genes with a fold change of ≥5 and ≤-5 were taken. Genes in the GO list were rearranged with GenMAPP to depict electron transport in (a) ZR75-1 and (b) MCF-7 cells.
