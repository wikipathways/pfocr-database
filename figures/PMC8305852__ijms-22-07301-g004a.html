Influence of Genotype on High Glucosinolate Synthesis Lines of Brassica rapa

Metabolic pathway profiling of genes derived from recombinant blocks between high and low GSL parents of doubled haploid lines BrYSP_DH005 (A) and BrYSP_DH059 (B). The pathway and product structures are adapted from previously published reports [,,,] and the Brassica database BRAD. Genes not identified in recombinant blocks are not highlighted. Structure of all products were drawn using ChemSketch software.
