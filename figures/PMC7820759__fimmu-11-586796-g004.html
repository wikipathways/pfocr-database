Schematic view of the molecular network and functions of human papillomavirus (HPV) E5, E6, and E7 oncoproteins in HPV-induced cancers

Schematic view of the molecular network and functions of human papillomavirus (HPV) E5, E6, and E7 oncoproteins in HPV-induced cancers. E5, E6, and E7 oncoproteins are responsible for the carcinogenesis and tumorigenesis of HPV-induced cancers via activating multiple signaling pathways, such as cell cycle signaling, EGFR pathway, migration, and invasion pathways, p53 pathway and some epigenetic pathway.
