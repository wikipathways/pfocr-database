Interfacing plant alkaloid secondary metabolic pathways with microbial metabolism

Interfacing plant alkaloid secondary metabolic pathways with microbial metabolism. S. cerevisiae is shown as the prospective host species. Refer to for abbreviations and color coding. Additional abbreviations: AAA path, aromatic amino acid pathway; Aro1, pentafunctional arom protein; Aro2, chorismate synthase and Flavin reductase; Aro3+Aro4, 3-deoxy-d-arabino-heptulosonate-7-phosphate (DAHP) synthase isoenzymes; Aro7, chorismate mutase; E4P, erythrose 4-phosphate; 4-HPAA, 4-hydroxyphenylacetaldehyde; PPP, pentose phosphate pathway; PEP, phosphoenolpyruvate; R5P, ribose 5-phosphate; TCA cycle, tricarboxylic acid cycle.
