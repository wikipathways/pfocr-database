Signaling pathways described in ARPE19 cells

Signaling pathways described in ARPE19 cells. This cartoon depicts some of the pathways described in ARPE19 cells (, ) and photodamaged rat retina (). The PLA2-acyl-CoA:cholesterol acyltransferase-20-hydroxyeicosatetraenoic acid activation by oxLDL/7KCh has been described only in macrophages in relatively few articles (–). However, this pathway may help to explain the activation of the various NFκB-related pathways observed in ARPE19 and other cell types ().
