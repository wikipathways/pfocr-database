Peritumoral (P) activated hepatic stellate cells (HSCs) and intratumoral myofibroblasts (T)

Pathway analysis showed functional networks identified between peritumoral (P) activated hepatic stellate cells (HSCs) and intratumoral myofibroblasts (T). Selected significant canonical pathways associated with PPAR signaling (a, P vs T upregulation) and P53 (b, P vs T downregulation) were shown, respectively. Yellow, orange and green marked nodes represented down-regulated genes, up-regulated genes and no significance, respectively.
