Major amino acid metabolic pathway involves AAD

The major amino acid metabolic pathway involves AAD. A.Homocysteine metabolism, B. Arginine metabolism, and C. Tryptophan metabolism. Hcy exacerbates vascular inflammation by inducing immune cells such as B cells and T cells, thereby increasing the incidence of AAD.
