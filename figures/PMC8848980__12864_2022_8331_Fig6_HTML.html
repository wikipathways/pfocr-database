Time-course RNA-seq analysis reveals stage-specific and melatonin-triggered gene expression patterns during the hair follicle growth cycle in Capra hircus

The putative pathway regulation model of the hair follicle growth cycle triggered by melatonin. The signal pathways related to cashmere shedding are marked with dotted line boxes, and the other solid line boxes mark the signal pathways which may promote the hair follicle development
