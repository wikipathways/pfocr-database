Tumor Microenvironment Drives Intrahepatic Cholangiocarcinoma Progression

Bioinformatic analysis of networks with ingenuity pathway analysis (IPA). The analysis reports annotated interactions between genes modulated by Crenigacestat treatment in the HuCCT1-xenograft mouse model. The top-ranked network showing a high degree of interconnectivity between genes and the IPA functional category of this network was cancer, cell morphology, and tissue development. The Figure shows that Crenigacestat downregulated key Notch signaling genes, confirming the specificity of the treatment. Up- (red) and down- (green) regulated genes are indicated.
