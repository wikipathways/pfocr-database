Major hub gene BrPLA2-ALPHA-centered gene coexpression network using probes with at least one (after correction)

Major hub gene BrPLA2-ALPHA-centered gene coexpression network using probes with at least one (after correction). Only genes that are connected with BrPLA2-ALPHA in the whole gene-metabolite coexpression network (Supplemental Fig. S2) are shown. Nodes represent genes or s. Genes from different pathways are shown in different node colors, while metabolites are shown in triangle-shaped nodes in purple. Edges represent high absolute correlations (r > 0.5). Edges connecting the major hub gene and genes from the same pathway (lipid degradation) are in blue, edges between the major hub gene and genes from the other pathways are in red, and edges connecting genes and s are in green. Edges connecting genes (other than with the major hub gene) of different pathways have been left out to improve the visibility of the network. The shapes of gene nodes indicate cis-s (chevrons), trans-s (squares), and cis-/trans-s (diamonds). Node names are coded by concatenating the gene name, cis-/trans-regulation, and the linkage group. For example, node FAD2_C-5 indicates gene FAD2, C for cis-, and 5 for linkage group A05, where the cis- was detected. In the case of a cis-/trans-, CER10_CT-9_1 indicates gene CER10 and CT-9_1 indicates a cis- on A09 and trans-s on A01. Solid lines indicate positive correlations, and dotted lines indicate negative correlations. All the gene names are prefixed with Br because of B. rapa gene nomenclature. Multiple occurrence of the same gene name represents genes with multiple paralogs or probes.
