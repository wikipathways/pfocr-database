Putative functions of EpsK in teichuronic acid synthesis and phosphate starvation in Bacillus licheniformis

The metabolic mechanism of EPS biosynthesis in B. licheniformis. The metabolic pathway was reconstructed based on BioCyc and KEGG analyses. Transcripts of B. licheniformis CGMCC 2876 and OEK1 are shown aside the pathway as a heat map based on fpkm in the transcriptome data.
