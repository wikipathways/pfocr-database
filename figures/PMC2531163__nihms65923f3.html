Developmental signaling pathways

Developmental signaling pathways. (A-E) Schematic depiction of five major pathways that mediate cell-cell communication during embryogenesis: Hh, Wnt, TGFβ, FGF, or Notch signaling. Ligands, receptors, downstream effectors or regulators, and transcription factors for each pathway are shown.
