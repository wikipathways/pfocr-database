Transcriptional reprogramming of the H-series strains H14, 20, 27, and 31 at 24 h post-induction of gene expression

Overview of transcriptional reprogramming of the H-series strains H14, 20, 27, and 31 at 24 h post-induction of gene expression. The heatmaps show transcriptional differences in log2(fold change) (p < 0.001) in H-series strains compared with control strain, and mainly covered the central metabolic and lipid production pathway
