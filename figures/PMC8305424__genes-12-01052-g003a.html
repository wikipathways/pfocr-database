Genomic Approach to Delineating the Occurrence of Scoliosis in Arthrogryposis Multiplex Congenita

Two examples representative of pathway analysis of the final gene set by Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (QIAGEN). (A) Actin cytoskeleton signaling canonical pathway. Genes that are part of the AMC-SC list are shown in purple. (B) Highest score pathway predicted by IPA connecting the largest number of gene list members. Solid lines indicate direct interaction between genes while dotted lines symbolize indirect connection. Molecule shapes indicate gene functions, legends can be found here: https://qiagen.secure.force.com/KnowledgeBase/KnowledgeIPAPage?id=kA41i000000L5rTCAS (accessed on Wednesday 13 January 2021).
