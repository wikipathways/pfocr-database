Intra- and Extra-Telomeric Role of TRF2 in the DNA Damage Response

DNA damage response factors involved with TRF2 at the telomeres or outside of the telomeres. At the telomeres, TRF2 will maintain the t-loop structure and inhibit the ATM kinase pathway activation. Outside of the telomeres, TRF2 was reported to be recruited at the DNA damage site and colocalized with other DDR factors.
