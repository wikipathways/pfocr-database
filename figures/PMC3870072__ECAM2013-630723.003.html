Two reversing pathways between the vehicle and baicalin groups

Two reversing pathways between the vehicle and baicalin groups. (a) Neurophysiological process: NMDA-dependent postsynaptic long-term potentiation in CA1 hippocampal neurons' pathway. (b) G-protein signalling: regulation of p38 and JNK signalling pathway.
