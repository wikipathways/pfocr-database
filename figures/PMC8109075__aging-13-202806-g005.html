Network of the relationship between circRNAs and miRNAs

Network of the relationship between circRNAs and miRNAs. (A) Red dots represent upregulated circRNAs in PC, blue dots represent downregulated circRNAs in PC, and yellow dots represent downstream miRNAs that bind to circRNAs; (B) Flow chart of the experiment and two circRNAs involved in the VEGF signalling pathway, one of which was upregulated and the other was downregulated.
