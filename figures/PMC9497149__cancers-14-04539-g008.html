Review on Annona muricata and Its Anticancer Activity

Regulation of epithelial-mesenchymal transition (EMT) by signaling pathways. The EMT process is activated by many signaling pathways involving extracellular cytokines and receptor tyrosine kinases (RTKs). TGFβ is the master regulator, activating not just the canonical Smad signaling pathway but also numerous other signaling pathways. It has been shown that the Wnt/-catenin signaling pathway is also crucial to EMT. Upon activation of EMT, the transcription factors (TFs) of the snail family and mesenchymal genes are upregulated, while the epithelial genes are downregulated [,].
