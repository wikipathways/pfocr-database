Focal adhesion signaling highlighted in KEGG pathway analysis in parallel with SPIA pathway analysis by using microarray result of SNU-2335 set

Focal adhesion signaling highlighted in KEGG pathway analysis in parallel with SPIA pathway analysis by using microarray result of SNU-2335 set. Genes that were upregulated more than two-fold were highlighted in red in KEGG diagram and the list from SPIA pathway. Genes that were downregulated more than two-fold were highlighted in blue in KEGG diagram and the list from SPIA pathway. Genes that were up- or downregulated more than 1.5-fold were highlighted in purple in KEGG diagram.
