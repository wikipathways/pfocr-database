The osteoclastogenesis signaling pathway and its lncRNA-induced regulation

Schematic of the osteoclastogenesis signaling pathway and its lncRNA-induced regulation. The regulatory effect of lncRNAs on miRNAs and their downstream target genes is shown. LncRNAs can directly (solid lines) or indirectly (dashed lines) inhibit key genes involved in osteoclast differentiation
