Gene expression changes in the TGFB signaling pathway

Gene expression changes in the TGFβ signaling pathway. Shown is the Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes TGFβ signaling pathway with differential gene expression values plotted for genes with false discover rate <0.05. The color of each gene represents the direction of regulation (red = positive; gray = no change; green = negative). log2FC = log2 fold change. The intensity of the color is the value relative to the most highly regulated gene (log2FC = 2.6) in the entire dataset.
