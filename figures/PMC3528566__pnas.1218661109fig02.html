Carbohydrate metabolism and ATP synthesis

Pathway diagrams illustrating up-regulation of autophagy-related genes (A) and down-regulation of carbohydrate metabolism and ATP synthesis (B). Green boxes indicate significant up-regulation, red boxes indicate significant down-regulation, and gray boxes indicate no significant change in expression. Gene abbreviations are provided in SI Methods. Only the results for the closest homolog to each D. melanogaster gene (determined by BLAST) are included. Consecutive arrows indicate steps where intermediate reactions are not pictured or the intermediate reactions are unknown.
