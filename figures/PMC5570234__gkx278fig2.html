MinePath viewer for BM20 region normal versus high Alzheimer's disease based on the CERAD classification

The MinePath viewer for BM20 region normal versus high Alzheimer's disease based on the CERAD classification. (A) The ‘Controls’ panel. (B) The ‘Viewer’ that visualizes the Chemokine signalling pathway for the specific analysis. (C) The download area. (D) The network layout adjustment functionality. (E) Gene's annotation information from KEGG, MSigDB and PharmGKB. (F) Relation scoring (polarity, P-value, FDR, coverage).
