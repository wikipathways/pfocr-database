Identification of key genes and pathway related to chemoresistance of small cell lung cancer through an integrative bioinformatics analysis

Hub gene screening and expression. (A) PPI network of the DEGs. (B) The top 10 hub genes calculated by the betweenness algorithm. (C) The top 10 hub genes calculated by the closeness algorithm. (D) The top 10 hub genes calculated by the degree algorithm. (E) Intersection of the 10 hub genes calculated by the 3 algorithms. (F) Hub genes’ expression in our microarray dataset. ***, P<0.001. PPI, protein-protein interaction; DEG, differentially expressed gene.
