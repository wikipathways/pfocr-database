New insights into the mechanisms underlying 5-fluorouracil-induced intestinal toxicity based on transcriptomic and metabolomic responses in human intestinal organoids

Pathway representation of apoptosis and cell cycle created with PathVisio based on transcriptomic data obtained from colon and SI organoids after 72 h exposure to different concentrations of 5-FU (10, 100 and 1000 µM). Blue colour represents downregulation and red colour represents upregulation. Colour gradient is based on the DEGs log2FC and indicates the strength of modulation
