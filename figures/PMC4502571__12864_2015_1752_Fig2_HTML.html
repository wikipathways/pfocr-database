Directed network of gene transfer for the Glycolysis/Gluconeogenesis metabolic pathway. KEGG Glycolysis/Gluconeogenesis metabolic pathway and directed LGT for the Denmark (DK), Australia (AU) and United States (USA) EBPR communities. Dashed boxes indicate LGT, with solid symbols indicating LGT predicted within a community, and hollow symbols indicating enzymes not inferred to be present in a community. EC numbers in gray correspond to enzymes not found in any community. See Additional file for taxonomic abbreviation guide
