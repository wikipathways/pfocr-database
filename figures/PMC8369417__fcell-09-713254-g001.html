Potential Role of RP105 in Regulation of Inflammation and Osteoclastogenesis During Inflammatory Diseases

Pathways involve in physiological osteoclastogenesis. RANKL/RANK complex recruits TRAF6 to express AP1 through MAPKs and NF-κB through NF-κB signaling pathway (). At the same time, Ca2+signaling pathway mediated by ITAM signaling promotes transcription of NFATc1 as well as the production osteoclastogenic factors CTSK, TRAP, and CLC-7. RANKL/RANK and ITAM signaling together complete the differentiation progress though the Ca2+signaling pathway (). RANKL/RANK also enhance NFATc1 via inhibiting GSK-3β (). ITAM, immunoreceptor tyrosine-based activation motif.
