An illustrated view of Netrin-UNC5B signaling

An illustrated view of Netrin-UNC5B signaling. The pathway map of UNC5B signaling represents the interaction between Netrins and UNC5B receptor and the downstream signaling events triggered by these interactions. These induced signaling events include protein-protein interactions, post-translational modifications, transport, activation/inhibition and gene regulation events. These events have been represented by various color codes as shown in the legend. The details of post-translational events including site and residue have also been provided in the map
