Triangle Relationship Between Long Noncoding RNA, RIG-I-like Receptor Signaling Pathway, and Glycolysis

Interaction of lncRNA with RIG-I-MAVS pathway. LncRNAs are regulators in NF-κB and IRFs via the RIG-I-MAVS pathway and finally modulate IFN/cytokines. LncRNAs also directly regulate IFNs, cytokines, and even interferon-stimulated genes (ISGs) through Janus Kinases (JAKs)/signal transducer and activator of transcriptions (STATs) pathway.
