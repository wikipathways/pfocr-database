IQGAP1 Scaffolding of PI3K Pathway Components at Plasma Membrane and Endomembrane

Multi-scaffolding molecule IQGAP1 assembles PI4K, PIP5KIα and PI3K at functional proximity for streamlining PI3,4,5P3synthesis from PI and AKT/PKB activation. IQGAP1 scaffolding of PI3K pathway components has potential to occur at any cellular membrane including plasma membrane and endomembrane compartments. This introduces novel concept and mechanism in activation of AKT/PKB. RTK, receptor tyrosine kinase; GPCR, G-protein coupled receptor; Int., integrins; PM, plasma membrane.
