Cytoscape Pathway Classification Network view of CRC versus adjacent mucosa metabolites

Cytoscape Pathway Classification Network view of CRC versus adjacent mucosa metabolites. Metabolites and metabolic pathways are represented by nodes with a numeric pathway impact score for (a) lipid (b) carbohydrate (c) amino acid and (d) cofactors and vitamin metabolism. Nodes are colored based on significance with green representing metabolites with lower expression in the CRC compared to adjacent mucosa. Red nodes represent metabolites and pathways with higher expression in CRC (p ≤ 0.05). Light green and light red coloring represents metabolites and pathways that showed trends towards significance (0.05 < p < 0.10)
