Characterization of Accessible Chromatin Regions in Cattle Rumen Epithelial Tissue during Weaning

Integrin-linked kinase (ILK) signaling pathway network. Genes included are from the IPA canonical pathway, and all of them are in differentially accessible regions (DARs) in weaning. Red edges represent significant co-expressed genes (>0.8) using RNA-seq data. The ITGB1 was selected as the hub gene for the network.
