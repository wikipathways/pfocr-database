Top-ranked and interested pathways in Holstein and Jersey cows

Top-ranked and interested pathways in Holstein and Jersey cows. Among the top five ranked KEGG pathways, the predominant pathway in Holstein and Jersey PBMCs is shown. The calcium signaling pathway of Holstein cows (A) and the IL-17 and TNF signaling pathways of Jersey cows (B) are presented with identified pathway associated genes. Blue and red colors indicate down-regulation and up-regulation, respectively.
