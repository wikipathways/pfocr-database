Five enriched pathways and their contributing DEGs are shown. The enriched pathways are for Mitotic Prometaphase (#1), Mitotic Metaphase and Anaphase (#2), Mitotic G1–G1/S phase (#3), G1/S phase Transition (#4), and DNA strand elongation (#5). Analysis for pathway enrichment was carried out using the Reactome package in R .
