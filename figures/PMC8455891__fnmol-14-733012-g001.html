Neurodevelopmental Disorders (NDD) Caused by Genomic Alterations of the Ubiquitin-Proteasome System (UPS): the Possible Contribution of Immune Dysregulation to Disease Pathogenesis

Genetic lesions identified in patients with NDD may affect any stage of the UPS pathway. UPS-related genes disrupted in NDD encode various components of the ubiquitin-conjugation system including E1 ubiquitin-activating enzymes (1), E3 ubiquitin ligases (2), Cullin-RING-type E3 ubiquitin ligases (3), proteasome subunits (4) and deubiquitinating enzymes (5), as indicated.
