Predicted target genes of miR-125a-5p and let-7e-5p in the Inflammatory response molecular network

Predicted target genes of miR-125a-5p and let-7e-5p in the Inflammatory response molecular network. The Inflammatory response molecular network was extracted using the IPA analysis knowledge database and used to display functional relationships with predicted target genes of miR-125a-5p (green), let-7e-5p (red), or both (yellow). Genes not predicted as targets are in black. The Toll-Like receptor pathway (boxed) shows a significant enrichment of genes predicted as direct targets of the miR-125a~99b~let-7e cluster (p = 1.42 × 10−5).
