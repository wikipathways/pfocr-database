Eukaryotic initiation factor 2 (EIF2) signaling pathway

Eukaryotic initiation factor 2 (EIF2) signaling pathway. Genes with overlapping actions observed in (A) Tat−/Meth vs. Tat−/Sal, and in (B) Tat+/Meth vs. Tat−/Meth and assigned to EIF2 signaling were loaded into Cytoscape via Genemania; significant changes associated to assigned comparisons (p < 0.01) were visualized by loading the node attributes table. Tat−/Meth vs. Tat−/Sal p = 7.37 × 10−12 and Tat+/Meth vs. Tat−/Meth p = 6.03 × 10−13, with 58% and 56.4% overlap, respectively.
