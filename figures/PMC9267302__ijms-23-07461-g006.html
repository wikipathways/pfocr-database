Investigation of the Molecular Mechanisms Underlying the Antiatherogenic Actions of Kaempferol in Human THP-1 Macrophages

In cells treated with IFN-γ + kaempferol, canonical pathways indicating Cardiac Hypertrophy signaling (enhanced) were found using Ingenuity Pathway Analysis (IPA). Upregulated genes are shown in red, whereas downregulated genes are shown in green.
