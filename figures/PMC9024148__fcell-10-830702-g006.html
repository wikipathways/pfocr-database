Multi-Omics Characterizes the Effects and Mechanisms of CD1d in Nonalcoholic Fatty Liver Disease Development

Differently expressed genes and differential metabolites network. (A) Network diagram of expression correlation between differential genes and differential metabolites between WT-HFD and WT-NCD mice. The figure shows the network diagram based on Pearson’s correlation analysis to calculate the correlation between the response intensity data of genes and metabolites, and the relationship pairs with p-value ≤ 0.05 were selected. The red line represents a positive correlation, and the green line represents a negative correlation. The thickness of the line represents the correlation coefficient. (B) Network diagram of expression correlation between differential genes and differential metabolites between CD1d KO-HFD and WT-HFD mice. (C) Differentially expressed genes and metabolite-based knowledge-guided machine learning (KGML) network diagram of WT-HFD and WT-NCD groups. Triangles represent genes, circles represent metabolites, and squares represent pathway names. Red is upregulated genes or metabolites, and green is downregulated genes or metabolites. (D) Differentially expressed genes and metabolites based on the KGML network diagram of the CD1d KO-HFD and WT-HFD groups. HFD, high-fat diet; KO, knockout; NCD, normal control diet; WT, wild-type.
