Environmental Phenol and Paraben Exposure Risks and Their Potential Influence on the Gene Expression Involved in the Prognosis of Prostate Cancer

The identification of key molecular alterations in PCa signaling KEGG pathway. Eight out of 13 genes (BCL2, CASP9, CCND1, CDKN1A, EGF, HSP90B1, MAPK3, and TP53) were identified and marked with the red star by KEGG signaling pathway for PCa.
