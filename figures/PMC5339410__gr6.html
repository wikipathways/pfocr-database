JAK-STAT and chemokine signaling pathwaysA

JAK-STAT and chemokine signaling pathwaysA. The JAK-STAT signaling pathway was activated in the Y2 sample. B. The chemokine signaling pathway was also activated in the Y2 sample. Expression of JAK-family coding genes was up-regulated in the Y2 sample. Red and green nodes indicate coding genes with up-regulated and down-regulated expression in the Y2 sample, respectively.
