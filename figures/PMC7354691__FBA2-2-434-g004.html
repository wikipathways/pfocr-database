Interaction network downstream of Atoh7

Interaction network downstream of Atoh7. Known interactions between downstream targets of Atoh7 from the STRING database visualized with Cytoscape (genes without known interactions are not represented). Node colours represent the log2 fold‐change of gene expression in lakritz vs wild‐type eyes. Node borders are coloured by gene ontology annotation: “neural retina development” (green), “cell cycle process” (yellow), “Wnt signaling pathway” (pink), and “chromatin remodeling” (orange) [Color figure can be viewed at wileyonlinelibrary.com]
