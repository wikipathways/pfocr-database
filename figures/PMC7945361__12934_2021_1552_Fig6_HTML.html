Proposed relationships of amphotericin biosynthesis with relevant metabolic pathways in S

The proposed relationships of amphotericin biosynthesis with relevant metabolic pathways in S. nodosus. The key metabolic pathways were marked by red, including central carbon metabolism, fatty acids metabolism, purine metabolism and one carbon pool by folate.
