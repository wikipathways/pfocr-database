Global MicroRNA Expression Profiling of Mouse Livers following Ischemia-Reperfusion Injury at Different Stages

Two microRNAs miR-125p and miR-501-3p are down-regulated in IR injury when compared to the sham control. Consequently, their target genes Myd88, A20, and c-Fos are up-regulated and activate the Toll-like receptor pathway.
