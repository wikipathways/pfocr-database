De Novo Mutations in Moderate or Severe Intellectual Disability

The Query genes included those listed in from this study (in bold) and known and candidate ID genes reported with predicted-damaging DNMs from other studies (). Known ID genes are in red. The resulting network of 38 interconnected proteins was found to be enriched for proteins whose Gene Ontology molecular functions are implicated in the glutamate receptor signalling pathway (GRIN1, GRIN2A, GRIN2B, GRIA1, CACNG2, SHANK3; FDR q-value = 7.04e-6).
