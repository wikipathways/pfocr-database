Regulation of Molecular Pathways in Ischemia Reperfusion Injury after Liver Transplantation

Molecular pathway representing downstream deregulated molecules involved in regulation of cell cycle progression and cellular proliferation associated with IR injury at early post-LT. Symbols and lines color codes are as follows. Symbols: Red (up-regulation), Green (down-regulation), Orange (predicted activation), Blue (predicted inhibition), White (not present molecules). Lines: Orange line (leads to activation), Blue (leads to inhibition), Yellow (findings inconsistent with state of downstream molecule), Gray (effect not predicted).
