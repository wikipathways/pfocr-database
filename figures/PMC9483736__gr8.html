Metabolomic and transcriptomic study to understand changes in metabolic and immune responses in steers under heat stress

Integrated transcriptomics pathway diagrams from Jersey steer peripheral blood mononuclear cells in different temperature humidity index conditions. The hypoxia-inducible factor-1 (HIF-1) signaling pathway, NF-κB signaling pathway, phospholipase D signaling pathway, calcium signaling pathway and cytokine–cytokine receptor interaction of Jersey steers are presented with identified pathway associated genes. Red color indicates an association with an increase in high temperature period (HTP). Blue color indicates an association with a decrease in HTP. Gray color or uncolored indicate no statistical significance (n = 8).
