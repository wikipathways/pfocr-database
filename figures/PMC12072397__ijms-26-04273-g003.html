The osteoblastogenesis signaling pathway and its lncRNA-induced regulation

Schematic of the osteoblastogenesis signaling pathway and its lncRNA-induced regulation. The regulatory role of lncRNAs on miRNAs and target genes is shown. LncRNAs can directly (solid lines) or indirectly (dashed lines) inhibit key genes for osteoblast differentiation
