Glucocorticoid Receptor-Dependent Gene Regulatory Networks

Pathway analysis was seeded with the 53 differentially expressed and GR-bound genes, plus the GR itself, as described in . Genes in colored, bold text were in the seed set, while all others were brought into the network by the pathway analysis program based on their known relationships to the genes in the seed set. Color indicates induction (red) or repression (green) of expression.
