Alteration of biomolecule synthesis and degradation pathways in Rag2 KO mice. (a) Upregulation of the noradrenaline degradation signaling; (b) upregulation of the putrescine degradation pathway; (c) upregulation of the acetate to acetyl-coA conversion signaling.
