Therapeutic Mechanism and Key Alkaloids of Uncaria rhynchophylla in Alzheimer’s Disease From the Perspective of Pathophysiological Processes

The UR targets involved in the Alzheimer disease pathway (hsa05010) are presented in the mechanistic diagram of AD pathology. The red font targets represent the UR targets involved in the Alzheimer disease pathway. The brownish red numbers 1 to 10 represent the main alkaloids of UR. The gradient color on the left side of UR alkaloids represents its frequencies. The number next to the target indicates the UR alkaloid that can act on the corresponding target.
