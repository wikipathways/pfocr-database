Combined transcriptomic and metabolomic analysis of alginate oligosaccharides alleviating salt stress in rice seedlings

Enrichment pathway of genes and metabolites in FL478 under salt stress mediated by alginate oligosaccharides. Glutathione metabolic pathway (A). Ascorbic acid and erythrosine metabolic pathway (B). The red highlighted areas in A and B represent significantly upregulated genes or metabolite levels. Blue represents significantly downregulated genes or metabolites with reduced abundance.
