IPA identified “LXR/RXR activation” as the canonical pathway in the ESPs of TC-1 cells with the highest predicted potential/significance affected by the treatment with caerin 1.9, predicted based on label-free quantitation. The up/downregulations of proteins induced by caerin 1.9 are indicated by nodes with a range of red and green intensities. The activation or inhibition of certain signalling was shown in orange or blue, respectively. Lines connecting the proteins represent known interactions, and arrows indicate the directions of up/downstream regulations.
