Cell plasticity modulation by flavonoids in resistant breast carcinoma targeting the nuclear factor kappa B signaling

Flavonoids as potential inhibitors of the NF-κB pathway, which may act as sensitizers of the chemoresistant BC and/or chemopreventive agents. Selected flavonoids can overcome BC chemoresistance by blocking TME, ABC transporters, crucial signaling pathways, CSCs, growth factors, and DNA damage and via inducing p53 activity, apoptosis, autophagy, and cell cycle arrest.
