Mechanism of modulation on sphingolipids

Mechanism of modulation on sphingolipids by apigenin (A), caffeic acid (B), CAPE (C), catechin (D) and chlorogenic acid (E). It is depicted with an asterisk (*) enzymatic pathway, with plus (+) red-regulated pathway and with minus (−) down-regulation ones. PTK: protein tyrosine kinase.
