HCoV-host interaction

HCoV-host interaction. Schematic diagram showing the host signaling pathways activated during HCoV infection. Black pointed arrows indicate activation, and red blunt-ended lines indicate inhibition. Viral components modulating the pathway are in red, and host proteins are in blue. See text for detail.
