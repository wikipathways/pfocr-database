Establishment of Mouse Teratocarcinomas Stem Cells Line and Screening Genes Responsible for Malignancy

The map of wnt signaling pathway included at least three different Wnt pathways: the canonical pathway, the planar cell polarity (PCP) pathway and the Wnt/Ca2+ pathway. Most of the interested genes enriched in canonical pathway. The highly-expressed genes identified from dataset are represented with red color, and the down-regulated genes are represented with green color. The key genes of wnt signaling pathway such as wnt and beta-catenin were identified and marked in the map.
