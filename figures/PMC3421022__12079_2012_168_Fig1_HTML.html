The prolactin signaling network in NetSlim

Schematic representation of the prolactin signaling network in NetSlim. Prolactin signaling pathway represents a set of reactions induced by the binding of prolactin to the prolactin receptor. Multiple colors differentiate protein-protein interactions, enzyme-catalysis events and translocation reactions in the pathway. The major downstream signaling modules that are activated include JAK/STAT, RAS/RAF/MAPK, PI3-Kinase/AKT and RAC signaling
