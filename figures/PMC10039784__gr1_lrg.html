Deficient radiation transcription response in COVID-19 patients

Expression of genes belonging to the p53 signalling KEGG pathway in COVID-19 patients. Colours indicate up –(red) and down- (blue) regulation of the genes as determined by Nanopore sequencing. The intensity of the colour indicates the extent of regulation, as indicated on the scale of colour vs log2 fold change value.
