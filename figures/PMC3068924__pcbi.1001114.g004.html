CAERUS: Predicting CAncER oUtcomeS Using Relationship between Protein Structural Information, Protein Networks, Gene Expression Data, and Mutation Data

(A) The biological network of 171 gene signatures identified in the breast cancer data set using our approach. Each gene is labeled as different colors based on it biological function annotation derived from its gene ontology terms. (B) The pathway organization of identified gene signatures involved in 36 human biological pathways when they were mapped to the Reactome database .
