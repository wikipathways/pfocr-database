Metabolic potential of Acidilobus-like populations from YNP deduced from coding sequences obtained from metagenome assemblies

Metabolic potential of Acidilobus-like populations from YNP deduced from coding sequences obtained from metagenome assemblies. Dotted lines indicate enzymes not identified in all assemblies (see Table S4 in the supplemental material).
