Layered network between adalimumab and MG

Layered network between adalimumab and MG. Diamond indicates drug, circles indicate drug target genes, rectangles indicate the MG risk path-ways, and hexagons indicate MG risk genes that regulate the MG risk pathway. Many MG risk genes carry validated miRNA targets.
