Network Pharmacology‐Based Study on the Active Component and Mechanism of the Anti-Gastric-Cancer Effect of Herba Sarcandrae

Gene pathway network of Herba Sarcandrae against gastric cancer. The topological analysis of 20 pathways and 23 genes was carried out with betweenness centrality. The blue round rectangle represents target genes and the pink V-shapes represent pathways. Big size represents the larger betweenness centrality.
