Top Kegg pathways enriched with aDMCs identified by the FatiGO analysis

Top Kegg pathways enriched with aDMCs identified by the FatiGO analysis. (A) The MODY pathway with identified genes of aDMCs and age-associated DNA methylation sites. Genes found with age-associated sites are shown with a blue background, while genes within an aDMC are shown with a green background. (B) The Hedgehog signaling pathway with identified aDMCs and age-associated DNA methylation sites. Genes found with age-associated sites are shown with a blue background, while genes within an aDMC are shown with a green background. (C) Type 1 diabetes pathway with identified aDMCs and age-associated DNA methylation sites.
