Tri-m-cresyl phosphate and PPAR/LXR interactions in seabream hepatocytes

. Schematic representation of a selected part of the PPAR signalling KEGG pathway using PathVisio 3.2.0. The pathway created was comprehensively modified from the KEGG pathway 03320, “PPAR signaling pathway” in Danio rerio to indicate the differentially expressed genes from the genomic analysis. Coloured squares next to the gene name represent the fold change in gene expression in TMCP-treated hepatocytes with respect to control. Marked up-regulation of genes is indicated by a red coloured box.
