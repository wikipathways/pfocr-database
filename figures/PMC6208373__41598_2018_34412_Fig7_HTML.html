Toll-like receptor signalling pathway shows similarities, but also differences between LPS and LPS + IFNG

Toll-like receptor signalling pathway shows similarities, but also differences between LPS and LPS + IFNγ. (A) Genes involved in Toll-like receptor signalling affected by LPS (left side of the square) or LPS + IFNγ (right side of the square) compared to control. (B) This graph shows the genes within the pathway that are significantly altered when comparing LPS + IFNγ to LPS. Red indicates upregulation; green downregulation. Data on KEGG graph– were rendered using Pathview.
