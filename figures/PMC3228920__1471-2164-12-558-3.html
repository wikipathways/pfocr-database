Summary of genes regulated by MetJ in the various metabolic pathways

Summary of genes regulated by MetJ in the various metabolic pathways. The extent of regulation for each gene in the MetJ regulon is mapped onto a compressed pathway of methionine metabolism. Genes are annotated for their presence in an order, as well as the percent of those genes that have metboxes. Symbiotic bacteria have been excluded from "Ent" (Enterobacteriales), but are included in "All". Key is in upper-right; other features are the same as in Figure 1.
