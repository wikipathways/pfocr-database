Location of si:ch211-167b20.8, socs9, eif4e1c and CALML6 in the insulin signaling pathway

The location of si:ch211-167b20.8, socs9, eif4e1c and CALML6 in the insulin signaling pathway. Zebrafish-specific si:ch211-167b20.8 and human-specific CALML6, encoding PP1 and PHK, respectively, which are two proteins that play opposite roles in insulin pathways. Zebrafish-specific genes are shaded in red, human-specific CALML6 is shown in the green box
