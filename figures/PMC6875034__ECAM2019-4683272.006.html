STRING analysis of integrin signaling pathways and response to wound healing pathways

STRING analysis of integrin signaling pathways and response to wound healing pathways. To determine the protein-protein interactions of the (a) 19 proteins among a total of 252 proteins from the proteome profiling of MFSCC, which are integrin signaling proteins and (b) 36 proteins among a total of 252 proteins, which are response to wound healing pathway mediators, were analyzed using STRING database, version 10.5 (http://string-db.org), and evaluated using Cytoscape Software version: Cytoscape_v3.7.1 (https://www.cytoscape.org). Interactions were predicted with a high confidence level of 0.400.
