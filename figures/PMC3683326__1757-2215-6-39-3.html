GenMAPP schematic of cell cycle pathway genes

A GenMAPP schematic of cell cycle pathway genes. Genes significantly overexpressed in ovarian cancer epithelia samples (CEPI) relative to ovarian surface epithelia (OSE) samples are colored red. The execution of the cell cycle is depicted from left to right. Genes involved in maintaining G1 are generally donregulated (Green) while genes involved in G1 to S progression, G2, and M are upregulated (Red) in CEPI.
