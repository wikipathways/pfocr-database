IFN-gamma signaling pathway

IFN-gamma signaling pathway. The IFN-γ signaling pathway is one of the top canonical pathways enriched (p-value 0.0001, FDR 0.005) in the VL-blood profile. Histograms of down-regulated genes from VL-blood (circled in black) are demonstrated in the map. The height of the histograms corresponds to the relative expression value for a particular gene/protein. Objects and interactions as well as direction of regulation are described in the legend
