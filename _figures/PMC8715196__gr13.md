  KDM6A deficiency leads to the increased expression of activin A. Activin A activates
  a noncanonical p38 MAPK pathway, which induces mesenchymal identity, tumor sphere
  formation, cell migration, and invasion in PDAC cells. This signaling promotes the
  formation of an aggressive type of PDAC with accelerated progression and metastasis.
