caption: 'cGMP-AKT signalling pathway and its signalling networks. Nitric oxide synthases
  mediate endogenous NO production by converting L-arginine into citrulline. NO activates
  GCs in adipocytes, thereby increasing the intracellular cGMP concentration which
  results in AKT phosphorylation and activates lipolysis and UCP1 expression. NP binds
  to NPR and induces cGMP production, due to its GC activity. PDE also inhibits cGMP
  and inhibits thermogenesis. NO: nitric oxide; GCs: guanylyl cyclases; cGMP: cyclic
  guanosine monophosphate; AKT: protein kinase A; NP: natriuretic peptide; NPR: natriuretic
  peptide receptor'
