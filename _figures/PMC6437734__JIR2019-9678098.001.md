caption: VPA inhibits LPS signaling in macrophages. VPA inhibits the production of
  proinflammatory cytokines (IL-6, IL-12, and TNF-α) after stimulation with LPS by
  affecting the phosphorylation of NF-κB, PI3K, Akt, and MDM2. Therefore, there is
  an increase in the levels of the NF-κB inhibitor p53 and in the levels of the negative
  regulator of Akt, PTEN. Green arrows indicate the processes, molecules, or mediators
  in the signaling pathway that are augmented and/or promoted by VPA. Red arrows indicate
  processes, molecules, or mediators in the signaling pathway that are inhibited by
  VPA.
