  treatment. The genes significantly regulated by nicotine are those with closed blue
  edges. To demonstrate the expression pattern of the pathway, all the genes with
  expression ratios > 1.1 or <0.90 relative to the controls are also shown. The genes

  ratios between 0.9 and 1.1) or were not measured in our analysis. The pathway was
