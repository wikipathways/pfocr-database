caption: TGFβ family signaling pathway. TGFβ superfamily including TGFβ, Activin,
  Nodal, and BMP ligands, transmits signals through intrinsic type I and type II serine/threonine
  kinase receptors on the stem cell surface. Upon ligand stimulation, the type I receptor
  phosphorylates SMAD proteins (SMAD2 and SMAD3 for TGFβ/Activin/Nodal signaling;
  SMAD1, SMAD5, and SMAD8 for BMP signaling) and accumulate in the nucleus form complexes
  with a co-SMAD (SMAD4). Meanwhile, the signaling cascade induces inhibitory SMADs
  (SMAD6/SMAD7) to provide negative feedback on these pathways
