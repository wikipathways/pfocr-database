  signaling pathway and supporting the normal decidualization. HAND2 mediates stromal-epithelial

  axis by suppressing HAND2, thereby impairing epithelial differentiation. Thus, Menin
  plays a crucial role in regulating endometrial receptivity and facilitating embryo
