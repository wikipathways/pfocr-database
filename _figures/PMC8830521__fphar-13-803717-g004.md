caption: The pro-apoptotic mechanisms of BBR in breast cancer. BBR exerts anti-cancer
  effects by influencing breast cancer related pathways. BBR induces apoptosis through
  downregulating MTDH (). BBR actives the mitochondrial apoptotic pathway and upregulates
  AMPK to promote apoptosis (; ; ). Furthermore, BBR downregulates AKT/ERK pathway
  to induce cell cycle arrest and apoptosis (). BBR inhibits NLRP3 and NF-κB inflammasome
  pathway to suppress inflammation and EMT, thus inducing apoptosis (; ; ). The mechanism
  of BBR inhibiting cell proliferation, invasion and growth may be induced by EGFR/ERK
  and EGFR/AKT signaling pathways (; ). BBR affects certain protein expression and
  structure via VASP, and downregulates AP-1 and PCNA to inhibit cell proliferation
  and migration, which also decreases resistance to apoptosis (; ; ).
