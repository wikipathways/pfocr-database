  intracellular signaling pathways from Gq-coupled FFAR1 to c-Raf. Black arrows: intracellular

  the ERK signaling pathway converges on mTORC1. Activation of mTORC1 induces phosphorylation
  of p70S6K, which leads to HASM cell proliferation.'
