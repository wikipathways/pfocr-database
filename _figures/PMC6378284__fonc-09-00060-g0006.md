caption: WWOX controls TGF-β, Hyal-2, and Wnt signaling pathways that cross talks
  with the Hippo signaling. A panel is shown for the Hippo, Hyal-2/WWOX/Smad4, Wnt
  and TGF-β signaling pathways. WWOX supports the Hyal-2 and TGF-β signal pathways.
  However, WWOX blocks the Wnt pathway by binding GSK3β and DvL2. WWOX binds AMOT
  and probably NF2 in the Hippo pathway, whereas the functional consequence of this
  binding is unknown.
