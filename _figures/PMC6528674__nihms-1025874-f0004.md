caption: Myeloid cell specific functions via non-canonical pathways involving MST1/2
  are shown. (a) Education by the endothelium (mediated via interactions with GPCR,
  LFA-1 and PECAM-1) induces MST1 and JFC1 dependent trafficking of Rab27a positive
  vesicles containing VLA-3, VLA-6 and NE. This mobilization is a prerequisite to
  overcome the basement membrane and successfully extravasate from postcapillary venules
  into inflamed tissue []. (b) Phagocytosis of bacteria leads to TLR/MyD88 mediated
  phosphorylation of MST1/2, increasing mitochondrial ROS (mROS) production []. *MST1
  also mediates LC3 phosphorylation to regulate autophagy-mediated bacterial clearance
  in non-phagocytic cells [].
