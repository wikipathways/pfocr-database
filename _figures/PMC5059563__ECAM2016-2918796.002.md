caption: The possible signaling cascades of NOB in cells. NOB may upregulate the activities
  of NMDA receptor and ERK and enhance CREB transcriptional activity. NOB may restore
  the activity of DARPP-32 through modulation of PKA signaling pathway. Ca2+/CaMKII
  signaling is also enhanced by NOB, leading to upregulation of CREB transcription.
  In addition, NOB increases the activities of PI3K/Akt and BDNF/TrkB signaling but
  upregulates the expression of PPARγ. However, it depends on different cell lines.
  Insulin sensitization has been elevated by NOB through Nrf2 signaling pathway.
