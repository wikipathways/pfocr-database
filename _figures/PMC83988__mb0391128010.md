caption: Signaling pathways for NF-κB activation by IL-1β. Following the interaction
  of IL-1 with the type 1 IL-1 receptor, the NIK-IKK pathway can be activated through
  the MyD88, IRAK, and TRAF6 signaling proteins. Alternatively, Rac1 and Cdc42 can
  activate the NADPH oxidase complex and induce NF-κB activity through the production
  of ROIs. Other pathways involve, in lymphoid cells, the cytoplasmic phospholipase
  A2, the 5-LOX, and FLAP complex and the production of ROIs or, in epithelial cells,
  the acid sphingomyelinase (SMase) and the production of ceramide. CPLA2, cytosolic
  phospholipase A2.
