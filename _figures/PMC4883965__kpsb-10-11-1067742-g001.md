caption: 'Current linear model of ethylene signaling pathway in Arabidopsis. Ethylene
  is sensed by 5 members of ethylene receptors family: ETR1 (ethylene receptor 1),
  ETR2, ERS1 (ethylene response sensor 1), ERS2, EIN4 (ethylene insensitive 4). When
  ethylene is absent, the receptors activate CTR1 (constitutive triple response 1),
  which directly phosphorylate the EIN2 and therefore suppresses the ethylene signal
  transduction. In the presence of ethylene, CTR1 stays inactive and EIN2 activates
  the transcription factors EIN3, EIL1 (EIN3 like1) and EIL2  and therefore initiates
  transcriptional cascade in response to ethylene.'
