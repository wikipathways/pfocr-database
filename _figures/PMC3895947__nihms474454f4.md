caption: A wide variety of glycoconjugates such as heparin sulfate proteoglycan (HSPG),
  chondrotin sulfate proteoglycan (CSPG), N-glycosylated cystatin C, and galectins-1
  are involved in signaling pathways inducing proliferation and self-renewal of NSCs.
  The Ras-MAPK pathway mediating the proliferation signal is modulated in the lipid
  rafts, cell-surface microdomains rich in GSLs (e.g., GD3). Modification of GSL expression
  represses the Ras-MAPK pathway but not the JAK-STAT pathway (inducing astrocyte
  differentiation) and the PI3K-Akt pathway (inducing survival), suggesting a specific
  role of GSLs in the Ras-MAPK pathway. The glycoconjugate antigens and related molecules
  are indicated in red. (Reproduced from [])
