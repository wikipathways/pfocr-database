caption: Possible signal pathways of umbilical cord mesenchymal stem cells to hematological
  tumors. TGFβ, IL6, and IL8 not only arrest cell cycle in Go or G1 phase inhibiting
  the proliferation of leukemia cells, but also promote apoptosis of leukemia cells.
  UC-MSCs or their secretions induce lymphoma cell apoptosis by oxidative stress pathway
  by enhancing SOD activity and decreasing GSH-Px activity. Forced expression of IDO
  enhance proliferation inhibitory effect on leukemia cells. PGE2 promoting leukemogenesis
  by inhibiting activity of p53 via cAMP-PKA pathway. SOD, superoxide dismutase; GSH-Px,
  glutathione peroxidase; IDO, indoleamine 2,3-dioxygenase.
