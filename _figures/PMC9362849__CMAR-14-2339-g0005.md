caption: The activated TAK1 phosphorylates IKKα and IKKβ complexed with NEMO, which
  is required for the NF-κB transcriptional activity. In the absence of regulation
  to terminate the signaling pathway, NFK stimulates the expression of many genes,
  some of which promote persistent activity. IKKβ also phosphorylates Ser568 of CYLD
  to stimulate its DUB activity. The activated CYLD deconjugates K63-linked ubiquitin
  chains that trigger a cascade of reactions to terminate signal transduction.
