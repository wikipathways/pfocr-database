  B: The excessive or non-functional rRNA degradation pathways include Dim1p pathway,
  nuclear exosome pathway, TRAMP pathway, and NRD pathway. C: The excessive RPs degradation
  pathways include directly proteasomal degradation pathway, ERISQ pathway, and UBE2O
  pathway. Abbreviation: RPs: ribosomal proteins; TRAMP: TRf4/5-Air1/2-Mtr4 polyadenylation;
  NRD: nonfunctional rRNA decay; ERISQ: excess ribosomal protein quality control;
  UBE2O: ubiquitin conjugating enzyme E2O.'
