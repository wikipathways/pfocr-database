caption: Schematic representation of androgen deprivation therapy (ADT) or androgen
  receptor (AR) antagonist in mediating therapeutic resistance and neuroendocrine
  (NE) differentiation of prostate cancer (PCa). Inactivates of the AR–SAM pointed
  domain-containing ETS transcriptional factor (SPDEF) signaling pathway via AR antagonist
  or by ADT induce the expression of ZBTB46, TGFBI, and CCL2 (Left). ZBTB46 acts as
  a transcriptional inducer for LIF, PTGS1, and Snail, which further enhance tumor
  progression, metastasis, therapeutic resistance, and castration-resistant PCa (CRPC)-NE
  differentiation of PCa (Right).
