caption: SALL4 activates the Notch signaling pathway. Co-overexpression of SOX2 and
  SALL4 results in tumor progression and cancer cells self-renewal via activation
  of the Notch signaling pathway through elevating the expression of main four types
  of signaling molecules, including Notch ligand DLL1; receptors Notch1/2/3; Notch
  pathway target genes HEY2, HES1, and c-Myc; and transcriptional coactivator MAML1.
  Upregulation of Notch3 also increases the expression of SALL4. Moreover, SALL4 induces
  MEIS1 expression by binding to its promoter, then alters the function of Notch signaling
  pathway.
