caption: Nutrient effects in activation of mTOR system and the end-organ results.
  Nutrient and hormones role (Insulin, IGF-1 and Leucine) in activating the mTORC1
  signalling through two pathways. The first through insulin and growth factors activates
  a PI3K/Akt-mediated pathway. Akt regulates cell growth through its suppression effects
  on the TSC1/TSC2 complex and activating mTORC signalling. The second is by amino
  acids (mainly leucine) through Rag GTPases. Activated mTORC1 subsequently activates
  downstream S6K. Peripheral activation of mTOR signalling result in protein synthesis
  and suppression of ketogenesis, cellular proliferation (pancreas, growth plate and
  muscles), autophagy or lipid metabolism.
