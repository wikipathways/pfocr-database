caption: p38 MAPK signaling in chondrocytes. p38α is the predominant p38 member in
  chondrocytes. TGF-β and BMP activate p38α signaling through TAK1. CTGF and BNDF
  activate p38α signaling through unknown mechanisms. During terminal differentiation
  of chondrocytes, the level of reactive oxygen species (ROS) increases and induces
  p38α activation. p38α regulates ATF2 activity in resting/proliferating chondrocytes.
  Moreover, it regulates MEF2C, HDAC4 and RUNX2 in pre-hypertrophic and hypertrophic
  chondrocytes.
