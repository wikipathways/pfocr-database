caption: Signal transduction pathways of cytokine receptors (A) and the JAK-STAT pathway
  (B). (A) JAKs non-covalently associate with cytokine receptors, and are activated
  by cytokine binding to the receptors. The major downstream pathways of JAKs are
  STATs and Ras-ERK. Activated STATs are translocated to the nucleus from the cytoplasm
  and work as transcription factors. The Ras-ERK pathway activated various transcription
  factors, including AP-1. (B) There are four types of JAKs and six types of STATs,
  and their combination depends on the cytokine receptors.
