caption: The role of NUAK1 in anti-oxidant signalling. Reactive Oxygen species mobilise
  the anti-oxidant master regulatory transcription factor NRF2 by inhibiting KEAP1
  and indirectly suppressing GSK3β. Inhibitory phosphorylation of GSK3β by AKT requires
  concomitant suppression of the PP1β phosphatase by NUAK1. NRF2 moreover drives expression
  of NUAK1, firmly placing NUAK1 in the oxidative stress response pathway. MYC has
  many influences on ROS homeostasis and establishes an ectopic dependency on the
  NUAK1/NRF2 pathway to sustain tumour cell viability
