  of PKC leads to the activation of the MAPK signaling proteins Raf, MEK, and ERK.
  The MAPK proteins induce NGF expression via the signal transduction factor CREB.
  The protein p90RSK acts as a regulator of NGF secretion, but appears to play no
  role in NGF transcription. The involvement of proteins shaded red were all directly
  validated in this investigation.
