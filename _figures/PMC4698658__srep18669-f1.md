  metabolic alterations that are relevant to metastatic progression of cancer cells.

  control glucose and fatty acid metabolism in cancer cells. The genes affecting FA
  transport were included in the pathway as potential contributors to the FA pool.

  -γ. Silver ovals = proteins/enzymes, gold ovals = transcription factors, blue box = metabolites,
