caption: Key signaling pathways affected by CD5 expression. (a) A cartoon representing
  the major kinases and signaling pathways whose activities are affected by CD5 expression
  in Jok-1 B cells. Only the major kinases and signaling pathways are shown based
  on the kinome array analysis and WB in the current study and data from the literature.
  (b) WB showing phosphorylation (top) and total protein levels (bottom) of Akt and
  S6K in Jok-1, Jok-E1A and Jok-E1B cells. (c) Immunoprecipitation with anti-CD5 mAb
  in Jok-E1A cells reveals that CD5 associates with Lyn, the p85 regulatory unit of
  PI3K, c-Cbl and Vav1. Representative of three independent experiments. ITAM, immune
  receptor tyrosine-based activation motifs; TK, tyrosine kinase; WB, western blotting.
