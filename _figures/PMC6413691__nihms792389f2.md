caption: Schematic depicting mTOR pathway components. Growth factor (GF) receptor-mediated
  signaling drives the mTOR pathway. Note the mTOR inhibitor rapamycin site of effect.
  At several key points, individual focal cortical dysplasia (FCD) subtypes are listed
  next to site of gene mutation in the pathway. For example, tuberous sclerosis complex
  (TSC) is associated with mutations in either TSC1 or TSC2, FCD with AKT mutations,
  and FFEVF to DEPDC5 mutations. Hemimegalencephaly has been linked to mutations in
  PI3K, MTOR. Megalencephaly (ME) has been associated with mutations in PI3K, TBC1D7,
  and MTOR. Pretzel syndrome (PS) is linked to STRADA mutations and is associated
  with ME and FCD. For other associations, see text.
