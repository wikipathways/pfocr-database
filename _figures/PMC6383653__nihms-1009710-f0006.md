  Akt. Akt transactivates S1P1 and small GTPase Rac1. Also, OxPAPC up-regulates intracellular
  cAMP, which in turn activates Rap1 and further activates Rac1. Rac1 exerts its activity
  of cytoskeleton rearrangement through PAK1.
