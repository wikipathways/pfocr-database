caption: Schematic diagram of the molecular function of KRT19 as a regulator of cancer
  properties through differential modulation of the Wnt/β-catenin/Notch signaling
  pathway in colon and breast cancers. KRT19 directly interacts with only β-catenin
  to regulate Wnt/Notch signaling pathway and control cancer properties in colorectal
  cancer (a). KRT19 directly binds to the β-catenin/RAC1 complex to regulate NUMB
  transcriptional activity, which is an upstream inhibitor of the Notch signaling
  pathway and negatively regulates breast cancer properties (b).
