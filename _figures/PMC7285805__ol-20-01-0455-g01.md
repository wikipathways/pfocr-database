caption: Targeting the IL-4/IL-13/STAT6 signaling pathway for cancer therapy. Upon
  cytokine-receptor binding, JAK1, JAK2 or tyrosine kinase 2 are recruited and activated,
  inducing the phosphorylation of STAT6. STAT6 homodimers translocate to the nucleus
  and bind DNA sequences at genes involved in apoptosis, proliferation and the immune
  response. Various drug targets have been evaluated to block IL-4, IL-4R, IL-13Rα1
  or STAT6. Points of potential therapeutic inhibition of the pathway are indicated.
  STAT6, signal transducer and activator of transcription 6; IL-R, interleukin receptor;
