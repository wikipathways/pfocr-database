  through SRC, which in turn inhibits LATS or promotes cytoskeleton rearrangement
  to activate YAP/TAZ. The activation of YAP/TAZ can not only promote the movement
  of VEGFR to the cell membrane to form positive feedback, but also promote angiogenesis
  and self-renewal of tumor stem cells. The solid arrow represents promotion, the
  solid dot represents inhibition.
