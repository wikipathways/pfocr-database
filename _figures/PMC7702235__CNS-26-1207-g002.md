  miR‐153 can regulate synaptic loss by AKT/GSK3β and other signaling pathways in
  neuron dendrites. miR‐26b, miR‐126, and miR‐93 can regulate neuroinflammation. miR‐96
  and miR‐27a can suppress autophagy in neurovascular units. miR‐501‐3p can decrease
  tight junction expression in white matter and the blood‐brain barrier
