caption: Proposed mechanisms of PARP1 activation in response to growth-inhibitory
  molecules. In response to the myelin-derived molecules MAG and Nogo-A, PARP1 is
  activated downstream of RhoA/ROCK, whereas astrocyte-produced CSPGs activate PARP1
  through an alternate pathway. Activated PARP1 results in PAR production, NAD+ depletion,
  and axon growth inhibition. Consistently, pharmacological inhibition or genetic
  loss of PARP1 can restore axon growth.
