caption: Autophagy regulation in autoimmune diseases. Autophagy displays differently
  in different autoimmune diseases. In SLE or IBD, autophagy enhancement contributes
  to ameliorating syndrome via reducing self-antigens production, cell-specific function
  regulation, and inflammatory pathway regulation. However, autophagy activation can
  aggravate RA disease syndrome by inhibiting RA-FLS apoptosis and increasing osteoclast
  differentiation. Inhibition of autophagy attenuates EAE by prohibiting T cell proliferation
  and activation.
