  CK2.3 activates SMAD, AKT, and ERK, resulting in the upregulation of the osteoblastic
  master transcription factor RUNX2, which promotes osteoblastogenesis. On the other
  hand, CK2.3 activates ERK, resulting in the downregulation of the master transcription
  factor NFATc1, which inhibits osteoclastogenesis.
