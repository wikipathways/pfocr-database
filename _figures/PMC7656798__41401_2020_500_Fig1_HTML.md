caption: Class I PI3K isoforms are heterodimers consisting of p110 and p85 or p87/p101
  subunits. Class IA PI3Ks can be activated by RTKs, GPCRs, RAS and other adapter
  proteins, while class IB PI3K is exclusively activated by GPCRs. When PI3K is activated
  by upstream signals, PtdIns(3,4,5)P3 (PIP3) is generated from PtdIns(4,5)P2 (PIP2)
  and activates downstream signaling pathways, such as the AKT/mTOR pathway. The activated
  PI3K pathway ultimately contributes to cell growth, proliferation, survival, motility
  and migration.
