caption: PI3K converts PIP2to PIP3, leading to the activation of Akt. Akt inhibits
  FOXO1, which when active, increases expression of various RTKs including EGFR, IR,
  and HER3. Akt also activates mTORC1 via inhibition of TSC1/2, leading to stimulation
  of protein synthesis via activation of S6K and inhibition of 4E-BP1. Inhibition
  of GSK-3 by Akt results in increased expression of cyclin D and c-Myc and decreased
  glycogen synthesis.
