  the PI3K/AKT/mTOR signaling pathway, to enhance autophagy. mTOR suppresses autophagy
  acting via P70S6K and STAT 3 expression. The activation of the EGFR/Ras/MEK/ERK
  pathway, JNK/cJun, and p38MAPK signaling pathways promotes autophagy. The Wnt/β-catenin
  signaling pathway attenuates Beclin1-mediated autophagy.
