  to DR4 and DR5 stimulates apoptosis in both the extrinsic and intrinsic pathways

  and Bcl-xL can negatively regulate apoptosis pathways. TRAIL, Tumor necrosis factor-related
  apoptosis-inducing ligand; DRs, Death receptors; FADD, FAS-associating death domain-containing
