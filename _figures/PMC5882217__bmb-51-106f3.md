  cues to suppress YAP/TAZ transcriptional activity. Many modulators in the Hippo
  pathway have been added via multiple approaches (left panel). The crosstalk of the
  Hippo pathway with other signaling pathways such as Wnt/β-catenin, Notch and TGF-β
  to regulate YAP/TAZ activity (right panel).
