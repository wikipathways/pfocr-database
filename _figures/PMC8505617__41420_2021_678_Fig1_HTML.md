caption: '“Wnt ON”: When Wnt ligands bind to a receptor complex, including Frizzled
  (Fz), and its co-receptor, LRP5/6, the receptor is activated and DVL phosphorylation
  occurs. This leads to the deactivation of the destruction complex, which is composed
  of AXIN, APC, GSK3b, and CK1a. Subsequently, stabilized β-catenin migrates to the
  nucleus to interact with TCF/LEF and trigger the downstream gene expression. “Wnt
  OFF”: In the absence of Wnt ligand, GSK3β and CK1α phosphorylate β-catenin, resulting
  in ubiquitin-mediated proteolysis of β-catenin.'
