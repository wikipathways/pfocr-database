caption: Assessing the potential value and mechanism of Kaji-ichigoside F1 on arsenite-induced
  skin cell senescence. Overall, our study provides some limited evidence that the
  ERK/CEBPB signaling pathway is involved in low-dose arsenic-induced skin cell senescence,
  through regulating oxidative stress. The second major finding was that Kaji-ichigoside
  F1 can downregulate the ERK/CEBPB signaling pathway and regulate the balance between
  oxidation and antioxidation, alleviating arsenic-induced skin cell senescence.
