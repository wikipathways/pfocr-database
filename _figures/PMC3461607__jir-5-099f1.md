caption: 'Overview of LPS/TLR-4 signaling pathway.Notes: LPS is delivered to CD14
  by LBP, which in turn transfers LPS to TLR-4. Upon LPS binding, TLR-4 initiates
  the MyD88-dependent and MyD88-independent pathways. The MyD88-dependent pathway
  ultimately upregulates the production of inflammatory mediators including cytokines
  and chemokines, and the MyD88-independent pathway ultimately increases the production
  of Type I interferons.Abbreviations: LBP, LPS-binding protein; LPS, lipopolysaccharide;
  MCP, monocyte chemotactic protein; MIP, macrophage inflammatory protein; MyD88,
  myeloid differentiation marker 88; TLR, toll-like receptor; TNF-α, tumor necrosis
  factor-α; TRIF, TIR domain-containing adaptor inducing IFN-β.'
