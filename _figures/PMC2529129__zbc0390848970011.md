caption: Proposed mechanism by which CCL2 induces PC3 cell survival upon serum  starvation.
  CCL2 activates the PI3K/Akt pathway, which in turn mediates  the activation of mTORC1,
  up-regulation of survivin, and down-regulation of  autophagosome formation, thus
  protecting PC3 cells from autophagic death.  Conversely, rapamycin down-regulates
  survivin and hyperactivates autophagy,  resulting in increased cell death.
