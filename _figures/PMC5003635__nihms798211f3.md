  effects. A) and B). At low concentrations, oridonin regulates Keap1-Nrf2-ARE and

  concentrations of oridonin inhibit proliferation and induce apoptosis by regulating
  ROS-mediated and other signaling pathways. These paradoxical activities may be correlated
  with partial signaling pathway modulation in different cell conditions and depend
