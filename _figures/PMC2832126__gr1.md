caption: 'Schematic illustration of the multi-stage autophagic process. A: Initiation
  of autophagy is under the control of the master regulator TOR, which negatively
  modulates autophagy by phosphorylation of downstream Atg proteins. Regulators of
  TOR activation/inactivation include the insulin signaling pathway, p53, and the
  Rheb GTPase. B: Membrane nucleation involves the formation of the Vps34 (Class III
  PI 3K) complex. This step is negatively regulated by Bc12 family members. C: Elongation
  of the membrane is mediated by two ubiquitin-like systems resulting in the formation
  of the autophagosome. D: The final stage of autophagy involves the docking and fusion
  of autophagosomes with lysosomes, with subsequent degradation of vesicular content
  by lysosomal proteases.'
