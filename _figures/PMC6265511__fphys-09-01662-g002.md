caption: Netrin-1 signaling pathways (adapted from ). Netrin-1 activates RhoA in glioblastoma
  cells and ECs, enhancing stress fiber formation and compromising the integrity of
  the F-actin cytoskeleton. Netrin-1 also induces the phosphorylation of CREB via
  the MAPK pathway, leading to up-regulation of CatB expression. Netrin-1 promotes
  the translocation of CatB from the lysosome to the cell surface as an enzymatically
  active form in a RhoA-dependent manner. Once at the cell surface, CatB acts to promote
  glioblastoma invasiveness and angiogenesis.
