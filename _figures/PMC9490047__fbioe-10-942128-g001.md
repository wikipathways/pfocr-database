caption: ASC mediates diverse signals to participate in osteogenesis. (A) BMP/Smads
  and Wnt/GSK-3β/β-Catenin pathways promote ASC differentiation into osteoblast. The
  lncRNA participates in the regulation of the Wnt/β-Catenin signaling pathway, and
  cAMP can act on Runx2 and Osterix genes. (B) OPG/RANKL/RANK, HIF-1a, and TGF-β pathways
  inhibit ASC differentiation into osteoclast. (C) Ca2+, integrin, PG, and NO signaling
  participate in bone resorption and formation. The miRNA enhances the activation
  of the K-ras/MEK/ERK pathway, which contributes to bone proliferation.
