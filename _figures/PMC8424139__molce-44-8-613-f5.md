caption: RPE expresses RDHs, including ADH1,7 and RDH5,10, to convert ROL to RAL,
  and vice versa. In PTEN-deficient RPE, AKT is activated to inactivate FOXO, resulting
  in the decrease of RDH expression. Consequently, at-ROL, which is taken up from
  photoreceptors through retinol visual cycle and/or supplied in diet, is accumulated
  in the RPE to cause the toxicity. Therefore, dietary replacement of at-ROL to β-carotene,
  which can be converted to at-RAL, can protect RPE from the ROL toxicity-induced
  degeneration.
