caption: A model depicting the β-catenin phosphorylation–degradation cascade. (a)
  Axin recruits CKI to phosphorylate β-catenin at S45. (b,c) S45 phosphorylation primes
  β-catenin for the succeeding GSK-3β phosphorylation cascade, finally hitting the
  S33/37 sites. (d) Phosphorylation at S33/37 creates a docking site for β-TrCP/E3RS,
  promoting the ubiquitination and degradation of β-catenin. (e) Wnt signaling, possibly
  through Dvl and CKI, regulates S45 phosphorylation.
