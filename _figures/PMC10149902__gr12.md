  also a novel regulator in vascular health and disease. Using the classical endoplasmic
  reticulum (ER)–Golgi apparatus secretory pathway, endothelial cells secrete and,
  hence, determine the circulating level of METRNL, the deficiency of which causes
  endothelial dysfunction promoting the occurrence and development of atherosclerosis.
