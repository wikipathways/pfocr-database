caption: Type I IFN plays an integral role in macrophage regulation of Mmp28 following
  stimulation with PAMP. Macrophage Mmp28 expression occurs following stimulation
  with LPS following a TLR4-TRIF-IFNAR1 dependent pathway that occurs independently
  of TLR2. In addition, poly(I:C) stimulation of macrophage Mmp28 occurs by a TRIF-IFNAR1-dependent
  pathway. Macrophages derived from Mmp28−/− mice have increased Ccl2, Ccl4, Cxcl10,
  and Il6 compared with wild type macrophages following poly(I:C) stimulation.
