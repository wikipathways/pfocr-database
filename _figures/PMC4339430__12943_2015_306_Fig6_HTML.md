  (A, B) Molecular crosstalk between tumor cells and LECs. 1) Tumor cells enhance
  the expression of VEGF-C in response to CCL21/CCR7 signaling and promote sprouting
  of new lymphatics. 2) CCR7-expressing LECs have a direct lymphangiogenic potential
  by stimulating the pro-lymphangiogenic traits of LECs. 3) LECs respond to CCL21/CCR7
  axis through the modulation of VEGFR-3 signaling pathway.
