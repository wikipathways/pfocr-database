caption: Summary of reverse MHC-I signaling pathways in various cell types. Summary
  of the main reverse MHC-I signaling pathways in immune (A–D) and non-immune cells
  (E–H). Question marks indicate steps in the pathway that are still unknown. Interactions
  represented by solid lines were confirmed to participate in reverse MHC-I signaling,
  while the interaction represented with a dashed line in (E) represents the authors’
  speculation. The asterisk symbol (*) indicates the antigen binding site of MHC-I.
  In the case of Endothelial cells (F), reverse MHC-I signaling has been well studied
  and only a brief overview is presented here. A more detailed description can be
  found in a review by Valenzuela and Reed, 2011 ().
