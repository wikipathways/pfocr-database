  down-regualte specific components that contribute to the mechanotransduction pathway.
  Hemi-channels and voltage sensitive ion channels are prone to inactivation via ERK1/2
  and CaMKII, respectively, while stretch activated ion channels are desensitized
  by changes in cell stiffness following reorganization of the cytoskeleton. Activation
  of transcription factors CREB and beta-catenin via prosteglandins are prone to inactivation
  by PP-2A as well as the loss of receptors through endocytosis
