  cells. CD147 is induced during ERS in human HCC cells. By interacting with integrin
  α3β1, CD147 activates the FAK–Src signaling pathway, which promotes p-TFII-I nuclear
  translocation and the subsequent transcription of Bip via binding to the ERSE sites.
  By inhibiting ERS-induced cell apoptosis and decreasing chemosensitivity, this CD147-mediated
  signaling pathway may contribute to cell survival after ERS in human HCC cells
