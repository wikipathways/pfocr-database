  cancer. In the cytoplasm of the cell, fatty acid metabolism includes uptake, de
  novo lipogenesis, and degradation. AA can be metabolized via two major pathways,
  namely the lipoxygenase pathway and the cyclooxygenase pathway. In mitochondria,
  CIC promotes the efflux of citrate from the mitochondria to the cytosol and CACT
  catalyzes acylcarnitine to translocate through the inner mitochondrial membrane.
