caption: Linc00152 is involved in protein activation. Linc00152 bound directly to
  epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR). The activated EGFR/phosphoinositide 3-kinase
  (PI3K)/AKT pathway promoted cell proliferation and migration. In addition, the complex
  of linc00152 and Sam68 promoted tumor progression through the activation of nuclear
  factor kappa-light chain-enhancer of activated B cells (NF-κB) pathway. DNA methyltransferase
  (DNMT) and NEDD4-1 (neural precursor cell expressed developmentally down-regulated
  protein 4-1) activated by linc00152 promoted cancer cell proliferation through inhibition
  of breast cancer type1 susceptibility protein (BRCA1) and phosphatase and tensin
  homolog (PTEN).
