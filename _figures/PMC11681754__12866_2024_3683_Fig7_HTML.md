caption: Diagram of the potential mechanism of LPL-RH in salvaging bone calcium loss
  in OVX rats. The probiotics LPL-RH promote protein expression of gut barrier proteins
  ZO-1, occludin, claudin 2, claudin 12, and claudin 15 to strengthen the intestinal
  barrier integrity and calcium absorption. The LPL-RH reduced LPS translocation intestinal
  oxidative stress to decrease type 17 immunity. The LPL-RH also downregulates osteoclast
  cell activity and RANKL/TRAF6/NF-κB/NFATc1/TRAP signalling pathway, which reduces
  osteolysis
