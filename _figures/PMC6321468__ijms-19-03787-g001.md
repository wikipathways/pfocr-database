caption: 'Schematic representation of the polyphenols’ major mechanisms of action
  on colorectal cancer (CRC) cells. Polyphenols: (i) Determine cell cycle arrest by
  downregulating cyclin-dependent kinase 2, 4 (CDK 2, 4), (ii) induce apoptosis by
  upregulating B cell lymphoma 2 associated protein X and B cell lymphoma 2 (Bax-Bcl-2),
  caspase-3 and caspase-8, (iii) inhibit cell adhesion and migration through the downregulation
  of matrix metalloproteinase 9 (MMP-9) and cyclooxygenase 2 (COX2) and (iv) stop
  tumor initiation by altering nuclear factor kappa-light-chain-enhancer of B cells
  (NF-kb)/β-catenin signaling pathway.'
