caption: Xiaoyaosan exerts its antidepressive effects in rats with chronic restraint-induced
  stress via regulating the activation of the immunoinflammatory response induced
  by the TLR4/NLRP3 inflammasome signaling pathway in the colon. Xiaoyaosan treatment
  reduced the expression levels of TLR4, MyD88, NF-κB-p65, TAK1, IRAK1, TRAF6, NLRP3,
  ASC, and caspase-1 in colonic tissue of depressed rats; reduced the serum levels
  of the inflammatory factors IL-6, IL-1β, and TNF-α; and ameliorated colonic inflammation.
  TLR4, Toll-like receptor 4; MyD88, myeloid differentiation primary response protein;
  IRAK1, interleukin 1 receptor-associated kinase 1; TAK1, transforming growth factor
  beta-activated kinase 1; TRAF6, tumor necrosis factor receptor-associated factor
  6; NF-κB-p65, nuclear factor kappa beta-p65. NLRP3, NOD-like receptor protein 3;
  ASC, apoptosis-associated speck-like protein.
