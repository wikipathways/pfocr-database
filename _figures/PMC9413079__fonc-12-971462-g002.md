caption: Potential positive feedback mechanism in gliomas between TNC expression and
  NOTCH pathway. TNC binds to integrin α2β1 on the glioma cell and upregulates JAG1
  expression which interacts with its receptor NOTCH. The interaction results in the
  release of intracellular domain that translocates to the nucleus to affect NOTCH-dependent
  transcription by binding to RBPJκ. Moreover, the human TNC promoter contains an
  RBPJκ-responsive element. Thus, the activation of NOTCH signaling increases TNC
  expression.
