  DHA-rich algae oil can modulate the expression levels of proinflammatory cytokines,
  Cl-caspase-3, and antiapoptosis of RGCs, as well as inducing expression of a neurotrophic
  factor (CTNF) in the retina via the ERK signaling pathway in the rAION model. In
  addition, ERK inhibition reduced the expression levels of inflammatory cytokines
  such as iNOS, TNF-α, and IL-1β in the retina.
