  pathway, the anthocyanin branch, and other subgroups of flavonoid end-products.
  Enzyme names are abbreviated as follows: PAL, phenylalanine ammonia lyase; C4H,
  cinnamic acid 4-hydroxylase; 4CL, 4 coumarate CoA ligase; CHS, chalcone synthase;
  CHI, chalcone isomerase; F3H, flavanone 3-hydroxylase; F3′H, flavanone 3′-hydroxylase;

  UDP-flavonoid glucosyl transferase; ANR, anthocyanidin reductase; LAR, anthocyanidin
  reductase; MT, methyltransferase; AAT, anthocyanin acyltransferase; GST, glutathione
  S-transferase; P-ATPase, P-type ATPase proton pump; MATE, MATE-type transporter.
  Boxes indicate the early and late biosynthetic genes (EBGs and LBGs) of the flavonoid
  pathway in dicots.'
