caption: CXCL12/CXCR4 signaling and related pathways in HCC progression. CXCL12/CXCR4
  signaling regulates the PI3K pathway and SHH expression, which correlates with tumor
  growth and cancer stem cell regeneration. In addition, CXCL12/CXCR4 signaling regulates
  the RAF/MEK/ERK signaling pathway for cell proliferation. The CXCL12/CXCR4 pathway
  crosstalks with TGF-β signaling to enhance cancer cell migration. Activated EphA1
  promotes the homing of endothelial progenitor cells to HCC for tumor neovascularization.
  CXCL12, C-X-C chemokine ligand 12; CXCR4, C-X-C chemokine receptor 4; HCC, hepatocellular
  carcinoma; PI3K, phosphoinositide-3 kinase; TGFβR, transforming growth factor β
  receptor; MEK, mitogen-activated protein kinase kinase; ERK, extracellular-related
  kinase; EphA1, ephrin receptor A1.
