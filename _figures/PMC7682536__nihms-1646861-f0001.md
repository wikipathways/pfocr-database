  in the encoding genes results in one or more RASopathies are indicated. The proteins
  depicted within the boxes largely reside at the cell membrane (indicated with the
  schematic of lipid bilayers, except LZTR1, which is associated with the Golgi apparatus).
  The proteins shown outside of the boxes principally reside in the cytosol. Abbreviations:
  CFCS, cardiofaciocutaneous syndrome; CS, Costello syndrome; NF1, neurofibromatosis
