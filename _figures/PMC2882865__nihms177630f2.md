caption: Transcriptional regulation by E2F complexes modulates cell-cycle control,
  cell fate, and survival. Examples of known mechanisms where E2F complexes mediate
  the transcriptional regulation required for proper cell development. E2F1 recruits
  the acetyltransferase p300 to acetylate histones and promote the expression of genes
  involved in cell-cycle progression (; ). An unknown E2F repressor mediates the necessary
  recruitment of p107 to the promoter of Notch1 during neuronal development (). E2F4
  mediates neuronal survival in adult neurons by recruiting a complex containing p130,
  HDAC1, and the methyltransferase Suv39H1 to the promoter of the proapoptotic gene
  B-Myb ().
