  gene expression. In the presence of pharmacological inhibitors, signalling through
  Akt is blocked resulting in an increase in CCN2 protein levels, which in turn activates

  Conversely, activated Akt downstream of TGFβ or other stimuli may be a negative
  regulator of MMP1 gene expression via additional unknown mechanisms.
