caption: Two MAPK pathways promoting axon regeneration in Caenorhabditis elegans.
  Injury signals including Ca2+ influx trigger activation of DLK-1 and the DLK-1–MKK-4–PMK-3
  pathway. In parallel to the DLK-1 pathway, the MLK-1–MEK-1–KGB-1 pathway is also
  activated. Whilst DLK-1 can activate both MKK-4 and MEK-1, MLK-1 can only activate
  MEK-1. CEBP-1, CCAAT/enhancer-binding protein 1; DLK-1, dual leucine zipper kinase
  1; JNK, c-Jun amino-terminal kinase; MAPK, mitogen activated protein kinase; MAP2K,
  mitogen-activated protein kinase kinase; MAP3K, mitogen-activated protein kinase
  kinase kinase; MKK-4, MAP kinase kinase 4; RPM-1, regulator of presynaptic morphology
  1.
