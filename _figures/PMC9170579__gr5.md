caption: The effect of lncRNA on proliferation and apoptosis. (A) LncRNAs can affect
  the proliferation of lymphoma cells by p38 MAPKs signaling pathway (e.g., HOTAIR),
  MAPK/ERK pathway (e.g., HOTAIR and PANDA), mTOR pathway (e.g., MEG3, HOTAIR, and
  GAS-5), Wnt signaling pathway (e.g., SMAD5-AS1 and FIRRE), cyclin D (e.g., LUNAR1,
  BICRNA, HULC, ANRIL, and LincRNA-p21), and NF-κB signaling pathway (e.g., ANRIL).
  (B) LncRNAs can affect the apoptosis of lymphoma cells by autophagy pathway (e.g.,
  MALAT1), caspase pathway (e.g., Link-A, BIC RNA, FIRRE, and ANRIL), p53 pathway
  (e.g., MALAT1, LUNAR1, LincRNA-p21, and PANDA), and Fas ligand pathway (e.g., FAS-AS1).
  MAPK, mitogen-activated protein kinase; HOTAIR, HOX transcript antisense RNA; ERK;
  extracellular-signal-regulated kinase; HOTAIR, HOX transcript antisense RNA; PANDA,
  p21-associated ncRNA DNA damage–activated; mTOR, mammalian target of rapamycin;
  MEG3, maternally expressed gene 3; GAS5, growth arrest-specific 5; ANRIL, antisense
  non-coding RNA in the INK4 locus; MALAT1, metastasis associated lung adenocarcinoma
  transcript 1; LUNAR1, leukemia-associated non-coding IGF1R activator RNA 1.
