caption: LEPR-Long contains tyrosine residues (Tyr 974, Tyr 986, Tyr1077 and Tyr 1138).
  Tyr 1138 recruits STAT3 while Tyr 1138 and Tyr 1077 recruit STAT5. Phosphorylation
  at Tyr 986 and on Tyr 974 leads to SHP2 binding. JAK2 auto-phosphorylation at the
  Box1 motif leads to phosphorylation of IRS1/2, which can activate PI3K/Akt pathway.
  SFKs are also activated by leptin. Akt, protein kinase B; JAK2, Janus kinase 2;
  IRS1/2, insulin receptor substrate 1/2; GRB2, growth factor receptor-bound protein
  2; MAPK, mitogen-activated protein kinase; PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3 kinase;
  SHP2, SH2 domain-containing tyrosine phosphatase 2; STAT3, signal transducer and
  activator of transcription 3.
