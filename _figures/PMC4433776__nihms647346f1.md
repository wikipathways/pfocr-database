caption: Model for HHLA2 interaction with two receptors to regulate T cell functions.
  Concomitant with T cell receptor (TCR) signaling, TMIGD2 on naïve T cells interacts
  with HHLA2 on APCs and costimulates T cell proliferation and cytokine production
  via a pathway involving AKT phosphorylation. With repetitive T cell activation,
  expression of TMIGD2 is gradually lost, allowing expression of a second receptor
  to become dominant. HHLA2 on APCs or tumor cells can interact with this second receptor
  and exert a coinhibitory function.
