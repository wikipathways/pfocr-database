caption: 'T1DM candidate genes acting at the beta-cell level mainly play a role in
  three pathways (): (1) Regulate the innate immune response and pathways important
  for antiviral activity, such as the type 1 IFN signaling pathway (IFIH1, TYK2, PTPN2).
  (2) Modulate susceptibility to proapoptotic stimuli (BACH2, TNFAIP3, ERBB3, HIP14,
  STX4, CTSH, PTPN2). (3) Affect beta-cell phenotypes, primarily insulin production
  (GLIS3). Candidate genes in green and red represent protective and predisposing
  candidates, respectively. Some candidate genes clearly participate in more than
  one pathway.'
