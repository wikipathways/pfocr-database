caption: Signaling pathways in the development stage of cervical cancer cells. E7
  oncoproteins binds to the PRB-E2F complex, a step that separates E2F from pRb. In
  the case of high E2F expression, cells will pass the G1/S phase and pRb will eventually
  be degraded by the proteasome. The STAT3 signaling pathway can be activated by IL-6
  to promote cell proliferation which can also be induced by HIF-1α, E5 can inhibit
  the expression of p21WAF1, thus promoting the separation of E2F from pRb, and regulate
  STAT3 by regulating EGFR.
