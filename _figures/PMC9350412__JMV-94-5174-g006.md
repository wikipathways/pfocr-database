caption: A schematic diagram describing the molecular mechanisms of SARS‐CoV‐2 ORF10‐mediated
  inhibition of the cGAS–STING signaling pathway. SARS‐CoV‐2 infection causes cGAS
  activation, which produces cGAMP and induces STING translocation from the ER to
  the Golgi. SARS‐CoV‐2 ORF10 prevents STING translocation and inhibits STING–TBK1
  interaction to impair STING activation, leading to the suppression of STING‐mediated
  IRF3 activation and IFN induction. ACE2, angiotensin‐converting enzyme 2; cGAMP,
