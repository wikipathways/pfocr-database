caption: BMP2 or BMP6 signaling enhances adipocyte function. The BMP/Smad pathway
  leads to transcriptional upregulation of target genes like Id1-4 and Pparg. PPARγ,
  in turn, acts as a transcription factor on known target genes like Glut4, Fasn,
  Plin and Lpl enabling mature adipocytes to more efficiently take up glucose (GLUT4)
  upon insulin receptor signaling, which results in a higher glycolysis rate and most
  likely enhanced lipogenesis (Fatty acid synthase) and storage (Perlipin) of lipids.
