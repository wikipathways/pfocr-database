caption: 'Small molecules guiding mesenchymal stem cell osteogenic cell fate determination.
  Wnt, BMP, and Notch signaling cascades are the three major pathways governing the
  expression of the master regulator runt-related transcription factor 2 during mesenchymal
  stem cell (MSC) osteogenic commitment. The activation of Wnt, Notch and BMP signaling
  pathways by small molecules promotes the osteogenesis of MSCs. The MAPK and PI3K/AKT
  signaling pathways interfere with GSK3 phosphorylation and β-catenin degradation
  and therefore exert an effect on osteogenesis. The AMP-activated protein kinase
  pathway has also recently been shown to be involved in osteogenic regulation. AMPK:
  AMP-activated protein kinase.'
