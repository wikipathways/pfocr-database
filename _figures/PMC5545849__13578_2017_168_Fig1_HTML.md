caption: Overview of the TGF-β signaling pathway. TGF-β signaling is transduced through
  two pathways of Smad (canonical) and non-Smad (non-canonical). In Smad pathway,
  cell surface complexes of TGFβRI and TGFβRII phosphorylate upon TGF-β ligand binding
  and activate Smad2 and Smad3. Smad4 and activated Smad2/Smad3 form a Smads complex,
  and then interacts with other transcription factors to regulate transcription of
  target genes. TGF-β signaling also activates non-Smad pathways, including PI3K-Akt,
  Ras-Erk, p38, JNK, and GTPases
