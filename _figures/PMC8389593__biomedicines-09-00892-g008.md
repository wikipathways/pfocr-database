caption: Genetic mutations in adrenocortical carcinoma. In adrenocortical carcinoma,
  mutations in the β-catenin system have been reported. (A) β-catenin pathway in non-ACC.
  (B) β-catenin pathway in ACC. AXIN2 is β-catenin regulator via GSK3B. ZNRF3 is a
  ubiquitin ligase that regulates the degradation of Frizzled and LRP6. Mutation of
  MED12 enhances the activation of Akt and further enhances the transcriptional activity
  of β-catenin.
