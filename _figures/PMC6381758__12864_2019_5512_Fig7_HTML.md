  transcriptional changes. Rectangles represent key genes within the pathway. Coloration
  in the left half and right half of each rectangle indicates the log2FC in gene expression
  following CCI versus naïve for male (left) and female (right). Only gene expression
  changes with an FDR < 0.05 are shown. Red shades indicated increased gene expression
  after CCI compared with naïve and blue shades indicated decreased gene expression
  as shown on the color key. Green frames identify genes that show differential gene
  expression between female and male DRGs with an FDR < 0.05. DRG dorsal root ganglion,
  CCI chronic constriction injury, FDR false discovery rate, FC fold change
