caption: Anticancer molecular mechanisms of PPs. (A) In the PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway,
  PI3K, PI3Kc2b, Akt, and mTOR are downregulated while Bax and caspase-3,9 and upregulated,
  leading to cell apoptosis. (B) In the ER stress pathway, PPI upregulates UPR, p-ERK,
  Bip, elF2α, ATF-4, and CHOP. PPD upregulates Bip/GRP78, PDI, and CHOP. Once CHOP
  becomes activated, it enters the nucleus and regulates UPR target genes, resulting
  in apoptosis. (C) In the JNK signaling pathway, PPI and PPD activate JNK and c-jun
  expression and result in cell apoptosis, while JNK inhibitor (SP600125) reverses
  the apoptosis.
