  p53 family members and suppresses apoptosis and cell cycle arrest. AURKA interacts
  directly with p53 by phosphorylating it at Ser215 () and 315 () causing its degradation
  through MDM2 or inactivating it at transcription level, respectively. AURKA regulates
  p73 and its downstream targets (). It also up-regulates the PI3 kinase pathway that
  enhances cell survival and proliferation either directly interacting with GSK-3β
  () or by regulating AKT (, ).
