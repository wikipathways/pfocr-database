caption: Gain-of-function of mutant p53 over proliferation, invasion and metastasis.
  The principal GOF activities of mutant p53 have nuclear and non-nuclear effects.
  The nuclear effects involve binding to transcription factors such as HIF-1α or p63
  and p73, while the non-nuclear effects are regulation of intracellular proteins,
  such as RCP, implicated in receptor translocation, DAB2IP scaffold protein implicated
  in the PI3K/AKT pathway, or SENP1, a protease related to Rac1 activity.
