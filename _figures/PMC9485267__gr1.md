caption: 'The oncogenic mechanism in tumors with PIK3CA helical domain mutations.
  PIK3CA helical domain mutations promote tumorigenesis through two independent pathways:
  (1) p110α helical domain mutant protein directly interacts with IRS1 to activate
  the canonical AKT pathway; and (2) p85β disassociates from p110α helical-domain-mutated
  PI3K complex and translocates into the nucleus to stabilize EZH1/2 and enhance H3K27
  trimethylation. Simultaneously targeting PI3K-AKT signaling and the nuclear p85β-EZH1/2
  pathways could be an effective therapy strategy for PIK3CA helical domain mutant
  cancers.'
