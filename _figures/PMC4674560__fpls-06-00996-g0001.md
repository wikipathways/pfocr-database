  6′-hydroxylase; U-6-P, Umbelliferone 6-prenyltransferase; U-8-P, Umbelliferone 8-prenyltransferase;
  MS, Marmesin synthase; PS, Psoralen synthase; P-8-M, Psoralen 8-monoooxgenase; P-5-M,
