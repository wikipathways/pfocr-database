caption: P53, p63, and p73 regulate the transcription of TIGAR by acting on the binding
  sites of the TIGAR promoter. SP1 can increase TIGAR transcription by binding to
  the TIGAR promoter. CREB increases TIGAR transcription by binding to CPB. HIF1α
  promotes TIGAR expression by binding to HRE. MiRNAs such as miR-144, miR-101, and
  miR-885-5p act on the 3′-UTR or 5′-UTR of the TIGAR promoter to inhibit TIGAR expression.
  In addition, NSD2 regulates TIGAR expression through histone methylation. The Wnt
  signaling pathway regulates TIGAR expression via Myc.
