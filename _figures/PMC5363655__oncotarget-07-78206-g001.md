  non-SMAD signaling pathways. The canonical SMAD-signaling pathway functions as a
  stimulator for cancer progression. However, non-SMAD signaling pathways, including
  PI3K/AKT, MAPK, NF-κB, and JAK/STAT signaling pathways, play bidirectional roles
  in various cancers. BMP signaling can cause the activation and inactivation of these
  non-SMAD signaling pathways to promote or suppress tumor progression.
