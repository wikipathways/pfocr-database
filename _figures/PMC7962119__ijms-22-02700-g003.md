  by a variety of stresses. The tumor suppressor p16 INK4, as well as the p53 and
  p21 proteins, function as cyclin-dependent kinase inhibitors, preventing phosphorylation
  of the Rb protein and consequently arresting the cell cycle. They release SASP factors
  which severely damage neighboring cells. Senescent cells can escape apoptosis by
  upregulation of pro-survival pathways (SCAPs = senescent cell antiapoptotic pathways).
