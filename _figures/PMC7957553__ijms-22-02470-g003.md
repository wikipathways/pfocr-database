  transduction signaling. Through a protein redox-sensor process, ROS can activate
  the MAPK pathway comprising MEK, ERK, p38 MAPK activities that, in turn, promote
  nuclear translocation of transcriptional factors including Jun, Fos, NRF2, NF-κB.
  These factors contribute to regulate several genes involved in antioxidant defenses
  (p53, NQO1, GSTs, SOD2, Ap-1, p53 and Prx I).
