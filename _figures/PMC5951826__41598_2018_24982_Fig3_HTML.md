caption: Wnt and Ras-MAPK pathway genes and interacting genes with AURKA, significantly
  deregulated (DEA) or in the most significantly deregulated gene module (Heinz) in
  the two cell lines, SW480 and Caco2. (A) Overview of AURKA, the genes in the Wnt
  and Ras-MAPK pathways, and the genes connecting these, either significantly deregulated
  (DEA; q-value < 0.05, red for down and blue for upregulation) or found in the most
  significantly deregulated gene module (Heinz, grey edges) in the two cell lines.
  (B) and (C) Interactions between AURKA (top-middle, purple) and the Wnt and Ras-MAPK
  signalling genes (green shades) deduced together with additional ‘connecting genes’
  (orange) using a shortest path analysis in SW480 (B) and Caco2 (C).
