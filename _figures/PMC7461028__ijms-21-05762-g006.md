caption: 'The PI3K-Akt signaling pathway is downregulated in U87 glioblastoma cells
  overexpressing ZNF554. (A) The Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) pathway
  diagram (KEGG: 04151) is overlaid with the computed log fold change of each gene.
  The downregulation is shown in dark blue, and the upregulation in dark red. The
  legend describes values on the gradient. (B) Gene log fold change bar plot. All
  genes in the PI3K-AKT signaling pathway were ranked according to log fold change,
  negative values were depicted in blue and positive values in red. The box and whisker
  plot on the left summarizes the distribution of all gene perturbations in this pathway,
  the box represents the first quartile, median and third quartile, while circles
  represent outliers. Akt serine/threonine kinase, Akt; phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase,
  PI3K.'
