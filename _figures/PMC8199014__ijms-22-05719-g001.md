caption: Schematic illustration of the BDNF signaling. The BDNF is initially translated
  as a precursor (proBDNF). proBDNF preferably binds with the p75-sortilin receptor
  complex and activates the JNK/caspase3 pathways which cause neural apoptosis and
  synaptic depression. proBDNF is proteolytically processed to a mature form (mBDNF).
  Binding of TrkB with mBDNF leads to activation of ERK1/2 and PI3K/Akt pathways to
  maintain cell survival and synaptic plasticity. mBDNF/TrkB also regulates Ca2+ homeostasis
  and neurotransmission via the PLCγ/IP3pathway.
