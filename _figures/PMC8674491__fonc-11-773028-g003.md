  changes including activation of aerobic glycolytic, enhanced FA synthesis and increased
  uptake of glutamine supply for bioenergetics through tricarboxylic acid (TCA) cycle
  and support biosynthesis of proteins. Nutrient depletion, accumulation of ‘waste’
  metabolites and aberrant signaling molecules in TME influence the function and proliferation
  of both cancer cells and immune or stromal cells. Gln, glutamine; Glu, Glutamate;
