caption: The mTOR kinase is a component of two distinct multiprotein complexes called
  mTOR complex 1 (mTORC1) and mTOR complex 2 (mTORC2). In addition to mTOR, mTORC1
  contains RAPTOR, mLST8, and PRAS40. mTORC1’s activity is modulated by the PI3K/Akt
  signaling pathway. Akt phosphorylates and inhibits TSC2, which leads to activation
  of Rheb, which in turns activates mTORC1. Akt also phosphorylates PRAS40. mTORC1
  activation releases mTORC1 from PRAS40 repression and leads to phosphorylation of
  4E-BPs and S6K. mTORC2 contains mLST8, RICTOR, mSIN1. The best-characterized substrate
  of mTORC2 is the AKT kinase.
