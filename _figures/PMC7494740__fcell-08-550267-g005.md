caption: SARA is a negative regulator of the TGFβ pathway during early neuronal development.
  Schematic representation of the working model. SARA basally controls the deactivation
  of TβRI through the recruitment of the inhibitory complex PP1c/GADD34 in the endosomes
  of polarizing neurons. The loss of function of SARA by the dominant-negative SARA-F728A
  mutant, overactivated the TGFβ pathway. This functionally impacts neuronal development,
  promoting axonal growth, and cortical migration of neurons during brain development.
