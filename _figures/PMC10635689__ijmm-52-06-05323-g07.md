caption: BJ-5ta Exo exert a protective effect against UVB-induced photoaging. BJ-5ta
  Exo protect against UVB-mediated winkle formation by inhibiting the MAPK/AP-1 signaling
  pathway and subsequent MMP-1 release and activating collagen synthesis. Furthermore,
  BJ-5ta Exo inhibited inflammation, apoptosis, cell cycle arrest and DNA damage caused
  by UVB exposure. Overall, these findings suggest that BJ-5ta Exo may be a valuable
  anti-photoaging agent for cosmetic products. BJ-5ta Exo, exosomes derived from BJ-5ta
  cells; UVB, ultraviolet B; MMP, matrix metalloproteinase.
