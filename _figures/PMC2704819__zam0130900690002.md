  B, utilization of proteins; C, utilization of lipids; D, glycolysis (modified EM

  ATP generation. Abbreviations: POR, pyruvate:ferredoxin oxidoreductase; VOR, 2-ketoisovalerate:ferredoxin

  oxidoreductase; SCS, succinyl-CoA synthetase; ACS, acetyl-CoA synthetase; AOR, aldehyde:ferredoxin

  fructose-1,6-bisphosphate aldolase; TIM, triosephosphate isomerase; GAPOR, glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate:ferredoxin
  oxidoreductase; GAPN, nonphosphorylating glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate dehydrogenase;
