caption: 20E up-regulates Hippo to induce PCD. A schematic model of our data is presented.
  A, 20E up-regulates the transcription of Hippo via EcRB1, USP1, and HR3. The exact
  mechanism remains unclear. B, Hippo limits the activity of Yki by phosphorylation
  and cytoplasmic retention. C, Yki initiates the expression of IAP to inhibit the
  activity of caspase. D, as an upstream regulator of the Hippo signaling pathway,
  HR3 is also a direct transcript target of Yki. These two signaling pathways coordinate
  PCD during insect metamorphosis.
