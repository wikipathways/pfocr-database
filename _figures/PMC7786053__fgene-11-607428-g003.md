caption: The regulation of DNA-PKcs-interacting TIP60 on the cell cycle-dependent
  choice of DNA DSBs repair pathway. Upon DNA DSBs signaling, TIP60 interacts with
  and acetylates DNA-PKcs to activate DNA-PKcs through autophosphorylation at S2056
  for executing NHEJ in G1 phase. Whereas, in S phase cells, SUMO2 modification (SU)
  of TIP60 at K430 is mediated by PISA4 E3 ligase, which hinders the interaction between
  TIP60 and DNA-PKcs, consequently inactivating DNA-PKcs to give way to HR proteins
  and facilitates HR repair.
