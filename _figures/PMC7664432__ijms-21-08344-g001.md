  via MK2 induce GLI2 transcription [,,]. Activation of SMO through the hedgehog pathway
  leads to GLI2 nuclear translocation [,]. Nuclear ZEB1 and GLI2 repress E-cadherin
  expression []. Interestingly, E-cadherin (X) expression reduces activation of the
  p38 signaling pathway [], potentially via nuclear localization [], thereby potentially
  counteracting its own downregulation.'
