caption: Non canonical Wnt signaling. In Wnt/PCP signaling (left), the binding of
  Wnt ligands to ROR-Frizzled receptor complex results in the activation of Dvl. The
  activated Dvl triggers the activation of small GTPase Rho by the de-inhibition of
  cytoplasmic protein DAAM. Rac1 and Rho together trigger ROCK and JNK and promote
  polarized cell migration. On the other hand, the WNT/Ca+2 pathway (right) activates
  PLC to produce DAG and IP3, leading to intracellular calcium fluxes that activate
  PKC isoforms other than calcineurin and calcium-modulated kinases (CAMKII), which
  then exhibit an NFAT-dependent transcriptional response.
