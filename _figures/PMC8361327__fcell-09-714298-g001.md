  regulating cell proliferation and contact inhibition. CADM1 forms a scaffold protein
  complex with NF2, KIBRA, SAV1, etc., recruits MST1/2 and LATS1/2 kinases to the
  cell membrane to activate the Hippo pathway, and then moves to the cytoplasm or
  nucleus after phosphorylation to inactivate YAP1 and inhibit the transcription of
  its target genes, thereby inhibiting cell proliferation.
