caption: 'CDK5 (A) and actin (B) signaling pathway in primary neurons. Analysis using
  the Ingenuity Pathway Analysis software showed that CDK5 and actin signaling pathways
  are involved in multiple functions related to neurons, and a majority of the genes
  in those pathways have their expression level changed. Red, upregulated; green,
  downregulated; white, no change. CDK5: pathway shows upregulation of multiple genes
  related to neurite outgrowth, neurotransmitter receptors and ion channels. Actin:
  there is a dynamic change in the expression of genes related to actin polymerization
  and focal adhesion, suggesting dynamic instability of actin cytoskeleton, a feature
  known to be needed for neurotransmitter secretion, and axon guidance.'
