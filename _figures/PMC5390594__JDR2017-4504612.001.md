caption: The AMPK and JNK signaling pathways are shown above. Besides the circulatory
  regulation of AMPK-SIRT1-LKB1, AMPK indirectly regulates NF-κB p65 by SIRT1. Additionally,
  AMPK might induce the association of PGC-1α. The interaction of NF-κB and IκB might
  result in nuclear accumulation and activation of NF-κB, leading to IL-10, IL-1β,
  and IL-6 transcriptions and expressions. Likewise, TNF-α inhibits AMPK via upregulation
  of protein phosphatase 2C (PP2C). In the JNK signaling pathway, the main pathway
  is that TNF-α activates Map4k4 by combining with TNFR1 to inhibit PPARγ. Nevertheless,
  TNF-α activates NF-κB by phosphorylation of JNK1/2. PPARγ might block the interaction
  of NF-κB and IκB.
