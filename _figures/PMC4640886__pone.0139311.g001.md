  induces the expression of steroidogenic acute regulatory protein (STAR). STAR is
  the rate-limiting step to transport intracellular cholesterol into mitochondrial
  inner membrane. P450 cholesterol side chain cleavage enzyme (CYP11A1); 3β-hydroxysteroid
  dehydrogenase 1 (HSD3B1); 17α-hydroxylase/C17,20-lyase (CYP17A1); 17β-hydroxysteroid
