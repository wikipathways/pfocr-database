caption: Endocytic internalization of canalicular transporters in EIC. Estrogen can
  induce the endocytic internalization of canalicular transporters through intracellular
  signaling cascades, such as PKCs-PI3K/p38 MAPK, GPR30-PKA/TGF1R-PI3K-Akt-MEK1/2-ERK1/2,
  leading to decreased expression of canalicular transporters and decreased bile flow.
  Besides, the increased level of hepatic TNFα in EIC may participate in the internalization
  of transporters by the activation of MEK1/2-ERK1/2 signaling pathway.
