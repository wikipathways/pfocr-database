caption: Figure 2. CagA-deregulated signals that mediate hummingbird phenotype. In
  gastric epithelial cells, CagA-deregulated SHP2 aberrantly activates the ERK MAP
  kinase signaling pathway via both Ras-dependent and -independent mechanisms. CagA-deregulated
  SHP2 also inactivates focal adhesion kinase (FAK) by dephosphorylating activating
  phosphorylation residues, Y576 and Y577, and thereby impairs focal adhesion turnover.
  The CagA-PAR1b interaction prevents PAR1-dependent inhibitory phosphorylation of
  GEF-H1, a RhoA-specific GEF, on S885 and S959. As a result, CagA potentiates GEF-H1-dependent
  RhoA activation, which in turn stimulates RhoA-dependent stress fiber formation.
