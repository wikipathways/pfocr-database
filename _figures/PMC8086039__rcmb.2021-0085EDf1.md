caption: RIPK-3 (receptor-interacting serine/threonine protein-kinase-3) regulates
  necroptosis and NLRP3 activation in the lung. During Streptococcus pneumoniae infection,
  RIPK-3 initiates mitochondrial ROS production to regulate MLKL-mediated necroptosis.
  In addition to necroptosis, RIPK-3 activates NLRP3 inflammasome via mitochondrial
  ROS–AKT pathway to induce an immune response against S. pneumoniae. MCU = mitochondrial
  calcium uniporter; MLKL = mixed lineage kinase domain-like; mPTP = mitochondrial
  permeability transition pore; NLRP3 = NLR family pyrin domain containing 3; ROS = reactive
  oxygen species.
