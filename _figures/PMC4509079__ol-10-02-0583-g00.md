  the activation of multiple signaling pathways. BMI-1 expression is upregulated by
  MYC, SALL4, Nrf2 and is downregulated by Mel-18. BMI-1 regulates cancer cell invasion/metastasis
  and chemosensitivity by regulating VEGF, PTEN and PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. BMI-1
  regulates CSC differentiation through repression of Hox genes, regulates self-renewal
  and malignant transformation through repression of Ink4a/Arf genes. CSC, cancer
