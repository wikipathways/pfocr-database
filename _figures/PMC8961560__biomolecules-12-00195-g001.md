caption: Biosynthetic pathway of N- (Asn-linked) and O- (Ser/Thr-linked) glycans with
  the LacdiNAc group in mammalian cells. The β4GalNAcT3 and β4GalNAcT4 can transfer
  GalNAc from UDP-GalNAc to the non-reducing terminal GlcNAc residue of both N- and
  O-glycans in a β-1,4-linkage to produce the LacdiNAc group. In some cases, this
  disaccharide receives sialylation, sulfation, and/or fucosylation.
