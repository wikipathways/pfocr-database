  injured meniscus with age using GeneGo MetaCore are shown. Pathway analysis showed
  that genes down-regulated with age represent extracellular matrix development pathways
  and demonstrate significant interactions with each other (A). In addition, the genes
  up-regulated with age were also inter-correlated and were associated with either
  cellular proliferation and inflammation (B) or immune response (C) pathways. Green
  arrows indicate activation and red arrows indicate inhibition.
