caption: Gα12/13-p115RhoGEF-Rho signalling pathway. G12/13-coupled receptor stimulation
  generates GTP-bound Gα12 or Gα13. Both Gα subunits interact with the RH domain of
  p115RhoGEF. The interaction with Gα13, but not Gα12, stimulates RhoGEF activity
  through the DH/PH domains, possibly through a conformational change of the linker
  region between the RH and DH/PH domains. The RH domain of p115RhoGEF has GAP activity
  for both Gα12 and Gα13, which terminates the signal.
