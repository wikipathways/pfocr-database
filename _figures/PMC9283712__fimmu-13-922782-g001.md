caption: Cell signaling pathways activated by TNF. TNFR1 forms a complex with TRADD,
  TRAF2, RIP, and IAP, leading to the activation of NF-κB (). JNK and p38MAPK are
  also in the downstream of TNFR1 and TRAF2, leading to the activation of AP-1 and
  other transcription factors (). The recruitment of TRADD, FADD, and caspase-8, activates
  caspase-3, which in turn induces apoptosis (). TNFR2 can also activate MAPK and
  NF-κB pathways (, ). TNFR2 can also realize signal transduction through PI3K/Akt-dependent
  pathway ().
