caption: FGF21 binding to the FGFR1/βKlotho complex triggers receptor auto-phosphorylation
  and subsequent activation of the MAPK pathway and then the mTORC1/S6K pathway. The
  Akt pathway is only weakly stimulated in response to FGF21. The mTORC1/S6K pathway
  next stimulates adiponectin secretion, glucose uptake, and UCP1 and FGF21 expression.
  Regulation of transcription is key for these events. FGF21 signals to many transcription
  factors.
