caption: 'Components of the TLR signaling network frequently mutated in lymphoid neoplasms.
  The TLR signaling network is divided in three sections: upper network, which comprises
  sensors, core network that is common to MYD88-dependent signaling and downstream
  effector pathways. Gene products that are found frequently mutated in lymphoid neoplasms
  are highlighted in yellow (upper and core network) or green (downstream signaling
  effector pathways). In the core network, the gray circles represent ubiquitin chains
  linked via Lysine 63 and the pale yellow hexagons represent linear ubiquitin chains.
  A pathway comprising all 77 network components investigated can be found in Supplementary
  Figure .'
