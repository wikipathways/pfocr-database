caption: Gene networks for differentially expressed genes (DEGs) observed when whole
  blood was stimulated with soluble leishmanial antigen (SLA) in the presence of anti–interleukin-10
  (anti–IL-10). The ingenuity pathway analysis network shows 292 DEGs (of the 454)
  belong to a single network, with TNF, IFNG, NFKBIA, IL6, and IL1B as the major hub
  genes in concert with a remarkable chemokine/cytokine storm. All hub genes except
  CEBPA have increased expression when comparing SLA plus anti–IL-10-stimulated with
  unstimulated blood. The more intense the shading of symbols the larger the fold
  change values.
