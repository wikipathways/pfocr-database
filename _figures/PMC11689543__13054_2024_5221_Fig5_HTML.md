  participate in host defense system by producing immunoregulatory platelets and interact
  with other immune cells through cytokines, cell–cell interaction and EVs. Lung megakaryocytes
  perform APC-like phenotype, while megakaryocytes in spleen and bone marrow show
  enhanced megakaryopoiesis and thrombopoiesis under infection. Targeting cGAS-STING
  signaling pathway and pyroptosis in megakaryocytes/platelets demonstrates diagnostic
  and therapeutic value for sepsis
