  system. Carbon monoxide (CO) can influence the cardiovascular system through (1)
  the vasodilatory effect, (2) anti-apoptotic activity, and (3) immune-modulation
  mechanisms. The roles of CO in the cardiovascular system may be executed in conjunction
  with other gasotransmitters, such as nitric oxide (NO) and hydrogen sulfide (H2S).

  line. The upward pointing arrow and the downward pointing arrow mean activation
