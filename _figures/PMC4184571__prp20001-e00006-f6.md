caption: Putative mechanism for protective effects of cilostazol against retinal damage
  induced by ischemia/reperfusion. The schematic demonstrates the putative mechanism
  of cilostazol interference in ischemia/reperfusion (I/R)-induced disruptions of
  retinal function including the production of tumor necrosis factor-α (TNF-α) and
  the activation of nuclear factor-κB (NF-κB) and activator protein 1 (AP-1). These
  effects of cilostazol lead to protective effects on retinal cell death and endothelial
  damage, resulting in decreasing expression levels of ZO-1 and claudin-5.
