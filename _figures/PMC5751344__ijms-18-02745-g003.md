  migration through the c-Jun N-terminal kinase (JNK) pathway. Also, gal-1 interacts

  signal-regulated kinase (ERK) pathway. Gal-3 promotes cell proliferation and cell
  migration by the activation of β-catenin through the Phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K)/Akt
  pathway, but also through Ras homolog family member A (RhoA) signaling. Glycogen
  synthase kinase 3β (GSK3β); Adenomatous polyposis coli (APC).
