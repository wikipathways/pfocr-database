caption: Schematic diagram of CTHRC1 function in the tumor microenvironment. (A) CTHRC1
  is upregulated by promoter demethylation and TGF-β-mediated Smad signaling. (B)
  CTHRC1 acts in an autocrine manner to promote tumor cell migration and adhesion
  via the non-canonical Wnt-mediated Rac1 pathway. (C) CTHRC1 acting in a paracrine
  manner activates ECs through the non-canonical Wnt pathway in human ECs. Activation
  of the noncanonical Wnt pathway regulates Ang-2 via the ERK-AP1 signaling cascade.
  (D) TEMs migrate to Ang-2–expressing tumor regions. CTHRC1, collagen triple-helix
  repeat-containing 1; TEMs, Tie2-expressing monocytes; TGF, transforming growth factor.
