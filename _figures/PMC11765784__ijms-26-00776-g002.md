caption: The mechanism of amino acid signal sensing in PCa. PCa cells transport amino
  acids through various membrane transporters such as LAT1, LAT3 and ASCT2. Leucine,
  glutamine and arginine can regulate the mTORC1 signalling pathway via distinct mechanisms.
  Amino acid deprivation or the reduced activity of ARSs can stimulate GCN2, leading
  to the phosphorylation of eIF2α (eukaryotic translation initiation factor 2α) and
  the subsequent upregulation of ATF4 (Activating Transcription Factor 4) expression
