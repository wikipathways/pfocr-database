  and the arrow facing down indicates a decrease, due to BPA. Bisphenol A increases
  the secretion of hCG and cell apoptosis, or activates the ERK signaling pathway
  to reduce estradiol and progesterone hormones, and also inhibits the expression
  of Wnt2/β-catenin. On the other hand, BPA can increase the levels of Bcl-2 and Hsp70
  and reduce the level of HIF-1α to reduce apoptosis.
