caption: VEGFR-2 signaling and 4 types of anti-angiogenic agents. VEGFR-2 activation
  promotes angiogenesis via up-regulating EC survival and proliferation along with
  vessel permeability through PI3K-Akt (-eNOS-NO) pathway, TSAd-Src-e-cadherin pathway,
  PKC-Raf-MEK-MAPK pathway and through regulating the secretion of IP3. Anti-VEGF
  mAb and anti-VEGFR mAb bind with VEGF-A and VEGFR-2 respectively. Decoy VEGF-trap
  receptor competitively binds with VEGF-A. VEGFR-TKIs block intracellular signaling
  of VEGFR-2.
