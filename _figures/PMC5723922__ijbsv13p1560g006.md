caption: 'Schematic representation of metformin-induced sensitization of NSCLC cells
  to EGCG. Upper panel: EGCG generates ROS and promotes Nrf2 acetylation, resulting
  in the translocation of Nrf2 into the nucleus. Nuclear Nrf2 activates HO-1 transcription
  and thus mediates EGCG resistance. Lower panel: Metformin upregulates SIRT1, which
  decreases the acetylation of Nrf2 and reduces the nuclear distribution of Nrf2.
  Inhibition of Nrf2 activation abolishes the expression of the antioxidant HO-1 and
  sensitizes NSCLC cells to EGCG.'
