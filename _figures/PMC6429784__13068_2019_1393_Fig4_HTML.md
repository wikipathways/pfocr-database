caption: A schematic diagram for anti-ethanol caused damage in K. marxianus. In the
  left part of this figure, accumulated ethanol in the medium imposes an osmotic pressure
  to the yeast cell and subsequently activates the osmotic responsive pathway. In
  the middle part, environmental ethanol permeates into the cell and disrupts cell
  membrane. In the right part, cell membrane lipids are synthesized to fortify and
  repair the damaged cell membrane. In the lower part, ethanol disturbs protein conformation
  and causes oxidative stress, thus the related sensors and response pathways are
  activated. The group numbers and colors for the gene’s differential expression are
