caption: Proposed model of the protective effect of Bacillus SC06 in DQ-induced oxidative
  stress and apoptosis. (1) Bacillus SC06 modulates the expressions of the apoptotic
  proteins Bcl2, Bax and caspase-3; (2) SC06 down-regulates AKT activity to decrease
  ROS production; and (3) SC06 induces autophagy via the activation of p38 MAPK, not
  the AKT/mTOR signaling pathway. Through this mechanism, Bacillus SC06 protects against
  DQ-induced oxidative damages and apoptotic cell death.
