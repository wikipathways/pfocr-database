caption: Pin1 in the metabolic pathway acts as a negative regulator of AMPK and modulates
  the Akt active form. Insulin promotes neuronal cytoskeletal dynamics via Akt phosphorylation.
  Activation of insulin signaling leads usually to activation of phosphatidyl-inositol-3-kinase/Akt
  (PI3K/Akt) pathway. After being activated through its phosphorylation mainly at
  Thr308 residue, Akt phosphorylates glycogen synthase kinase-3β (GSK-3β) at Ser9
  and inactivates GSK-3β kinase activity. GSK-3β is also the major protein kinase
  regulating tau phosphorylation in the brain. Pin1 modulates all these players involved
  in the signaling cascade.
