caption: Lamina propria IL-10 impacts the intestinal epithelium. (1) IL-10 supports
  the maturation and function of differentiated IECs such as Paneth cells and goblet
  cells while preserving stem cell renewal., , , ,  (2) IL-10 sustains barrier integrity
  through regulating intercellular junctions and supports cell repair.,,,, (3) IL-10
  exerts antiapoptotic effects on IECs by acting on IEC gene expression and extrinsic
  apoptosis-inducing factors.,,, (4) IL-10 regulates the inflammatory response by
  inhibiting chemokine production by IEC and the IFN-γ–induced major histocompatibility
  complex class (MHC) II expression on IECs. (5) IL-10 upregulates SERT and downregulates
  fucosylated glycans, which shapes mucosal tolerance to microbes and gut homeostasis.
  casp, caspase; FasL, Fas ligand; FLIP, Fas-associated death domain IL-1–converting
