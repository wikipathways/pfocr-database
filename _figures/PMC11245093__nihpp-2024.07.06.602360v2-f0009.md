caption: a, Gene clusters potentially driving D-glucuronate (GlcA) utilization by
  Bl. infantis JG_Bg463 and glucuronide (GluA) utilization by B. breve Bg41721_1C11.
  Orthologous genes are linked, and the link color represents the sequence identity
  between corresponding protein products. b, Reconstructed D-glucuronate and glucuronide
  utilization pathways. c, Growth curves of selected Bifidobacterium strains in MRS-AC
  supplemented with 1% D-glucuronic acid. Data represent the mean ± s.d. of three
  biological replicates. d, Gene clusters potentially driving the utilization of (i)
  sugar alcohols D-mannitol (Mtl), D-sorbitol (Stl), and myo-inositol (Ino), (ii)
  sugar acids D-gluconate (Gco) and D-galactonate (Glt), (iii) L-ascorbate (Asc).
  e, Reconstructed utilization pathways for monosaccharide derivatives. f, Gene cluster
  (nan) driving sialic acid (Neu5Ac) utilization by B. breve strains. g, Neu5Ac utilization
  pathway in B. breve. h, Growth curves of selected B. breve strains in MRS-AC supplemented
  with 1% N-acetylneuraminic acid. Data represent the mean ± s.d. of three biological
  replicates
