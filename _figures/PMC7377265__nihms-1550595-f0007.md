  to activate GABAB1R in the GABAB1R/CaSR heteromers to block G-protein signaling
  of the CaSR/CaSR homomers in PTGs, thus promoting PTH secretion, and that increased

  in different HPT states. The presented stoichiometric composition of the receptor
  complex is speculative and remains to be determined.
