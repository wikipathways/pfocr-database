caption: JAK/STAT canonical pathway in human DKD as assessed by Ingenuity Pathway
  Analysis software. The JAK/STAT family members represented include JAK1, JAK2, and
  JAK3 and STAT1, STAT2, STAT3, STAT4, STAT5A, STAT5B, and STAT6. The mRNA expression
  changes for JAK/STAT family members in early (Pima Indians, n = 22–24) (left panel)
  and progressive (Caucasian, n = 7–11) (right panel) human DKD are indicated in red
  for increased expression and green for decreased expression, compared with the corresponding
  control group (living donors (LD) and minimal change disease (MCD), n = 7–12). Adapted
