caption: Models for the action of the Nck/Dock signaling pathways in R-cell growth
  cones. During R-cell axon guidance and growth-cone expansion, Pak is activated by
  the combined action of an InR-Dock-linked signal and a Trio-Rac-linked signal, thus
  allowing Pak to modulate cytoskeletal changes. Dock may also link the guidance signal
  to the actin cytoskeleton via the Sra-1-Kette-SCAR pathway. During R1-R6 targeting,
  a Dock-linked stop signal activates Msn, which in turn phosphorylates Bif. Bif then
  restructures the actin cytoskeleton.
