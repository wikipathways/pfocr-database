caption: Transgenic mouse model with thyroid specific PAX8/PPARγ translocation (PPFP)
  and PTEN deletion develops follicular thyroid carcinoma. At a molecular level, PPFP
  weakly induces a subset of adipocyte PPARγ target genes, promotes cell cycle progression
  and activates the WNT/TCF pathway. Pioglitazone (a PPARγ agonist that also binds
  PPFP) causes PPFP to strongly activate adipocyte PPARγ target genes, resulting in
  trans-differentiation of the thyroid cancer cells into adipocyte-like cells. In
  addition, pioglitazone-activated PPFP recruits immune cells in the tumor microenvironment.
