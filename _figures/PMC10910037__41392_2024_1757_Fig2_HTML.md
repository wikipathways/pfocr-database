caption: The crosstalk between NF-κB signaling and other signaling pathways. (1) The
  activation of PI3K by BCR and IL-7R via the cIAP-IKK pathway results in the stimulation
  of NF-κB. Hepatitis B virus X protein induces aerobic glycolysis and produces lactate
  through the NF-κB/hexokinase 2 pathway, activating the PI3K/AKT signal; (2) NF-κB
  inhibits TNF-α-mediated JNK signaling; (3) The product of NF-κB signaling, IL-6,
  can activate STAT3. JAK-STAT3 can act as an upstream regulator of NF-κB, promoting
  NF-κB signal transduction, while STAT1 inhibits NF-κB-mediated tumor cell survival;
  (4) TAK1 promotes NF-κB transcriptional activity; (5) Wnt/β-catenin signaling activates
  NF-κB in the cytoplasm. Dvl inhibits NF-κB signaling in the nucleus; (6) Notch1
  binds to NF-κB, promoting NF-κB transcriptional activity. APC adenomatosis polyposis
  coli protein, CK1α casein kinase 1 alpha, DAP12 DNAX-activating protein of 12 kDa,
