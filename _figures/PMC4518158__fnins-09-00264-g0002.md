caption: Modulators of the PERK-eIF2 α pathway. (A) CCT020312 activates PERK, inducing
  activation of the PERK-eIF2α pathway independently of ER stress. (B) GSK2606414
  and GSK2656157 both selectively suppress PERK-mediated eIF2α phosphorylation. (C)
  Salburinal selectively inhibits of the activity of phosphatase complexes that dephosphorylate
  p-eIF2α, including both GADD34/PP1 complex and CReP (constitutive repressor of eIF2a
  phosphorylation)/PP1 complex. Guanabenz selectively binds to GADD34 and inhibits
  the activity of GADD34/PP1 complex, attenuating eIF2α dephosphorylation.
