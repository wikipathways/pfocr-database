caption: A model of the HGF/Met-FOXM1 positive feedback loop. FOXM1 transcriptionally
  regulated the expression of Met, activated HGF/Met signaling, and stimulated the
  expression and transcriptional activity of FOXM1 via the RAS/ERK1/2, PI3K/AKT, and
  STAT3 signaling pathways, and the HGF/Met-FOXM1 positive feedback loop promoted
  PDA growth and resistance to Met inhibition. Treatment with HGF/Met signaling inhibitor,
  Met-specific siRNA (siMet), the FOXM1 inhibitor, or RAS/ERK1/2, PI3K/AKT, and STAT3
  signaling pathway inhibitors abolished the positive feedback loop.
