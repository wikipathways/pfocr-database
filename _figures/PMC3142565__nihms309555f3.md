caption: p110 isoform-specific inhibition in treating PTEN-deficient tumors.In a given
  tumor driven by PTEN loss, the p110 isoform dependence may be determined by input
  signals which are amplified by loss of PTEN. If these signals arise from a GPCR,
  then p110β will be the major isoform carrying the load. When the signals activating
  the PI3K pathway come from coexisting oncogenic components e.g. RTKs, Ras or activating
  mutants of p110α (*RTKs, *Ras, *p110α), then p110α takes command.
