caption: Schematic diagram for 4F promoting SDF-1α expression in vascular endothelial
  cells. 4F can upregulate the levels of HIF-1α in mice and endothelial cells by activating
  PI3K/Akt/ERK signaling pathway and thus promoting the level of SDF-1α. Elevated
  SDF-1α in blood circulation may mobilize EPCs in the bone marrow to migrate to the
  site of vascular intimal injury where EPCs differentiate into mature ECs and participate
  in the repair of vascular endothelial injury.
