  functions in Drosophila innate immunity. From top to bottom, the three rows represent
  the immune pathway (the Toll, Imd, JNK and JAK/STAT pathways are depicted,from left
  to right) (A), the immune response (B) and immunological functions (C). The red,
  blue, green and yellow lines indicate that the processes are related to the Toll,
