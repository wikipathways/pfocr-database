caption: 'TIR and NF-κB signaling pathways. Immune receptor signaling pathways leading
  to NF-κB activation can be grouped into four categories on the basis of the surface
  receptors involved: members of the TIR superfamily (IL-1Rs/TLRs), antigen receptors
  (TCR and BCR), members of the TNF-R superfamily (TNF-Rs), and RANK, VEGFR3, and
  EDAR. The two proteins of the TIR signaling pathway (MyD88 and IRAK-4) and the two
  proteins of the NF-κB signaling pathway (NEMO and IκBα) responsible for primary
  immunodeficiencies are shown.'
