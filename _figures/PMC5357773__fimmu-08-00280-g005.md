caption: FcγRIIIb initiates signaling pathways to the nucleus. In human neutrophils
  (PMN), FcγRIIa activates the classical immunoreceptor tyrosine-based activation
  motif-mediated signaling pathway leading to efficient phagocytosis. By contrast,
  FcγRIIIb induces a robust increase in phosphorylated ERK in the nucleus, and also
  efficient phosphorylation of the nuclear factor Elk-1. The FcγRIIIb signaling pathway
  remains a mystery and further future research is needed in this area. P represents
  a phosphate group; Syk, spleen tyrosine kinase; PI3K, phosphatidylinositol-3 kinase;
  MEK, ERK kinase; ERK, extracellular signal-regulated kinase.
