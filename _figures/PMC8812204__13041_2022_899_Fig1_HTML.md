caption: Core mechanisms of autophagy initiation in ischemic stroke. Energy failure
  could activate AMPK and inhibit mTORC1, which subsequently phosphorylates ULK1 and
  leads to ULK1 activation. Hypoxia promotes the production and release of HIF-1,
  which promotes the expression of BNIP3 that can mediate the dissociation of beclin-1
  and Bcl-2. ULK1 activation and beclin-1 release can promote the formation of the
  PtdIns3K complexes and the ULK1-Atg13-FIP200 complexes that are both essential for
  autophagy initiation. NF-κB can also mediate autophagy initiation through p53 signaling
  pathway. Excessive excitotoxity prevents the fusion of autophagosomes and lysosomes,
  thereby inhibiting autophagic flux
