caption: Nucleic acid sensors in endothelial cells. RNA sensors RIG-I/MDA5 respond
  to exogenous RNA and lead to a type I interferon response via the MAVS pathway in
  ECs. Conversely, TREX1 degrades ssDNA or dsDNA in the cytosol, thereby preventing
  activation of the cGAS-STING pathway that activates type I interferons. Activation
  of the RIG-I pathway or inhibition of the TREX1 pathway will inhibit tumor angiogenesis
  by upregulating interferon responses.
