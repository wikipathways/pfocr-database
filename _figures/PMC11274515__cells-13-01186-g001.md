caption: The signaling cascades of autophagy in hepatocellular carcinoma cells (HCCs).
  The EGFR/IGF/MAPK signaling pathway downregulates the PI3K/mTORC1 pathway and upregulates
  the C-Jun and Ras/Raf pathways. On the other hand, the AMPK pathway induces autophagy
  through the phosphorylation of ULK1 and PSC1/PSC2. The NF-κB pathway induces autophagy
  by activating the Beclin1 and repressing the Bcl-2/X1. The P53 gene also activates
  autophagy and inhibits cell proliferation of HCC. Figure made with BioRender.com
