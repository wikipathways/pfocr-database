caption: The downstream signaling pathways include the Rat sarcoma (Ras), mitogen
  activated protein (MAP), extracellular signal regulated kinase (ERK), phosphoinositide
  3-kinase (PI3K), Akt, Janus activated kinase (JAK3) and Signal Transducer and Activator
  of Transcription (STAT3) signaling pathways. In general, the RAS/MEK/ERK pathway
  drives cell proliferation, and the PI3K/Akt and JAK3-STAT3 pathways play pro-survival
  roles. Although different ALK fusions may differentially activate downstream signaling
  pathways, EML4-ALK signals through ERK and PI3K. Activation of the RAS/MEK/ERK and
  PI3K/Akt pathways play key roles in EML4-ALK–mediated cellular oncogenesis.
