  β4 (in lipid rafts) results in the activation and recruitment of Shc to tyrosine
  1526 on β4. The data suggest that this event is sufficient to activate the classical
  MAP kinase cell cycle progression pathway. Importantly, the activated EGF receptor
  (EGFR) competes with tyrosine phosphorylated β4 for Shc binding and can independently
  induce mitosis. PTP, protein tyrosine phosphatase.
