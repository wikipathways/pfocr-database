  enhance Id gene expression. Noggin sequesters BMP and antagonizes BMP signaling.
  (B) Canonical pathway of Notch signaling. Notch receptors bind to ligands on neighboring
  cells, inducing gamma‐secretase‐mediated cleavage in the signal‐receiving cells
  (lower). Cleaved Notch receptor (NICD) translocates into the nucleus and activates
  the expression of Hes genes.
