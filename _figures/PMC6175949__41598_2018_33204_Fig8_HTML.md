caption: The proposed positive feedback mechanism for Th17 cell-secreted IL-17A and
  NLRP3 inflammasome activity in a circuit. In this proposed mechanism, specific natural
  compounds, such as luteolin, and wedelolactone, exhibited anti-colitis activity
  and suppressed IL-17A expression in Th17 cells and IL-17R-activated signaling at
  the post-transcriptional level, which further activated the specific TLR pathway
  and NLRP3 inflammasome. We also included the possibility that these phytochemicals
  can suppress the expression of specific inflammasome components, such as NLRP3 and
  NLRP1, via yet unknown mechanisms.
