caption: Mechanisms underlying SALL4 regulation and function in colorectal cancer.
  In colorectal cancer, miR-508 and miR-219-5p suppress SALL4 expression by interacting
  with its 3′ UTR sequence, while miR-3622a-3p promotes SALL4 mRNA degradation as
  a tumor suppressor. SALL4 knockdown in CRC cells inhibits Bcl-2 expression and induces
  cell apoptosis. In CRC cells, SALL4 regulates Gli1 promoting the invasion and migration.
  SALL4 also targets β-catenin in colorectal cancer, further activating the Wnt/β-catenin
  pathways
