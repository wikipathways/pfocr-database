caption: Synthetic metabolic pathways involved in the conversion of OPEFB lignin derivatives
  to converge to a single intermediate (protocatechuic acid) (1) and a linear precursor
  (β-ketoadipic acid) (2) that are needed for adipic acid and levulinic acid production.
  Pathway genes (in bold) and enzymes are fcs (feruloyl-CoA synthetase), ech (enoyl-CoA
  hydratase), vdh (vanillin dehydrogenase), vanAB (vanillate O-demethylase), pobA
  (p-hydroxybenzoate hydroxylase), pcaGH (protocatechuate 3,4-dioxygenase), pcaB (3-carboxy-cis,cis-muconate
  cycloisomerase), pcaC (4-carboxymuconolactone decarboxylase) and pcaD (β-ketoadipate
  enol-lactone hydrolase). Chemical structures were drawn using ACD/ChemSketch.
