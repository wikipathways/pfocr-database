  including AKT, STAT3 and RAF/MEK/ERK mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK). EGFR
  mutant lung cancer cells produce high IL6 levels, which subsequently activates the
  gp130/JAK/STAT3 pathway. Unphosphorylated-STAT3 (U-STAT3), induced due to activation
  of the STAT3 in response to EGFR mutations or ligands such as IL-6, activates genes
  such as RANTES.
