caption: Enzymatic cascade of the renin-angiotensin system (RAS) and the key receptor
  systems. The RAS cascade showing the angiotensin peptide metabolic pathway. Angiotensinogen,
  as the starting substrate, is cleaved by renin to AngI. AngI is cleaved by ACE to
  AngII, which is cleaved by ACE2 to Ang(-). AngII acts on AT1 and AT2 receptors.
  Ang(-) acts on Mas receptors and counterbalances the AngII/AngII AT1R actions. RAS,
  renin-angiotensin system; AngI, angiotensin I; AngII, angiotensin II; ACE, angiotensin-converting
