caption: (A) DNA damage activates IKKβ, which can phosphorylate Bcl-xL and enhance
  its degradation (arrow 1). Reduction of Bcl-xL levels triggers the activation of
  caspases, which cleaves Htt. IKKβ inhibition block degradation of Bcl-xL, caspases
  activation, and proteolysis of Htt. Similar to the inhibition of IKKβ, elevation
  of Bcl-xL also prevents caspase activation and Htt proteolysis. On the other hand,
  etoposide treatment reduces the activity of IKKα (arrow 2). This may enhance IKKβ
  activation and/or block expression of neuroprotective proteins that are essential
  for interfering with caspase activation and maintaining Htt levels. Elevated IKKα
  expressed from a lentivirus overcomes these deficiencies and prevents Htt proteolysis.
