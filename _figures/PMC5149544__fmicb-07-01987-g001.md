caption: The PGs biosynthetic pathway. In response to different types of stimuli (hormones
  or cytokines), phospholipase A2 is activated to produce AA from membrane phospholipids.

  then converted into prostanoids through the action of various synthases differentially
  expressed in cells. PG, Prostaglandins; TX, Thromboxane; NSAIDs, Nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory
