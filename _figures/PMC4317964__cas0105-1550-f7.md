caption: Model of tumor cell resistance to antineoplastic therapy through angiopoietin-like
  protein 2 (ANGPTL2) expression. Tumor cell-secreted ANGPTL2 induces spleen tyrosine
  kinase (Syk), which activates phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K) and nuclear factor
  of activated T cells c (NFATc). The PI3K pathway activates nuclear factor-κB (NF-κB)
  signaling and induces expression of anti-apoptotic BCL-2 family members to inhibit
  apoptosis. In addition, ANGPTL2 upregulates itself through NFATc.
