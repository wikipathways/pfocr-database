  of class I PI3K inhibitor GDC-0941. BM stromal cells activates PI3K/AKT/mTOR signaling
  and interferes with FLT3-ITD inhibitor sorafenib-induced Pim-1 and Mcl-1 inhibition.
  Combined treatment with GDC-0941 and sorafenib inhibits FLT3-ITD–bearing AML cell
  growth through apoptosis induction and G1 cell-cycle arrest by blocking the separate
  signaling pathways that coordinately increased Pim-1, Mcl-1, and represses p27Kip1
  expression.
