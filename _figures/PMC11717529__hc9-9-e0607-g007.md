caption: 'The mechanism of action of hsa_circ_0109623 and hsa-miR-146b-3p and their
  downstream target gene SORT1 in the pathogenesis of AIH. Hsa_circ_0109623 competitively
  binds to hsa-miR-146b-3p, leading to an increase in the expression of SORT1, which
  in turn enhances the activation of CD4+ T cells, ultimately promoting the development
  of AIH. The hsa_circ_0109623/hsa_miR_146b-3p/SORT1 axis can also regulate the STAT1/STAT4
  signaling pathway mediating the progression of AIH. Abbreviations: AIH, autoimmune
  hepatitis; SORT1, Sortilin 1'
