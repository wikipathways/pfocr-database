caption: RNA-sequencing analysis revealed the role of trimethylamine-N-oxide (TMAO)-mediated
  signaling in vascular dementia. Elevated TMAO levels following choline diet result
  in altered mRNA expression profile in the ischemic cortex of the left brain hemisphere.
  The predicted signaling pathways shown in the scattered plot include pathways of
  neurodegeneration-multiple diseases, IL-17 signaling, chemokine signaling, and PI3-Akt
  signaling. The expression changes of the signaling molecules (italics) in these
  processes ultimately lead to the development of vascular dementia.
