  in idiopathic fibrosis promoted by TGF-β1. After TGF-β1 activates Wnt/β-catenin,
  it degrades the complex formed by GSK-3β and β-catenin, axin and APC, then β-catenin
  is released. Additionally, TGF-β1 promotes the production of β-catenin by combining
  with Smad2/3, which ultimately leads to an increase in the production of CBP.
