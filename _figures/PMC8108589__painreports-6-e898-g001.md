caption: 'Proinflammatory pathways regulated by LRP-1: The interaction between LRP-1
  and ApoE represses the formation of IRAK1–TRAF6 complex and therefore the NFκB-mediated
  pathway. Shedding of LRP-1 releases the extracellular soluble LRP-1 (sLRP-1) and
  the intracellular domain (ICDLRP-1). ICDLRP-1 can translocate to the nucleus and
  arrest IRF-3, inhibiting the TRIF-mediated pathway. Likewise, cytoplasmic ICDLRP-1
  can arrest p-JNK, inhibit its translocation to the nucleus, and therefore suppress
  this MAPK pathway. Images from https://smart.servier.com/, under the Creative Common
