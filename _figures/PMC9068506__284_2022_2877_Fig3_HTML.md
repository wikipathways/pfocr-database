caption: Endosomal and cytosolic innate immune surveillance regulated by enteroviruses
  and bacteriophages. The viral DNA and RNA are signaled via TLRs or PRRs. The endosomal
  TLR signals are processed through MyD88 and TRIF. The cytosolic RIG-1 and MDA5 signals
  through MAVS to stimulate the expression of IFN I and IFN III, which attach through
  IFNAR 1&2 (IFN α, IFN β) and IL10R2 (IFNλ) receptors to induce antiviral response.
  The intracellular DNA is sensed via cGAS-STING pathway, producing 2′, 3′-cGAMP to
  activate STING stimulating the expression of IFNs through NFkB
