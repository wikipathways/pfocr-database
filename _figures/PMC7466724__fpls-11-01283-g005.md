  or sulfur, cysteine and methionine metabolism using KEGG database, respectively.

  genes, and up-regulated proteins, respectively. Abbreviations are as follows: AGXT2,

  MDH1, malate dehydrogenase; metE, 5-methyltetrahydropteroyltriglutamate-homocysteine

  isomerase; PPC, Phosphoenolpyruvate carboxylase; PCKA, phosphoenolpyruvate carboxykinase(ATP);
