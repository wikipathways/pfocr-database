caption: The transmembrane E3 ubiquitin ligases Rnf43/Znrf3 negatively regulate Wnt
  signalling by ubiquitinating the cytoplasmic tails of Fzds to promote their membrane
  clearance by endocytosis and degradation, ultimately downregulating Wnt signalling.
  When R‐spondins (Rspo1–4) are present, Rnf43/Znrf3 mediated degradation of Fzds
  is inhibited and Fzd/Lrp accumulation on the plasma membrane upregulates Wnt signalling.
  In an independent mechanism, Rnf43 present on the nuclear membrane interacts with
  and prevents LEF/TCF‐mediated transcription.
