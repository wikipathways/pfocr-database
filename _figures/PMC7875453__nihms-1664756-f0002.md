caption: 'BCR is activated by antigens. BCR’s adaptor Igα and Igβ contain ITAM. Engaging
  BCR by antigen recruits Src and Syk to phosphorylate Igα β ITAM. Phosphorylated
  ITAM recruits BCAP to activate the PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. Src also phosphorylates
  ITIM of CD22 and CD72, which recruits signaling suppressors SHP1 and SHIP1 to enforce
  a negative feedback regulation of BCR signaling. Signal transduction downstream
  of Syk can be classified into three major pathways: (1): BTK→BLNK→PLC-γ2→PKCβ→CBM
  complex→TRAF6/TRAF2→TAK1→NF-κB1; (2): Vav→Rac→Raf→JNK→c-Jun/AP-1; (3) BCAP→PI3K→AKT→Foxo1/Blimp-1/ID2
  (See details in text).'
