caption: Role of GDF15 in ionizing radiation (IR)-induced angiogenesis in glioblastoma.
  Radiation upregulates GDF15 expression in the endothelial cells (ECs). EC-derived
  GDF15 activates the p-MAPK1/SP1 pathway in glioblastoma cells. SP1 stimulates the
  promoter activity of VEGF. The upregulated VEGF levels induce angiogenesis in glioblastoma
  through p-KDR on ECs.
