caption: The noncanonical inflammasome pathway. The LPS of gram-negative bacteria
  activates caspase-11 (mouse) and caspase-4/5 (human), and the active caspase-11/4/5
  cleave GSDMD to produce GSDMD-NT to form pores in the cell membrane; NLRP3 is activated
  by k+ efflux, which activates a series of pyroptosis-related proteins such as ASC
  and caspase-1, then caspase-1 induce GSDMD-mediated pyroptosis; IL-1β and IL- 18
  are released from the GSDMD pores.
