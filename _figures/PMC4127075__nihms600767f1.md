  pathway. In the case of DNA damage, p53 induces the expression of KLF4, and KLF4
  can also be upregulated by HDAC inhibitors and CDX2. Overexpression of KLF4 leads
  to altered expression of a series of its downstream target genes which mediate the
  suppressor role of KLF4 in GI cancer tumorigenesis and proliferation, and the promoter
  role in cancer differentiation.
