caption: Autophagy mechanism. The autophagy pathway. During this process, the endoplasmic
  reticulum or other membranous cellular structures respond to stimuli by generating
  a double-membrane structure called a phagophore. On this phagophore, ATG16L1 forms
  a complex with an ATG5-ATG12 conjugate, which multimerizes and then lipidates LC3
  (LC3-II). Simultaneously, the phagophore elongates to envelop the cytoplasm or organelle
  to be degraded, forming an autophagosome, a unique double-membrane organelle. The
  outer membrane of the autophagosome then fuses with a lysosome to form an autolysosome,
  and the inner membrane degrades and absorbs its contents.
