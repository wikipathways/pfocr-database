caption: Proposed scheme showing how acupuncture inhibits 2VO-induced inflammatory
  response via the miR-93-mediated TLR4/MyD88/NF-κB signaling pathway. Acupuncture
  treatment alleviates cognitive dysfunction and decreases IL-6 and TNF-α expressions.
  These effects involve suppression of miR-93 expression and the inhibition of activation
  of the TLR4/MyD88/NF-κB signaling pathway. TLR4 antagonist TAK-242 mimics the effects
  of acupuncture, while TLR4 agonist LPS reverses the beneficial effects of acupuncture.
