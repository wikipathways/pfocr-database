caption: A) Transcriptional silencing of c-Myc by Smad complexes is a core event in
  TGFβ mediated growth suppression. The TGFβ switch to a promoter of cancer cell growth
  is based on NFAT mediated transcriptional induction of c-Myc. B) TGFβ induces expression
  and nuclear accumulation of NFAT, which binds to the TIE element of the proximal
  c-Myc promoter. Upon promoter binding, NFAT stimulates p300-dependent histone acetylation
  and creates a local chromatin structure permissive for the inducible recruitment
  of ELK-1, a protein required for maximal activation of c-Myc transcription.
