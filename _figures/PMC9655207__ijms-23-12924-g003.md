caption: Amyloid precursor protein (APP) processing. Two proteolytic pathways, amyloidogenic
  and non-amyloidogenic processing, exist for APP processing. The amyloidogenic pathway
  involves β and γ secretases and releases N-terminal soluble APPβ fragments, and
  Aβ peptides in the extracellular region. The non-amyloidogenic involves α and γ
  secretases and releases N-terminal APPα fragments, and P3 peptides in the extracellular
  region. Both proteolytic pathways release APP intracellular domain (AICD) fragments
  intracellularly.
