caption: Schematic representation of xanthophyll synthesis in red algae. DOXP-synthase
  (DXS), DOXP reductoisomerase (DXR), isopentenyl pyrophosphate (IPP), dimethylallyl
  pyrophosphate (DMAPP), geranylgeranyl pyrophosphate (GGPP), GGPP synthase (GGPPS),
  phytoene synthase (PSY), phytoene desaturase (PDS), z-carotene desaturase (z-CDS),
  lycopene β-cyclase (LCYB), lycopene ε-cyclase (LCYE), and zeaxanthin epoxidase (ZEP).
  The lightning symbol indicates that light has an effect over the carotenogenic gene
  expression of the enzyme. The upstream lycopene synthesis pathway is as described
  for plants and algae, modified from []. Downstream of lycopene corresponds to the
  synthesis pathways particularly of Pyropia yezoensis, although enzymes unique to
  Bangiales have not yet been described; modified from [].
