caption: Autophagy regulation in response to stress. (a) ER stress stimulates autophagy
  through the PERK-eIF2α pathway, the IRE1-JNK1 pathway and Ca2+ release. Activation
  of eIF2α by PERK may upregulate transcription of certain autophagy genes, such as
  ATG12. Phosphorylation and activation of eIF2α by two other eIF2α kinases, GCN2
  and PKR, upon amino acid starvation and viral infection, respectively, are also
  depicted. (b) Autophagy induction by mechanisms sensing hypoxia or oxidative stress.
  JNK1, DAPk, and BNIP3 induce autophagy by disrupting the Bcl-2-Beclin 1 interaction
  and activating Beclin 1.
