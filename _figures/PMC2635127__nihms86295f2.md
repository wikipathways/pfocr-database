  the formation of K63-linked poly-ubiquitin chains on TRAF6. Poly-ubiquitinated TRAF6
  recruits TAK1 to activate JNK/p38. Activated JNK/p38 act in conjunction with Smads
  to regulate apoptosis and EMT by controlling the activities of downstream transcription
  factors. JNK can also regulate R-Smad activity directly by phosphorylation.
