  elegans. Each pathway corresponds to one of the two main types of olfactory pathways
  found in invertebrates and vertebrates. AWA activates a TRPV channel and AWC activates
  a cyclic nucleotide-gated channel. The nuclear receptor odr-7 controls the identity
  of the AWA neurons, by inducing AWA-specific genes and repressing AWC-specific genes.
  The homeobox ceh-36 controls the identity of the AWC neurons.
