caption: αB-crystallin negatively regulates multiple steps in the intrinsic (mitochondrial)
  apoptotic pathway. In the intrinsic apoptotic pathway, genotoxic stress results
  in p53-dependent activation of the proapoptotic Bcl-2 family member Bax, which translocates
  to the mitochondria and induces mitochondrial outer membrane permeabilization, cytochrome
  c (abbreviated “c”) release, and Apaf-1-dependent activation of caspase-9 in the
  apoptosome. Caspase-9 in turn activates the executioner caspase-3. αB-crystallin
  binds to p53 to inhibit Bax activation and also binds to Bax to block its mitochondrial
  translocation. In addition, αB-crystallin binds to procaspase-3 and the p24 intermediate
  to suppress proteolytic activation of caspase-3.
