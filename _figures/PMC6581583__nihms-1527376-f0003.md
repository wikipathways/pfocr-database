caption: Inflammatory response due to the signaling cascade when HMGB-1 interacts
  with surface receptors TLR2/TLR4, TREM1, and RAGE. Upon interacting with TLR2/4,
  HMGB-1 activates the MyD88, or TRIF only for TLR4 and results in the upregulation
  of NF-κB, c-fos, and AP-1, which are inflammatory cytokines. When HMGB-1 interacts
  with TREM1, the DAP12 or ITAM pathway is triggered and results in the activation
  of inflammatory cytokines, c-fos, ELK1, and NF-κB, and it results in the inhibition
  of apoptosis. Finally, when HMGB-1 interacts with RAGE the m-Di9–1 pathway is activated.
  This results in NF-κB and AP-1 upregulation and enhancing the inflammatory response.
  Ultimately, the synergistic interactions of HMGB-1 and these surface receptors results
  in the increase of the inflammatory response and causes ECM disorganization.
