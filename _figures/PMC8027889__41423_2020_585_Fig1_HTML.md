caption: 'AHR and its signaling pathway. A Schematic representation of AHR protein
  domains. B The AHR signaling pathway. The inactive form of AHR is localized in the
  cytosol in a complex composed of HSP90, AIP, p23, and c-SRC. AHR agonists induce
  conformational changes in AHR that result in its translocation to the nucleus. In
  the nucleus, AHR interacts with ARNT, and the heterodimer is responsible for the
  transcription of XRE-containing genes. Notes: (AHR) aryl hydrocarbon receptor, (N)
