caption: Proposed model of the protective effect of BaSC06 in DQ-induced oxidative
  stress and apoptosis. (1) BaSC06 inhibited the AKT-FOXO signaling pathway by inhibiting
  the expression of p-AKT, p-FOXO and increasing the expression of SIRT1, thereby
  increasing the gene expression of the ATG5-ATG12 complex to induce autophagy. (2)
  BaSC06 attenuate apoptosis by modulating the activities of antioxidant enzymes,
  the expression of the apoptotic proteins Bcl2, Bax, Caspase 3 and Caspase 8 to alleviate
  oxidative stress.
