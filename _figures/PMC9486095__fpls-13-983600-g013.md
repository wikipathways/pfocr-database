  by mediating ABA signaling pathway. Simulating drought stress with 200 g⋅L–1 PEG
  6000 induced the expression of IgWRKY50 and IgWRKY32 in Iris germanica. IgWRKY50

  the expression of related genes in ABA-dependent and –independent pathways, increasing
  the activity of antioxidant enzymes, and reducing oxidative damage.
