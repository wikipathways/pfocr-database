caption: Schematic diagram summarizing the regulatory role of the Id1/IGF2/VEGF/VEGFR
  pathway in esophageal squamous cell carcinoma (ESCC) progression. In the tumor microenvironment,
  IGF2 secreted by Id1-expressing cancer cells activates adjacent fibroblasts to become
  αSMA+ myofibroblasts, and to reduce expression of p53 causing reduction in miR-29c
  expression and subsequent de-repression of VEGF. In the tumor macro environment,
  VEGF secreted from the activated fibroblasts travels through the blood to the bone
  marrow instigating VEGFR+ bone marrow cells to migrate to the lung where CXCL5 expression
  attracts the invading cancer cells.
