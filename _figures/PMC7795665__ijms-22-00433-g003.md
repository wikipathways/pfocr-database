caption: PPARγ involved in trophoblast differentiation. The activation of PPARγ increased
  the expression of chorionic gonadotropin beta-subunit (hCGβ) and chorion-specific
  transcription factor (GCM-1), which promoted cytotrophoblast (CTB) differentiation
  into the STB. miR-1246 promoted STB differentiation through inhibiting the WNT/β-catenin
  signaling pathway and increasing the expression of the crucial transcription factor
  PPARγ. The loss of the ERK/MAPK cascade caused the reduced expression of PPARγ,
  affected the expression of GCM1, and inhibited the process of CTB differentiation
  into the STB.
