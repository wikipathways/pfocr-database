caption: Ethanol impairs Akt signaling by disrupting the interaction of Akt with the
  plasma membrane, disturbing the interdomain conformational change of Akt, which
  normally exposes Thr308 and Ser473 for subsequent phosphorylation and activation.
  Deactivation of Akt results in activation of downstream kinase GSK3β and subsequent
  increased β-catenin degradation. SC-79 blocks the interaction of Akt with the plasma
  membrane while inducing a conformational change in cytoplasmic Akt in the Akt/GSK3β/β-catenin
  signaling pathway. PIP3, phosphatidylinositol 3,4,5-trisphosphate; PH, pleckstrin
  homology; mTOR, mammalian target of rapamycin; GSK-3β, glycogen synthesis kinase-3β.
