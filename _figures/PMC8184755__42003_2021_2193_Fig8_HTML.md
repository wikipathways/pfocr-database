caption: REG3B/REG3A binds to its receptor, EXTL3 receptor on the acinar cell membrane,
  and promotes ADM by activating the downstream RAS-RAF-MEK-ERK signaling pathway,
  in the absence of oncogenic Kras mutation. Targeting REG3B/REG3A, neutralizing its
  receptor EXTL3, or inhibiting downstream signaling molecules, such as B-RAF (LY3009120)
  or MEK1/2 (Trametinib), could interrupt the ADM process and potentially prevent
  early PDAC carcinogenesis.
