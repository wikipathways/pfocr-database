  by activating p53 in MCF-7 cells. A major ingredient of coffee, caffeine reduces

  Caffeine and some ingredients cooperatively inhibit the activation of the MEK-ERK
  pathway and Akt, which are critical for the proliferation of breast cancer cells.

  activating p53 in MCF-7 cells.
