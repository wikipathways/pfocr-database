  at the Sn-2 position. PLC hydrolyzes the glycerol-oriented phosphodiester-bond,
  and PLD hydrolyzes the alcohol-oriented phosphodiester-bond. PLB and Lyso-PLAs can
  hydrolyze at both the Sn-1 and Sn-2 positions showing both PLA1 and PLA2 activity.
