  (MAPK) in host cell death. Activation of p38 MAPK in response to environmental stress
  triggers host cell death either by apoptosis or by necrosis. p38 MAPK initiates
  caspase activation via the extrinsic and intrinsic pathway to execute apoptosis.

  permeability transition pore (mPTP). Bcl-2 indicates B-cell lymphoma 2; ROS, reactive
  oxygen species.
