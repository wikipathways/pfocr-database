caption: Schematic showing the mechanism(s) of activation of the NLRP3 inflammasome
  and NF-κB signaling pathways by vMyxM013-KO virus infection in human monocytic cell
  line THP-1. Infection of differentiated THP-1 cells by vMyxM013-KO virus activates
  the NLRP3 inflammasome by the release of ROS and cathepsin B from lysosomal destabilization.
  vMyxM013-KO virus activates the NF-κB pathway for the synthesis of cytokines via
  activation of the MEK-ERK1/2 pathway and sensing by the TLRs.
