  compounds consist of naringenin chalcone (11), naringenin (7), dihydrokaempferol
  (12), dihydroquercetin (13), dihydromyricetin (14), leucopelargonidin (15), leucocyanidin
  (16), and leucodelphinidin (17). Catalysts are acetyl-CoA carboxylase (ACCase),
  phenylalanine ammonia-lyase (PAL), cinnamate 4-hydroxylase (C4H), 4-coumarate:CoA
