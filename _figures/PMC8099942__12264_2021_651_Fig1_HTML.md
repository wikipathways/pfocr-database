caption: Transmitted α-synuclein (especially aggregated species) reaches adjacent
  microglia. Toll-like receptor (TLR) 2 mediates the intracellular signaling network,
  together with the NLRP3 inflammasome pathway, activating inflammatory responses
  in microglia. The interaction of α-synuclein and TLR4 induces transcriptional up-regulation
  of p62 via the NF-κB signaling pathway, directing it for degradation by lysosomes.
  The internalized α-synuclein can proceed to endosomes, multiple vesicle bodies,
  and lysosomes for degradation; alternatively, exosomes are released to spread the
  pathology.
