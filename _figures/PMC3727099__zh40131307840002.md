caption: 'Signaling diagram for AC5 mediating cardiac stress and longevity. Left:
  cardiac dysfunction: signaling diagram for AC5 regulation of MnSOD transcriptionally
  through the sirtuin 1/forkhead box O3a (SIRT1/FoxO3a) pathway is shown. Imbalance
  between reactive oxygen species production and the intracellular antioxidant system
  results in the intolerance of AC5-Tg to stress (). Right: longevity: disruption
  of AC5 activates the Raf/MEK/ERK signaling pathway. The activation of ERK activates
  antioxidative stress and cell survival mechanism, which leads to longevity in AC5-KO
  mice (). Arrows indicate the direction of signaling. Figures used are modified with
  permission from Lai et al. () and Yan et al. ().'
