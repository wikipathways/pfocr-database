  microglia-induced neuroinflammation. LPS or stress activates TLR4 and induces microglia

  provides a self-limiting mechanism by which microglia avoid inappropriate long-term

  and nuclear exclusion of FoxO1, and inhibit microglia TLR4 expression and TLR4-mediated
  inflammatory cytokine release
