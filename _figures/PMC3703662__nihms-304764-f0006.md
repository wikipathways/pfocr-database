caption: Proposed model for NV-128-induced casapase-independent cell death in OCSCs.
  By targeting the mitochondria, NV-128 activates two independent cell death pathways.
  Degradation of Cox-IV leads to ATP loss and increase mitochondrial ROS. ATP loss
  leads to inhibition of mTOR pathway and autophagic cell death. ROS activates the
  ERK/Bax axis leading to loss of mitochondrial membrane potential and EndoG-dependent
  DNA fragmentation.
