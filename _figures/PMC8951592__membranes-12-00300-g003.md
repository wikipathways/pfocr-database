caption: Crosstalk between E-cadherin/β-catenin and NF-κB signaling pathways under
  Leptospira LRR20 treatment. rLRR20 protein interacts with E-cadherin through several
  vital residues and consequently activates β-catenin. The nuclear translocation of
  activated β-catenin promotes the expression of its target genes, including MMP7.
  Subsequently, MMP7 is secreted to the extracellular region. The expression and secretion
  of MMP7 promote the degradation of E-cadherin on the cell surface and downregulate
  the cell surface levels of E-cadherin. Meanwhile, the degradation of E-cadherin
  on the cell surface induces the activation of the NF-κB:p65 signal transduction
  pathway, which subsequently promotes the expression of downstream target gene NGAL.
