caption: p58IPK and UPR-associated inflammatory signaling pathways. During ER stress,
  activated IRE1 forms a complex with TRAF2 and activates IKK. Activated IKK leads
  to IκB degradation and subsequent activation of NF-κB, which transcribes pro-inflammatory
  genes. Furthermore, the IRE1-TRAF2 complex activates JNK and consequently activates
  other pro-inflammatory transcription factors. Activated PERK promotes NF-κB activation
  via translational attenuation of IκB. p58IPK attenuates the PERK/CHOP pathway through
  inhibition of PERK activity.
