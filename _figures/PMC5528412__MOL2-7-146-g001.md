  signaling are depicted in red; proteins that inhibit in green. Upon ligand binding
  to the receptor tyrosine kinase (RTK), PI3K is activated, leading to formation of
  PI(3,4,5)3. AKT is phosphorylated at threonine 308 (TP) by PDK1, and on serine 473
  (SP) by mTORC2. Note that most kinases are activated by phosphorylation, whereas
  4EBP1 is inactivated by phosphorylation.
