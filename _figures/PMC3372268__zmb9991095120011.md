caption: Model depicting miR-155-mediated modulation of macrophage apoptosis. M. bovis
  BCG induces the upregulation of miR-155 by triggering the TLR2/PI3K pathway. PKCδ
  and RAS-RAF1-MAPKs play vital roles in M. bovis BCG-induced miR-155 expression through
  transcriptional factors NF-κB and c-ETS. Induced miR-155 targets PKI-α, a negative
  regulator of PKA signaling, resulting in the robust activation of the PKA signaling
  pathway. Overall, miR-155 induces the upregulation of proapoptotic genes by modulating
  PKA signaling, resulting in macrophage apoptosis.
