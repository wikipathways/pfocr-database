caption: 'The canonical tetrahydrofolate biosynthetic pathway. Abbreviated enzyme
  names are in bold, with the homologs in C. trachomatis serovar D listed in red.
  A question mark is present if the Chlamydia homolog is not known. (A) GTP to tetrahydrofolate.
  FolE, GTP cyclohydrolase I; FolQ, dihydroneopterin triphosphate pyrophosphohydrolase;
  Ptase, nonspecific phosphohydrolase; FolB, dihydroneopterin aldolase; FolK, 6-hydroxymethyl-7,8-dihydropterin
  pyrophosphokinase; FolP, dihydropteroate synthase; FolC, dihydrofolate synthetase;
  FolA, dihydrofolate reductase. Full metabolite names: 7,8-dihydroneopterin triphosphate
  (H2NTP), 7,8-dihydroneopterin monophosphate (H2NMP), 7,8-dihydroneopterin (H2N),
  6-hydroxymethyl-7,8-dihydroneopterin (HMH2N), 6-hydroxymethyl-7,8-dihydroneopterin
  diphosphate (HMH2NDP), 7,8-dihydropteroate (H2Pt), 7,8-dihydrofolate (DHF), tetrahydrofolate
  (THF). (B) PABA biosynthesis. PabA, aminodeoxychorismate synthase component II;
  PabB, aminodeoxychorismate component I; PabC, aminodeoxychorismate lyase.'
