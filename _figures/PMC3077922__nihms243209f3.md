caption: Gene expression changes in response to zebularine therapy in vivo. (A) Hierarchical
  cluster analysis of liver tumors derived from the zebularine sensitive Huh7luc+-xenografts
  identifies a list of 484 differentially expressed genes by Bootstrap-t (P≤.01, 5,000
  repetitions). Control tumors, raloxifene alone (n=6), and the treatment group zebularine
  and raloxifene (n=7). (B, C) Ingenuity Pathway Analysis shows zebularine and raloxifene
  treatment caused a preferential deregulation of genes controlled by the oncogene
  MYC (B) and up-regulation of initiator caspases (e.g. CASP2 and CASP9) (C).
