caption: Epigenetic machinery downstream to the Wnt signaling pathway. (A) β-catenin
  can form complexes with different epigenetic modulators and activate gene expression
  upon Wnt activation. CBP is a well-known interaction partner of β-catenin which
  helps in opening up of the chromatin by catalyzing H3K9 acetylation. (B) Histone
  demethylase KDM4 is recruited via BRG1, an interactor of β-catenin which stimulates
  the expression of the ADAM group of genes. (C) β-catenin can also form an elaborate
  complex with the chromatin remodelers composed of the MLL complex, Pygo and Bcl-9
  leading to H3K4me3 modification. This activity requires prior ubiquitination of
  H2BK120.
