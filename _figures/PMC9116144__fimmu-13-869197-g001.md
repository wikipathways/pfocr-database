caption: Regulation of T cell activation by metabolic pathways. T cell receptor (TCR)
  activation activates AMPK via 2 pathways (black arrows). Via an LKB1-dependent pathway
  and via and LKB1-independent pathway, through calcineurin and CAMKK. Both AMPK and
  uptake of free fatty acids (FFA) activate mitochondrial respiration, which has been
  shown to be necessary for T cell activation. Activation of glycolysis is also essential
  for T cell activation (blue arrows). TCR activation activates c-Myc which turns
  on the expression of glycolytic metabolic genes. CD28 activation also activates
  c-Myc via the PI3K-AKT-mTORC1 pathway. Image created with BioRender.com
