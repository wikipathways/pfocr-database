  There are 10 differentially expressed component boxes with an impact factor of 10.3.
  The upregulated genes include TGFβ (control: 0.57 ± 0.18, mean ± s.d.; IGF2: 2.3
  ± 0.94), TGFβ RI (control: 0.85 ± 0.23; IGF2: 7.4 ± 12), Noggin (control: 0.47 ±
  0.28; IGF2: 1.9 ± 0.53), THBS1 (control: 0.76 ± 0.25; IGF2: 2.7 ± 1.6), Smurf1 (control:
  0.73 ± 0.14; IGF2: 2.0 ± 0.91), Smurf2 (control: 0.75 ± 0.25; IGF2: 1.8 ± 0.79)
  and Activin RI (control: 0.92 ± 0.12; IGF2: 7.3 ± 12) while the downregulated genes
  are Smad1 (control: 1.9 ± 0.62; IGF2: 0.53 ± 0.37), Id (control: 1.4 ± 0.41; IGF2:
  0.65 ± 0.37) and Pitx2 (control: 1.6 ± 0.70; IGF2: 0.66 ± 0.27).'
