  lung cancer cell proliferation. The AKT pathway and STAT3/p-STAT3 signaling pathways

  circ-CMPK1, JPX, circPVT1, NEAT1, LINC00667, circ-100146, and LCAT1 act as ceRNAs
  to regulate cell proliferation by targeting cell cycle regulators.
