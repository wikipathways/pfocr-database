  (E2) are preferentially metabolized to form 4‐OHE 1 and 4‐OHE 2, respectively. COMT
  detoxifies 4‐OHE 1/2 to 4‐OCH 3E1/2. If the activity or expression of COMT is low,
  catechol estrogens are converted to estrogenic quinone metabolites, and then to
  DNA adducts, which will result in DNA mutation.
