caption: TLR signaling upon intrinsic cellular defenses. Viral ligands are recognized
  by each TLRs, TLR2 and 4 which are localized on the cellular membranes, TLR3, TLR7,
  and TLR8, TLR9 are expressed on the endosomal membrane. Only TLR3 uses MyD88 independent
  signaling pathway, while other TLRs use MyD88 dependent pathway. Through these pathways,
  transcription factors (IRF-3, 5, 6 and NF-κB) are activated. Because they contain
  nuclear localization signal (NLS), they are able to enter the nucleus. Then, these
  transcription activators are thought to play an important role in the regulation
  of pro-inflammatory cytokines and type I IFNs. TLR, Toll-like receptor; IRF, interferon
  regulatory factor; IFN, interferon; dsRNA, double-strand RNA; ssRNA, single-stranded
  RNA.
