caption: 'Simplified central carbon and amino acid metabolic network. Proteins from
  pathways involved in glycolysis, pentose phosphate pathway, TCA cycle and amino
  acid metabolism identified as differentially abundant. Enzymes found to be significantly
  higher abundant (R ≥ 1.42, NW8 v 9 p ≤ 0.0155; NW8 v 10 p ≤ 0.0165) are labeled
  in red; higher abundant proteins (1.25 < R < 1.42, NW8 v 9 p ≤ 0.0155; NW8 v 10
  p ≤ 0.0165 < p < 0.05) are labeled in pink; significantly lower abundant proteins
  (R ≤ 0.704, NW8 v 9 p ≤ 0.0155; NW8 v 10 p ≤ 0.0165) are labeled in blue, and the
  lower abundant proteins (0.704 < R < 0.8, NW8 v 9 p ≤ 0.0155; NW8 v 10 p ≤ 0.0165
  < p < 0.05) are labeled in cyan. Proteins unchanged are labeled in gray (=). Left
  arrows/ = : low maltase expression strain NW9 versus parental strain NW8 (NW8 v
  9); right arrows/ = : high maltase expression strain NW10 versus parental strain
  NW8 (NW8 v 10). n.d. denotes not identified proteins.'
