caption: Role of GSDMD in non-canonical inflammasome activation. The canonical pathway
  of pyroptosis. When LPS enters cells, it directly participates in and activates
  human caspase-4 and caspase-5 and mouse caspase-11. Caspase-11 cleaves GSDMD, forming
  GSDMD-C and GSDMD-N. GSDMD-N cluster and bind to the plasma membrane. Formation
  of GSDMD pore, the formation of GSDMD pore activates inflammasome to bind to caspase-1,
  activated caspase-1 activates IL-18 and IL-1β, and cytokines IL-18 and IL-1β are
  released extracellularly through GSDMD pore.
