caption: 'Signaling cascades in axon specification. In one immature neurite (the future
  axon), the extracellular matrix (ECM) activates PI3-kinase through interaction with
  adhesion molecules or receptors, thereby producing PIP3. Accumulated PIP3 drives
  two major signaling cascades: the Akt/GSK-3β/CRMP-2 pathway and the positive feedback
  loop composed of Cdc42, the Par complex, and Rac1. These signaling cascades regulate
  cytoskeletons, endocytosis, protein trafficking, and transcriptions to promote neurite
  elongation and to determine axon or dendrite fate.'
