  by Anza-Borrego isolates of Bradyrhizobium and Methylocaldum, respectively. The
  required genes for de novo cobalamin synthesis are present in the Bradyrhizobium
  genome. The absence of the initial required genes for de novo synthesis and the
  presence of two paralogs of the cobalamin outer membrane cobalamin receptor and
  transporter gene, btuB, indicates that Methylocaldum relies on a salvaging pathway
  of cobalamin
