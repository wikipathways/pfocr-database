caption: A schematic of Se-Duox expression under the control of PKA signalling pathway.

  AC can increase the amount of cAMP in the cytosol which induces PKA. Induced PKA
  causes CREB phosphorylation and subsequent translocation to the nucleus. CREB in
  the nucleus acts as a transcription factor and upregulates Se-Duox expression.
