caption: Protein-protein interaction network (STRING database) generated by combining
  all biomarkers of different omic types, reviewed earlier in the report. The network
  is done using Cytoscape visualization. Four dominant sub-networks associated with
  preeclampsia are shown (blue, green, yellow, purple), each enriched with biological
  functions. Antioxidant pathway involving PRDX2, PRDX3, HSPE1, GCLM and SOD1 (green).
  Proteasome and cytokine signalling pathway involving PSMA1, PSMD8, NFKB1, NFKB2
  (blue). Protein regulation and transport pathways involving C1s, TFRC, CXCL1, TTR,
  TIMP3 (yellow). Angiogenesis signalling pathway involving FLT1, PGF, FGF, ENG (purple).
  The other red targets are still associated with preeclampsia but not within the
  four dominant sub-networks.
