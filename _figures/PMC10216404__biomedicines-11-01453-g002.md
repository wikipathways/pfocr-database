  while suppressing PPARγ and Sox9 to inhibit adipocyte and chondrocyte differentiation,
  respectively. Meanwhile, the transcriptional activity of SIRT1-FoxO increases SOD2

  which favour osteoclastogenesis. Abbreviation: SIRT1: silent information regulator
