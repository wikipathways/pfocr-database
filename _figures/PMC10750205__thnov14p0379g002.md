  endosialin and endothelial cell expressed CLEC14A simultaneously bind with MMRN2
  at the interface between the endothelium and pericytes to promote tumor angiogenesis.

  Endosialin could activate Wnt/β-catenin signaling and upregulate two angiogenic
  factors, OPN and SERPINE1, in pericytes to promote tumor angiogenesis.
