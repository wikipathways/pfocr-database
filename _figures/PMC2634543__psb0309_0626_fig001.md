  F3′H, flavonoid 3′-hydroxylase; F3′5′H, flavonoid 3′5′-hydroxylase; DFR, dihydroflavonol

  off from the anthocyanin pathway (FLS, flavonol synthase; LAR, leucoanthocyanidin
  reductase; ANR, anthocyanidin reductase).
