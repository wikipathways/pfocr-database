  protein in macrophages. Macrophages priming by TNF and/or viral PAMPs such as viral

  of the NLRP3 inflammasome. Core triggered NLRP3 activation is induced by HCV-core-mediated
  calcium mobilization through phospholipase C-dependent pathways.
