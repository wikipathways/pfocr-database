caption: Coexisting signaling pathways in the HL-1 cell plasma membrane. (A) Schematic
  of the M2R-GIRK channel signaling pathway in HL-1 cell membranes. (B) M2R, A1R,
  and β1AR pathways in HL-1 cell membranes. Both M2R and A1R activate GIRK by releasing
  free Gβγ and thus hyperpolarize the membrane, which slows heart rate. Gαs(GTP) generated
  by β1AR activates AC, which synthesizes cAMP. cAMP facilitates HCN channel opening,
  which depolarizes the membrane and increases heart rate
