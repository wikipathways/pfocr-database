caption: 'Results show the expression of genes involved in: (A) DNA damage checkpoints;
  (B) DSB repair and (C) SSB repair, analyzed during secondary CD8 responses generated
  in the absence and presence of CD4 cells. Analysis was performed using mouse DNA
  damage signaling pathway and DNA repair RT2 profiler PCR arrays (Qiagen). CD8 T
  cells were pooled from 4–6 mice for each experiment in absence of CD4s and 3 mice
  in presence of CD4s. Results were normalized as described in , expressed as fold
  change relative to memory cells. They represent the average of 4 to 5 independent
  experiments. Responses without CD4s are shown in red; with CD4s in green. Up and
  down arrows indicate up and down regulation, respectively; rectangles indicate no
  significant changes in gene expression. Asterisks indicate statistical significance:
  *, p < 0,05; **, p ≤ 0,01; ***, p ≤ 0,001.'
