caption: Feedback signaling following mTORC1 inhibition. (A) The baseline status of
  the PI3K signaling cascade, indicating negative feedback from p70S6K to IRS1. (B)
  Inhibition of mTORC1 blocks the negative feedback loop, activating IRS1, and leading
  to PI3K and AKT activation. (C) Paradoxical activation of PI3K and AKT in the setting
  of mTORC1 inhibition can be overcome by dual PI3K/mTOR inhibitors, which also inhibit
  PI3K, or dual mTORC1/2 inhibitors, which block mTORC2-mediated AKT activation.
