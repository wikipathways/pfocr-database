caption: HTT is cleared from the brain to the CSF by active mechanisms. A, HTT is
  secreted from neurons in the CNS via a late endosomal/lysosomal secretory pathway.
  Pharmacological inhibition of nSMase using small-molecule inhibitors, GW4869 in
  vitro and PDDC in vitro or in vivo, can reduce secretion of HTT. B, Top, Once in
  the extracellular space, HTT is cleared from the parenchyma by convective bulk CSF
  flow facilitated by AQP4 water channels localized at astrocyte end-feet. Bottom,
  Under neurodegenerative conditions, HTT is released from dying neurons into the
  extracellular space and is cleared by the glymphatic system into the CSF and blood.
  Medium spiny neuron (MSN).
