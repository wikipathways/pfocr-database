  β-catenin levels are kept at a minimum through the action of the destruction complex
  composed of APC, Axin, GSK3 β, and CKI. In the nucleus, Tcf factors associate with
  transcriptional repressors, such as Groucho to block target gene activation. b In
  the presence of Wnt stimulation, the destruction complex is destabilized, and β-catenin
  accumulates in the nucleus to activate the transcription of the Tcf target genes
