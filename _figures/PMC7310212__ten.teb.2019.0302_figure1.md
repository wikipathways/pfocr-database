caption: Crosstalk between the IGF-1R/mTOR and Hippo pathways via the mechanical microenvironment.
  The canonical IGF-1R/mTOR pathway (left; yellow and orange proteins; pink backdrop)
  shares substantial interaction with various mechanosensitive proteins (right; pink,
  orange, and green proteins; blue backdrop). Mechanical signals are generated from
  integrin binding and from the stress on the actin cytoskeleton (pink proteins).
  Several interactors are involved in both signal cascades (orange proteins). Nuclear
  YAP/TAZ promotes signaling through mTOR by inhibiting PTEN through microRNA (red
  proteins and signal molecules).
