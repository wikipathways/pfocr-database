caption: Mechanism of pinocembrin induction of apoptosis in cancer cells. In the intrinsic
  pathway, the expression of the pro-apoptotic proteins B-cell lymphoma-2 (Bcl-2)
  associated X protein, Bcl-2 homologous antagonist/killer, and Bcl-2-like protein
  11 increases and that of the anti-apoptotic proteins Bcl-2 and Bcl-extra-large decreases.
  Cytochrome c translocates from the mitochondria to the cytosol, leading to apoptosis.
  In the extrinsic pathway, pinocembrin leads to apoptosis via the Fas-associated
  protein death domain/caspase-8/caspase-3 signaling pathway.
