caption: Metabolic scheme of targeted oxylipins. Oxylipin synthesis via different
  biosynthetic pathways, COX, 5-LOX, 12/15-LOX, and CYP from their corresponding precursor
  fatty acids, arachidonic acid, eicosapentaenoic acid, dihomo-γ-linolenic acid, and
  linoleic acid (). All shown metabolites were targeted except for 5-HPETE (5-hydroperoxy-eicosatetraenoic
  acid), LTA4 (Leukotriene A4), and PGH2 (prostaglandin H2) and classified by biosynthetic
  pathway. Nondetectable targeted oxylipins (17-HDoDE, Resolvin D1, and Resolvin D2)
  are not shown on the scheme. A list of full metabolite names is provided in Table S4.
