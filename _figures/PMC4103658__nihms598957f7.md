caption: GATA6 potentiates the upregulation of FGFR2, thereby cell-autonomously stimulating
  the FGF/ERK pathway, activated by FGF4. Increased ERK signaling leads to repression
  of NANOG, which in turn releases GATA6 repression. This feedback loop reinforces
  a PrE fate. In EPI cells, very low levels of GATA6 maintain but do not up-regulate
  FGFR2. Baseline levels of the receptor allows for marginal induction of FGF/ERK
  signaling, which in turn moderate NANOG levels.
