caption: Cytokines regulate KCa3.1 expression and activity. TGF-β1 inhibits catalase,
  thereby synthesizing hydrogen peroxide to activate KCa3.1. IL-4 and IL-1β upregulates
  AP-1 through JAK3/RAS/MEK/ERK and NF-κB signaling pathway, resulting in the initiation
  of KCa3.1 transcription. AP-1 can be upregulated by activated CaMKIV/CREB and p38
  MAPK pathway, either. KCa3.1 activity is controlled by elevated cAMP in response
  to agents. (Created with BioRender.com).
