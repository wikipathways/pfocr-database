caption: The many functions of Ns1 and Ns2. (A) Ns2 and Ns1-Ns2 complex home to the
  mitochondria where the majority of these proteins reside. The Ns formed degradosome
  also traffics to the mitochondria while Ns1 homes to the nucleus. (B) Ns1 inhibits
  viral replication. (C) Ns1, Ns2, and degradosome interactions with the RIG-I and
  TLR pathway showing which proteins and at what steps they inhibit IFN induction
  and signaling. (D) Ns1 and Ns2 upregulate anti-apoptotic proteins and prevent apoptosis.
  (E) Ns1 suppresses Trm and Th17 differentiation while promoting Th2 cell differentiation
  and the percentage of T cells expressing Il-4. Both Ns1 and Ns2 inhibit DC maturation.
