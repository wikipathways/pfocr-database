caption: Upstream and downstream targets of the PI3K/Akt signaling pathway. The activation
  of the PI3K/Akt signaling pathway is initiated by insulin binding to its receptor.
  Upon activation, insulin receptor substrates (IRS) stimulate PI3K, leading to the
  conversion of PIP2 to PIP3. PIP3 recruits and activates PDK1 and mTORC2, which phosphorylate
  Akt at T308 and S473, respectively. Activated Akt regulates multiple downstream
  targets, including AS160, GSK3, FOXO, and mTORC1
