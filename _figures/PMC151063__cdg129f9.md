  induction and activation of DDR1 activates the Ras/raf/MAPK cascade and AKT pathway.
  DDR1 activation of the Ras/raf/MAPK cascade predicts a positive feedback loop in
  which p53 induction of DDR1 activates the Ras/raf/MAPK cascade, causing further
  accumulation of p53, p21, p19ARF and Mdm2. These signaling effects and other unknown
  downstream effectors promote cell survival in stressed environments.
