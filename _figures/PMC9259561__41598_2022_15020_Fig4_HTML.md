caption: Identification of key PD-L1 related genes. (A) Protein–protein interactions
  (PPI) displayed the top 20 important proteins of differentially expressed genes
  using MCC algorithm. The color darkness indicates the importance of the protein
  in the network. (B) A key sub-network consisting of 21 nodes was constructed by
  the plug-in MCODE in Cytoscape. (C) The Venn diagram showed eight PD-L1 related
  genes by overlapping the WGCNA module gene set (117 genes), the MCC algorithm gene
  set (20 genes) and the MCODE sub-network gene set (21 genes). (D) Correlation analysis
  of PD-L1 and enriched genes in PI3K-AKT signaling pathway and ECM-receptor interaction.
  Blue represents positive correlation and red represents negative correlation. (E)
  Overall recapitulation of DEGs enriched in the ECM/PI3K-AKT signaling pathway.
