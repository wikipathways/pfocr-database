  genes are repressed (tumor suppressors). Abbreviations: MDR1, multi-drug resistance
  1; FasL, Fas ligand; IGF-1R, insulin-like growth factor 1 receptor; EGFR, epidermal

  transcription factor 2; DTL, denticleless protein homolog). Solid gray arrows represent
  activated signaling pathway; solid blunt lines represent inhibition of signals;
  dotted gray lines represent indirect signaling pathways.'
