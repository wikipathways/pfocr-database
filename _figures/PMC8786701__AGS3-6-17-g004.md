caption: Key pathways in colorectal carcinogenesis. Epithelial growth factor (EGF)
  binds to the epithelial growth factor receptor (EGFR), which activates the Jak/STAT,
  Ras/Raf/Mek/Erk, and PI3K/Akt/mTOR pathways. Antibodies targeting EGFR include cetuximab
  and panitumumab. Vascular endothelial growth factor (VEGF) binds to the vascular
  endothelial growth factor receptor (VEGFR) which activates the PI3K/Akt/mTOR pathway.
  Bevacizumab is an antibody that targets VEGF itself, and ramucirumab targets the
  receptor VEGFR‐2
