caption: HBSS starvation induces HIF-1α protein expression through enhancement of
  IRES activity which is positively regulated by Beclin 1-independent macroautophagy.
  The Beclin 1-independent macroautophagy is controlled by ROS/AMPK and JNK signaling
  pathways. Furthermore, p38 which is activated by ROS-mediated AMPK also positively
  regulates HBSS-induced HIF-1α IRES activity but in a macroautophagy-independent
  manner. In addition, hypoxia not only stabilizes HIF-1α through inhibition of proteasome
  degradation pathway, but also induces IRES activity through an ATG5-independent
  macroautophagy pathway.
