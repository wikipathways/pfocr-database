caption: Proposed schematic of intracellular immune response to murine norovirus (MNV)
  infection. MNV may infect macrophages or dendritic cells in the gut. Infected cells
  sense MNV via MDA-5 and produce IFNs through this or the STAT-1 pathway. Type I
  IFNs inhibit MNV uncoating via ISG15 and may inhibit replication complex formation
  via an Atg complex. Type II IFNs block MNV translation via RNA-activated protein
  kinase (PKR). VF1-related immune suppression by MNV is not included in this schematic.
