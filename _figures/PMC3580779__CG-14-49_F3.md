caption: Phosphorylation signaling pathway of alternative splicing regulated by mechanical
  stress. In response to mechanical stimulation, Ca2+ influx is induced by Ca2+ channel.
  The pathways of CaMKII-TAK1-SAPKs and NF-κB-IL-6 and PKC are activated by Ca2+ influx,
  then regulate the alternative splicing of pre-mRNA. Integrins which are sensitive
  to mechanical stimulation, transmit mechanical signals into cells by ECM and activate
  FAK/ERK/MAPK path way which regulate alternative splicing directly or under help
  of RhoA/Rho-ROCK signaling. PI3K is another possible signaling that modulates alternative
  splicing in cells exposed to mechanical stress.
