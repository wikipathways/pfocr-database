  HNSCC cells, galanin stimulates ERK1/2 activation and suppresses cell proliferation.
  Galanin stimulation increases expression of the cyclin-dependent kinase inhibitors,
  p27 and p57, and it also reduces cyclin D1 expression. These signaling pathways
  are sensitive to pertussis toxin (PTX). GALR1 does not appear to be associated with
  apoptosis.
