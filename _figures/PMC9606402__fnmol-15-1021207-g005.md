caption: Roles of growth arrest and DNA damage-inducible beta (Gadd45b) in cerebral
  ischemic disease. (A) Gadd45b-I specifically binds to MKK-7 and inhibits JNK-mediated
  neural death. (B) Gadd45b promotes neurogenesis and synaptic remodeling by altering
  the demethylation of BDNF. (C) Gadd45b decreases the release of Bcl-2 and Bax to
  inhibit apoptosis. (D) Gadd45b inhibits cellular autophagy by stimulating PI3K/P53
  signaling pathway and suppressing the P38 signaling pathway. (E) Gadd45b may be
  associated with microglia polarization. (F) Gadd45b promotes axonal regeneration
  through the camp/PKA/CREB signaling pathway (Figure was created with BioRender.com).
