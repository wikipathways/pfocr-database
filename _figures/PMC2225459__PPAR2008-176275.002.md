caption: 'Schema illustrating the effects of (a) metformin- or AICAR-induced AMPK
  activation, (b)adiponectin, and (c) TZDs or PPAR alpha ligands on the rat granulosa
  cell steroidogenesis. (a) Metformin or AICAR treatment decreases MAPK ERK1/2 phosphorylation
  and progesterone secretion through AMPK activation [, ]. Metformin decreases also
  oestradiol secretion through an independent AMPK pathway []. (b) Adiponectin treatment
  increases IGF-1-induced IGF-1R -subunit tyrosine phosphorylation and MAPK ERK1/2
  phosphorylation and progesterone secretion []. (c) The PPAR ligand, Wy-14 463, inhibits
  oestradiol secretion whereas TZDs or PGJ2 increases progesterone secretion and inhibits
  estradiol secretion in eCG-primed immature rats or increases estradiol secretion
  in gonadotropin-primed immature rat [, ]. 3HSD: 3-hydroxysteroiddehydrogenase, STAR:
  Steroidogenic acute regulatory protein, CYP11a1: P450 sidechain cleavage, Adipo
  R1/2: Adiponectin receptor type I and II, MAPK ERK1/2: Mitogen Activated protein
  kinase Extracellular Regulated kinase, 1/2, PGJ2: prostaglandine J2.'
