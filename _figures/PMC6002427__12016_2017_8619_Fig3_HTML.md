caption: Main signaling pathways in mast cell biology include Kit and FcεRI signaling.
  While the first pathway (in green) is associated with the cell survival and proliferation,
  the second pathway (in blue) has a key role in the production of the delayed de
  novo mediators and degranulation of the immediately released preformed mediators.
  SCF binding to the first three Ig-like domains of extracellular region of KIT induces
  structural changes resulting in dimerization of two KIT receptors following phosphorylation
  of intracellular regions and activation of signaling pathways. Crosslinking of IgE-bound
  allergen triggers the FcεRI signaling pathway (blue) initiates and results in mast
  cell degranulation
