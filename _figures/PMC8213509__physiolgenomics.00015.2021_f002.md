caption: General depiction of coronavirus life cycle. The steps in the coronavirus
  lifecycle are 1) binding of the virion to the cell via the S1 spike protein receptor
  interaction; 2) proteolytic cleavage of S protein into S1 and S2, followed by fusion
  of cellular and viral membranes and release of viral genome; 3) viral genomic RNA
  translation into replicase; 4) viral RNA synthesis; 5) viral structural proteins
  are translated; 6) moved into the endoplasmic reticulum (ER); 7) then into the Golgi-ER
  compartment and assembly of virions; 8) virions bud from Golgi-ER compartment; and
  9) leave cell via exocytic pathway (, ). Used with permission from Ref. .
