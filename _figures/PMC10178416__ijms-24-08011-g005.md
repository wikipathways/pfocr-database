caption: 'Insights into the multi-target actions of ferulic acid in acute lung injury/acute
  respiratory distress syndrome. Ferulic acid (FA) can directly target TLR4 and inhibit
  the TLR4/NF-κB pathway, decreasing the expression of phospho-NF-κB and downstream
  inflammatory mediators. Moreover, FA inhibits the expression of MAPK signaling pathway-related
  proteins. Additionally, ethyl ferulate improves acute lung injury/acute respiratory
  distress syndrome (ALI/ARDS) in an AMPK/Nrf2-dependent manner. AMPK: adenosine monophosphate-activated
  protein kinase; HO-1: heme oxygenase-1; MAPK: mitogen-activated protein kinase;
  Nrf2: nuclear factor erythroid-2-related factor-2; TLR4: toll-like receptor4.'
