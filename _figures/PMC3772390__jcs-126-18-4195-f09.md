caption: Signaling pathways regulating the phosphorylation of plectin S4642 in HeLa
  cells. EGF, PMA and hyperosmotic shock (sorbitol) stimulate the phosphorylation
  of plectin S4642 via the ERK1/2 MAPK pathway (dotted arrows indicate indirect activations).
  EGF effect is independent of PKC. Sorbitol-induced hyperosmotic stress also stimulates
  plectin S4642 phosphorylation in a PKA-dependent manner. Phosphorylation of plectin
  S4642 decreases its binding to IFs. Protein Phosphatase 2A (PP2A) directly or indirectly
  mediates pS4642 dephosphorylation.
