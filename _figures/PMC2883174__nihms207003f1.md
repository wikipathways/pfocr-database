  six enzymes: a bifunctional glutamine amidotransferase (GAT) and carbamoyl-phosphate
  synthetase (CPS), aspartate carbamoyltransferase (ACT), dihydroorotase (DHOtase),
  dihydroorotate dehydrogenase (DHODH), orotate phosphoribosyltransferase (OPRT) and
  orotidine-5’-monophosphate decarboxylase (OMPDC). L-Gln, L-glutamine; CP, carbamoyl
  phosphate; Asp, L-aspartic acid; CA, carbamoyl aspartate; DHO, dihydroorotate; OA,
  orotic acid; OMP, orotidylate; UMP, uridylate. Enzyme names are displayed in blue.
  The rescue pathway used to bypass mitochondrial PfDHODH in transgenic parasites
  harboring yeast DHODH is displayed in grey.'
