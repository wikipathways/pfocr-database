  distinct pathways, pore and leak. The cytokine IL-13 induces claudin-2 expression

  size- and charge-slective pore pathway. Conversely, TNF activates myosin light chain
  kinase (MLCK) that triggers caveolin-mediated occludin removal from the tight junction.
  This results in increased macromolecular flux across the low capacitry, relatively
  nonslective leak pathway.
