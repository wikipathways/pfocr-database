caption: The molecular signaling mechanisms and pathways of pyroptosis, necroptosis,
  and ferroptosis in cardiac cells. Necroptosis can be triggered by RIPK3 and RIPK1,
  which recruit MLKL to form the necrosome. The caspase-1 induced classical inflammasome
  pathway, caspase-4, caspase-5, and caspase-11 induced non-classical pathways, and
  caspase-3 and caspase-8 induced alternative pathways can trigger pyroptosis. Ferroptosis
  can be activated by iron accumulation and low levels of GSH-induced lipid peroxidation.
