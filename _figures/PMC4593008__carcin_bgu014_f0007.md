caption: Schematic diagram illustrating the feedback loop of FGF/FGFR signaling and
  brachyury. FGF binds to FGFR to induce FGFR phosphorylation and further activate
  MEK/ERK, which results in an increased brachyury expression. Brachyury in turn promotes
  the secretion of FGF and enhances FGF/FGFR signaling and, subsequently, MEK and
  ERK. This feedback loop of FGF/FGFR signaling and brachyury may be critical for
  chordoma cell growth, survival and EMT.
