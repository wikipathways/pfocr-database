  regulation of the signaling pathway: PTPRD can suppress the phosphorylation of signal
  transduction and activation of transcription 3 (STAT3) (on Tyr 705) and the subsequent
  STAT3 nuclear translocation to the downregulation of the IL-6/JAK/STAT3 signal transduction
  pathways in hepatocellular carcinoma (HCC). PTPRD dephosphorylates β-catenin and
  negatively regulates β-catenin/TCF signaling and its target CD44 to suppress the
  migration of HCC. PTPRD is a potential regulator of CXCL8 and reduces its expression
  to inhibit the carcinogenic effect of CXCL8 in HCC.'
