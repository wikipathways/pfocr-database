  resistance and hyperglycemia stimulate IGF-1 signaling and activate multiple pathways.
  This leads to the activation of oncogenic signaling pathways, such as PI3K/Akt,
  JNK/MAPK, Wnt, mTOR, and Ras/ERK. These pathways promote cellular proliferation,
  increased invasion and angiogenesis, and decreased apoptosis that may foster the
  development of HCC.
