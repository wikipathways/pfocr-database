caption: Effects of CORM-A1 on glycolysis and pentose phosphate pathway (PPP) metabolites
  in platelets. Human washed platelets (WP) suspended in PBS containing glucose (1
  g/L) and glutamine (2 mmol/L) were untreated (8 min; C), treated with CORM-A1 (300
  μmol/L; 8 min; A1), treated with thrombin (0.1 U/mL; T) or treated with CORM-A1
  and thrombin (2+6 min; A1/T). Data show amounts of metabolic by-products in glycolysis
  and PPP. Data are presented as means±SD (nmoL/109 platelets) of 4 independent experiments.
  DHAP indicates dihydroxyacetone phosphate; F-1,6-BP, fructose-1,6-bis-phosphate;
  GA3P, glyceraldehyde 3-phosphate; PEP, phosphoenolpyruvate; and TCA, tricarboxylic
  acid cycle. *P<0.05 vs control.
