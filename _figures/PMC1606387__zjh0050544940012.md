caption: Proposed pathway mediating protein overload-induced ET-1 expression. Ultrafiltered
  plasma proteins, by binding to specific receptors on podocyte surface, cause cytoskeleton
  rearrangement and new F-actin stress fiber formation via Rho kinase pathway. Rho
  kinase-triggered actin reorganization leads to FAK phosphorylation. FAK activation
  integrates signals from cytoskeleton to the downstream activation of NF-κB and Ap-1
  which in turn translocate to the nucleus and promote pre-pro ET-1 gene transcription.
