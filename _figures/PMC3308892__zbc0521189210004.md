caption: ER stress-associated miR-346 induction. Step 1, induction of miR-346 transcription.
  ER stress activates IRE1α, which splices the XBP1 mRNA, resulting in the translation
  of sXBP1 protein. sXBP1 enters the nucleus and activates pri-miR-346 transcription.
  Step 2, target mRNA degradation. Pri-miR-346 enters the miRNA maturation pathway,
  and in the cytosol, miR-346 is generated. Mir-346 binds to the 3′-UTR of target
  transcripts and recruits the RNA-induced silencing complex, leading to mRNA decay.
