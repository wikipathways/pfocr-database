caption: Antiviral apoptotic pathway is mediated by IRF-3 and Bax. Many innate immune
  signaling pathways cause activation of IRF-3 as a transcription factor. Cytoplasmic
  dsRNA signals through RIG-I and IPS-1 to trigger both transcriptional and apoptotic
  antiviral pathways. For apoptosis, IRF-3 binds to Bax, activates it and translocates
  it to mitochondria, which cause cytochrome c release to the cytoplasm followed by
  caspase activation and apoptosis. Both pathways contribute to the antiviral state.
  Viruses employ many evasion strategies to block various steps of these pathways.
