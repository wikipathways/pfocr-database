  of osteoblast differentiation and inhibition of osteoclast differentiation. Same3A
  regulates osteoblasts through Wnt/β-catenin pathway. Sema3A inhibits osteoclast
  differentiation through the arresting of PLCγ activation and calcium oscillation.
  Additionally, the inhibition of RhoA suppresses the migration of osteoclast precursors
  that are bone marrow-derived monocyte/macrophage precursor cells (BMMs).
