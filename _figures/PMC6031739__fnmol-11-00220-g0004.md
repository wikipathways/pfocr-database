caption: Schematic change in signaling pathway mediated by OX1R–kappa opioid receptor
  (KOR) heterodimers. OX1R mainly activates the Gq subtype, which subsequently induces
  a downstream cascade response. KOR binds the Gi subtype, which inhibits the activity
  of cAMP and p-CREB. The dimerization of OX1R and KOR changes the primary signaling
  pathway, but activates Gαs subtypes, which increase the activity of cAMP and p-CREB.
