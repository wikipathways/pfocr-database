caption: Core phosphatases of the PI 3-Kinase pathway. PTEN and INPP4b phosphatases
  inactivate PIP-lipid second messengers to prevent AKT activation. Functionally,
  they both antagonize class I PI 3-Kinase dependent membrane recruitment of AKT.
  PHLPP1 and PHLPP2 revert AKT activation by dephosphorylation at Serine 473 to antagonize
  the phosphorylation that the mTOR Complex 2 carries out on this site. AKT activation
  signals mTORC1 activation via inhibition of the TSC tumor suppressor complex.Outcome
  prediction. Phosphatase status at the DNA, RNA or protein level can be used to predict
  disease outcome.
