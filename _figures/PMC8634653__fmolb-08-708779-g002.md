caption: Schema of integrin αvβ6/TGF-β receptor/uPAR, integrin α2β1/CDH17, integrin
  α2β1 (or α5β1)/EGFR mediated signal transduction pathways in CRC. Integrin αvβ6
  separately interacts with uPAR and TGF-β receptor, initiating intracellular signaling
  contributes to the activation of transcription factors, such as Ets-1 and SMAD2/3/4
  through the FAK/PI3K, RAS/ERK, and SMAD pathways. CDH17 provokes a conformational
  change in the β1 subunit, activating the downstream FAK/RAS pathway to increase
  cell adhesion. The crosstalk between integrin α2β1/α5β1 and EGFR can induce PI3K/AKT
  signaling to promote EMT through stimulating transcription factor snail in CRC cells.
  On the other hand, integrin α2β1/EGFR also can activate FAK/ERK and PI3K/AKT survival
  pathway resulting in anoikis resistance in the absence of ECM.
