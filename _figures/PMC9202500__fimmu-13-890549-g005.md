  signaling pathways, the death receptor-mediated pathway, and the mitochondrial pathway.
  Viroporins can induce apoptosis by changing calcium ion concentration, reducing
  mitochondrial membrane potential, recruiting apoptosis-related factors, and activating
  endoplasmic reticulum stress. Some viroporins can also inhibit apoptosis. Ub, Ubiquitin.
