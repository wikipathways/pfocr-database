caption: Schematic diagram representing possible antifungal mechanism (a–e) of CIN
  and EUG in C. glabrata. (a) By inhibition of membrane ATPase pump which mediates
  secretion of hydrolytic enzyme or by degradation of hydrolytic enzymes; (b) by ROS
  generation which results in release of cyt c and activates proapoptotic pathways;
  (c) by disturbing cell membrane integrity via removal of membrane ergosterol; (d)
  by inhibiting/altering the functionality of biosynthetic pathway enzymes similar
  to ergosterol; and (e) by blocking import of extracellular sterol or downregulating
  the expression of ABC sterol importer and drug transporter.
