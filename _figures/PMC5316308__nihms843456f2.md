caption: Upon stimulation with an agonist such as CRP which induces GPVI-mediated
  signaling, DREAM binds to PI3K class Iβ (PI3K-Iβ) and facilitates the signal transduction
  pathway, thereby enhancing granule secretion and cytosolic Ca2+ levels. ADP-induced
  signaling pathways further activate αIIbβ3 integrin and amplify integrin outside-in
  signaling which also activates PI3K-Iβ through DREAM. Binding of fibrinogen to activated
  αIIbβ3 integrin results in platelet-platelet aggregation and thrombus formation.
