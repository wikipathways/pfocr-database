  CGA on E. coli-induced endometritis. E. coli infection activates the TLR4/NF-κB
  signaling pathway. Activated NF-κB can be translocated from the cytosol to the nucleus,
  where it activates the transcription of genes that include IL-1β, IL-6, IL-8, IL-12,
  and TNF-α. Astragalin and CGA could inhibit the inflammatory response by blocking
  TLR4 activation.
