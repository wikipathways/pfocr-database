  on striatal circuitry. A, Gq-protein-coupled signaling upon hM3Dq overactivation

  signaling upon hM3Dq overactivation in MSNs in vivo induces neuronal dysfuction
  via JNK, which translates into loss of hyperlocomotion and motor discoordination
  (upon D1R-MSN dysfunction) or into hyperlocomotion and wakefulness (upon D2R-MSN
  dysfunction).
