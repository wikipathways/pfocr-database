caption: Enriched canonical pathways of miRNA target genes. A, IGF1 signaling pathway.
  The pathway map of Kyoto Encyclopedia of Genes and Genomes-derived IGF-1 signaling
  pathway drawn using IPA, which was the top significantly enriched canonical pathway
  for differentially expressed miRNA targets in THC-MDSC versus BM MDSC precursors
  (supplemental Table S2). B, miRNA targets were analyzed for their association with
  pathways involved in myeloid development, differentiation, and function using IPA.
  Differentially expressed miRNA in THC-MDSC are highlighted in red for overexpressed
  and in green for underexpressed. The direct targets of differentially expressed
  miRNA in the pathway are indicated in bold letters and denoted with gray ovals.
