caption: Scheme of role of 67-kDa LR in vasogenic edema formation and AQP4 expression.
  Blockade of 67LR induced by its neutralization and SE increases p38 MAPK and ERK1/2
  phosphorylations in endothelial cells and astrocytes, respectively. Subsequently,
  both kinases activate PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. Increased AKT activity enhances
  endothelial permeability and triggers astroglial loss. However, AKT activation reduces
  AQP4 expression in astrocytes, which worsens vasogenic edema.
