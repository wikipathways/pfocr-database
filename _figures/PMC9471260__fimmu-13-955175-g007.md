  EVs transport pro-inflammatory signals to macrophages. SEN cells release more EVs
  that contain miR-30b-5p. With the uptake of SEN EVs by macrophages, miR-30b-5p is
  released from EVs and reduces the level of SIRT1, thereby mediating the increase
  of p65 entry into the nucleus, and then increasing the synthesis of cytokines IL-6
  and IL-1β.
