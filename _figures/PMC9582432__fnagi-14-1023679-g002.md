caption: Molecular network of mTOR between autophagy and metabolism. Growth factors
  and energy levels are major upstream regulators of mTOR. Multiple growth factors
  can act on cell surface receptors to affect mTOR activation through various signaling
  pathways. Hypoglycemia and the reduction of ATP/AMP inhibit mTOR by activating AMPK.
  Low amino acid levels inhibit mTOR via the Rag-Ragulator complex pathway. Autophagy
  and metabolism are major downstream targets of mTOR. Activated mTOR inhibits nucleation
  and induction of phagophores by inhibiting the ULK1 complex. Meanwhile, activated
  mTOR promotes cell growth and proliferation by enhancing the synthesis of basal
  metabolites including proteins, nucleotides, and lipids through P70S6K, 4EBPs, and
  SREBP pathways.
