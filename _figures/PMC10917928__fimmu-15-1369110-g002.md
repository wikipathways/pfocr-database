caption: TCM polysaccharides’ role in regulating tumor adaptive immune response. Once
  recognized directly or indirectly by the TCR, TCM polysaccharides activate the PTK,
  and the latter plays an important role in antitumor by regulating the expression
  of cytokines, including IL-2, IL-4, IL-6, IL-8, and INF-γ, via PI3K or MAPK signaling
  pathway. B lymphocytes can also recognize TCM polysaccharides through mIg/CD79 complex
  or TLR 2/4 signaling cascades, which will lead to the elevation of nuclear translocation
  of NFAT/AP-1/NF-κB and regulate the expression of B lymphocyte-related genes, through
  the activation of the calcineurin/PKC/MAPK (ERK) signaling cascade
