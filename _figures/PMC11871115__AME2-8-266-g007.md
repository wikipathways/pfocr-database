  disease (AD) through modulation of the WβC signaling pathway. Dysregulation of this

  GSK‐3β and DKK1, and contributing to the formation of amyloid‐β plaques and neurofibrillary
  tangles—hallmarks of AD. Polyphenols counteract this dysregulation, helping restore
  balance in the WβC pathway and prevent AD progression. DKK1, Dickkopf‐1; Dvl, disheveled;
