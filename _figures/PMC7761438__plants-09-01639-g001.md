  the contribution of PGKs in the photosynthesis (consuming ATP), and glycolytic pathway

  synthase (SPS), Sucrose-6-P phosphatase (SPP), UDP-glucose pyrophosphorylase (UGPase),

  (C5), hexose sugar phosphates (C6), septose sugar phosphates (C7), Ribulose1,5,bP
