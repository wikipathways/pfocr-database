  part in, mTORC1 and mTORC2. MTORC1 increases protein translation through phosphorylation

  through activation of the S6 ribosomal protein, 4EBP1 phosphorylation promotes protein
  synthesis by releasing eIF4E that can then bind to eIF4G to form, along with other
  proteins, the initiation complex eIF4F.
