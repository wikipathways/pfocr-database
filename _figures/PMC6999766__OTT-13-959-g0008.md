caption: Schematic diagram summarizing the therapeutic effects of the flavonoid ingredients
  isolated from Abelmoschus manihot (L.) Medik flowers in multiple myeloma. HKC exerts
  protective effects on the survival of MM mice and may promote osteoblastogenesis
  and suppress osteoclastogenesis in murine cell lines, thus improving the bone marrow
  microenvironment. One flavonoid compound was determined to directly inhibit MM cell
  proliferation by modulating TGF-β1 signaling, inducing IL-6, TNF-α expression and
  downregulating the β-catenin pathway.
