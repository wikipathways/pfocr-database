caption: Overview of central metabolism in the S. glossinidius metabolic network.
  Functional (yellow) and nonfunctional (white) pathway components are indicated,
  with the S. glossinidius and S. praecaptivus gene associations given in black and
  blue type, respectively. Reactions for which new evidence has suggested that they
  might be functional are given in orange. The TCA cycle is complete, and transporters
  for l-arginine, l-glutamate, and l-aspartate are present. PPC and the reactions
  that connected ornithine with the TCA cycle are pseudogenized. There is evidence
  from the new genome annotation that the l-arginine biosynthesis pathway from l-glutamate
  may not be entirely pseudogenized, as previously thought (). Selected coreactants
  are included. Asterisks indicate biomass components.
