caption: A hypothetic model of the role of the AC122108.1 in Wnt signaling stimulation.
  (A) Wnt signaling is off-state; β-catenin is degraded by a dedicated cytoplasmic
  “β-catenin degradation protein complex”. (B) Wnt signaling is on-state in PC14/B
  cells that express high levels of the AC122108.1; a complex of AC122108.1 and ALDOA
  binds to GSK-3β, disassembling the “β-catenin degradation protein complex”. The
  activation of the Wnt cascade leads to the accumulation and nuclear translocation
  of β-catenin, and results in the expression of Wnt target genes. ALDOA, aldolase
  A.
