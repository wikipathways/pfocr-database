  manner. Activated Fyn transmits positive signals to PI3K, leading to PI3K activation
  that accounts for calcium mobilization as well as for Akt phosphoryaltion. MAPKs
  also become phosphorylated following Ras and Fyn activation that are responsible
  for production of most cytokines. Paxillin is phosphoryated indirectly by Shp2 upon
  FcεRΙ stimulation. The direct substrates of Shp2 in FcεRΙ signaling remain to be
  defined. Another acknowledged signaling pathway Lyn/Syk/LAT is also shown.
