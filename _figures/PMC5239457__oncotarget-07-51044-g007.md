caption: Induction of eIF2αP in cells subjected to oxidative stress leads to ATF4
  upregulation at the translational level, which in turn inhibits the expression of
  E3 ubiquitin ligases NEDD4.2 and WWP1 that target LATS1 for degradation [, ]. LATS1
  stabilization results in the phosphorylation and cytoplasmic sequestration of YAP/TAZ,
  which impairs YAP/TAZ-dependent expression of genes involved in cell survival and
  proliferation. Interaction of YAP/TAZ with FOXO1 has been demonstrated to play a
  key role in the induction of anti-oxidant genes and cell survival under oxidative
  stress [].
