caption: Irisin reduces gluconeogenesis by downregulating the expression of PCK1 (also
  known as PEPCKC) and G6PC via AMP-activated protein kinase (AMPK) and the phosphoinositide
  3-kinase (PI3K)–AKT–FOXO1 signalling pathway. In addition, it stimulates glycogenesis
  via the PI3K–AKT–glycogen synthase kinase 3 (GSK3)–glycogen synthase (GS) pathway.
  Irisin inhibits palmitic acid-induced lipogenesis and lipid accumulation, as well
  as oxidative stress, by downregulating expression of PRMT3. This leads to reduced
  expression of several lipogenic markers (for example, liver x receptor-α (LXRα),
  sterol regulatory element-binding protein 1C (SREBP1C), acetyl-CoA carboxylase (ACC)
  and fatty acid synthase (FAS)) and inflammatory markers (such as the genes TNF and
  IL6, and the proteins cyclooxygenase 2 (COX2), nuclear factor-κB (NF-κB) and p38
  mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK)).
