  effect of storax. Storax can activate PI3K/Akt signaling pathway and inhibit NF-κB

  anti-oxidative stress and pro-angiogenic pharmacological effects, as well as anti-apoptosis
  by elevating Bcl-2 and inhibiting the expression of Caspase-3 and Bax, thus playing
  a co-protective role in the brain and heart.
