  ISGs. IFN-α/-β binds to their receptors IFNAR-1 and IFNAR-2 on cell membrane, which
  activates JAK/STAT pathway. “P” besides STAT1 and STAT2 indicates phosphorylation.
  PRRSV nsp1β inhibits ISGF3 nuclear translocation via inducing degradation of KPNA1,
  which is essential for mediating the nuclear import of ISGF3. N protein also inhibits
  ISGF3 nuclear translocation. And nsp2 reduces ISG15 production and conjugation via
  its deubiquitination activity.
