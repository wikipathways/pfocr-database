  (1) the de novo synthesis pathway, which occurs in the endoplasmic reticulum; (2)

  compartment of the late endosomes/lysosomes. A-CDase, acid ceramidase; A-SMase,
  acid sphingomyelinase; CerSs, ceramide synthases; CK, ceramide kinase; C1P, ceramide-1-phosphate;

  reductase; SMases, sphingomyelinases; SMSs, sphingomyelin synthases; SphKs, sphingosine
  kinases; S1P, sphingosine-1-phosphate; SPP, S1P phosphatase; SPT, serine palmitoyl
  transferase.'
