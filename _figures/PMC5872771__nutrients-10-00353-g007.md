caption: Potential mechanisms involved in the enhanced anti-cancer effect of the combination
  of GCM and ABT-737. ABT-737 induces apoptosis by targeting anti-apoptotic Bcl-2
  family proteins, leading to disruption of the mitochondrial membrane potential,
  activating caspase, and inducing apoptosis. In the meantime, ABT-737 activates TOPK,
  resulting in up-regulating survivin, which confers the cancer cells resistant to
  ABT-737. GCM potentiates the cancer cells to ABT-737 by suppressing TOPK/survivin
  axis and inhibiting de novo lipogenesis through activating AMPK pathway.
