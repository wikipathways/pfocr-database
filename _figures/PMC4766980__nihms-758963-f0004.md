caption: The described neural circuit for fat loss functions as a positive regulatory
  loop in which octopamine/adrenaline-like signaling acts as a permissive cue to maintain
  serotonin production and function. Serotonin production in the ADF chemosensory
  neurons activates the serotonergic chloride channel MOD-1 in the URX body cavity
  neurons to elicit fat loss. Serotonin signaling relays a neuroendocrine instructive
  signal from the nervous system to the distal intestine via unknown neuroendocrine
  effectors.
