caption: An overview of PI3K/AKT/mTOR signaling pathway shows activation of PI3K through
  receptor tyrosine kinases (RTK) and G-protein coupled receptors (GPCR). It acts
  directly on AKT or indirectly via mTORC2. Activation of inhibition of adenosine
  triphosphate (ATP)-citrate lyase, glycogen synthase kinase 3 (GSK3), forkhead box
  protein O (FOXO), Rab GTPase-activating protein (AS160) and tuberous sclerosis 1/2.
  As a result fatty acid synthesis, glycogen synthesis, cell survival, proliferation,
  autophagy, glycolysis and protein synthesis occur.
