caption: The integrative role of IR/IGF1R→IRS2 signaling in pancreatic β-cell function.
  The diagram shows the relation between the IRS2-branch of the insulin signaling
  pathway and upstream and downstream mechanisms regulating β-cell growth and function.
  Since IR and IGF1R are constitutively active in β-cells, activation of GLP1→cAMP→PKA→CREB,
  glucose→Ca2+→CRTC2, and calcineurin→NFAT induce IRS2 expression to regulate PI3K→AKT
  cascade, which places β-cell growth, function, and survival under the control of
  glucose and incretins.
