caption: (a)Insulin inhibits isoproterenol-induced lipolysis by increasing PDE3B expression
  via AKT. The elevation of PDE3B catalyzes the hydrolysis of cAMP, which reduces
  the cellular level of cAMP. The lowering of cAMP further dephosphorylates PKA and
  thereby results in a decrease in hormone-sensitive lipase (HSL) and lipolysis. (b)PID1
  promotes lipolysis and induces a noticeable inhibition of the phosphorylationof
  AKT and PDE3B expression, which further increases cAMP levels and the phosphorylation
  of PKA and HSL, leading to increased lipolysis.
