caption: Signaling pathways induced following CB2 engagement with cannabinoid ligands.
  Black and red arrows indicate CB2-mediated signaling events, while grey arrows represent
  other receptor-mediated pathways. CB2 ligation by cannabinoid ligands regulates
  adenylate cyclase activation (A), which controls PKA activation (B). PKA crosstalks
  with the TCR signaling pathway attenuating cytokine production (B). CB2 ligation
  also directly activates MAPK resulting both the positive and negative regulation
  of cellular functions (C, D). CB2 signaling can also block MPAK signaling induced
  by other external stimuli (E).
