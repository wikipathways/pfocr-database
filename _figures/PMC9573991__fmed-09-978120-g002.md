caption: Flavonoids promote scar-free regeneration by regulating signaling pathways.
  Flavonoids like icariin and epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG) limit fibroblast proliferation
  and motility by modifying transforming growth factor-1 (TGF-1) and so suppressing
  the PI3K/AKT signaling pathway. Apigenin drastically lowers focal adhesion kinase
  (FAK) activity and, as a result, changes the phosphorylation of extracellular signal-regulated
  kinase (Erk1/2). Catechin, by binding to Scr, reduces extracellular matrix deposition
  and enhances scar-free wound healing. FAK, Focal Adhesion Kinase; Src, Sarcoma gene;
  Erk, Extracellular regulatxory protein kinase; MAPK, Mitogen-activated Protein Kinase;
  Alk5, Activin receptor-like kinase 5; PI3K, Phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase; Akt,
  Protein kinase B.
