caption: Under non-stimulation, TRAF2 and TRAF3 form a complex with cellular inhibitor
  of apoptosis 1 and 2 (cIAP1 and cIAP2) and NFκB-inducing kinase (NIK). cIAP1/2 degrades
  NIK, ablating the non-canonical NFκB pathway (, ). After CD40/CD40L engagement,
  the complex is destabilized, permitting the release of NIK from the complex inducing
  the non-canonical NFκB signaling. Furthermore, it induced the recruitment of TRAF2
  and TRAF3 to CD40 tail and the degradation of TRAF3 by cIAP1/2 proteins.
