caption: 'On the cytoplasmic face of the ER, dolichol phosphate is modified by glycosyltransferases,
  which utilize nucleotide-activated sugar donors, to form Man5GlcNAc2-PP-Dol. This
  structure is then flipped across the ER membrane to the luminal face, where additional
  glycosyltransferases use lipid-activated monosaccharides as sugar donors in order
  to form the final LLO donor: Glc3Man9GlcNAc2-PP-Dol. The oligosaccharide is then
  transferred from the dolichol donor onto the polypeptide by the OST enzyme complex.
  The affected enzymes for B4-2-1 (DPM2), MI8-5 (ALG6), and MI5-4 (hexokinase) are
  indicated in gray italics.'
