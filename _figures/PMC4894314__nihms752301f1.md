caption: Effect of HDACI on cell survival signaling pathway in hemorrhagic shock.
  HDACI induce phosphorylation of AKT by inhibition of TRB3 and PTEN. While AKT stimulates
  transcription of cell survival genes through several other pathways, phosphorylated
  AKT phosphorylates GSK-3β. Phosphorylated GSK-3β becomes inactivated form, which
  cannot degrade β-catenin. HDACI can also directly inhibit GSK-3β. Moreover, HDACI
  induce acetylation and nuclear translocation of β-catenin, leading to downstream
  survival gene transcription. P phosphorylation, Ac acetylation
