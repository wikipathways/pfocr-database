caption: Under normoxia, HIF-2α protein is hydroxylated and degraded to maintain a
  low level. Under hypoxia, HIF-2α accumulates and dimerizes with ARNT to active target
  gene expression. PHD inhibitors block HIF-2α degradation and activate the HIF-2α
  signaling pathway. The binding of an antagonist disrupts HIF-2α/ARNT interaction
  and blocks the HIF-2α signaling pathway. The binding of an agonist enhances HIF-2α/ARNT
  interaction and activates target gene expression.
