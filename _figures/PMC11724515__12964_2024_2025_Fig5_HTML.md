caption: 'Effects of oestrogen, melatonin and NPY on obesity and bone. Oestrogen:
  E2 inhibits VMH AMPK signaling and increases BAT lipolysis and thermogenesis. Oestrogen
  promotes LEPR expression and increases leptin sensitivity. Oestrogen inhibits HIF-1α
  signaling activation, inhibits RANKL production, and improves bone loss. SIRT6-mediated
  stability of ERα is impaired, transcription of Fas ligands is inhibited, and osteoclast
  numbers are increased. Melatonin: Melatonin activates SIRT1, upregulates RUNX2 to
  promote osteogenic differentiation, and down-regulates PPARγ to inhibit adipogenic
  differentiation. It improves OP by inhibiting NLRP3 through Wnt/β-catenin pathway.
  NPY: NPY inhibits SIRT1, hyperacetylated PPARγ, and exacerbated fat accumulation.
  NPY decreased the expression of Tead1 and Junb by inhibiting cAMP/PKA/CREB signaling
  through Y1R, which inhibited osteogenic differentiation and promoted adipogenic
  differentiation of BMSCs. Abbreviation: E2, Estradiol; LEPR, leptin receptor; ERα,
  estrogen-related receptor; NPY, Neuropeptide Y'
