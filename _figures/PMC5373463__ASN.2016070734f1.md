caption: Schematic illustration of regulatory networks that control intrarenal RAS
  activity. This local system is subjected to tight control by complex regulatory
  networks consisting of both positive regulators of (pro)renin receptor, Wnt/β-catenin
  signaling, and the PGE2/EP4pathway, and negative regulators of fibroblast growth
  factor 23/Klotho, vitamin D/VDR, and LXRs. Imbalance of the two opposing regulatory
  networks may be an important determinant of intrarenal RAS activity. FGF23, Fibroblast
