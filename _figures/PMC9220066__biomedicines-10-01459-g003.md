caption: The molecular pathways involved in regulating PC metabolism through autophagy.
  KRAS stimulation includes a GEF-triggered GDP–GTP exchange, permitting KRAS-GDP
  phosphorylation to GTP, leading to effector functions, including activation of PI3K-AKT-mTOR-targeted
  autophagy activation. The LKB1–AMPK pathway inhibits mTORC1, causing autophagy initiation
  in PC cells. Moreover, several nuclear autophagy-related genes initiate phagophore
  formation, activating autophagy in PC cells.
