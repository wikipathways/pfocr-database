  stimulation of a cell may lead to IKK phosphorylation and activation which, in turn,
  may lead to the phosphorylation of the IκB/NF-κB complex retained in the cytoplasm.

  enter into the nucleus and bind DNA to initiate the transcription of some targeted
  genes implicated in the inflammatory response.
