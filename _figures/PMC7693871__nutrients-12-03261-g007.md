  responses of Ac2-26 in HaCaT cells. This pathway represents the phosphorylation
  of IκB-α, p65, STAT3, and JAK2 induced by TNF-α/IFN-γ, and was inhibited upon Ac2-26
  treatment in HaCaT cells. (B) Anti-wrinkle effect upon the inhibition of Ac2-26
  in Detroit cells. This pathway represents the inhibition of the protein levels -
  MMP-1 and MMP-8 leading to collagen degradation, and increases the protein level
  of COL1A1 leading to collagen synthesis. (Tumor necrosis factor-α; TNF-α, interferon-γ;
  IFN-γ, mitogen-activated protein kinase; MAPK, nuclear factor- κB; NF-κB, matrix
