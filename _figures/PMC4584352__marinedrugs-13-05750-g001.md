caption: The anti-oxidative effects of astaxanthin in neurological diseases. Astaxanthin
  facilitates the dissociation and nuclear translocation of nuclear erythroid 2-related
  factor (Nrf2), through activation of the PI3K/Akt and ERK signaling pathways, which
  contributes to increased expression of Nrf2-regulated enzymes like HO-1, NQO-1,
  and GST-α1 that resist oxidative stress. In addition, astaxanthin negatively regulates
  Sp1/NR1 signaling pathway, alleviating the production of intracellular ROS and oxidative
  stress.
