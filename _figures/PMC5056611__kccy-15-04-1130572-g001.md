caption: SETD1A-induced miRNAs suppress gene expression modules. The model shows that
  SETD1A-induced miRNAs suppress p53 downstream target genes, thus counteracting the
  p53 pathway. A subset of SETD1A-induced miRNAs cumulatively repress EZH2-targets
  that are also modulated through EZH2-mediated transcriptional gene silencing (TGS).
  Tajima et al. demonstrate that SETD1A, linked to transcriptional activation (TA),
  through miRNA-mediated post-transcriptional gene silencing (PTGS), can cause an
  imbalance in signaling nodes implicated in tumorigenesis.
