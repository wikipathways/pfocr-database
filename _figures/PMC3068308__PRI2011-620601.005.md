caption: The TGF-β signaling pathway. The TGF-β signaling pathway is activated by
  binding of TGF-β to a type II receptor, which facilitates the recruitment and phosphorylation
  of the type I receptor. This pTGF-βRI activates either SMAD2 or SMAD3 by phosphorylation.
  The phosphorylated SMAD2/3 forms a complex with SMAD4 and transports to the nucleus
  where it influences SMAD target gene transcription. SMAD7 can inhibit TGF-β signaling
  through inhibition of TGF-βRI phosphorylation.
