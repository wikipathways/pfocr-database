caption: Functions of HT in AD. HT removes mitochondrial ROS and activates certain
  molecular targets. For example, HT activates JNK/p38-NF-κB signaling to release
  proinflammatory interleukins and cytokines; improves the JAK2/STAT3, ERK/RSK2, and
  AMPK/AKT signaling pathway for neuronal survival; repairs the phase 2 enzyme system
  by activating Nrf2/Keap1 signaling; and activates AMPK/PGC-1α to trigger mitochondrial
  biogenesis. HT, hydroxytyrosol; JNK, C-Jun NH2-terminal kinase. Color images are
  available online.
