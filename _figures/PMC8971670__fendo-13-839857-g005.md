caption: The mechanisms involved in AR signal pathway and PROTACs targeting AR. (A)
  Dihydrotestosterone (DHT) binds to inactivated AR, and cause the dissociation of
  heat shock protein (HSP) from AR-HSP complex. DHT-bound AR translocate to the nucleus
  after suffer phosphorylation and dimerization, then interacts with the androgen
  response element (ARE) and control target gene transcription. (B) PROTAC AR degraders
  can induce proteasome-mediated degradation of AR protein, and effectively suppress
  the mRNA levels of AR-regulated genes, which resulted in cellular proliferation
  inhibition, induction of cell apoptosis, and antitumor activity in xenograft model.
