  in both microglia (left) and macrophages (right). In microglia, oxytocin (OXT) dampens
  inflammatory factor transcription by preventing the phosphorylation of p38 and ERK
  in the MAPK pathway. In macrophages, OXT dampens inflammatory factor transcription
  by preventing phosphorylation of the p65 subunit of NF-κB. LPS downregulates vasopressin
  receptor 2 (VR2) expression in macrophages, while the effect on vasopressin receptors
  (AVPR; subtype not yet specified) in microglia is not yet known
