  Drp1 and Bax translocate from the cytosol to mitochondria. Drp1 has been proposed
  to encircle mitochondria to mediate constriction and this is followed by scission,
  which results in the formation of two separate mitochondria. Mitochondrial fission
  may play a role in the removal of dysfunctional mitochondria with reduced mitochondrial
  membrane potential, through an autophagy-lysosomal pathway named “mitophagy.”
