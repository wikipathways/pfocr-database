caption: C-PC ameliorates the senescence of MSCs through ZDHHC5 mediated autophagy
  via PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway. Oxidative stress induced senescence of MSCs and increased
  the level of ZDHHC5 in vitro. ZDHHC5 activated PI3K/AKT/mTOR signaling and inhibited
  autophagy which finally promoted MSCs senescence. C-PC downregulated and reversed
  all the roles of ZDHHC5 on MSCs senescence.
