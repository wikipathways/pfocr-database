caption: 'Biochemical pathways involved in central carbon catabolism in P. aeruginosa
  PAO1. The metabolic network was constructed around six main metabolic blocks, which
  are identified with different colors as follows: (i) the peripheral pathways that
  encompass the oxidative transformation of glucose, acetate, and glycerol (orange);
  (ii) the Embden-Meyerhoff-Parnas pathway (EMP; nonfunctional in P. aeruginosa due
  to the absence of 6-phosphofructo-1-kinase) (purple); (iii) the pentose phosphate
  pathway (PPP) (red); (iv) the Entner-Doudoroff pathway (EDP) (green); (v) the tricarboxylic
  acid cycle and glyoxylate shunt (blue); (vi) the anaplerotic and gluconeogenic bioreaction
  metabolic block (gray).'
