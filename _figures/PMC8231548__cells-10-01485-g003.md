caption: Integration of insulin and adiponectin signaling pathways. (A) Upon adiponectin
  or insulin stimulation, PKC phosphorylates APPL1. This results in insulin receptor
  1/2 (IRS1/2) and Akt recruitment, resulting in APPL1/IRS1/2/Akt complex formation.
  This complex then associates with the insulin receptor (IR). (B) After the binding
  of insulin (INS) to the IR, APPL1 “piggybacks” Akt and IRS1/2 on the IR. (C) Dephosphorylated
  APPL1 dissociates from the complex. (D) IRS1/2 phosphorylates phosphatidylinositol-3
  kinase (PI3K), and PI3K phosphorylates Akt, which activates its further substrates
  in the insulin signaling pathway.
