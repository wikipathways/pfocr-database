caption: Roles of let-7 in oxLDL induced VSMCs and ECs apoptosis, proliferation, migration
  and autophagy. OxLDL induces EC apoptosis via let-7c and subsequent Bcl-xl and Caspase-3.
  In oxLDL treated VSMCs, oxLDL suppresses let-7g expression via LOX-1/Ca2+/PKC/OTC-1
  signaling pathway, the let-7g then targets on Beclin-1, LC3-II (LC3-I), Atg5, Caspase-3,
  Bax, Bcl-2, and Bcl-xl, and regulates the proliferation, migration, autophagy and
  apoptosis of VSMC.
