caption: 'Schematic illustration of regulatory effect of L-Arg/NO on protein turnover.
  L-Arg/NO suppresses protein synthesis via mTOR/p70S6K pathway in a muscle fibre-dependent
  manner in chickens. (A) In myoblasts from chicken embryo pectoralis major muscle
  (fast-twitch glycolytic fibres), L-Arg/NO increases protein deposition by inhibiting
  protein synthesis via mTOR/p70S6K and suppressing protein hydrolysis via atrogin-1/MuRF-1
  pathways. (B) In myoblasts from chicken embryo biceps femoris muscle (slow-twitch
  oxidative fibers), the effect of L-Arg/NO shows a weak effect. Solid arrowhead:
  strong positive effect; dotted arrowhead: weak positive effect; solid T-line, strong
  negative effect; dotted T-line, weak negative effect. L-arginine = L-Arg; NO = nitric
  oxide; NOS = nitric oxide synthase; p70S6K = protein S6 kinase beta-1; MuRF1 = muscle
  RING finger 1.'
