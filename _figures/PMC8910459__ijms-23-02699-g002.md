caption: Dopamine clock-resetting pathway in the RPE. Schematic illustration of the
  proposed mechanism by which D2R signaling phase shifts the circadian clock in the
  RPE. Activating these receptors by dopamine (DA) leads to MEK activation, then Erk1/2
  (ERK), and finally the phosphorylation of p90RSK, which in turn phosphorylates CREB
  at Ser133, thereby activating the Per1/2 promoters. Alternatively, ERK can also
  induce Per1/2 transcription via the phosphorylation of CBP, causing it to bind to
  and activate the BMAL1:CLOCK [].
