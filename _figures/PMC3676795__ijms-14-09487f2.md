  apoptosis. Receptor tyrosine kinase detects survival stimuli such as growth factors
  and induces phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K)/Akt signaling cascades that ultimately
  result in the inhibition of the pro-apoptotic protein, Bad. Pro-apoptotic and anti-apoptotic
  proteins govern the intrinsic cell death pathway, which results in the release of
  cytochrome c from the mitochondria and induction of the caspase cascade. Signaling
  through death receptors initiates the extrinsic pathway of apoptosis.
