  bone resorption. RBP-J suppresses NFATc1 induction by attenuating c-Fos activation
  and suppressing induction of Blimp1, thereby preventing downregulation of transcriptional
  repressor, IRF-8, that blocks osteoclast differentiation. RBP-J also restrains ITAM
  signaling and limits crosstalk between RANK or TNFR and ITAM-associated receptors
  by suppressing PLCγ2 expression and keeping PLCγ2-calcium signaling in a repressed
  basal state in a TGF-β-dependent pathway
