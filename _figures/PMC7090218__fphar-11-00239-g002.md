  Pathway and some related mechanisms of action. Several pharmacological inducer have

  inhibit iron death from various pathways (eg Fer-1、lip-1、BP、DFO). AA, Arachidonic
  acid; ACSL4, Acyl-CoA synthetase long-chain family member 4; AKR1C1-3, Aldo-keto
