caption: 'Schematic of the different apelinergic signaling pathways. The activation
  of apelin receptor by its endogenous ligands, apelin or Elabela, triggers various
  signaling pathways that exert protective cardiovascular and metabolic effects in
  the organism. Some of the transcriptional gene regulation effects following the
  activation of apelinergic pathway are depicted in the nucleus. Abbreviations: AC,
  adenylate cyclase; ACE2, angiotensin-converting enzyme 2; Ang II, angiotensin II;
  APLNR, apelin receptor; AT1R, angiotensin type 1 receptor; βARR, β-arrestins; eNOS,
  endothelial nitric oxide synthase; ERK1/2, extracellular-regulated kinases 1/2;
  FABP4, fatty acid binding protein 4; FOXO1/3, forkhead box protein O1/3; KDR, kinase
  insert domain receptor (also known as VEGFR2); MLCK, myosin light chain kinase;
  NEP, neprilysin; SIRT3, sirtuin 3; VEGFA, vascular endothelial growth factor A.'
