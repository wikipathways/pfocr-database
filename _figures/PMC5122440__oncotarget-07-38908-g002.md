caption: HIF-1α serves as a key activator of glycolysis through the induction of GLUTs
  and many glycolytic enzymes. The receptor tyrosine kinases (RTKs)-mediated PI3K-Akt-mTOR
  signaling pathway plays a pivotal role in the metabolic switch to aerobic glycolysis
  in tumor cells via the activation of HIF-1α, NFκB and c-Myc, and the subsequent
  expression of glycolytic enzymes. Some oncogenes and tumor suppressors such as Ras
  and p53 are involved in the regulation of the Warburg effect.
