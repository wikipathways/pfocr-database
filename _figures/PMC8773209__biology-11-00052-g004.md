  under force: E-cadherin (purple circles) forms strong homophilic interaction between
  adjacent cells. Adaptor proteins, such as, β- and α-catenin form cytosolic adhesion
  complex (orange circles) and link E-cadherin to the actin cytoskeleton (red units).
  In response to force, the RhoA pathway (yellow) is activated, which in turn activates
  the actomyosin pathway culminating in force generation by non-muscle myosin (blue
  ovals) and further reinforcement of the actin cytoskeleton.'
