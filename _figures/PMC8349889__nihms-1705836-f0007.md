caption: In ABC DLBCL, higher EGR1 expression is due to JAK1 through targeting the
  histone H3 and chronic activation of BCR signaling. Based on our EGR1 ChIP-seq and
  RNA-seq analyses, EGR1 upregulates gene expression in multiple oncogenic pathways
  such as MYC and E2F while damping the type I interferon pathway genes. In particular,
  EGR1 genomic occupancy largely overlaps that of BRD4, likely through inducing CBP/p300
  expression and H3K27 acetylation. JQ1 is a BRD4 inhibitor and mithramycin is an
  EGR1 inhibitor. Lenalidomide is an inducer of the type I interferon signaling pathway.
