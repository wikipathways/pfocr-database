caption: The circadian clock signaling pathway. Bmal1-Clock or Bmal1-Npas2 protein
  heterodimers transactivate the Cry and Per genes to induce transcription. Translated
  Per and Cry proteins form heterodimers and translocate into the nucleus to repress
  Bmal1. Timing is tuned to approximate 24 hours through posttranslational modifications,
  including CKI-dependent phosphorylation and FbxL3-dependent ubiquitination, which
  lead to proteosomal degradation of the Per and Cry proteins, respectively.
