  its own expression. RstA positively affects sporulation through an undetermined
  regulatory pathway. RstA negatively influences both motility and toxin production
  through the flagellar-specific sigma factor, SigD. In addition, CodY directly represses
  tcdR transcription (), and CcpA directly represses tcdR, tcdA and tcdB gene expression
  (, ). However, CodY- and CcpA-dependent toxin regulation is not influenced by RstA
  in the conditions tested.
