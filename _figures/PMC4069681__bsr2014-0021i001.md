  RhoGTPases acting on this pathway are shown in black boxes. Grey boxes indicated
  regulatory elements for the RhoGTPases, while other regulatory molecules, including
  extracellular and intracellular molecules are shown in blue. The central regulatory
  point is the IκBα, but other regulatory points are also shown (see text for details).

  inhibitors. Positive regulation is shown by complete orange arrows and inhibition
