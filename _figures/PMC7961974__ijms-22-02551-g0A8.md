caption: Pathway activation patterns of the NOTCH pathway in the different CD-groups.
  In healthy tissue, genes for transmembrane receptor protein Notch 1, 2 and 3 are
  highly expressed at the basal crypts of morphologically normal colon mucosae [].
  NOTCH1 interacts with the Wnt-pathway and activates cell proliferation. We find
  elevated mRNA expression of NOTCH1 in subgroups CD-H and CD-L (FC 1.38, p < 0.01)
  compared to Reference samples. Interestingly, the activation patterns of the NOTCH-pathway
  varies in a group-specific fashion. The genes PSENEN, PSEN1 and AP1A activate specifically
  in CD-H (and to a less degree in M) while DTX2, RBPJL and PRCRA were found activated
  in CD-L.
