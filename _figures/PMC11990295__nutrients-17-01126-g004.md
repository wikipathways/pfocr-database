caption: Role of anthocyanin in modulating PI3K/Akt/GLUT4 signaling pathway in obesity.
  Anthocyanins enhance insulin signaling by upregulating the phosphorylation of key
  molecules in the PI3K/Akt signaling pathway, including IRS, PI3K, PDK1, and AKT.
  This leads to the activation of AS160, facilitating the translocation of GLUT4 vesicles
  to the cell membrane. Consequently, glucose uptake is increased, improving insulin
  sensitivity and mitigating obesity-related insulin resistance. Upward arrows indicate
  upregulation, while the downward arrow represents inhibition
