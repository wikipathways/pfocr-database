  stress and apoptosis. DDT up-regulates SIRT1-mediated PI3K/Akt signaling, which
  causes Nrf2 expression and nuclear translocation. Nrf2 activation enhances the downstream,
  HO-1 expression, which finally protects cells from oxidative stress and apoptosis.
