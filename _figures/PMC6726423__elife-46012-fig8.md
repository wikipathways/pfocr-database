caption: CCN1 in the milieu is sensed by ECs by binding to membrane integrin αvβ3
  and VEGFR2 receptors, thus activating the MAPK and PI3K-YAP/TAZ signalling pathways,
  leading to activation of YAP/TAZ. YAP/TAZ transfer to the nucleus facilitates transcriptional
  regulation of CCN1, which may sustain the CCN1-VEGFR2-MAPK, PI3K-YAP/TAZ signalling
  circuit for maintaining tip cell activity in ECs. In addition, CCN1-induced mDia1
  is necessary for YAP/TAZ activation and leads to induce filopodia formation through
  increased Cdc42 Rho GTPase activity.
