caption: Possible pathways of Cd-induced MAPK/ERK signaling. (1) The interaction of
  Cd with membrane ERα leads to EGFR-mediated activation of MAPK with the assistance
  of G-proteins, Src kinase, MMP-9/-2, and HB-EGF. (2) ERα regulates adaptor protein
  Gab2 which enhances MAPK/ERK signaling. (3) Cd interacts with an un-identified membrane
  receptor that sends signal to Raf whose activity is controlled by the basal activities
  of EGFR and Src kinase.
