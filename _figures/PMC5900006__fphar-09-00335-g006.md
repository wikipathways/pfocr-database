  signaling pathway. PA induced a decrease in IGF-1R, PI3K, β catenin, and Bcl2 expression
  and a decrease in the phosphorylation of Akt and GSK3β in the hippocampus. Estradiol
  administration to adult rats submitted to PA was able to revert these changes, restoring
  the protein levels to similar values to those seen in control animals.
