caption: Signaling pathways activated by FGF/FGFR. FGFs induce dimerization, kinase
  activation and transphosphorylation of tyrosine residues of FGFRs, leading to activation
  of downstream signaling pathways. Multiple pathways are stimulated by FGF/FGFR signaling
  such as Ras-MAP kinase, PI-3 kinase/AKT and PLC-γ pathways. Furthermore, FGF signaling
  can also stimulate STAT1/p21 pathway. FGF/FGFR signaling also phosphorylates the
  Shc and Src protein. FGF/FGFR play crucial roles in the regulation of proliferation,
  differentiation and apoptosis of chondrocytes via downstream signaling pathways.
