caption: Summary of WBP2 translational implications in Hippo-dysregulated cancers.
  (A) Targeting WBP2 expression could be carried out at the transcriptional, post-transcriptional
  and post-translation levels. (B) Targeting WBP2 interacting partners could be facilitated
  by the design of small molecules that target the PPxY motifs in the C terminus of
  WBP2. This is expected to block the interaction of WBP2 with its interacting partners
  in the Hippo pathway to subvert WBP2-driven cancer progression. (C) Combinatorial
  therapy involving potential WBP2 inhibitors and other therapeutics to improve treatment
