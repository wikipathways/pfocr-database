caption: Oncogenic and tumor suppressive autophagic pathways involved in miRNAs. Autophagic
  pathways include several oncogenic pathways, such as PI3KCI/Akt/mTORC1 signaling
  pathway, Ras-Raf-MAPK cascade and BCR-ABL as well as other tumor suppressive pathways,
  for example, Beclin-1 complexes and p53 signaling. Moreover, some oncogenic miRNAs
  such as miR-18a, miR-106a, miR-181a, miR-199a-5p, miR-221/222 and miR-376b, as well
  as tumor suppressive miRNAs including miR-9*, miR-30a, miR-34a, miR-101, miR-130a,
  miR-204 and miR-375 may have their key roles in the regulation of the aforementioned
  autophagic pathways in cancer
