caption: Schematic diagram illustrating the molecular mechanism by which lactate promotes
  granulosa cell autophagy via the HIF-1α/BNIP3/Beclin-1 signaling pathway. Under
  hypoxic conditions, lactate activates the HIF-1α/BNIP3/Beclin-1 pathway, leading
  to upregulation of BNIP3. BNIP3 promotes granulosa cell autophagy by competitively
  binding to Bcl-2, thereby increasing the availability of free Beclin-1 to initiate
  the autophagic process
