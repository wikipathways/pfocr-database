caption: Schematic diagram illustrating the proposed signaling pathway involved in
  S. aureus-induced VCAM-1 expression in HPAEpiCs. S. aureus induces JNK1/2 and p38
  MAPK activation via a c-Src/PDGFR pathway, which in turn initiates the activation
  of AP-1. Activated AP-1 is recruited to the promoter regions of VCAM-1, leading
  to an increase of VCAM-1 promoter activity and the expression of VCAM-1 mRNA and
  protein in HPAEpiCs. Moreover, resveratrol can reduce lung inflammation via the
  inhibition of VCAM-1 expression, monocyte adhesion, and the activation of c-Src,
  PDGFR, JNK1/2, p38 MAPK, and AP-1 induced by S. aureus.
