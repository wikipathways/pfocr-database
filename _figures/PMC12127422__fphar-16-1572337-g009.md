  of the NLRP3 inflammasome. Moringa oleifera is an inhibitor of IL-1β production
  mediated by the NLRP3 inflammasome, acting on more than one element of the pathways
  involved (inhibition of IκBα phosphorylation and degradation; inhibition of translocation
  of the NF-κB complex to the nucleus, inhibition of NF-κB expression) and an upregulator
