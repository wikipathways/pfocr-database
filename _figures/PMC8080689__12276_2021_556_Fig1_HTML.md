caption: mTORC1 inhibition and AMPK activation positively regulate the ULK1 complex
  through a series of phosphorylation events. The ULK1 complex subsequently activates
  VPS34/PI3K complexes, which leads to PI3P synthesis and the nucleation of preautophagosomal
  structures. PI3P then recruits PI3P effector proteins, namely, WIPIs and the ATG12-ATG5-ATG16L1
  complex, which is essential for autophagosome membrane elongation. Subsequent fusion
  to lysosomes results in the degradation of a variety of substrates, such as protein
  aggregates, infectious agents, and damaged mitochondria.
