caption: Schematic presentation of the signaling pathway involved in histamine-induced
  up-regulation of H1R mRNA expression in HeLa cells. Binding of histamine to H1R
  activates PKCδ and translocates PKCδ from the cytosol to the Golgi where PKCδ phosphorylates
  MEK and ERK. Phosphorylated ERK then activates PARP-1. Poly(ADP)-ribosylation of
  an unknown protein (or PARP-1 itself) by PARP-1 causes activation of a transcription
  factor, resulting in up-regulation of H1R gene transcription.
