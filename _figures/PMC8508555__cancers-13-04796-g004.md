  causing release of Nrf2, which allows it to translocate to the nucleus. (B) SFN
  also induces epigenetic modulation of HDACs and DNMTs, causing increased Nrf2 transcription
  and translation. Consequently, the increased nuclear Nrf2 binds to ARE and maf to
