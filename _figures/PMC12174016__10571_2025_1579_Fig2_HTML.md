caption: The mechanism of therapeutic strategies targeting pyroptosis for AD. Glyburide
  binds to KATP channels, MCC950 inhibits ASC oligomerization, OLT1177 suppresses
  the ATPase activity of NLRP3, CSB6B inhibits the NF-κB signaling pathway, Salidroside
  inhibits the TLR4/NF-κB pathway or TXNIP expression, and Oridonin binds with the
  cysteine 279 of NLRP3 in NACHT domain. All these inhibitors ultimately suppress
  NLRP3 expression or activation. VX-765, Z-DEVD-FMK, DJ-1, and L7 inhibit caspase-1.
  MAF, NecrosulfonamideDJ-1, Disulfiram, and miRNA-22 inhibit GSDMD cleavage
