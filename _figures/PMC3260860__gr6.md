  Up-regulated EGR-1 can activate transcription of its target gene GGPPS, which is
  responsible for geranylation of Ras GTP-binding proteins. Thus, geranylated Ras
  can bind to the plasma membrane and reactivate the ERK 1/2 MAPK pathway.
