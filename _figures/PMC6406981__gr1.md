caption: 'Polyunsaturated fatty acid (PUFA) metabolic pathway. Animals are unable
  to endogenously synthesise linoleic (LA) or α-linolenic acid (LNA) and therefore
  these essential fatty acids must be consumed from dietary sources. Endogenous desaturases
  and elongases are responsible for conversion of LA and LNA to the key metabolites:
  arachidonic acid (AA), docosapentaenoic acid (DPA), eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA)
  and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA). Desaturase enzymes are the same for both omega-3
  and omega-6 pathways, and therefore DHA production may be limited when the LA to
  LNA ratio of the diet is high.'
