  to induce birth defects in the chick embryo as a result of signaling through the
  GR [also known as NR3C1 (nuclear receptor subfamily 3, group C, member 1)] pathway.
  Gene members of the GR pathway are shown in red (associated with embryonic development)
  or green (associated with developmental disorders). In embryos with inhibited signaling
  of the GR pathway via cortexolone, the iAs-induced birth defects are prevented.
