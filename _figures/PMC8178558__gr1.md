caption: Scheme of the DNA sensing pathway in cGAS-STING in dendritic cells and macrophages.
  In dendritic cells silica induces mitochondrial stress with the release of mtDNA
  activating the cGAS-STING pathway and release of type I interferons (IFNI), IL-1β,
  TNFα and IL-6 causing inflammation. In macrophage cell-free DNA released by phagocytosis
  of dead cells in response to silica particles activate in addition to cGAS, alternative
  DNA sensors such as IFI284 and DDX41, with the release of inflammatory mediators
  as reported [].
