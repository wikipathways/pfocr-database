caption: Synthetic lethality of PARP-inhibitors in HR-deficient tumors. Several stress
  can generate the single strand breaks (SSBs) that are repaired by poly(ADP-ribose)
  polymerases (PARPs) through the BER pathway. PARP inhibition prevent the repair
  of SSBs, resulting in the generation of double strand breaks (DSBs). The DSBs are
  repaired in cells through the functional HR-mediated DNA repair pathway, but in
  the presence of impaired HR pathway the DSBs cannot be effectively repaired resulting
  in DSB accumulation, genomic instability, and cell death.
