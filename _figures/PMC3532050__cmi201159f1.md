caption: Mechanisms of NF-κB inhibition by A20 in the TNFR1 signaling pathway. A20
  is induced by NF-κB in the TNFR1 pathway and functions in a negative feedback loop.
  The A20 adaptor molecule TAX1BP1 is phosphorylated on Ser593 and Ser624 by IKKα
  which nucleates the A20 ubiquitin-editing complex and is essential for interactions
  between TAX1BP1, Itch, RNF11 and A20. The A20 ubiquitin-editing complex inhibits
  RIP1 K63-linked polyubiquitination to terminate NF-κB signaling downstream of TNFR1.
  IKK, IκB kinase; RNF11, ring finger protein 11; TAX1BP1, Tax1-binding protein 1;
  TNFR1, TNF receptor 1.
