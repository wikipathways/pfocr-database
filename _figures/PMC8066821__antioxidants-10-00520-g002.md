caption: The biosynthetic (transsulfuration) and catabolic (oxidation) pathways of
  H2S. Transsulfuration pathway involves the enzymes cystathionine β-synthase (CBS),
  cystathionine γ-lyase (CSE) and pyridoxal-5′-phosphate (PLP)-independent 3-mercaptopyruvate
  sulfurtransferase (3MST). Mitochondrial H2S oxidation pathway involves the enzymes
  sulfide:quinone oxidoreductase (SQOR), sulfur dioxygenase (SDO; also known as ETHE1
  or persulfide dioxygenase), sulfite oxidase (SO), thiosulfate sulfurtransferase
  or rhodanese (TST) and thiosulfate reductase (TR).
