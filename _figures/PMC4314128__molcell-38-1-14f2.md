  through the activation of Wnt/PCP pathway by interacting with CNK1 and Dsh. In addition,

  complex, which has a major role in stabilizing TJs. Finally, accumulation of unphosphorylated
  ephrinB1 in TJs causes disruption of TJs and cell adhesion.
