caption: 'Upper part: In nutrient-sufficiency conditions, the presence of serum, amino
  acids, or glucose would foster the PB1-like interaction between Gαq and p62, leading
  to basal mTORC1 stimulation and preservation of homeostatic autophagy levels. Upon
  starvation, Gαq/p62 dissociation would facilitate the PB1-domain-dependent participation
  of p62 in the autophagic pathway. The release of internal nutrients during autophagy
  would in turn facilitate Gαq/p62/mTORC1 complex formation and reactivation in order
  to terminate autophagy. Lower part: Autophagy levels diagram. In response to starvation,
  there is a controlled and transient upregulation of autophagy, with a subsequent
  return to basal levels upon the partial recovery of nutrients.'
