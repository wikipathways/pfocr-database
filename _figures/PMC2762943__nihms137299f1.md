caption: 'Epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR) pathway. Ligands, such as epidermal
  growth factor (EGF), transforming growth factor (TGF)-α, or others, bind to the
  homo- and heterodimer kinase domain (TK), resulting in activation and receptor transphosphorylation.
  This creates docking sites for the adaptor proteins, Grb2 and Sos, which recruit
  Ras and phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase (PI3K), leading to the formation of two major
  signalling pathway branches, Ras/MAPK and PI3K/Akt. These networks result in, amongst
  others, proliferation, evasion of apoptosis and angiogenesis. MAPK: mitogen-activated
  kinase-like protein.'
