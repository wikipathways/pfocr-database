caption: Effect of BPA on the translocation of GLUT4 in muscle. The administration
  of BPA alters the proteins involved in the translocation of GLUT4, resulting in
  a lower disposition of glucose. BPA alters the insulin-dependent dose translation
  signaling and therefore alters the translocation of GLUT4 from the cytosol to plasma,
  making the gastrocnemius muscle an insulin-resistant tissue and consequently generates
  an elevation of blood glucose and the consequent increase in circulating insulin
  levels [].
