  to IDC. E2 induced basement membrane disruption in breast glandular ducts by promoting
  MMP-3 and IL-1β secretion via the GPER signaling pathway. In turn, the secreted
  IL-1β activated the IL-1R1/MyD88 signaling pathway to increase IL-1β and MMP-3 expression.
  Finally, in our model, estradiol induces apoptosis and pyroptosis of epithelial
  cells, thereby disrupting the glandular ducts and promoting DCIS metastasis to IDC.
