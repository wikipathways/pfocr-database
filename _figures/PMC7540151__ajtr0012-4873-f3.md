caption: 'Regulation of EMT by miR-187. TGFβ is an inducer of EMT via Smad and non-Smad
  pathways, such as MAPK and PI3K/AKT. MiR-187 activated the mitogen-activated protein
  kinase (MAPK) pathway through the phosphorylation of ERK and phosphatidylinositol
  3-kinase/protein kinase B (PI3K/AKT) signaling via the phosphorylation of AKT. MiR-187
  inhibited the expression of TGFβ by inhibiting the three target genes of SOX4, NT5E
  and PTK6. TGFβ works positively by promoting Smad2 phosphorylation, thereby activating
  Smad signaling and inducing EMT. In addition to PTRT, ZEB2, Dab2 and S100A4, the
  three downstream target genes of miR-187, all promoted EMT. EMT: epithelial-to-mesenchymal
  transition.'
