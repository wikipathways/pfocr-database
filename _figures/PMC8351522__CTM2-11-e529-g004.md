caption: The mitochondrial control system can be regulated by multiple dimensional
  molecular networks. OMA1, YME1L, and PARL are inner membrane proteases to regulate
  mitochondrial fusion by cleaving the OPA1. HUWE1, MARCH5, and Parkin are ubiquitin‐ligase
  enzymes to contribute to mitophagy or directly affected the activity of MFN1/2 or
  DRP1. In addition, DRP1 could be regulated by INF2, and spire1C, MAPL, calcineurin,
  which was related to mitochondrial fission and mitophagy. PKA/AMPK signaling pathway
  could regulate PINK/Parkin and affect mitophagy
