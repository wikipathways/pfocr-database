caption: The signaling pathway of NOD1/2 and most TLRs are interconnected through
  complex pathways. Once stimulated with microbial ligands, NOD1 and NOD2 associate
  with RIP2 via a CARD-CARD interaction. This sets off the downstream cascade that
  can activate NF-κB by releasing P65/P50 subunit for entry into the nucleus and/or
  activation of AP1, resulting in the release of proinflammatory cytokines and chemokines.
