caption: The possible mechanism of anti-inflammatory effect of OEA on colitis. PPAR-α
  is highly expressed in intestinal epithelial cells and macrophages. OEA inhibits
  phosphorylation of IκBα and p65, and suppresses the activation of the NF-κB signaling
  pathway, resulting in the reduction of the expression of IL-8 and IL-1β in epithelial
  cells. IL-8 reduction leads to suppression of neutrophil infiltration, and IL-1β
  reduction leads to suppression of inflammatory reaction in the colon.
