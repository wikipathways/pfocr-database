caption: A schematic representation of the molecular mechanism proposed for the induction
  of apoptosis by chrysotile asbestos in A549 human bronchoalveolar carcinoma cells.
  The results from the present study support a pathway by which chrysotile asbestos
  induces apoptosis via the activation of c-Jun N-terminal kinase (JNK), resulting
  in mitochondrial disruption, the release of cytochrome c and finally the activation
  of a cascade of caspases. JNK, c-Jun N-terminal kinase; MAPK, mitogen-activated
  protein kinases; BAX, B cell lymphoma-2 (Bcl-2) associated X protein; BAK, Bcl-2
  homologous antagonist killer; Cyt C, cytochrome c; PARP, poly (ADP-ribose) polymerase;
  Sp00125, JNK inhibitor.
