caption: Model for ovarian cancer ascites-mediated protection from TRAIL-induced apoptosis.
  TRAIL binding to death receptors TRAIL-R1 and TRAIL-R2 results in death- inducing-signaling
  complex (DISC) formation, activation of procas-pase-8, Bid cleavage, release of
  cytochrome c from mitochondria, and caspase-9 and caspase-3 activation. Binding
  of ascites survival factors to avβ5 integrin leads to the phosphorylation of FAK
  and Akt. Akt activation up-regulates the levels of c-FLIP protein leading to inhibition
  of caspase-8. Ascites also activate the ERK pathway leading to up-regulation of
  the anti-apoptotic Mcl-1 protein.
