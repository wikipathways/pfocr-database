caption: Regulation of cancer hallmarks by the crosstalk between VEGF and Rho GTPase
  signaling pathways. The formation of cytoplasmic structures, cell migration, invasion,
  EMT, ROS production, and cell proliferation can be modulated by binding of VEGFA,
  VEGFB, or VEGFC to their corresponding VEGFR or NRP receptors. VEGFC interacts with
  Rho GTPases to upregulate Src and p38 MAP kinase activity. VEGFA and VEGFB modulation
  of cancer hallmarks involves the activation of Rho GTPases and their downstream
  effectors (ROCK, mDia, and p27) as well as the MAPK pathway.
