caption: Influences of DAB or LPS on the TREM1 signaling pathway. DAB/LPS activates
  TREM1 and initiates the classical pathway of complement activation. TREM1 interacts
  with DAP-12, activates inflammasome Casp1 and Casp5, and induces IL-1β. The activations
  of TLR4 and complementary components (e.g., C3a and C5a) enhance MYD88 and NF-κB.
  Consequently, pro-inflammatory response induces the nuclear transcriptions of cytokines,
  chemokines, and cell surface molecules.
