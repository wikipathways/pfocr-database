caption: 'Schematic overview of the mechanisms whereby exosomes derived from ischemic
  heart extract-pretreated MSCs can promote HUVEC migration and proliferation. Ischemic
  heart extract-derived IL-22 can activate the IL22RA1/STAT3/DMBT1/VEGF pathway within
  MSCs. The activation of p-STAT3 also induced HIF-1α/β-catenin/VEGF pathway activation.
  HUVECs were then able to take up DMBT1 and VEGF-enriched MSC-Exos, with elevated
  DMBT1 levels promoting PI3K-Akt/GSK3β/β-catenin/VEGF signaling within these cells,
  thereby enhancing their migratory and proliferative activity. MSC: mesenchymal stem
  cell; HUVEC: human umbilical vein endothelial cell; HIF-1α: hypoxia inducible factor–1α;
  DMBT1: deleted in malignant brain tumors 1; VEGF: vascular endothelial growth factor.'
