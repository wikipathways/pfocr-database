caption: Inhibition of IFN α/β (type I IFN) signaling pathways by DENV virus. The
  pathway of formation for IFN-stimulated gene complex 3 (ISGF3) before translocating
  into the nucleus to induce IFN-stimulated genes by binding with IFN-stimulated response
  element (ISRE) is illustrated. DENV antagonizes this pathway by either inhibiting
  the active phosphorylation of STAT1 (by NS2A, NS4A and NS4B) or by direct degradation
  of STAT2 (by NS5). This illustrates mechanisms will occur in the absence of enhancing
  antibodies. IFNAR1, Interferon alpha and beta receptor subunit 1; IFNAR2, Interferon
  alpha and beta receptor subunit 2; Tyk2, Tyrosine kinase 2; JAK1, Janus kinase 1;
  IRF9, Interferon regulatory factor 9; NS2A, Non-structural 2A protein; NS4A, Non-structural
  4A protein; NS4B, Non-structural 4B protein; NS5, Non-structural 5 protein; STAT1,
  Signal transducer and activator of transcription 1; STAT2, Signal transducer and
  activator of transcription 2; ISGs, Interferon-stimulated genes.
