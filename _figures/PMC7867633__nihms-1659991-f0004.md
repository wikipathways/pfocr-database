caption: Role of signaling pathways in SMA. Multiple signaling cascades are impacted
  by insulin-like growth factor 1 (IGF-1) and its receptor, both are aberrantly expressed
  in SMA. Prolactin (PRL) and human growth hormone (GH) enhance SMN2 transcription
  by activating JAK/STAT pathway. Ca2+/calmodulin-dependent protein kinase (CaMK)
  can also enhance SMN2 transcription through recruitment of cyclic AMP response element
  binding protein (CREB) to the SMN2 promoter. Details of pathways depicted here are
