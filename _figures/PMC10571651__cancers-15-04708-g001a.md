  Cbl binding leads to ubiquitination. Upon ubiquitination, the MET receptor is degraded,
  and signal transduction ceases. (B) Oncogenesis pathway: when exon 14 is skipped
  and Cbl fails to bind to its receptor and is not ubiquitinated, or when the signaling
  system is amplified by MET amplification or chromosome fusion, it continuously activates
  tyrosine kinases and causes oncogenesis. Savolitinib inhibits this MET signaling
  pathway.'
