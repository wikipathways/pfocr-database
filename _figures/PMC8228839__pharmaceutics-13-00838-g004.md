caption: β-arrestin2 affects the production of NO. Along with GRK2, β-arrestin2 inhibits
  phosphorylation of the Akt/eNOS pathway, which decreases the amount of NO production.
  Furthermore, β-arrestin2 impedes NF-κB signaling by stabilizing IκBα, resulting
  in a decrease in NOS II expression and eventually decreasing the rate of NO production.
  Altogether, the result is increased blood pressure.
