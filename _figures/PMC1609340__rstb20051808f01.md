  TLRs, proximal adapters, signalling machinery and transcription factors. The phosphorylation
  (P) or ubiquitination (U) states of molecules are indicated, as are the known interactions
  (lines) between molecules or complexes. Arrows indicate interfaces between signalling
  and the activation of transcription. The colour-coding of the signalling molecules
  indicates the specificity (and overlap) in their use by the different TLRs. For
  example, TLR3 uses TRIF, and TLR9 uses MyD88, while TLR4 uses TRIF, TIRAP and MyD88.
