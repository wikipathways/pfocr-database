caption: Endothelial to mesenchymal transition (EndoMT) in PAH. EndoMT in PAH is thought
  to be an important process contributing to vascular remodeling. Activation by transcriptional
  factors, hypoxia, haemodynamic forces, inflammation, and TGF-β/BMP pathway signaling
  pulmonary endothelial cells (PAECs) undergo cellular transition to a mesenchymal
  phenotype, in which PAECs lose endothelial markers and gain mesenchymal markers,
  such as αSMA and TWIST. These mesenchymal-like cells present an invasive character
  and hence contribute to vascular remodeling in PAH.
