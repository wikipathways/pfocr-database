caption: lncRNAs in Wnt/β‐catenin signalling. UCA1 down‐regulates expression of GSK3β,
  resulting in the subsequent increase in stabilization and translocation of its substrate
  β‐catenin into the nucleus. Both UCA1 and CCAT2 are able to up‐regulate the expression
  of β‐catenin, leading to activation of downstream target genes. MALAT1 expression
  is induced by canonical Wnt/β‐catenin signalling, leading to an increase in cancer
  cell proliferative potential. NBAT1 modulates the canonical Wnt/β‐catenin signalling
  by inducing the expression of DKK1, a well‐known antagonist of the Wnt/β‐catenin
  signalling pathway.
