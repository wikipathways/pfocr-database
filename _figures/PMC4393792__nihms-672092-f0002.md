caption: Proliferating tumor cells deplete critical nutrients such as tryptophan (Trp)
  or glucose. The increased metabolic activity of tumor cells results in high concentration
  of metabolites such as lactate or kynurenines (Kyn), which can activate the Aryl
  Hydrocarbon Receptor (AHR). Both nutrient depletion and metabolite production negatively
  impact T cell effector function, metabolism, and survival by modulation of the PI3K/Akt/mTOR
  pathway, AMPK, and SirT1. In addition, immunosuppressive regulatory T cells (Treg)
  are favored in tumor microenvironment. Kyn, kynurenines; Trp, tryptophan; AHR; Aryl
  Hydrocarbon Receptor.
