caption: The agonist (e.g., acetylcholine, carbachol, pilocarpine, etc.) binds muscarinic
  receptors. M1, M3, and M5 receptors couple the PLC signaling pathway with a stimulatory
  effect (+) that triggers intracellular calcium release (↑ Ca2+). M2 and M4 couple
  the AC signaling pathway with an inhibitory effect (-) that induces cAMP decrease
  (↓ cAMP). While non-tumorigenic ovarian cells have no response to cholinergic stimulation,
  in ovarian cancer cells the activation of muscarinic receptors determines the intracellular
  calcium release (↑ Ca2+) and has a mitogenic action.
