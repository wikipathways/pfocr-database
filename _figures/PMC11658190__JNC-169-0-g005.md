caption: 'Dual inhibition mechanism of the p53 pathway by Olig2. Olig2 suppresses
  the transcriptional activity of p53, thereby inhibiting the expressions of p53 target
  genes. Furthermore, by stabilizing the Ddx20 and SMN complex, Olig2 facilitates
  the correct splicing of Mdm2, which in turn promotes the degradation of p53. Thus,
  the Olig2 transcription factor suppresses p53 through two pathways: transcription
  regulation and RNA metabolism regulation via Ddx20. This dual pathway of inhibition
  contributes to the proliferation of neural stem/progenitor cells (NSPCs) and oligodendrocyte
  progenitor cells (OPCs)'
