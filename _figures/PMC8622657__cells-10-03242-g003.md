caption: FGF signaling pathway. When a ligand interacts with an FGFR, it causes conformational
  changes in the receptor, leading to receptor dimerization and thus FGFR activation.
  When FGFR is activated, FRS2a is phosphorylated, causing FGFR substrate to attach
  to the Src Homology 2 (SH2) domain-containing adaptor growth factor receptor-bound
  protein 2 (GRB2). GRB2 binds SOS, GRB2-associated-binding protein 1 (GAB1), and
  Casitas B-cell lymphoma (Cbl) protein via the SH3 domain and stimulates Ras/Raf
  and mitogen-activated protein kinases (MAPK), including ERK, p38, and JNK. In addition
  to MAP kinases, STAT, PI3K, and PLCγ pathway is also activated.
