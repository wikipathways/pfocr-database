caption: The hierarchy of repair pathways choice in mammalian cell. NHEJ (nonhomologous
  end joining) is the first choice DSB (double strands break) repair pathway in mammalian
  cells, occurring in all cell cycle phase. Preliminary end resection is necessary
  for the DSB repair according to HDR (homology-directed repair), SSA (single-strand
  annealing), and alt-NHEJ (alternative NHEJ) mechanisms. Resection during alt-NHEJ
  is relatively short, and extended resection favors HDR. SSA can function opportunistically
  on ssDNA (single strand DNA) ends or on recombination intermediates, especially
  RAD51, by hijacking the HDR process.
