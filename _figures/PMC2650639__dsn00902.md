caption: 'Proposed glycerol and glucose metabolic pathways in L. reuteri JCM 1112T.
  The pathways are color coded as follows: blue, glycerol metabolism; green, biosynthesis
  of reuterin; red, glucose metabolism. Dashed lines indicate an unidentified enzyme
  in L. reuteri JCM 1112T. The bottom part of the figure shows the structure of the
  pdu-cbi-cob-hem gene cluster in L. reuteri JCM 1112T. Genes are depicted with arrows
  indicating the transcription direction with the following colors: yellow, pdu including
  gupCDE genes; pink, cbi genes; orange, cob genes; blue, hem genes; red, pocR; green,
  eut; sky blue, transposase gene; and white, other genes. Several lines connect corresponding
  genes between the pathway and the cluster (enzymes, red; transporters, blue).'
