caption: Regulation of cell signaling pathways involved in cancers by KDM2B. First,
  the activation of FGF-2-KDM2B-EZH2 pathway contributes to cell proliferation, migration,
  angiogenesis and self-renewal of cancer stem cells. Second, KDM2B inhibits cell
  apoptosis by repressing c-Fos/c-FLIP pathway. Finally, KDM2B also activates PI3K/Akt/mTOR
  pathway and inhibits P53 pathway in cancers. In contrast, KDM2B also inhibits Wnt/β-catenin
  signaling pathway by inducing degradation of β-catenin.
