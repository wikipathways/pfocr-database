caption: TGF-β ligands bind to TGF-β type II receptors (TGFBR2), subsequently recruiting
  and phosphorylating TGF-β type I (TGFBR1). The phosphorylated TGFBR1 phosphorylates
  the signaling proteins SMAD2 and SMAD3. Following phosphorylation, they form a complex
  with SMAD4. The complex translocates to the nucleus and regulates downstream gene
  expression. Inhibitors, termed I-SMADs, attach directly to TGFBR1 and intercept
  the phosphorylation of SMADs or hinder the formation of the SMAD2, 3/SMAD-4 complex.
