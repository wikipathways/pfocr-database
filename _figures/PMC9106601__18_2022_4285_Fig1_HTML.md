caption: Key components of the canonical WNT signaling pathway. a Posttranslational
  modification and secretion of WNT ligands. Porcupine conjugates palmitoleate residues
  to WNTs, whereas NOTUM removes them. b ON-and OFF-state of canonical WNT signaling.
  In the absence of WNT ligands β-catenin is kept in the degradosome, while WNT activation
  provokes formation of a signalosome at the membrane, elevation of cytoplasmic β-catenin
  levels, its nuclear recruitment and formation of the β-catenin-dependent TCF/LEF
  enhanceosome. APC adenomatous polyposis coli, BCL-9 B-cell lymphoma 9, BRG1 Brahma-related
  gene-1, CBP CREB-binding protein, CK1 casein kinase 1, CtBP C-terminal-binding protein,
  DKK Dickkopf, DVL Dishevelled, HDAC histone deacetylase, GSK3 glycogen synthase
  kinase 3, LEF lymphoid enhancer-binding factor, LRP5/6 low density lipoprotein receptor-related
