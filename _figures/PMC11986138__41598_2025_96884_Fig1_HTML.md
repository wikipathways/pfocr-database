caption: 'Expression changes of genes associated with protein processing in the endoplasmic
  reticulum by the correction of LRRK2 by BAC DNA. The changes in gene expression
  associated with the KEGG pathway "Protein Processing in the Endoplasmic Reticulum"
  (KEGG ID: 04141) in NSCs harboring the LRRK2 G2019S mutation, comparing cells corrected
  with BAC DNA to uncorrected controls at passage 2. Genes are color-coded based on
  log2 fold change (log2FC) values, with red indicating upregulation and green indicating
  downregulation. Key changes include the upregulation of chaperones (Hsp40, Hsp70),
  protein disulfide isomerases (PDIs), and oligosaccharyltransferases (OSTs), along
  with the downregulation of the ASK1-MKK7-JNK signaling pathway'
