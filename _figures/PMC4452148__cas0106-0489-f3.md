caption: Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway. (a) Without ligands, the Axin/adenomatous
  polyposis coli (APC)/glycogen synthase kinase-3β (GSK-3β) complex phosphorylates
  β-catenin. Then β-catenin is ubiquitinated and degraded. In nucleus, Groucho/histone
  deacetylase (HDAC) and C-terminal binding protein (CtBP) bind to the Tcf/Lef complex
  and suppresses transcription. Nemo-like kinase (NLK) inhibits binding of the Tcf/Lef
  complex to the promoter region. Duplin and inhibitor of β-catenin and TCF-4 (ICAT)
  inhibit binding of β-catenin and the Tcf/Lef complex. (b) When Wnt binds to the Frizzled
  (Frz)/low-density lipoprotein receptor-related protein (LRP)5·6 receptor complex,
  phosphorylation and ubiquitination of β-catenin are inhibited and β-catenin is stabilized.
  Then β-catenin is transported to nucleus and binds to the Tcf/Lef complex and activates
  transcription of target genes. CREB binding protein (CBP)/p300 is a histone acetyl
  transferase and activates transcription. Pygopus/Lgs stabilizes β-catenin. Dkk,
  Dickkopf; SFRP, secreted Frizzled-related protein.
