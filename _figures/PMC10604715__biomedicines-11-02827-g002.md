caption: 'Polyamine degradation pathway under normal conditions. SPM and SPD are converted
  to N1-acetylspermine and N1-acetylspermidine, respectively, via the enzymatic activity
  of SSAT. N1-acetylpolyamine oxidase (APAO) preferentially catalyzes the oxidation
  of N1-acetylspermine and N1-acetylspermidine to SPD and putrescine, respectively.
  This degradation process produces non-toxic 3-acetamidopropanal. Abbreviations:
  SPM, spermine; SPD, spermidine; SSAT, spermidine/spermine N-(1)-acetyltransferase;
  APAO, N1-acetylpolyamine oxidase.'
