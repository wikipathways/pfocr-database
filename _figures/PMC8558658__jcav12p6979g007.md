  in melanoma cells. Combined with the previous and the present study, we depict that
  Hey1 promotes the expression of GRB2. GRB2 recruits and activates phosphatidylinositol-3-kinase
  (PI3K) to produce second messenger PIP3 by binding with Gab1 (GRB2 associated binding
  protein). PIP3 then phosphorylates and activates AKT, which acts on P21, thus affecting
  the biological process of the tumor.
