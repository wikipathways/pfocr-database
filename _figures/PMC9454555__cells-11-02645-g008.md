  SIRT4 in MGCs. Under normal conditions, SIRT4 depletion in MGCs inhibits ATP generation

  autophagy. Under oxidative stress, dysfunctional autophagy can induce disturbed
  mitochondrial dynamics by increasing mitochondrial fission and decreasing fusion,
  leading to MGC apoptosis.
