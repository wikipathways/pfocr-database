caption: Schematic representation of TLR4 inflammatory pathway. When LPS, bound by
  LBP, interacts with TLR4, phosphorylation of MAPK occurs through MyD88-dependent
  pathway. TAK1 and AKT phosphorylate IκB, leading to nuclear translocation of NF-κB.
  This process activates transcription factors such as AP-1, ATF-2, and P65 Rela in
  nucleus, which induce expression of inflammatory mediators including TNF-α, IL-6,
  iNOS, and COX-2. These mediators subsequently drive inflammatory response
