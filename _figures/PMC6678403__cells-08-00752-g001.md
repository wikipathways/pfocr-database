  Abbreviations: Dll4, Delta Like Canonical Notch Ligand 4; Jagged1, a Notch ligand

  1 and 2, Notch target proteins; Hes1, hairy and enhancer of split-1, a Notch target
  protein; Ids, inhibitor of DNA binding proteins; VEGF, vascular endothelial growth
