  to form membrane pores and triggers IL-18/IL-1β release. The non-classical pathway
  involves LPS-induced activation of pro-caspase-4/-5/-11, which cleave GSDMD to generate
  membrane pores, thereby promoting the release of IL-18 and IL-1β
