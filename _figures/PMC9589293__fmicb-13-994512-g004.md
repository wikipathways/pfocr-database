  top pathways constructed. (A) The “lipid metabolism, molecular transport, and small
  molecule biochemistry” network. (B) The “cell death and survival, humoral immune
  response, and inflammatory response” network. Only the top two networks are shown.
  Additional networks, and their associated proteins, are listed in . Protein levels

  in gray, direct interactions shown with solid lines, and indirect interactions depicted
  with dashed lines. Molecular types as indicated in key.
