  modifying signal transduction pathways in breast cancer and thyroid cells, based
  on the results of the inhibitor studies. The IGF-I receptor/PI3K pathway is likely
  responsible for the tRA-induction of NIS through formation of RAR/RXR heterodimers.
  The p38 MAPK cascade is required for NIS expression in both cells types. Pax8, Paired
  box protein-8; CREB, cAMP-responsive element binding protein; Rap1, Ras-related
