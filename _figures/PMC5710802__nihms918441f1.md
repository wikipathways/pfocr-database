  damaged mitochondrial DNA) binds to cGAS, leading the synthesis of cGAMP from ATP
  and GTP. cGAMP binds to STING dimers, resulting in STING phosphorylation and activation,
  further triggering downstream pathways for production of IFN-I and proinflammatory
  cytokines.
