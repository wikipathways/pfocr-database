caption: 'A model of miR-499-dependent anti-fibrotic signaling pathway. Overall proposed
  model of the anti-fibrotic mechanism of miR-499-5p in skeletal muscles in Duchenne
  muscular dystrophy (DMD). The activation of TGF β signaling pathway induces fibrosis.
  Our findings suggest that MMP-1 secreted from WJ-MSCs increases miR-499 expression
  level. Increased miR-499-5p down-regulates to the TGFβ receptor I and blocks smad2/3
  phosphorylation, leading to fibrotic muscle recovery by the inactivated TGFβ signaling
  pathway (: block, : inactivation).'
