caption: 'Interaction mechanism between intestinal flora and cAMP-PKA signaling pathway
  in neurological diseases.The intestinal flora influences the cAMP-PKA signaling
  pathway through the regulation of metabolites, neurotransmitter precursors, key
  synthetic enzymes, metabolic enzymes, and receptors. Activation of the cAMP-PKA
  signaling pathway can subsequently impact the synthesis and release of neurotransmitters
  that can, in turn, feedback on the composition and function of the intestinal microbiota.
  Together, these interactions may play a significant role in neurological diseases.
