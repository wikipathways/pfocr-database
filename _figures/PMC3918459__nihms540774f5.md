  (a) Synthetic cofactors applied for inhibition of chromatin-modifying enzymes. Cofactor-derived
  portions of each molecule are highlighted in gold. (b) NAD+ salvage pathway and
  NAMPT inhibitors. Blockade of NAD+ salvage has been shown to deplete cellular NAD+
  levels and inhibit SIRT HDAC activity. (c) Chemical inducer of dimerization approach
  used to localize genomic function of HP1, which stimulates local KMT and DNMT activity.
