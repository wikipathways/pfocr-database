caption: Leptin receptor signaling is mediated by JAK-STAT, PI3K-Akt, and SHP2-ERK
  pathway. Leptin binds to ObRb and activates JAK2, and induced to the phosphorylation
  of Tyr985, Tyr1077, and Tyr1138 of ObRb. PY-985, PY-1077, and PY-1138 bind to their
  downstream molecules and proceeds to phosphorylation of JAK2-STAT3, JAk2-STAT5,
  PI3K-IRS-Akt, SHP2-ERK pathways. These signaling are negatively regulated by SOCS3.
  Dysregulation of the leptin receptor signaling is involved in the onset of leptin
  resistance, inflammation and cancer.
