caption: NF-κB signaling pathways downstream of TNFR1, TLR4 and TCR/CD28. TNFR1, TLR4
  and TCR/CD28 engagement trigger the K63-linked ubiquitination of RIP1, TRAF6 and
  MALT1, respectively. Each of these ubiquitinated signaling molecules are targeted
  for deubiquitination by either A20, CYLD, Cezanne or USP21, as indicated. MALT1
  cleaves A20 to inactivate its function. CYLD also removes polyubiquitin chains from
  TAK1 in the TCR pathway.
