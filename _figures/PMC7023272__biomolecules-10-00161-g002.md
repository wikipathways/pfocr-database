caption: Resveratrol (RES) crosses the blood‒brain barrier via tight junctions [].

  cancer cells, RES exerts proapoptotic activities via influencing the cell cycle.
  RES induces oxidative stress, leading to autophagy. Moreover, RES influences cancer
  cells via various signaling mechanisms, including the PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway, Wnt,
  or STAT3.
