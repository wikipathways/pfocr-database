caption: Schematic model of the mechanism by which Tp0136 modulates platelet signaling.
  Tp0136 promotes human platelet activation and aggregation through PAR1 and generates
  an S42FLLRN-tethered ligand that interacts with the receptor to induce PAR1 signaling.
  In particular, Tp0136 activates the Gqsignaling pathway through PAR1, thereby inducing
  PAR1-dependent Akt-PI3K activation, p38 phosphorylation and Ca2+ flux, and the Gipathway,
  thereby attenuating the release of cytosolic cAMP.
