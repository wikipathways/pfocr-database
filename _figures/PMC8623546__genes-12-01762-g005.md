caption: 'Regulation of phosphoinositide 3-kinases (PI3K)/protein kinase B (Akt)/mammalian
  target of rapamycin (mTOR) signalling in renal cilia: (A) The PC1 CTT oversees the
  membrane trafficking of tuberculosis sclerosis complex 2 (TSC2), essential for homeostatic
  (negative) regulation of mTOR pathway. (B) Graphical representation of PI3K-AKT
  kinases and its link to mTOR pathway. Upon flow, the expression of tumor suppressor
  kinase Liver Kinase B1 (LKB1) increases which activates AMP-activated protein kinase
  (AMPK). AMPK negatively regulates mTORC1. Expression of downstream effectors like
  ribosomal protein S6 kinase B1 (pS6K) is enhanced upon mTOR activation. This leads
  to proliferation, cell growth, and increased protein synthesis. (Modified and redrawn
