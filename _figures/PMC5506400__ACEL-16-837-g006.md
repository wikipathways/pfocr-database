caption: Proposed mechanisms by which kallistatin inhibits endothelial senescence
  and aging. Kallistatin via its heparin‐binding site antagonizes TNF‐α‐mediated miR‐21
  synthesis, ROS formation, PAI‐1 and p16INK4a expression, and telomerase activity
  in EPCs. Kallistatin via its active site prevents miR‐34a‐mediated inhibition of
  antioxidant genes SIRT1, eNOS, and catalase and SOD‐3. The tyrosine kinase inhibitor,
  genistein, blocks kallistatin's ability to modulate miR‐34a and antioxidant gene
  expression.
