  metastasis. FSS stimulation upregulates a series of cytokines or mechanosensitive
  molecules, such as IGF-2, VEGF and Cav-1, activating PI3K/AKT, c-Jun and NF-kB pathway
  and promoting invasion and metastasis; FSS can also combine integrin receptors,
  activating the ROCK-LIMK-cofilin signaling axis, inducing nuclear translocation
  of YAP1 and promoting invasion and metastasis.
