caption: Potential sources and consequences of nucleotide-binding and oligomerization-like
  receptor pyrin domain-containing protein 3 (NLRP3) activation signaling pathway
  during myocardial ischemia and reperfusion (MI/R). MI/R promotes potassium efflux,
  which directly increases the expression of NIMA-related kinase 7 (NEK7) to form
  the NLRP3 complex. MI/R promotes sodium-hydron exchange and sodium-calcium exchange
  and sarcoplasmic reticulum and endoplasmic reticulum release calcium. Calcium overload
  causes mitochondrial instability and NLRP3 inflammasome activation. MI/R promotes
  mitochondrial dysfunction and reactive oxygen species (ROS) production. ROS activates
  the NLRP3 inflammasomes by regulating nuclear factor-κB, caspase-11, and thioredoxin-interacting
  protein. Adobe Illustrator CC 2019 (Adobe Software, San Jose, California, United
