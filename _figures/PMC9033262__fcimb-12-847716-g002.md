caption: Autophagy induced by PI3K-Akt-mTOR signaling pathway in H. pylori-associated
  gastric cancer. When infected with H. pylori, Akt mediates beclin-1 phosphorylation,
  which activates the autophagy system, increases Vimentin and 14-3-3 protein simultaneously.
  Increase the expression of Vimentin can induce autophagy, and then promote the Akt-induced
  carcinogenesis. When Beclin-1 gene is mutated, autophagy can still be activated
  via PI3K-Akt-mTOR if Akt-mediated phosphorylation fails.
