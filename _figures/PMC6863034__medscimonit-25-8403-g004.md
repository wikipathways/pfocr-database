caption: '(A–C) The most significance predictive genes for renal ischemia-reperfusion
  injury (IRI) in the rat model. The Molecular Complex Detection (MCODE) algorithm
  showed that MCODE 1 contained 11 gene nodes, including ITGAM, IGF1, MYC, THBS1,
  TIMP1, LOX, MMP3, CCL2, STAT3, CXCl1, and CD44 with 53 edges. All 11 predictive
  genes in module 1 were analyzed by Gene Ontology (GO) and the Kyoto Encyclopedia
  of Genes and Genomes (KEGG) database, which identified the HIF-1α signaling pathway.
  Differentially expressed genes (DEGs) were analyzed using the Molecular Complex
  Detection (MCODE) algorithm. MCODE 1: 10.600. MCODE 2: 5.250. MCODE 3: 5.000.'
