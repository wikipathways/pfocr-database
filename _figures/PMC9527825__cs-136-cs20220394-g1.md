caption: 'Based on the ‘seed and soil’ hypothesis, the mechanisms can be divided into
  two parts. First, ANGPTL1 weakens the CRC cells: 1. ANGPTL1 blocks the EMT program
  of CRC cells by miR-138/Twist2 pathway or inhibiting Slug. 2. ANGPTL1 suppresses
  the CRC stemness via Foxo3a/Sox2 pathway. Second, ANGPTL1 regulates the TME. 3.
  ANGPTL1 inhibits tumor angiogenesis through Erk1/2 or Akt inactivation. 4. ANGPTL1
  packaged in exosomes is uptake by KCs, thus inactivating the JAK2-STAT3 signaling
  pathway and down-regulating the MMP9 expression in KCs, leading to less vascular
  leakiness in liver PMNs.'
