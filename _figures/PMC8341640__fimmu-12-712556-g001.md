caption: PARPs regulate cGAS-STING-mediated innate immune responses. PARP1, PARP2,
  and PARP3 participate in the DNA repair process upon the DNA damage response (DDR)
  caused by DNA double-strand breaks (DSB) or DNA single-strand breaks (SSB). The
  treatment of PARP inhibitor (PARPi) prevents DNA repair, resulting in the accumulation
  of damaged DNA, which is recognized by cyclic GMP-AMP (cGAMP) synthase (cGAS) in
  the cytoplasm. The induction of type I interferon (IFN-I) is mediated by cAMP-STING-IRF3/NF-κB
  signaling pathway.
