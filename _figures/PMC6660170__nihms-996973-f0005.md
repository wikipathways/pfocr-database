caption: Nanoparticle-mediated autophagy. (a) Autophagy involves the sequestration
  and subsequent degradation of nanomaterials. After entrance into the cytoplasm,
  nanomaterials may be sequestered and deposited within autophagosomes, and fusion
  of autophagosomes with lysosomes will form autolysosomes, which will result in the
  breakdown and degradation of encapsulated foreign or aberrant nanomaterials. (b)
  Nanomaterials may also induce autophagy through upregulating oxidative stress and/or
  affecting the intracellular signaling pathways (e.g., Akt-mTOR signaling pathway)
  which may regulate the expression of genes/proteins necessary for autophagosome
