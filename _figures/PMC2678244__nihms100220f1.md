caption: Abl kinases are activated in response to TCR stimulation in a pathway that
  requires Lck kinase activity. The activated Abl kinases promote the phosphorylation
  of ZAP-70 and LAT leading to activation of IL-2 transcription. Abl kinases also
  regulate actin polymerization through the Abi/Wave complex and HS1. The Wave complex
  promotes actin polymerization downstream of activated Rac leading to Arp2/3-mediated
  actin polymerization. The role of Abi and Wave phosphorylation by Abl is unclear.
  HS1 is also phosphorylated by Abl and regulates F-actin stabilization.
