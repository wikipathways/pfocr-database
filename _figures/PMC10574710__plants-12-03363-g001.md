caption: Overview of the centelloside biosynthetic pathway and gene expression profile.
  L1 denotes the control line, while LST, LS, and LT represent the transformed lines.

  SQS (encoding squalene synthase), β-AS (encoding beta amyrin synthase), CYP83 (cytochrome
  CYP716A83), CYP19 (cytochrome CYP714E19), CYP11 (cytochrome CYP716C11), and UGT
  (UGT73AD1). The bluish heatmap reflects the fold change in gene expression of At-SQS
  (squalene synthase from Arabidopsis thaliana).
