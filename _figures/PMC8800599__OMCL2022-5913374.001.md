  Nrf2 translocates into the nucleus, acting on downstream antioxidant genes and alleviating
  OS damage. (b) ROS activates the NF-κB-mediated inflammatory signaling pathway,
  exacerbating cardiac OS damage. Meanwhile, activated Nrf2 causes a feedback inhibition
  of NF-κB pathway. (c) Upregulation of SIRT1 suppresses ROS generation and alleviates
  OS damage through antioxidant enzymes (GSH-Px and SOD) and PCG-1A/HO-1 pathway.
