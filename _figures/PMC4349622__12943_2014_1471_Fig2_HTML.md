caption: Schematic diagram showing the signaling regulation network of LRRC4/NGL-2
  in glioma. LRRC4/NGL-2 can delay the cell cycle in late G1 by increasing the expression
  of cell cycle inhibitory molecules (p21, p27) and reducing the expression of cell
  cycle regulatory proteins (CyclinD1, CDK2, CyclinE, CDK4) via the inhibition of
  K-Ras/c-Raf/ERK/MAPK, PI-3K/AKT/NF- κB, p70S6/PKC and STAT3, and the upregulation
  of the JNK2/c-Jun/mp53 signaling pathway. LRRC4 /NGL-2 inhibits the expression of
  CXCR4 and SDF-1 alpha/CXCR4 axis-mediated downstream intracellular pathways leading
  to proliferate, chemotactic and invasive effects.
