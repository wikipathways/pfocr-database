caption: A model for ING1-induced senescence. ING1b regulates p53 function and epigenetic
  control by modulating chromatin structure (①②). ING1a upregulates ITSN2 through
  epigenetic control (②), which in turn inhibits CME (③). This may result in activation
  of integrin signaling, while inhibiting EGFR signaling. Downstream of these signaling
  events, the p16/Rb pathway induces cell cycle arrest and senescence (③④). It is
  yet unclear whether ING1b also blocks CME via ITSN2.
