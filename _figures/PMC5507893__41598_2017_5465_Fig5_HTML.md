caption: Changes in metabolism of nucleobase-containing compounds in the hippocampus
  of pallid mice. (A) Overview of changes in purine nucleosides and the adenosine

  ATP, adenosine triphosphate; Met, methionine; SAMe, S-adenosyl-L-methionine; MTA,

  CDP-choline, cytidine diphosphate choline; ChoP, phosphocholine; LPC, lysophosphatidylcholine;
  Gln, glutamine; Glu, glutamate. Blue arrows, upregulation in hippocampus of pallid
  mice; red arrow, downregulation; Ø, no change. Greyed-out metabolites were not observed
