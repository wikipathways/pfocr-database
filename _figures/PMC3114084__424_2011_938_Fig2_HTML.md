  a mechanical signaling network. Shown is the domain structure of the N2BA-titin
  isoform, but the splice pathway of the N2B isoform is indicated as well (blue line

  mechanosensing: the NH2 terminus in the Z-disk, the N2-B and N2-A/PEVK regions in
  the I-band, and the COOH terminus in the M-band. Thick dashed lines indicate differential
  splicing. Small numbers indicate titin domain numbering according to Ref. []. Abbreviations:
  ERK2 extracellular signal-regulated kinase-2, FHL1/FHL2 four-and-a-half-LIM-domain
  protein-1 and -2, FN3 domain fibronectin-type-3-like domain of titin, Ig domain
  immunoglobulin-like domain of titin, MARPs muscle ankyrin repeat proteins, MEK1/2
  MAPK/ERK kinase-1 and -2, MLP muscle LIM protein, MURF1/2 muscle-specific RING-finger
  proteins-1 and -2, NFAT nuclear factor of activated T cells, Nbr1 neighbor of BRCA1
