caption: Activation of the canonical signalling pathway to NFκB results in nuclear
  translocation of the transcription factor. NFκB is sequestered in the cytosol in
  resting cells due to binding to IκB. IκB can be degraded (illustrated by the red
  cross) upon signalling from the activated NEMO/IKKα/IKKβ complex (IKK complex).
  The IKK complex can be activated by various stimuli that engage the TCR, TLRs, IL-1R,
  TNFR and NLRs.
