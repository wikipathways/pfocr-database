caption: The normal human steroidogenic pathway. Under normal conditions, the human
  adrenal cortex produces only small amounts of estrone, estradiol, testosterone,
  and adrenostenediol. The main adrenal secretory products are aldosterone (produced
  in the zona glomerulosa), and cortisol, dehydroepiandrosterone (DHEA), and androstenedione
  (produced in the zonae fasciculata and reticularis). StAR, steroidogenic acute regulatory
  protein; P450scc, side-chain cleavage, HSD, hydroxysteroid dehydrogenase; P450c17,
  17-hydroxylase; POR, P450 oxoreductase; P450c21, 21-hydroxylase; P450c11, 11-hydroxylase;
  P450aro, aromatase; 5α-red, 5-alpha-reductase; DHT, dihydrotestosterone. Reproduced
  from () with kind permission from Elsevier.
