caption: Schematic representation of steroidogenesis including the classic androgen
  pathway (dark blue) and the alternative androgen biosynthesis pathway (light blue),
  both resulting in the synthesis of potent 5α-dihydrotestosterone (DHT). Alternative
  pathway steroids are 5α-reduced upon pathway entry and therefore cannot be aromatized.
  The electron donor enzyme P450 oxidoreductase (POR) supports the activities of CYP21A2
  21-hydroxylase (yellow), CYP17A1 17α-hydroxylase (white), CYP17A1 17,20-lyase (blue),
  and CYP19A1 aromatase (pink) in a mutation-dependent, differential manner.
