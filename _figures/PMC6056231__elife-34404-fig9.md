caption: During neural crest induction, Lrp6 mediates two independent signaling events
  triggered by Wnt8 and Dkk2 converging on β-catenin to promote neural crest formation
  (snai2 induction). The Lrp6/Wnt/Fzd complex signals through disheveled (DVL) leading
  to inhibition of GSK-3β and stabilization of β-catenin. The Lrp6/Dkk2 complex signals
  through an alternate pathway converging on β-catenin. The components of this alternate
  pathway are unknown.
