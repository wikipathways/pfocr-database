caption: β-AR-stimulation using isoproterenol increases phosphorylation and sumoylation
  of NF2. This pathway may involve β1-AR and cAMP/PKA pathway since β1-AR antagonism
  and H89 inhibit β-AR-stimulated increase in NF2 phosphorylation and sumoylation.
  Direct activation of adenylyl cyclase mimics the effects of β-AR-stimulation on
  NF2 phosphorylation and sumoylation. β-AR-stimulation increases levels of phosphorylated
  and sumoylated NF2 in the nucleus. β-AR-stimulation increases phosphorylation of
  MST1/2 and YAP, downstream targets of NF2. Expression of WT-NF2 using adenoviruses
  stimulates mitochondrial death pathway by activating JNKs, increasing Bax expression
  and enhancing cytosolic levels of cytochrome c.
