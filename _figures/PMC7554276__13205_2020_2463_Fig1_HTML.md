  membrane, to Fe3 + (the figure also shows two electronic pathways through which
  Cyc2 acquires electrons in the oxidation process in A. ferrooxidans. Downhill pathway:
  Fe → Cyc2 → Rus → Cyc1 → Cytc → O2. Uphill pathway: Fe2 + → Cyc2 → Rus → CycA1 →
  bc1 → ubiquinone pool → NADH. Rus: rusticyanin, aa3: aa3 cytochrome oxidase, CycA1:
  a diheme cytochrome c4, SdrA1: a short-chain dehydrogenase, QH2: quinol pool, bc1:
  cytochrome bc 1 complex)'
