caption: Shh and BMP signaling. Both signaling pathways antagonize each other to regulate
  proliferation of GCPs by regulating the levels of the bHLH transcription factor
  Atoh1/Math1. Whereas Shh induces proliferation, BMP inhibits proliferation and induces
  the irreversible differentiation of GCPs. BMP induces cell growth arrest by the
  rapid posttranscriptional downregulation of Atoh1/Math1 protein by the proteasome.
  In medulloblastomas, Atoh1/Math1 levels are high whereas the genes regulating the
  BMP pathway are downregulated.
