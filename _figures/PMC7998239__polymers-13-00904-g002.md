caption: Potential signaling cascades and factors required for chitosan tolerance
  in C. albicans. Mutant library screening has revealed that the Hog1, Cek1/Cek2,
  Mkc1, Ras1-cAMP, and calcineurin cascades as well as the Ssn3-mediated Sef1 pathway
  and other factors are involved in chitosan resistance. The roles of these pathways
  and factors in mediating cell wall integrity might affect mitochondrial function.
  Furthermore, low-MW chitosan directly represses mitochondrial function, leading
  to ATP production inhibition.
