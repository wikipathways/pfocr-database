caption: Related molecules, genes, signaling pathways and biological function in HCC
  of miR-200 family. Two IncRNAs, HULC and GIHCG, can inhibit the miR-200 family.
  The miR-200 family can target ZEB1, ZEB2, SPAG9, RBBP4, Foxa2, MACC1 and VASH2.
  And they can regulate Caspase-3, BMI1, DNMT3a, PBX3, PTEN and Bcl-2. Finally, through
  PI3K/AKT, Rho/ROCK or JNK signaling pathway, the miR-200 family can mediate many
  tumor cell biological functions, like EMT, migration, invasion, proliferation and
  apoptosis.
