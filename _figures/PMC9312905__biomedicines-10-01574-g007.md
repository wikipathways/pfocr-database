caption: Proposed mechanism of action of Klotho in the modulation of ion channels
  and intracellular calcium handling in human atrial fibroblasts. Klotho inhibits
  the activation of the PLC-IP3signaling and suppresses thapsigargin-induced ER Ca2+
  release, and subsequently attenuates SOCE by inhibition of TRPC6 currents. In addition,
  Klotho may also inhibit the PLC-DAG pathway and negatively modulate the inward currents
