caption: Signaling pathways related to the activity of oxaliplatin and 5-FU for colon
  cancer cell lines. For the NCI-60 colon cancer cell lines, the AA variants of KRAS,
  JAK, and MTOR correlate with the activity of both oxaliplatin and 5-FU (indicated
  by both blue and red dots) from MAPK, JAK-STAT, and PI(3)K–AKT–mTOR pathways, respectively
  ( and ). The AA variant of NF1 correlates with the activity of oxaliplatin only
  (indicated by red dot) from MAPK pathway (). The AA variants of TGFBR2 and AXIN1
  correlate with the activity of 5-FU only (indicated by blue dot) from TGF-β and
  Wnt pathways, respectively ().
