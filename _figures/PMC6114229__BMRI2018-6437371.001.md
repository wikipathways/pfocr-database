caption: Schematic presentation of MAPK/Ras signaling pathway. Membrane-raft bound-Ras
  upon activation by variety of extracellular stimuli undergoes a conformational change
  facilitated by its membrane localization in its switch regions, which is then recognized
  by other downstream effector proteins in the pathway. This enables signaling events
  to get amplified downstream producing distinct biological outputs ranging between
  cell growth, differentiation, apoptosis, and vesicle transport. Any kind of alteration
  in Ras itself or raft membrane results in various syndromes. Adapted from S. Kapoor,
  Dissertation TU Dortmund (2012).
