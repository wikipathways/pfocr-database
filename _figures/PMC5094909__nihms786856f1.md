  the PI3K/Akt signaling network, whereas recruitment of Grb2 to the IRS results in
  Sos-mediated activation of the Ras-MAPK pathway. The PI3K/Akt pathway effects changes
  in carbohydrate and lipid metabolism and modulates glucose uptake, whereas the Ras-MAPK
  pathway is involved in cell growth and differentiation and protein synthesis.
