caption: Small peptides encoded by circRNAs and lncRNAs regulate tumor proliferation.
  aCircSHPRH encodes SHPRH-146aa, which protects full-length SHPRH from ubiquitin
  protease degradation. SHPRH ubiquitinates PCNA as an E3 ligase. bCirc-AKT3 encodes
  AKT3-174aa, which competitively interacts with PDK1 to negatively regulate the PI3K/Akt
  signaling pathway. cCircPINT encodes PINT87aa, which interacts with PAF1 and inhibits
  transcriptional elongation of oncogenes. dCirc-FBXW7 encodes Fbxw7-185aa, which
  prevents interaction between USP28 and FBXW7a by competitively binding USP28 and
  destabilizing c-Myc. eCircE7 encodes the E7 oncoprotein, which promotes tumor proliferation.
  f The lncRNA UBAP1-AST6 encodes UBAP1-AST6, which is a cancer-promoting factor
