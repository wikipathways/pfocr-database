caption: Schematic representation of TZD action in osteocytes. TZDs activate two distinct
  signaling pathways. TZDs bind GPR40 and rapidly activate Erk1/2 and p38 through
  a Ras-dependent mechanism. This pathway leads to recruitment of Bax to the outer
  membrane of the mitochondria and osteocyte apoptosis. TZDs also bind to PPARγ, and
  this results in activation of the sclerostin gene (SOST) and sclerostin up-regulation.
