caption: Possible cell-cycle checkpoint pathways in centrosomal (centriolar) integrity
  or morphology. Loss of centrosomal integrity by knockdown of centrosomal components
  activates p38–p53–p21 pathway. The existence of extra centrosome induced by cytokinetic
  failure activates Hippo signaling pathway, resulting in p53 stabilization. Impairment
  of centriolar duplication by PLK4 inhibition activates USP28–53BP1–p53–p21 pathway.
  Ciliogenesis induced by the knockdown of trichoplein or Ndel1 in proliferating cells
  results in the elevation of p27. P21 or p27 may suppress CDK activities, resulting
  in cell-cycle arrest
