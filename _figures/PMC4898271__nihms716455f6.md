  blocks the farnesylation of Ras-GDP forcing it to remain in the cytoplasm where
  it cannot activate the Ras signal transduction pathway. Synergistic inhibition of
  tumor growth by lonafarnib after treatment with radiation and temozolomide suggests
  that resistance to DNA damage is mediated by Ras-GTP or other farnesylation dependent
  pathways. FT = Farnesyl Group; FT = Farnesyltransferase; SCH = Lonafarnib (SCH66336);
