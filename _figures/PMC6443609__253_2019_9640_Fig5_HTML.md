  decarboxylase (hydroxypyruvate intermediate) or via serine decarboxylase and amino
  acid oxidase (ethanolamine intermediate). Activity of serine-consuming serine hydroxymethyltransferase
  glyA has been attenuated in the literature by the deletion of 4-aminobenzoic acid
  biosynthetic genes (pabABC) since the glyA deletion mutant is inviable. sera 3-phosphoglycerate
  dehydrogenase, serC phosphoserine aminotransferase, serB phosphoserine phosphatase,

  SDC serine decarboxylase, AAO amino acid oxidase, eutBC ethanolamine ammonia lyase'
