caption: Molecular mechanism of SATB1 in breast cancer. (A) SATB1 stimulates the expression
  of the most critical EMT-related transcription factors, Snail and Twist1, providing
  breast cancer cells with a strong distant metastatic ability. (B) SATB1 plays a
  synergistic role with HER2 in breast cancer. SATB1 can directly upregulate HER2
  expression, promote cell proliferation, inhibit cell apoptosis and promote angiogenesis
  through a series of signaling pathways. (C) MiR130a-3p downregulates SATB1. H19,
  a long non-coding RNA, sponges miRNA-130a-3p, resulting in SATB1 upregulation, thus
  promoting breast cancer progression
