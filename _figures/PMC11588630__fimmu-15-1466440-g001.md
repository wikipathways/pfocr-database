caption: 'The AIM2 inflammasome activation signaling pathway. After AIM2 recognizes
  the virus dsDNA in the cytoplasm, it forms AIM2-ASC-Pro-Caspase-1, activates Caspase-1,
  cleaves Pro-IL-18, Pro-IL-1β and GSDMD, secretes IL-18, IL-1β and causes pyroptosis,
  which is the classic signaling pathway; Non-classical signaling pathways: GBPs cleave
  PCV, expose bacterial dsDNA, activate the classical signaling pathway of the AIM2
  inflammasome after recognition by AIM2, and the remaining dsDNA generates GBPs through
  the cGAS-STING signaling pathways for bacterial lysis'
