  Wnt is able to bind LRP 5/6 and its co-receptor, frizzled. This activates the Wnt
  signaling pathway, which eventually leads to osteoblast differentiation, proliferation
  and survival and, hence, increased bone formation.Abbreviation: MAb, monoclonal
