caption: Schematic diagram of the natural and synthetic 4HBA biosynthetic routes.
  (A) Chorismate pyruvate-lyase (ubiC) mediated 4HBA in bacteria and the CoA-dependent
  4HBA pathway in plants. E4P, D-erythrose-4-phosphate; PEP, phosphoenolpyruvate;
  DHAP, dihydroxyacetone phosphate; CHOR, chorismite; L-TYR, L-tyrosine; 4HBA, 4-hydroxybenzoic
  acid. (B) The synthetic CoA-independent 4HBA pathway. L-amino acid deaminase (LAAD)
  from P. mirabilis; HmaS, hydroxymandelate synthase (HmaS) from A. orientalis; (S)-mandelate
  dehydrogenase (SMDH) from P. putida; benzoylformate decarboxylase (BFD) from P.
  putida; aldehyde dehydrogenase (ALDH) from S. cerevisiae.
