caption: Schematic diagram of the intracellular signaling pathway following sequential
  treatment with celecoxib and bortezomib in human colorectal cancer cells. The expression
  of p53 is required for ER stress-mediated Ca2+ translocation to promote autophagy-associated
  cell death following the sequential treatment with celecoxib and bortezomib. The
  consecutive treatment of colon cancer cells with celecoxib and bortezomib also enhances
  autophagy-associated cell death in p53−/− colon cancer cells. ER, endoplasmic reticulum;
  JNK, c-Jun N-terminal kinase; p38 MAPK, p38 mitogen-activated protein kinase; LAMP1,
