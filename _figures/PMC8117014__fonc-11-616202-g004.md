caption: Circular RNAs (circRNAs) associated with radiochemotherapy resistance in
  head and neck cancers (HNC). CircRNAs that are known to regulate radiochemotherapy
  resistance processes and their validated targets in HNC. CircRNAs regulate the radiochemotherapy
  resistance of HNC through phosphatase and tensin homolog (PTEN)/PI3K/Akt/mTOR, epidermal
  growth factor receptor (EGFR), platelet-derived growth factor receptor A (PDGFR)
  signaling pathway and transcription factor forkhead box Q1 (FOXQ1). Red circle,
  upregulated circRNAs in HNC; green circle, downregulated circRNAs in HNC; yellow
  arrow, positive regulation; red arrow, negative regulation.
