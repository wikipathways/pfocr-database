caption: MAPK signaling pathway in the pathogenesis of AAV. ANCA activates the p38MAPK-mediated
  signaling pathway in human neutrophils. This signal cascade is responsible for the
  translocation of ANCA-specific antigens from the cytoplasmic granulosa to the neutrophil
  surface. This translocation enables these antigens to bind with their specific autoantibodies.
  IL-18 initiates ANCA-induced production of neutrophil superoxide by phosphorylating
  p38MAPK.
