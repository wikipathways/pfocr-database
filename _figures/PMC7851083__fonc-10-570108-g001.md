caption: 'Important events during research development of macropinocytosis in cancers.
  The references and themes are as follows: Pinocytosis. (1) Induction of membrane
  ruffling and fluid-phase pinocytosis in quiescent fibroblasts by Ras proteins. (2)
  The small GTP-binding protein rac regulates growth factor-induced membrane ruffling.(3)
  Macropinocytosis.(4) Developmental control of endocytosis in dendritic cells by
  Cdc42 (). Rab5 is a signaling GTPase involved in actin remodeling by receptor tyrosine
  kinases (). Phosphoinositides in cell regulation and membrane dynamics (). Spatiotemporal
  dynamics of RhoA activity in migrating cells (). Active RAS triggers death in glioblastoma
  cells through hyperstimulation of macropinocytosis (). Macropinocytosis of protein
  is an amino acid supply route in RAS-transformed cells (). The Utilization of Extracellular
  Proteins as Nutrients Is Suppressed by mTORC1 (). Communication by Extracellular
  Vesicles: Where We Are and Where We Need to Go (). EGFR-Pak Signaling Selectively
  Regulates Glutamine Deprivation-Induced Macropinocytosis (). Plasma membrane V-ATPase
  controls oncogenic RAS-induced macropinocytosis (). Syndecan 1 is a critical mediator
  of macropinocytosis in pancreatic cancer (). The JNK signaling pathway plays a key
  role in methuosis (non-apoptotic cell death) induced by MOMIPP in glioblastoma ().
  Lipid-gated monovalent ion fluxes regulate endocytic traffic and support immune
  surveillance (). Water loss regulates cell and vesicle volume ().'
