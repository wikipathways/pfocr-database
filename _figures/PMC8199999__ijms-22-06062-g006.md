caption: U snRNP biogenesis pathway. In the physiological state, once the U snRNA
  is transcribed and capped it associates with multiple factors which form the export
  complex and allow U snRNA export into the cytoplasm. Subsequently, in a process
  mediated by SMN complex and Gemin proteins, the Sm proteins interact with the U
  snRNA at the Sm site. The SMN complex dissociates, followed by 3′ processing/trimming
  and cap hypermethylation. The final product is imported into the nucleus. Further
  modifications occur on the U snRNA, and snRNP (U1, U2, U4, U5 or U11/U12, U4atac)-specific
  proteins associate with U snRNP Sm core [].
