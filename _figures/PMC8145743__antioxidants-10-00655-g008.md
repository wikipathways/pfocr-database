caption: Schematic depiction representing the effects of CDDO-Me on astroglial autophagy
  (clasmatodendrosis). CDDO-Me activates Nrf2-mediated antioxidant defense mechanisms
  and SP1 suppression, which inhibit further ROS synthesis and HSP25 induction, respectively.
  In addition, CDDO-Me facilitates ERK1/2-SP1-mediated HSP25 suppression that abrogates
  PI3K/AKT activation. CDDO-Me also abolishes the PI3K-AKT-Bif-1 signaling pathway
  by repressing Src-CK2-mediated PTEN inhibition.
