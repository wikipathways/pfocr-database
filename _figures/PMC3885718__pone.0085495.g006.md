caption: Resveratrol stimulates multiple second messengers that lead to AKT activation
  and the induction of differentiation. Low dose resveratrol stimulates SirT1, which
  activates AKT to induce differentiation. High dose resveratrol stimulates AMPK,
  which inhibits the mTORC1 pathway relieving the S6K1 inhibition on IRS-1, allowing
  activation of AKT and the induction of differentiation. Ultimately, resveratrol
  induces a multi-signaling cascade that results in robust vascular smooth muscle
  differentiation.
