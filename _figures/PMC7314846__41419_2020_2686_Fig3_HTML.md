caption: The RISK pathway is activated by opioid peptides, bradykinins, adenosine,
  receptor tyrosine kinase G protein-coupled receptors, erythropoietin, cytokines,
  insulin, or insulin-like growth factor-1. The cGMP/PKG pathway is activated by external
  stimuli (ANP and BNP) via the activation of natriuretic peptide receptors A and
  B. The SAFE pathway is activated by IL-6, IL-11, leukemia inhibitory factor (LIF),
  or TNF-α via the glycoprotein 130 receptor or TNF-α type 2 receptor. Among these
  pathways, the potential m6A methylation targets have been labeled with pink circle.
