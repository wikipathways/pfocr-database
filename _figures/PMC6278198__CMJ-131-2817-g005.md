caption: 'Schematic summary of the possible mechanism for GLCCI1 in glucocorticoids
  pathway. GLCCI1 might modulate the formation of GC-GR complex or the activation
  of GR. GLCCI1 deficiency leads to less activation of GR, leading to less improvement
  of MKP-1 pathway, more obvious phosphorylation of p38 MAPK and less inhibition for
  the pro-inflammatory genes under glucocorticoids, which induce poor response to
  glucocorticoids in asthma. GC: Glucocorticoid; GR: Glucocorticoid receptor; GRE:
  Glucocorticoid response element; HSP 90: Heat shock protein 90; MKP-1: Mitogen-activated
  protein kinase phosphatase-1; MAPK: Mitogen-activated protein kinase; TNF-α: Tumor
  necrosis factor-α; IL-6: Interleukin-6; IL-1β: Interleukin-1β.'
