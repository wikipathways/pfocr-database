caption: In a healthy liver, ECM1 interacts with αV integrin to maintain the latent
  state of TGF-β. After liver injury, ECM1 levels are decreased, leading to the activation
  of TGF-β by αV integrin, which in turn activates HSCs through the TGF-β/Smad pathway.
  ECM1-HF-MSCs can inhibit the activation of TGF-β through exogenous supplementation
  with ECM1, which in turn affects the activation of HSCs, thereby alleviating LC.
