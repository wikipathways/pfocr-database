  progressively induce Irg1 expressions in DC via a mitochondrial superoxide-dependent
  pathway, impairs effective antigen presentation and airway inflammatory response.

  of antioxidative programs, which in turn influences DC effector functions and limit
  the development of airway inflammation and T cell-mediated allergic immune responses
  in asthma.
