caption: cGMP/PKG signaling in cardiomyocyte cGMP-PKG signaling is enhanced by two
  pathways. The former is NO-sGC-cGMP pathway and the latter is NP-NPR-pGC pathway.
  cGMP derived from NO-sGC pathway takes hydrolyzation by PDE5, and cGMP from NP-pGC
  pathway by PDE9 and PDE5 (especially in stressed conditions). cGMP/PKG signaling
  exerts protective effects in cardiomyocyte by phosphorylate various proteins like
  RGS2/4, Troponin I, TSC2, cMyBP-C, or Titin.
