caption: Model for the regulatory role of miR-221 in HCMV infection. HCMV infection
  induces the downregulation of SOCS1 protein level through promoting the expression
  of miR-221. As a negative regulator, SOCS1 downregulation leads to the enhanced
  activation of NF-κB pathway upon HCMV infection. Activated NF-κB enter the nuclear
  and facilitates the transcription of type I IFN (IFNα and IFNβ), leading to the
  activation of downstream ISGs effectors. The releases of type I IFN and ISGs setup
  an inflammation in the HCMV infected cell, and establishes an antiviral state against
  HCMV.
