caption: The ferroptosis pathway and role of p53 in ferroptosis. p53 can promote iron-dependent
  cell death via transcriptional inhibition of expression of solute carrier family
  7 member 11 (SLC7A11) expression. At the same time, TP53 controls transcription
  of GPX4 and SLC7A11 via nuclear factor erythroid 2-related factor 2 (NRF2). p53
  can also mediate the expression of arachidonate 15-lipoxygenase (ALOX15) by inducing
  spermidine/spermine N1-acetyltransferase 1 (SAT1), thereby promoting iron-dependent
  cell death.
