caption: Multifunctional effects of CMKLR1 activation. A. Interaction of chemerin
  or chemerin peptides with CMKLR1 expressed on macrophages, DCs, adipocytes or ECs
  induces the activation of intracellular signaling molecules, such as Syk, ERK1/2,
  p38MAPK, Akt or PI3-K, leading to reduced immune responses and enhanced adipogenesis
  and angiogenesis. B. Similar to chemerin, resolvin E1 down-regulates cytokine/chemokine
  production in macrophages by reducing the activation of ERK1/2 and NF-κB. However,
  RvE1 appears to activate a distinct signaling pathway to stimulate nonphlogistic
  macrophage phagocytosis via CMKLR1.
