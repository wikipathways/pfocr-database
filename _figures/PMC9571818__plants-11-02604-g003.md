caption: Signal transduction pathway of SLs and protein interaction. (A) Degradation
  of D14 into ABC-FTL and HMB. The figure also shows the open (inactive) and closed
  (active) conformation of D14 with the “D” ring. (B) Interaction of D14 (DWARF 14)
  with F-box protein D3 and a repressor D53 mediating the signaling pathway. In the
  absence of SLs, D53 arrests SLs transduction, however, SLs repressor is degraded
  through controlled ubiquitination followed by SLs release and successful transduction.
