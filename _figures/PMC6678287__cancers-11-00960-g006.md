caption: Simplified pathways showing the effect of F-6 cell proliferation and death.
  (A) Leukemic cell line K562 cells. F-6 suppresses cell proliferation by inhibiting
  JNK activity that prevents c-Jun activation, and PI3K-Akt-mTOR pathway. It also
  suppresses Akt:BAX-mediated mitochondrial protection and promotes intrinsic pathway
  of apoptosis. (B) MDA MB-231 breast cancer cells. F-6 suppresses cell proliferation
  by inhibiting PI3K-Akt-mTOR pathway. It also suppresses Akt:BAX-mediated mitochondrial
  protection and promotes intrinsic pathway of apoptosis. Therefore, F6 suppresses
  cell proliferation and promotes apoptotic cell death in both of these types of cancer
  cells.
