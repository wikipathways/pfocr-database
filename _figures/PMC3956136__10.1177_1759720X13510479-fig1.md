caption: Canonical Wnt β catenin signaling pathway. When a Wnt ligand binds its low-density
  lipoprotein receptor related protein (LRP5/6) Frizzled (Fzd) coreceptors on the
  cell surface of osteoblasts, Disheveled (Dvl) is activated, which inhibits glycogen
  synthase kinase 3β (GSK3β) from phosphorylating β catenin. The cytoplasmic level
  of β catenin consequently rises, and β catenin translocates into the nucleus to
  bind with transcriptional factors T-cell factor (Tcf)/lymphoid enhancer-binding
  factor (Lef-1), upregulating the target gene expression. (Reproduced from )
