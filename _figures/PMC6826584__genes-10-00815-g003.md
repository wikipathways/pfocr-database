  Frizzled, and co-receptors LRP5/6. The activated receptor associates with the Dishevelled
  (DVL) protein and inhibits the β-catenin destruction complex. The stabilized β-catenin
  translocates and accumulates in the nucleus, where it activates the Wnt-dependent
  transcriptional program. For details see the text.
