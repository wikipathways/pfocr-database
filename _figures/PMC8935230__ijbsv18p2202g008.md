caption: Schematic of melatonin's protective effect on NP cells in the presence of
  TNF-α. Melatonin upregulates YAP protein levels via activating MTNR1B, which subsequently
  recruits Gαi2 protein. YAP upregulated by Gαi2 activation results in anabolic enhancement
  of NP cells (left). Meanwhile, melatonin-mediated YAP upregulation increases the
  expression of IκBα and suppresses the TNF-α-induced activation of NF-κB pathway
  (right), which thereby inhibiting the catabolism of NP cells.
