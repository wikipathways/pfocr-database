caption: 'HBsAg induces the HCC tumorigenesis by promoting proliferation. After HBV
  infects hepatocytes, the envelope protein produced induces more active cell proliferation
  through multiple intracellular signal transduction pathways to promote HCC tumorigenesis,
  including: a activate the Src/PI3K/Akt pathway through proximal stimulation of PKCα/Raf1
  signaling; b upregulate cyclin D1 and c-Myc through increasing the expression of
  LEF-1 in the WNT signaling pathway; c upregulate long non-coding RNA through the
  activation of the NF-κB signaling pathway; d trigger the PKC-dependent activation
  of the c-Raf-1/MEK/Erk 2 signaling pathway to regulate the AP-1; e induce autophagy
  and NF-κB activation. PI3K, phosphoinositide-3-kinase; PKC, protein kinase C; LEF-1,
  lymphoid enhancer-binding factor 1; MEK, mitogen-activated extracellular signal-regulated
  kinase; Erk 2, extracellular signal-regulated kinase 2; AP-1, activator protein
  1'
