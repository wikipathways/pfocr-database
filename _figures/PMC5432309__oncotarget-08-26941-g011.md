caption: AG treatment inhibits Akt phosphorylation via the PI3K; this inhibits IκBα
  phosphorylation and degradation, suppresses the translocation of p65, and, in turn,
  decreases the Bcl-2 and Bcl-xl expression, leading to apoptosis of NSCLC cells.
  AG treatment also inhibits LPS- or TNFα-induced NF-κB activation, sensitizing NSCLC
  cells to TNFα-induced apoptosis. In addition, AG treatment activates JNK, but inhibits
  ERK signaling, which finally leads to decreased cell survival.
