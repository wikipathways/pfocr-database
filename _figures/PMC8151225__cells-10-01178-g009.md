caption: Graphical representation of proposed mechanisms by which DIM induces apoptosis
  and inhibits metastasis in HCC. DIM induces ER stress, accumulation of ROS, and
  Ca2+ release into the cytosol, which acts on the mitochondria and causes a loss
  of mitochondrial membrane potential, leading to the activation of hepatic cell death.
  DIM-mediated ER stress also modulates the Smad2/3 pathway, inhibits EMT, and reduces
  cell invasion and migration ability, thus suppressing cancer metastasis.
