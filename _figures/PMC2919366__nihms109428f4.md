caption: STAT3 signaling pathway. Multiple cytokines, including Il-6, IL-10, IL-21,
  IL-22, and IL-23 activate STAT3 phosphorylation. Dimerized STAT3 enters the nucleus
  to affect transcription of various genes. STAT3 can also dimerize with other STATs.
  Normal STAT3 signaling leads to the upregulation and down regulation of the cytokines
  shown in the right lower corner. IL-10 and SOCS (suppressors of cytokine signaling)
  can downregulate STAT3 activity.
