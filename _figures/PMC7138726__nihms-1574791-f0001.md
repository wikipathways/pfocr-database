caption: Bisphosphonates, likely through binding to a membrane-bound phosphatase,
  induce the opening of Cx43 hemichannels, leading to the activation of the Src/MEK/ERKs
  signaling pathway in the cytoplasm of osteoblasts and osteocytes. Activated ERKs
  phosphorylate p90RSK, which in turn phosphorylates and inactivates the pro-apoptotic
  protein BAD. p90RSK also phosphorylates C/EBPβ leading to the formation of a caspase-binding
  domain that prevents caspase activation and apoptosis.
