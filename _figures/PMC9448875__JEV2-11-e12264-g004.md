  CRC‐EVs are transported into macrophages, CDC42 is delivered from CRC‐EVs to cells
  and switched to a GTP‐bound active state, which mediates NOD1 activation, with consequent
  RIP2 phosphorylation; it triggers downstream NF‐κB and p38‐MAPK‐dependent inflammatory
