caption: 'Schematic diagram of mechanisms involved in alcohol-mediated suppression
  of type I IFN pathway in human hepatocytes. Alcohol not only inhibits the expression
  of the IRF-5 and IRF-7 (the key positive regulators of type I IFN production), but
  also suppresses the expression of STAT-1 and -2 (two key elements in JAK-STAT pathway).
  In addition, alcohol induces the expression of SOCS-2 and -3 (the negative regulators
  of JAK-STAT pathway). Abbreviations: IRF, interferon regulatory factor; ISGF3, IFN-stimulated
  gene factor-3; JAK, Janus kinase; Mx, myxovirus-resistance proteins; OAS, oligoadenylate
  synthetase; PKR, protein kinase; SOCS, suppressors of cytokine signaling; STAT,
  signal transducer and activator of transcription.'
