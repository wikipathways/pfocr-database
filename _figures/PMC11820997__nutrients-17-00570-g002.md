caption: Beneficial effects of oleate on insulin signaling by affecting the expression
  of PI3K signaling pathway proteins. Oleate, a monounsaturated fatty acid, has a
  beneficial effect on insulin signaling by altering the PI3K signaling pathway. In
  a dose-dependent manner, it downregulates p85α, a regulatory subunit of PI3K, and
  the p85α/p110β ratio while upregulating IRS-1 (insulin receptor substrate 1) and
  p110β, a catalytic subunit of PI3K. Maximizing PI3K pathway activation promotes
  glucose metabolism and lessens insulin resistance, which increases insulin sensitivity
