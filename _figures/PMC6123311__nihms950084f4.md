  levels. TXNIP promotes activation of the NLRP3 inflammasome and downstream secretion
  of IL1β. In parallel, TXNIP negatively regulates TXN and thereby promotes activation
  of an ASK1-JNK-c-Jun pro-apoptotic pathway. Also shown are TXNRD and NADPH, which
  are essential components of the TXN system that regenerate reduced TXN from its
  oxidized form.
