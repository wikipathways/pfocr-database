caption: Model of BMP4-mediated anti-cancer effects in ER+ breast cancer. BMP4 activates
  ALK3 to drive P-SMAD1/5, leading to transcriptional changes. p21 (CDKN1A) induction
  drives cell-cycle arrest via inhibition of multiple CDKs, which inhibit P-Rb and
  G1-to-S cell-cycle transition. Estrogens induce ER transcriptional activity, including
  upregulation of cyclin D1, which drives CDK4/6 activation and G1-to-S transition.
  Anti-estrogens and CDK4/6i antagonize these events.
