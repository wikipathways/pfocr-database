caption: Model of the expression level of CD160 on total NK cells in HIV-infected
  individuals and its underlying mechanism for NK cell functions. CD160 expression
  was reduced on total NK cells from HIV-infected individuals, the reason for the
  reduction of CD160 on total NK cells may be due to the higher plasma levels of TGF-β1
  in HIV-infected individuals. The activation of CD160 can enhance GLUT1 expression
  and the glucose uptake of NK cells in HIV-infected individuals, and finally promotes
  NK cell functions through the PI3K/AKT/mTOR/s6k signaling pathway.
