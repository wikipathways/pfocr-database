  by Kaneko et al. in V. grandiflorum. a The nitrogen source for Veratrum alkaloids
  is likely l-arginine (). b Based on the observation that solanidine accumulates

  a C-nor-D-homosteroidal skeleton upon illumination, , ) hypothesized that this conversion

  from which veratramine and jervine can be formed (, )
