caption: Signaling pathway involving mTORC1. Various stimuli converge on mTORC1 pathway
  via a number of routes. mTORC1 activation impacts on different cellular physiology,
  such as protein synthesis, cap-dependent mRNA translation, and autophagy. Here mutations
  in TSC1/2, PTEN, FMR1, and NF1 cause constitutive mTORC1 activation and syndromic
  ASD. Akt, protein kinase B; eIF4E, eukaryotic initiation factor 4E; ERK, extracellular

  protein kinase kinase; mGluR, metabotropic glutamate receptor; mTOR, mammalian target

  kinase; PI3K, phosphatidylinositol 3-kinase; PTEN, phosphatase and tensin homolog;
