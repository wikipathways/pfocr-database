caption: Model showing a role for Yap in governing cardiac growth and survival by
  interlinking the Hippo, IGF, and Wnt pathways. Yap promotes cardiomyocyte proliferation
  through activation of IGF signaling and stabilization of the active form of β-catenin.
  Inhibition of IGF signaling or β-catenin blocks Yap-induced cardiomyocyte proliferation.
  Yap interlinks the Hippo pathway (MST1/2 and LATS1/2), the Wnt pathway [Wnt, Disheveled
  (Dvl), Axin, APC, and GSK-3β], and the IGF pathway (IGF, IGF1R, PI3K, Akt, and GSK-3β)
  to control β-catenin signaling and regulate cardiac growth and development. Each
  of the three pathways influenced by Yap is demarcated by a box.
