caption: Regulatory mechanism of calcium on HIF1α induction. Calcium channels regulating
  intracellular calcium levels induce HIF1α expression by inducing gene transcription,
  translation, and stabilization. STIM1, MCU, and TRPM2-activated intracellular calcium
  signaling increase HIF1A mRNA expression. TRPM2 and TRPC1 activate the Akt/mTORC1
  pathway, which increases HIF1α translation. TRPM8 and TRPC6 stabilize HIF1α via
  the calcineurin/RACK pathway and by PHD-mediated prolyl hydroxylation, respectively.
