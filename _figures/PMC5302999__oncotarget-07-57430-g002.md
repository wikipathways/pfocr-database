caption: In the process of vertebrate segmentation, Tbx6, Mesp2 and downstream Ripply1/2
  are important genes involved in somite segmentation. Tbx6 stimulated by the Wnt
  and FGF pathway signaling, later induces the expression of NICD (also induced by
  Notch signal) and Mesp2. Mesp2 initially expressed in a restricted area (one segment
  length), later with the Ripply1/2, (a feed-back inhibitor to Tbx6 and Mesp2) expression
  in the posterior half region, which defines the future segment boundaries.
