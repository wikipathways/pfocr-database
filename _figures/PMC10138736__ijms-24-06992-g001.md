caption: 'Mechanism of progesterone action. Progesterone effects are primarily mediated
  by the classical signaling pathway that progesterone receptors bind the ligand and
  then translocate to the nucleus, where they function as ligand-activated transcription
  factors and initiate transcription of progesterone-responsive genes. Progesterone
  also has rapid, non-genomic effects mediated by cell membrane receptors, cytoplasmic
  PGR, or receptor-independent intracellular signaling cascades. PR-A and PR-B share
  common structural and functional elements, but PR-A can repress the transcriptional
  activity of PR-B. DBD: DNA-binding domain; LBD: ligand-binding domain.'
