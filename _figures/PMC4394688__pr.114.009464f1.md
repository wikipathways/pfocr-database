caption: Ligand recognition and second-messenger signaling at PTHR1. The PTHR1 is
  a family B GPCR that mediates the actions of two peptide ligands—parathyroid hormone
  and PTH-related protein. In response to either ligand, the PTHR1 can couple to a
  variety of signal transduction pathways, including most prominently, the Gαs/cAMP/PKA
  pathway but also the Gαq/PLC/PKC pathway, the Gα12/13/RhoA/PLD pathway, and the
  ERK-1/2–MAP-kinase pathway, the latter via G protein–dependent and G protein–independent/β-arrestin–dependent
  mechanisms.
