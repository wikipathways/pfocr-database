caption: Intrinsic and extrinsic apoptotic pathways in human endometrial stromal cells
  induced by p,pʹ-bis-[4-chlorophenyl]-1,1,1-trichloroethane (DDT). In this model,
  we propose that p,pʹ-DDT promotes the induction of cytochrome C by elevating expression
  of Bcl-2-associated X protein (Bax) and decreasing expression of B-cell lymphoma
  2 (Bcl-2). This alteration in Bax and Bcl-2 expression then acts in conjunction
  with apoptotic protease activating factor 1 (APAF-1) to activate procaspase 9, which
  in turn triggers the extrinsic pathway, leading to apoptosis. Mito, mitochondria
