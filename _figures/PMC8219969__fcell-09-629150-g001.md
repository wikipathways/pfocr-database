caption: The regulatory mechanisms of ferroptosis-related drugs or genes. Sorafenib,
  sulfasalazine, erastin/analogs and p53 inhibit SLC7A11 to trigger ferroptosis. GPX4
  is directly inhibited by (1S, 3R)-RSL3, F2N56, FINO2, HSPAS. NADPH–FSP1–CoQ10 ferroptosis
  surveillance pathway acts in parallel to the GPX4 pathway. Low-density, lipoprotein,
  Nanoparticles can regulate GSH to induce ferroptosis. H2O2/Fe3O4-PLGA nanosystem
  can regulate Fenton reaction to induce ferroptosis.
