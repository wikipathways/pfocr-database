caption: Apoptotic pathways induced in beta cells in type 2 diabetes. Islet amyloid
  polypeptide, glucose and palmitate have been proposed to induce cellular stress
  through activation of endoplasmic reticulum (ER) stress, oxidative stress or NLRP3
  inflammasome activation and IL-1β production. These stresses induce the intrinsic
  apoptosis pathway through activation of Bcl-2 family molecules. Islet amyloid poly-peptide
  (IAPP) has also been shown to directly activate the extrinsic apoptosis pathway.
