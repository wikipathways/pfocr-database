caption: Oxidative stress pathway in normal conditions and in HNSCC. In non‐stressful
  conditions, KEAP1 ubiquinates NFE2L2 and promotes its degradation. Under conditions
  of oxidative stress such as head and neck cancer (HNSCC), KEAP1 is modified and
  inactivated. NFE2L2 is then stabilizes, accumulates, and translocates into the nucleus.
  NFE2L2 induces genes regulated by the antioxidant response element (ARE). Downstream
  defense enzymes include NAD(P)H quinone oxidoreductase 1 (NQO1) and heme oxygenase‐1
  (HO1)
