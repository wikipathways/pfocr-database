  (8), precolibactin-795b (9), precolibactin-886 (10) and precolibactin-969 (11).

  clb pathway utilizes ClbKPKS and ClbL to produce 8 (Route A); or skips ClbKPKS but

  modules to produce 10 which is the precursor for the assembly of 11 (Route C). A,
