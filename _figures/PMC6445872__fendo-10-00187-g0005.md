  chromatin level. SIRT6 suppresses the high-fat diet (HFD)-, LPS-, and IL-6-induced
  I-κ kinase (IKK)-nuclear factor-κB (NF-κB) pathway and Janus activating kinase 2
  (JAK2)-signal transducer and activator of transcription 3 (STAT3) pathway, resulting
  in reduced M1 macrophage polarization and macrophage migration. Additionally, SIRT6
  deacetylates pyruvate kinase M2 (PKM2), preventing STAT3 from phosphorylation.
