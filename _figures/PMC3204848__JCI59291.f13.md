caption: Hyperglycemia synergizes with collagen stimulation in platelets to activate
  the AR-dependent polyol pathway, leading to increased ROS production (via GSH depletion
  and NADH oxidase [Nox]). Excessive ROS can increase TXA2 production by potentiating
  PLCγ2/PKC/p38α MAPK signaling, thus providing more arachidonic acid substrate for
  COX-1 and TXA2 synthase (TS) to produce TXA2, leading to platelet hyperactivity.
  The oxidative stress can also lead to enhanced surface expression of TP, which increases
  the potency of circulating TXA2.
