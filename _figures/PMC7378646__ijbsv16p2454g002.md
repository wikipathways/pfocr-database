caption: The crosstalk between the Hippo-YAP1/TAZ pathway and Wnt signaling. The Wnt/
  β-catenin signaling and alternative Wnt signaling participate in the modulation
  of the Hippo-YAP1/TAZ pathway. Conversely, the Hippo-YAP1/TAZ pathway plays a role
  in the regulation of Wnt signaling. The crosstalk between the Hippo pathway and
  Wnt signaling affects cancer biological behavior, cardiomyocyte proliferation and
  cardiac growth. Line arrows indicate activation, whereas connector lines imply inhibition;
  dotted lines mean translocation.
