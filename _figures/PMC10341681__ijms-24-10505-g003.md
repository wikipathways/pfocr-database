caption: The signaling pathways and targets of saponin-regulated tau protein phosphorylation.
  (1). PF11 and xanthoperidinol, regulate targets of LCMT-1/PP2A signaling pathways;
  (2). PF11, regulates targets of IRS-1/P13K/Akt/GSK-3β and calpain I/CDK5 signaling
  pathways; (3). Ginsenoside Rb1 and Ginsenoside Rd, regulate the activity of PP2A;
  (4). Theasaponin E1, inhibits tau protein phosphorylation by regulating GSK-3β,
  CDK5, c-Jun JNK, MAPK, ERK1/MARK, and PP2A; (5). Xanthoceraside, regulates the activity
  of GSK-3β. The above-mentioned saponins inhibit tau protein phosphorylation by modulating
  the targets of these signaling pathways
