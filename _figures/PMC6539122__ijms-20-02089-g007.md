  transduction pathways. Red indicates upregulated ubiquitination; blue indicates
  downregulated ubiquitination; yellow indicates simultaneous up- and downregulated
  ubiquitination at different Lys sites in the CALM protein. Proteins involved in
  the cancer pathway are encircled by green, proteins associated with the immune system
  are encircled by purple, and signal transduction proteins are encircled by orange.

  of the gradient color bars indicates alteration folds, and minus means downregulation;
  the darker the color (red or blue) of the rectangle, the larger the fold of the
  ubiquitination alteration (up- or downregulation).
