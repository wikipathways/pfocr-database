caption: (Left panel) MCPIP1 regulates the levels of miRNA thereby actively influencing
  the levels of SFRP4, KREMEN1, ZNRF3, CXXC4 and CSNK1A1 and inhibiting the Wnt pathway
  by inactivating β-catenin and consequently inhibiting the EMT process. (Right panel)
  The loss of MCPIP1 RNase activity protects miRNA-519a-3p, miRNA-519b-3p, and miRNA-520c-3p
  from degradation; these miRNAs mature and inhibit the expression of negative regulators
  of the Wnt/β-catenin pathway, thus regulating the level of active β-catenin and
  the EMT process.
