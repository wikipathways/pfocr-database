caption: Steroid synthesis pathway. Mineralocorticoid, glucocorticoid, dehydroepiandrosterone,
  and androstenediol synthesis take place in the adrenal gland. Testosterone is converted
  to DHT in peripheral tissue. Abiraterone inhibits both the 17α-hydroxylase and 17,20-lyase
  activity of the cytochrome p450 enzyme CYP17. Orteronel and galeterone have increased
  specificity for 17,20-lyase relative to 17α-hydroxylase. VT-464 has 10-fold in vitro
  specificity for the 17,20-lyase reaction over 17α-hydroxylase. Androgens in the
  5α-androstenedione pathway for production of DHT are noted with *. 3βHSD, 3β-hydroxysteroid
  dehydrogenase; SRD5A, steroid 5 alpha reductase; DHT, dihydrotestosterone; DHEA,
  dehydroepiandrosterone. Reproduced with permission from ().
