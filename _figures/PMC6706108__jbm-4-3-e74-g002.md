caption: A. Structural changes of Fura-2 by esterase activity and Ca2+ binding. Fura-2
  AM ester is Ca2+ insensitive and nonpolar. Once inside the cell, esterase enzymes
  sequentially cleave the AM groups to leave Fura-2-free acid (Ca2+ sensitive, polar)
  trapped inside the cell, where it is able to bind Ca2+. B. Carbachol is a muscarinic
  receptor agonist that initiates a signaling pathway, resulting in the release of
  Ca2+ within seconds. Fura-2 exhibits a calcium dependent excitation spectral shift
  to report the 340/380 ratio.
