caption: 'Mechanotransduction in tumor cells: sensing and transducing mechanical signals
  from the ECM into intracellular biochemical signals. Mechanical signals are primarily
  transduced into the cell via integrins, the Rho signaling pathway, and the Hippo
  signaling pathway. Integrin activation promotes invasion and cytoskeletal remodeling
  through the FAK-Src pathway and actin signaling. In the Rho pathway, RhoA maintains
  actin structure, while Rac1 facilitates lamellipodia formation and regulates glucose
  uptake. The Hippo pathway, through phosphorylation and dephosphorylation of YAP/TAZ,
  translocates these factors to the nucleus, where they regulate actin-associated
  and invasion-related gene expression'
