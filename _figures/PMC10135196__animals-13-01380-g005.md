caption: Diagram depicting the pathways involved in osteocalcin affecting FLHS in
  chickens. Osteocalcin derived from osteoblasts protects chicken from FLHS via (1)
  activating the JNK and AMPK signaling pathways and PPARα signaling down-regulation
  to reduce the effects of damaging factors (nutritional status, hormones, toxic and
  harmful substances, and intestinal microbiota) in the FLHS liver; and (2) activating
  pancreatic β-cells to stimulate insulin synthesis, thereby reducing insulin resistance
  and associated liver damage.
