  Intermediate Species F. ramosissima.Three distinct CO2 concentrating mechanisms,
  the NADP-ME type (green), the NAD-ME type (blue) C4pathway, and the photorespiratory
  Gly shuttle (orange), operate in parallel in this C3-C4 intermediate. F. ramosissima,
  with a working C4cycle, can compensate for the massive ammonia imbalance introduced
  by the photorespiratory CO2 pump, by adjusting the ratios of the transport metabolites
  Ala/pyruvate and Asp/malate.
