  glycolysis to lactate and/or other acids and biosynthesis of EPS in L. lactis. Glucose

  Pgm, GalU (UDP-glucose pyrophosphorylase), GalE (UDP-galactose epimerase), Rfb (dTDP-rhamnose
  biosynthetic system consisting of RfbA and glucose-1P thymidylyltransferase), RfbB
  (dTDP-glucose-4,6 dehydratase), RfbC (dTDP-4-keto-6-deoxy-D-glucose-3,5 epimerase),
  and RfbD (dTDP-4-keto-L-rhamnose reductase).'
