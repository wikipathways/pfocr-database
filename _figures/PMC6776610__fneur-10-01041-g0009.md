  MCAO rats. Dexmedetomidine (Dex) improved glutamate transporter-1 (GLT-1) expression
  by activating α2 adrenergic receptor through PI3K/Akt signaling pathway. Upregulation
  of GLT-1 enhanced the uptaken of extracellular glutamate (Glu) to protect neurons
  from excitotoxicity. The effect of dexmedetomidine improved the neurological deficit
  and reduced the infarct volume in MCAO model rats.
