caption: We have previously proposed a direct link between antioxidant PPARs activity
  and Wnt signaling pathway . Based on our present results we hypothesize that PPARs
  are able to modulate mitochondrial dynamics trough different cellular mechanisms.
  Complementary to the Wnt signaling pathway (FZD, frizzled; LRP 5/6, low density
  lipoprotein receptor-related protein; DVL, disheveled; βcat, β-catenin), PPARs might
  act inducing directly the expression of several mitochondrial-related proteins (UCP,
  uncoupled protein; DRP1, dynamin related protein 1; Mfn, mitofusin; mtTFA, mitochondrial
  transcription factor). Additionally, the PPARs-mediated calcium balance might also
  offer another control point for mitochondrial dynamics modulation (VDCC, voltage
  dependent calcium channels; NMDAR, NMDA receptor; VGCC, voltage-gated calcium channels;
  PKC, protein kinase C).
