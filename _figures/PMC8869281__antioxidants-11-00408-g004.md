caption: 'Natural and synthetic antioxidants: Classification of natural and synthetic
  antioxidants and the endogenous Nrf2 pathway, which regulates the activation of
  ARE genes. Kelch-like ECH-associated protein 1 (Keap1) represents a negative regulator
  of Nrf2. Under physiological conditions, Keap1 forms a ubiquitin E3 ligase complex
  with Cullin3 in the cytoplasm that targets Nrf2 for polyubiquitination and rapid
  proteasomal degradation. During oxidative stress, cysteines in Keap1 are modified
  and inactivated, and Nrf2 can quickly translocate into the nucleus, where it binds
  to small musculoaponeurotic fibrosarcoma oncogene homolog (sMaf) proteins, upregulates
  downstream ARE genes, and maintains redox homeostasis.'
