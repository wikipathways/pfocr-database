  progenitor cells (EPCs) and endothelial cells (ECs). SHH molecule binds and activate
  the 12-pass transmembrane cell surface receptor Patched 1 (PTCH 1) that inhibits
  the smoothened (SMO) receptor to activate the binding of suppressor of fused (SUFU)
  and glioma-associated oncogene homolog1-A (GlLI 1A) to form a complex by autophosphorylation.
  EPCs are mostly activated by the non-canonical SHH signaling pathway in angiogenesis.
