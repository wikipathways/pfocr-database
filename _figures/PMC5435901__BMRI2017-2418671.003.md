caption: Curcumin plays a protective role at the priming and the activation stage
  of renal fibrosis. At the priming stage, curcumin reduces proinflammatory molecular
  activity and blocks inflammation associated signaling pathways. At the activation
  stage, curcumin inhibits the expression of renal fibrosis markers, rebuilds the
  redox balance, blocks MAPK/ERK pathway and TGF-β/Smads pathway, and increases PPAR-γ
  expression. NF-κB, nuclear factor-kappa B; MCP-1, monocyte chemotactic protein 1;
