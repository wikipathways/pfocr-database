caption: deficiency-responsive signaling pathway.  deficiency stabilizes the BTS protein
  that interacts with bHLH104 and bHLH105. bHLH34, bHLH104, and bHLH105 can form homodimers
  or heterodimers to activate the transcription of bHLH38/39/100/101 and PYE. FIT
  interacts with bHLH38/39/100/101 to activate the transcription of FRO2 and IRT1.
  PYE regulates the expression of ZIF1 and NAS4 negatively. MYB10 and MYB72 regulate
  the expression of NAS4 positively. The induction of MYB10/72 by  deficiency is partially
  dependent on FIT; bHLH34/104/105 are required for the induction of FIT and MYB10/72.
