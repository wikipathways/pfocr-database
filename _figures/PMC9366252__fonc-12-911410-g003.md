  cells. TAMs activate EMT- IF by secreting various factors, such as TGF-β, TNF-α,
  and IL-8, which act on intracellular related signaling pathways, including TGF-β-
  SMAD signaling pathway, MAPK signaling pathway, WNT-β-Catenin signaling pathway,
  NF-KB signaling pathway, and PI3K-AKT signaling pathway. EMT-IF can inhibit the
  expression of epithelial cell markers and promote the expression of mesenchymal
  cell markers, which leads to EMT in tumor cells.
