  signaling pathway. AMOT2 protein may potentially activate the Hippo-YAP pathway
  by sensing the mechanical alteration from the cell-cell junction. With the assistance
  GPCR, biological activation mediate Lats1/2 function in different circumstances.
  In addition, the SAV1/WW45 and RASSF can activate the MST1/2 through the interaction
  with SARAH domain. Caspase proteins from apoptotic processes can cleave the MST1/2
  and promote human Hippo pathway. While PP2A, STRIPAK complex and TAO may own the
  potential effect on this pathway.
