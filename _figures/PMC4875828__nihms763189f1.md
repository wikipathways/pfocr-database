caption: Cytoskeletal tension is dependent on many factors, including the material
  properties of the ECM (e.g. elastic modulus). In turn, cytoskeletal tension regulates
  the TGFβ pathway at several hierarchical levels, playing a role in TGFβ mRNA and
  protein expression and ligand activation; in receptor spatial organization and multimerization;
  in the choice among canonical Smad2/3 and non-canonical effectors; and in expression
  and function of lineage-specific transcription factors. These transcription factors
  bind to promoters of TGFβ-regulated lineage-specific ECM proteins which, through
  mechanisms that remain unclear, define the material properties of the ECM.
