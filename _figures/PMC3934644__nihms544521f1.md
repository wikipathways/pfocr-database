  or physiological stimuli, upstream pathways, primarily involving cascades of protein
  kinases, may either activate (PI3K/Akt) or inhibit (AMPK) mTOR via modulation of

  multiple downstream pathways/effectors that individually modulate cell growth and
  protein synthesis, cell cycle and proliferation, and cell death/apoptosis. Abbreviations:
  4EBP1 – elongation factor 4E binding protein 1; AMPK – AMP-activated protein kinase;
  eIF4E – elongation initiation factor 4E; mTOR – mammalian target of rapamycin; PI3K
  – phosphatidylinositide-3 kinase.'
