caption: Regulation of platelet activation by CXCR chemokine receptors. Different
  chemokine receptors like CXCR6, CXCR4 and CXCR7 induce GPCR signaling in platelets.
  The chemokines CXCL16, and CXCL12/SDF-1α activate the phosphatidylinositol 3-kinases
  (PI3K) pathway leading to the activation of protein kinase B (Akt) and calcium mobilization
  resulting in platelet activation. Simultaneously, AC is inhibited through Gαi downstream
  of CXCR4 ligation by CXCL12/SDF-1α. MIF binding to CXCR7 substantiates platelet
  survival through PI3K-Akt pathway and downregulates phosphatidyl serine (PS) exposure.
