caption: a. SnoN promotes TGFβ-induced transcription and biological responses by acting
  as an adaptor inducing a Smad-SnoN-ING2 multiprotein complex on promoters of TGFβ-responsive
  genes. b. Sumoylation is critical for the ability of SnoN to inhibit TGFβ-induced
  EMT. In epithelial cells, the SUMO E3 ligase PIAS1 sumoylates SnoN and thereby maintains
  the expression of epithelial markers and phenotype. Activation of TGFβ pathway results
  in the degradation of PIAS1 and subsequent reduction in the level of sumoylated
  SnoN, thus facilitating EMT.
