caption: This figure briefly shows miR-15a/GATA2/MDM2/p53 axis in osteosarcoma. Firstly,
  miR-15a is transported to osteosarcoma cells by serum-derived exosomes, and then
  miR-15a directly binds to GATA2 and inhibits its expression. Subsequently, the downregulated
  expression of GATA2 downregulates the MDM2 translation and activates the p53 signaling
  pathway. Finally, the activated p53 pathway contributes to cell cycle arrest and
  apoptosis of osteosarcoma.
