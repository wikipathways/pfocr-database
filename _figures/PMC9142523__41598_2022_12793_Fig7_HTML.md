caption: Schematic summary of the inflammatory response-promoting effect of hResistin
  in inflammatory IVDD. Resistin activates NF-κB and p38 MAPK through TLR4 in IVD
  cells to express NLRP3, pro-IL-1β and caspase-1. Pro-inflammatory IL-1β induces
  an inflammatory response through IL-1RI. In this process, hResistin promotes the
  production and secretion of inflammatory cytokines of IL-1β, IL-6, and IL-8 and
  ECM-catabolic enzymes of MMP-1, MMP-3, and MMP-13 through the IL-1β pathway.
