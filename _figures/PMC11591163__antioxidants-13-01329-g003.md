caption: Mechanistic diagram of the Nrf-2/AREs signaling pathway. The first step is
  that the activation of the Nrf-2/AREs signaling pathway is initially attacked by
  the electrophile during ischemia and reperfusion. The cys assembles on Keap-1 as
  the redox sensor induces a conformational change that facilitates enhanced separation
  from Nrf-2 as ischemia and reperfusion upset the neuronal homeostatic balance. This
  partition stimulates the translocation of Nrf-2 from the cytoplasm into the nucleus,
  binding to sMaf and regulating the transcription of HO-1, Prdx-6, NQO-1, and AKR1B1,
  among others
