  mediated endogenous signaling pathways. Exogenous signaling pathways: (1) TNF-α—induced
  apoptosis was enhanced by inhibition of NF-κB. (2) Both TNF-α and FasL simultaneously

  release of Cyt C causes oxidative stress on its surface to mediate apoptosis. (3)
  Er stress induces Er ROS generation, leading to mitochondrial dysfunction and enhanced
  mitochondrial ROS production'
