caption: 'Schematic of BMP signalling that leads to osteogenic differentiation. BMPs
  induce Smad-dependent and non-Smad-dependent signaling. In the Smad-dependent pathway,
  Smad (1, 5, or 8) is phosphorylated, complexes with Smad 4, then translocates to
  the nucleus where co-factors are recruited (including Runx2) for the regulation
  of osteogenic gene expression. In the non-Smad-dependent pathway, TAK1 recruits
  TAB1 to stimulate activation of MKK3/6 which in turn activates p38α/β. p38α/β phosphorylates
  Runx2, Dlx5, and Osx, all of which regulate osteogenic gene expression. BMP: Bone
  morphogenetic protein; BMPR: BMP receptor.'
