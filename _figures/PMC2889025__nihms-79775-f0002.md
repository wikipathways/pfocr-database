caption: 'Mechanisms of p53 stabilization. Two primary pathways serve to activate
  p53: oncogneic stress and DNA damage. Without stimulus, p53 is ubiquitinated and
  targeted for proteosomal degradation with the help of mdm2, mdmx and p300. Oncogenic
  stress triggers an up-regulation of p14, which can stabilize p53 by binding to mdm2
  and preventing p53 degradation. DNA damage activates ATM/ATR which can phosphorylate
  p53, mdm2 and mdmx directly or indirectly through chk1/2.'
