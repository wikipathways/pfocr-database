caption: Hic-5 can affect multiple signaling pathways. Hic-5 promotes fibrosis by
  activating the TGF-β/Smad, while inhibiting Smad7. Hic-5 binds and interacts with
  Smad1 to inhibit apoptosis of prostate tumor cells. Hic-5 inhibits β-catenin upon
  interaction with Smad4, thereby promoting the proliferation of OS cells and inhibiting
  apoptosis. Hic-5 promotes inflammation via the NF-κB pathway and apoptosis via the
  TLR4/FADD pathway. Hic-5 promotes hepatocarcinogenesis via the ROS/JNK pathway.
  Hic-5 induces EMT by activating RhoA/ROCK I, thus promoting invasion and metastasis.
  Hic-5/ARA55 inhibits prostate cancer growth by suppressing the expression of c-myc.
  Hic-5, hydrogen peroxide-induced clone 5; OS, osteosarcoma; EMT, epithelial-mesenchymal
  transition; TLR4, Toll-like receptor 4; FADD, Fas-associated protein with death
  domain; ROS, reactive oxygen species.
