  A simplified network of proteins involved in the BCR signaling pathway. Several
  of the proteins (BTK, B-cell linker protein, PAG1, SYK, CD19, CD22, LCK, LYN, PLCγ,
  and caspase recruitment domain-containing protein 11) have been identified with
  p-Tyr either only in BL and FL cell lines or with a higher spectral count in BL

  with a small P symbol in a red circle. The design of this figure was aided by materials
