  into several parts: iron metabolism; lipid peroxidation; Xc-GSH-GPX4 pathway; FSP1–CoQ10–NAD(P)H
  pathway; GCH1–BH4–DHFR pathway. Mitochondria and energy stress also play an important
  role. Abbreviations: BACH1: BTB domain and CNC homolog 1; Fth1: ferritin heavy chain
  1; Ftl1: ferritin light chain 1; NCOA4: nuclear receptor coactivator 4; Hmox1: heme
  oxygenase-1; Keap1: Kelch-like ECH associated protein 1; Nrf2: nuclear factor erythroid
  2-related factor 2; PE: phosphatidyl ethanolamine; PUFA: poly-unsaturated fatty
  acid; PUFA-PE: poly-unsaturated fatty acid-phosphatidyl ethanolamine; Sp1: special
  protein 1; ACSL4: acyl-CoA synthetase long-chain family member 4; LPCAT3: lysophosphatidylcholine
  acyltransferase 3; LOX: lipoxygenase; ACC: acetyl-CoA carboxylase; AMPK: AMP-activated
  protein kinase; BECN1: beclin 1; SLC7A11: subunit solute carrier family 7 member
  11; SLC3A2: solute carrier family 3 member 2; Glu: glutamate; Gly: glycine; GSH:
