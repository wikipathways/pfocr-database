caption: Working model of the Smac-mimetic IAP-inhibitors in radiosensitization. SH-130
  enhances ionizing radiation-induced apoptosis via the intrinsic (mitochondrion)
  pathway, by inhibiting XIAP/cIAP-1 activity and blocking caspase activation. On
  the other hand, SH-130 blocks NF-κB activation induced by TNFα as well as ionizing
  radiation, indicating the suppression effect of radiation-mediated pro-survival
  signaling that attenuates apoptosis.
