  of carrageenan biosynthesis. Question marks indicate open questions into the biosynthetic
  step. It remains unsure if formation of the 3,6-anhydro-bridge is catalyzed in the

  the dashed purple are not necessarily found in the Golgi apparatus. An asterisk
