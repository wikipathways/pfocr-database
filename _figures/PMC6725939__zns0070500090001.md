caption: Abbreviated ganglioside biosynthetic pathway. In the GalNAcT-/- mice, disruption
  of the β-1,4-N-acetylgalactosaminyl transferase gene (Unigene name, Galgt1) results
  in the absence of all of the complex gangliosides beyond GM3 and GD3 (). In the
  GD3s-/- mice, disruption of the α-2,8-sialyl transferase II gene (Unigene name,
  Siat8a) results in the absence of b-series gangliosides and a compensatory overexpression
  of a-series gangliosides, including GD1a (; ). WT mice express varying levels of
  all gangliosides shown. Nomenclature is according to the IUPAC-IUB Joint Commission
  on Biochemical Nomenclature (1998) and Svennerholm (). CER, Ceramide; ○, galactose;
  □, N-acetylgalactosamine; •, glucose; ♦, N-acetylneuraminic acid/sialic acid. Carbon-carbon
  linkages are also shown.
