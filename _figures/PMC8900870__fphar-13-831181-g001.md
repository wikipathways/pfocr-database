caption: Overview of metabotropic NMDA receptor (NMDAR) signaling pathways involved
  in cerebral ischemia. Excessive glutamate binds to the GluN2 subunit of NMDA receptors
  and initiates several pro-death signaling pathways, such as PI3K-PKC-NOX2, Src-PanX1
  and nNOS-MKK3-p38. Glycine binds to the GluN1 subunit of GluN2A-containing NMDA
  receptors, activates Akt-CREB signaling pathway and promotes the survival of neurons.
