caption: The theoretical regulation of bevacizumab on claudin5 signaling pathway.
  Low-dose bevacizumab up-regulates the expression of P-PI3K, which up-regulates P-JNK
  and claudin5, so that maintain the integrity of tight junction protein that reduces
  permeation of vascular ECs. While high-dose bevacizumab up-regulates TGFβ1, then
  down-regulates claudin5, which increases the permeation of vascular ECs.
