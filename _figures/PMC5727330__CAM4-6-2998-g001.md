caption: Downstream effectors of Ras pathway including Ral pathway and their biological
  roles. A series of downstream effectors of Ras exert a myriad of biological effects
  which in case of overactivation can contribute to progression toward neoplasia.
  Ral pathway is activated by RalGDS a member of RalGEF family. Although RalGDS shares
  sequence homology with Ras‐GEFs, it does not show any affinity for Ras. Activation
  of PLD by Ral results in generation of a series of second messengers such as phosphatidic
  acid, lysophosphatidic acid, and diacylglycerol. Ral also activates Cdc42 in a cross‐talk
  with the PI3K pathway.
