caption: Possible apoptotic pathway induced by photoactivated ZnPcSmix in MCTSs 24
  h post PDT. BNIP3 induces apoptosis by dimerization and inserting into the mitochondria.
  In the mitochondria, BNIP3 can interact with BAX resulting in the permeabilization
  of the mitochondrial outer membrane which may directly cause apoptosis. Alternatively,
  permeabilization of the membrane may result in the release of cyt c which activates
  caspases. However, in this study, gene expression profiling revealed downregulation
  of caspases, therefore apoptosis was independent of caspases.
