  several phenotypes that are disruptive to the host. PMT activates Gαi, Gαq/11, and
  Gα12/13 but not Gαs. PMT also triggers the release of Gβγ from the heterotrimeric
  complex, which leads to activation of the phosphoinositide-3-kinase (PI3K) pathway.
