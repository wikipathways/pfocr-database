caption: A synthetic GEF re-routes PKA signalling to activate the CDC42 pathway. The
  Dbl homology–pleckstrin homology domain (DH–PH) from a GEF involved in CDC42 signalling
  is combined with a PDZ domain and a PKA target peptide. The PDZ domain is normally
  bound to the target peptide, inactivating the DH–PH. Forskolin activates PKA that
  phosphorylates the target peptide. Phosphorylation of the target allows the DH–PH
  to activate CDC42 signalling ().
