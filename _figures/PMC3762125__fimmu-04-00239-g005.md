caption: Model of synergy between RasGRP and SOS in TCR signaling. TCR stimulation
  is connected to activation of RasGRP via tyrosine phosphorylation of the adapter
  molecule LAT and activation of PLCγ1, that metabolizes PIP2 into IP3 and DAG to
  trigger two second messenger pathways; Ca2+ and DAG. Activated RasGRP can enhance
  the full activation of SOS by providing Ras·GTP, allosterically activating SOS.
  In principle, the TCR-LAT-PLCγ1 pathway can also indirectly facilitate SOS activation
  via DAG; DGK metabolizes DAG and converts it to PA, which is a possible target for
  the HF and/or PH domains in SOS.
