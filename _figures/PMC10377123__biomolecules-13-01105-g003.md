caption: How betulin modulates the HO-1/Nrf2 signaling pathway. The antioxidant response
  elements (ARE) and Maf proteins work together to create a heterodimer with Nrf2
  after betulin releases it from the Keap1:Nrf2 complex. This promotes the transcription
  of antioxidant genes while inhibiting the AhR signaling axis. By promoting the production
  of phase II cytoprotective enzymes and heme oxygenase-1, betulin exerts cellular
  antioxidant effects.
