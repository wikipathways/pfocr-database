  due to failure to internalize or shed cell surface TNFR1, (2) resulting in TNF-stimulated

  c-Rel-mediated reinforcement of the inhibition of apoptosis, and (3) transcriptional
  activation of cell survival pathways, increased pro-inflammatory cytokine secretion
  and the onset of TRAPS.
