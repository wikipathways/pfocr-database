caption: Sialic acid metabolic pathway. Sialic acid biosynthesis begins from uridine
  diphosphate (UDP)-N-acetylglucosamine, which is produced via the hexosamine pathway.
  GNE catalyzes the first committed step in sialic acid biosynthesis. Sialic acid
  is activated by coupling to cytidine monophosphate (CMP) via the action of CMAS,
  which produces CMP-sialic acid. CMP-sialic acid is used by sialyltransferases for
  sialylation of glycoproteins, which are then exported to the cell surface.
