caption: The regulatory mechanisms of FGD5-AS1 in lung cancer. FGD5-AS1 promoted NSCLC
  cell proliferation through a series of pathways, including the FGD5-AS1/miR-107/FGFRL1
  pathway, the FGD5-AS1/miR-493-5p/DDX5 pathway, and the FGD5-AS1/miR-140-5p/WEE1
  pathway. FGD5-AS1 increased the level of FGFRL1 by sponging miR-107. FGD5-AS1 also
  significantly downregulated miR-493-5p levels to promote the expression of DDX5
  and enhanced the expression of WEE1 by sponging miR-140-5p.
