caption: AZD1208 treatment inhibits PIM activity; thereby elevating ROS levels which
  subsequently results in resistance through activation of p38, AKT, and mTOR signaling.
  Combined treatment of AZD1208 with either a p38 inhibitor (SCIO-469) or an AKT inhibitor
  (MK2206) prevents feedback activation of mTOR and restores PIM inhibitor sensitivity.
  Single mTOR inhibitor treatment (AZD8055) also prevents cell growth, indicating
  the dependency of hematopoietic tumor cells to this pathway.
