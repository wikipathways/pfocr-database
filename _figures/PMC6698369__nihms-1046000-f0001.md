caption: The RNAi pathway restricts viral replication. Viral RNA is sensed by Dcr2,
  and a subset is reverse transcribed by endogenous retrotransposon reverse transcriptases
  into viral DNA. dsRNA is detected by Dcr2 and diced to a 21-nucleotide length so
  that one strand can be loaded into Ago2 for viral RNA silencing. Viral DNA and RNA
  and RISC can be transported out of infected cells to promote viral transcript silencing
  in a cell extrinsic manner.
