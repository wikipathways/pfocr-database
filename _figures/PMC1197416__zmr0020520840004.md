caption: Phosphoinositide signaling pathway at the plasma membrane (PM). The sequential
  actions of Stt4 and Mss4 at the cell surface generate PI4,5P2, which recruits Rom2
  to the plasma membrane through its PH domain for interaction with the cell surface
  sensors. The sensors activate Rom2, a GEF for the Rho1 GTPase. Sfk1 appears to be
  a plasma membrane tethering factor for Stt4. Mss4 cycles between the plasma membrane
  and the nucleus for reasons that are not yet clear. Yck1 and -2 are plasma membrane-associated
  casein kinase 1 isoforms that phosphorylate Mss4 and stabilize it at the plasma
  membrane.
