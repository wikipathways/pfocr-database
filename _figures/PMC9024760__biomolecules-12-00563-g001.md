  on downstream signaling of the insulin receptor. GSK1904529A selectively blocks
  IR and IGF1R autophosphorylation, in this way blocking the downstream signaling
  of IR and IGF1R. VO-Ohpic inhibits PTEN, thereby upregulating PI3K-AKT-mTOR signaling.

  inhibiting SHIP2.
