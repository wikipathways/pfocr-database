caption: Synthesis of nitric oxide and transduction cascades. Neuronal nitric oxide
  synthase (nNOS) is activated by a calcium-dependant calmodulin. NOS produces nitric
  oxide (NO) upon oxidation of arginine into citrulline. NO diffuses and act on presynaptic
  or postsynaptic targets. A well-known pathway of NO is through the activation of
  guanylyl cyclase (GC) that activates a protein kinase G (PKG) leading to Erk activation
  and the stabilization of TORC1 a CREB co-activator. CAT, cation and anion transporter;
  PL. M, plasma membrane. Adapted from Gallo and Iadecola ().
