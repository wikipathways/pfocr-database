  proteins involved in tissue development, protein synthesis, and cellular compromise.

  response, and protein synthesis. Each protein is represented as a node, and edges
  represent interactions between proteins. The intensity of color represents the relative
  levels of proteins (brighter red nodes indicate higher levels). Proteins are separated
  based on the cellular compartments.'
