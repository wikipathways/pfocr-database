caption: The SLN1 signaling pathway in S. cerevisiae. In response to changes in osmotic
  balance, the Sln1p sensor-histidine kinase undergoes autophosphorylation followed
  by a series of reversible phosphorelay reactions involving the phosphotransfer protein
  Ypd1p and the response regulators Ssk1p and Skn7p. The dephospho form of Ssk1p activates
  the Hog1 MAP kinase pathway and genes controlling the osmotic response. Phospho-Skn7p
  is a transcription factor that regulates cell wall and cell cycle genes.
