caption: Schematic diagram of the cholinergic anti-inflammatory pathway (CAP). Stimulation
  of the vagus nerve induces acetylcholine (ACh) release, which binds to a7-nicotinic
  acetylcholine receptors (nAChR) on macrophages. Activation of a7-nAChR initiates
  the Jak2- STAT3 signaling pathway, and inhibits NF-kB nuclear translocation, leading
  to the inhibition of pro-inflammatory cytokine release. OP exposure should activate
  CAP by inhibiting acetylcholinesterase (AChE), causing an increase in acetylcholine
  levels.
