caption: Schematic of folate and one-carbon metabolic pathway. The standard pathway
  includes dihydropteroate synthase (DHPS), dihydrofolate synthetase (DHFS), para-aminobenzoate
  synthase (PabA), dihydrofolate reductase (DHFR), serine hydroxymethyltransferase
  (GlyA), 5,10-methylenetetrahydrofolate dehydrogenase/cyclohydrolase (FolD), 5,10-methylenetetrahydrofolate
  reductase (MetF/MTHFR), thymidylate synthase (ThyA), cobalamin-independent homocysteine
  transmethylase (MetE), cobalamin-dependent methionine synthase (MetH), phosphoribosylglycinamide
  formyltransferase (PurN), and AICAR transformylase/IMP cyclohydrolase (PurH). The
  sulfonamides sulfachloropyridazine (SCP) and sulfamethoxazole (SMX) target DHPS.
  Metabolized para-aminosalicylic acid (m-PAS) and trimethoprim (TMP) target DHFR.
