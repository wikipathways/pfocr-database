  autophagic protein degradation and autolysosome clearance. Loss of TGM2 expression
  synergizes with loss of CDKN1A expression to promote malignant transformation of

  are potentially two critical barriers in the TP53 pathway that prevent oncogenic
  transformation of HMECs.DOI: http://dx.doi.org/10.7554/eLife.07101.037'
