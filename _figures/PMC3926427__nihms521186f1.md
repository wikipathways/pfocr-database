caption: The FGF/FGFR signaling pathway. FGF ligand binding triggers formation of
  the FGF/FGFR/HSP complex, leading to autophosphorylation of the FGFR. Docking proteins
  such as FGFR substrate 2 (FRS2α) and phospholipase C gamma (PLCγ) activate downstream
  pathways, including RAS/RAF/MEK, phosphoinositide 3-kinase (PI3K)/AKT/mammalian
  target of rapamycin (mTOR), and signal transducer and activator of transcription.
  The pathway can be positively (HSP, SFBP) or negatively (SEF, Sprouty, MKP3) regulated
  at many different nodes. Intermediaries of FGF signaling are also activated by additional
  cytokines and growth factors (e.g. IL-6, EGF, and TGFβ).
