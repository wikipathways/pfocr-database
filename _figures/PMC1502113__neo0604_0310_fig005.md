caption: The wnt signaling pathway. Binding of wnt to its receptor, frizzled, leads
  ultimately to translation of β-catenin and Tcf into the nucleus, resulting in transcription
  of genes controlled by this transactivator. The large complex involved in regulating
  free cytoplasmic concentrations of β-catenin contains axin1 and axin2, APC, and
  GSK-3β. Phosphorylation of β-catenin by GSK-3β in the complex leads to degradation
  of β-catenin by the ubiquitin system.
