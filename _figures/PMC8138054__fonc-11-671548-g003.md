  pathway, Wnt/β-catenin pathway, Shh/Gil1 pathway, HIF-1α/SPHK-1 pathway, Micro RNA,

  fates including cell cycle arrest, apoptosis, autophagy, migration, invasion, metastasis,
  EMT, CSCs, angiogenesis, etc.
