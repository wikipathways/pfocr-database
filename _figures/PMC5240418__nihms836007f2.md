caption: 'Hypoxia can activate notch, and androgen-dependent gene expression. Hypoxia
  increases the expression of notch ligands and triggers the notch signalling pathway
  (1). HIF-1α is recruited to notch-responsive promoters and induces expression of
  the notch target genes HES1 and HEY1. PSA expression can be induced by hypoxia owing
  to the presence of a hypoxia-responsive region in the human PSA promoter (2). Hey-1
  competes with AF-1 to repress transcription of androgen-dependent gene expression
  (3). Hey-1 and HIF-1α might, therefore, compete to maintain androgen-dependent gene
  expression. Abbreviations: ARE, androgen responsive element; NED, notch extracellular
  domain; NICD, notch intracellular domain; RBPJ, recombining binding protein suppressor
  of hairless.'
