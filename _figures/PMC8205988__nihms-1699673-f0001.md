caption: Once secreted from adipocytes, adiponectin travels through the circulation
  to different organs/tissues. Adiponectin binds to AdipoRs, and prevents cell cycle
  progression and inhibits cell proliferation by upregulation of p21/p27 siganling
  and down regulation of Akt/mTOR dependent pathway. On the other hand, adiponectin
  increases the secretion of CXCL1 from colon cancer cells. CXCL1 induces senescence
  in stromal fibroblasts in a p53- and p16-dependent manner and upregulates VEGF expression.
  Elevated VEGF increases angiogenesis in the colon tumor. (The figure is created
