caption: Pattern recognition receptors, including Toll-like receptors, RIG-I-like
  receptors and DNA receptors, interact with their cognate adaptors and activate the
  canonical IKKαβγ complex. The TRAF molecules and TAB-TAK1 complex link distinct
  adaptors to the IKKαβ kinase. IKKα or IKKβ phosphorylates inhibitors of NF-κB that
  is subsequently degraded by the proteasome, releasing the p65-p50 dimer to translocate
  into the nucleus and up-regulate the expression of a large set of inflammatory cytokines.
  Dashed lines indicate that the signaling events are not well defined.
