caption: 'Overview of the PTEN/PI3K/Akt signaling pathway. PI3K is initially activated
  by membrane receptor (such as cytokine receptor, RTK, CD19, and integrin). An increase
  in PI3K function leads to the accumulation of PIP3, which subsequently activates
  PDK1 and Akt. PTEN regulates Akt by dephosphorylating PIP3, the product of PI3K.
  Increased Akt activity results in subsequent downstream functions.Abbreviations:
  PDK1, pyruvate dehydrogenase kinase isozyme 1; PI3K, phosphoinositide 3-kinase;
  PIP2, phosphatidylinositol-4,5-bisphosphate; PIP3, phosphatidylinositol 3,4,5-triphosphate;
  PTEN, phosphatase and tensin homolog deleted on chromosome 10; R, cell surface receptor.'
