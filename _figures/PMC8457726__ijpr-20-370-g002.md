caption: Noncanonical Wnt signaling pathways. (A) Schematic representation of mediators
  involved in Wnt5a/Ror signaling pathways. Activation of ubiquitin ligase Shiah2
  by Wnt5a represses Wnt/β-catenin. (B) Wnt/Ca2+signaling pathway. Wnt/Fz interaction
  may activate phosphodiesterase 6 (PDE6) causing Ca2+ to decrease cGMP. The release
  of Ca2+ induces NFAT, NFкB, and CREB translocation into the nucleus regulating the
  expression of genes. (C) Wnt/Planar cell polarity pathway. Van Gogh (Vangle2) forms
  a complex with prickle (Pk) responsible for antagonizing PCP pathway. Wnt/Fz/ Ror/Ryk/PTK7/Dvl
  complex also recruits Dishevelled associated activator of morphogenesis (Daam1)
  involve in actin cytoskeleton rearrangement
