caption: AhR signaling pathway. AhR is a ligand-activated transcription factor and
  inactive AhR forms a complex with Hsp90, XAP2, c-Src, and p23 in the cytoplasm.
  As AhR binds to the ligand, conformational changes occur, and they form complexes
  with the ARNT to enter the nucleus. the genomic regulatory region of AhR target
  genes contains the XRE, and in the nucleus, besides recognizing the XRE, the AhR-ARNT
  complex also interacts with other transcriptional regulators to control the expression
  of multiple target genes. AhR, aryl hydrocarbon receptor; ARNT, aryl hydrocarbon
  receptor nuclear transport protein; XRE, exogenous response element; CYP, cytochrome
  P450.
