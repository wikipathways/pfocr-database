caption: Upon Wnt signalling activation, DOT1L-containing complexes bind Wnt target
  gene chromatin. DOT1L interacts with SIRT1 and inhibits its function, preventing
  Wnt pathway hyper-activation. When Wnt signalling is activated in the absence of
  DOT1L function, high SIRT1 activity mediates the recruitment of transcriptional
  activators to LEF1 and TCF1 genes. High Wnt signalling leads to deleterious downstream
  effects and loss of cartilage homeostasis.
