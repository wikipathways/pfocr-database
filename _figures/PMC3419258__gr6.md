  to the FGFR1c-αKlotho complex and activates ERK1/2 kinase leading to SGK1 phosphorylation.
  SGK1 in turn phosphorylates NHERF-1, leading to internalization and degradation
  of NaPi-2a. PTH signals through activation of PKA and PKC, which also leads to phosphorylation
  of NHERF-1. KL, αKlotho; PKA, protein kinase A; PKC, protein kinase C.
