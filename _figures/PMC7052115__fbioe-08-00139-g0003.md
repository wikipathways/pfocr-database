  XDH, xylitol dehydrogenase; XKS, Xylulose kinase; XR, xylose reductase. Intermediates:
  E-4-P, erythrose-4-phosphate; F6P, Fructose 6-phosphate; G-3-P, glyceraldehyde-3-phosphate;
