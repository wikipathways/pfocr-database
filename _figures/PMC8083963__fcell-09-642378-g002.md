caption: 'Sorting nexin 27 (SNX27) domain architecture. SNX27 contains a modular architecture
  with three established domains: the N-terminal PDZ domain (light blue), middle PX
  domain (gray), and C-terminal FERM domain (purple), which is divided into F1, F2,
  and F3 sub-domains. Key interactions in the SNX27/retromer pathway are indicated.
  VPS26 (dark blue) binds the PDZ domain. Ras binds the F1 sub-domain, and FAM21 engages
  both the SNX27 F3 sub-domain and retromer VPS35 subunit. All three SNX27 domains
  directly engage protein partners embedded in membranes enriched for PI3P.'
