caption: Proposed mechanisms of Notch and Wnt/β-catenin signaling in GC progression.
  When ligands bind to the Notch receptors, activated NICD translocates into the nucleus
  and forms a complex with activated β-catenin. The complex formation results in the
  regulation of target genes to induce cell proliferation and metastasis and inhibits
  apoptosis. Luteolin blocks the complex formation and inhibits cell proliferation
  and metastasis, and increases cell apoptosis, suggesting an anti-tumorigenic effect.
  Luteolin may be a drug for GC treatment
