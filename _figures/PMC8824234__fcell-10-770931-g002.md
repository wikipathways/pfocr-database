caption: 'Signaling pathway of HO due to mechanical stimulation: Mechanical stimulation
  through mTORC1 leads to an increase in Sirt1 translocation into the nucleus, followed
  by a decrease in SOST secretion. SOST can bind to LRP5/6 to inhibit β-catenin. Mechanical
  loading can also activate Runx2/3 gene expression through YAP/TAZ. Thus mechanical
  stimulation promotes osteogenic gene expression through mTORC1 and YAP/TAZ. Meanwhile,
  mechanical stimulation can inhibit PPARγ gene expression through the TGF-β pathway,
  thereby suppressing lipogenic differentiation. These combined effects lead to a
  stem cell fate shift.'
