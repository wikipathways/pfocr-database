caption: Estrogens stimulate ERα complex formation with cytoplasmic signaling proteins
  like MNAR, cas, and c-SRC. This complex activates the ERK1/2 signal transduction
  pathway, and phosphorylation of RSK. RSK1 signaling inhibits apoptosis via phosphorylation
  and inactivation of the pro-apoptotic protein Bad. RSK2 can translocate to the nucleus
  where it regulates nuclear targets that drive proliferation. In the nucleus RSK
  directly phosphorylates ERα stimulating its transcriptional activity.
