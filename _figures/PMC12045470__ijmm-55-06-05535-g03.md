caption: Mechanisms of sepsis-induced kidney injury. LPS regulate kidney inflammation
  by modulating the NLRP3/NF-κB or TLR4/Myd88/NF-κB signaling pathways; regulate oxidative
  stress and ferroptosis by modulating the NOX4, Nrf2 or P13K/Akt signaling pathways;
  and LPS-induced inflammatory mediators can lead to renal microcirculation disorders,
  renal hypoperfusion, ischemia and hypoxia and exacerbate sepsis-induced kidney injury.
