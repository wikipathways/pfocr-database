caption: (A) TCR stimulation induces NFATc1-mediated PD-1 upregulation. PD-1 is phosphorylated
  by TCR-proximal Src-family kinases Lck and Fyn and recruits SHP-2 phosphatase which
  downregulates TCR- and CD28-mediated signals thereby preventing induction of glycolysis
  by these pathways. (B) Besides, glycolysis, PD-1 inhibits amino acid transport and
  metabolism and promotes lipolysis and FAO by inducing expression of ATGL and CPT1A,
  respectively. PD-1 also enhances the TGF-β signaling through Smad3 activation, a
  pathway known to promote FAO, OXPHOS, and Treg differentiation.
