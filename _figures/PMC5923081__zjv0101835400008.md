caption: Proposed model of caspase-dependent suppression of type I IFN responses during
  KSHV reactivation. KSHV lytic reactivation triggers both the pathogen-sensing pathway
  and the activation of caspases. (Left) The presence of active caspases causes attenuation
  of IFN-β induction, while apoptosis is blocked by KSHV downstream of caspase activation.
  (Right) In the absence of caspase activity, IFN-β is induced, which reduces KSHV
  lytic reactivation.
