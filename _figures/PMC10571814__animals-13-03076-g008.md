caption: Schematic representation of the oxytocin signalling pathway, with yellow
  boxes representing proteins identified in this study and green circles representing
  metabolites identified in this study. Four consecutive boxes were used to represent
  expression at E75, E85, E105, and E125, with expression levels annotated by using
  log2 values relative to the expression in different colours. Solid arrows indicate
  direct action, dashed arrows indicate indirect action, solid flat lines indicate
  inhibition, “+p” represents nitration, and “−p” represents denitration. This visual
  representation can better explain the roles of the identified proteins and metabolites
  in the oxytocin signalling pathway during hair follicle (HF) development.
