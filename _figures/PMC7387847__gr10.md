caption: Proposed mechanism of Nrf2/SIKE-mediated hepatic injury induced by long-term
  PM2.5exposure. After PM2.5 long-term challenge, down-regulated activation of Nrf2
  repressed SIKE expression, contributing to oxidative stress and inflammatory response
  through reducing anti-oxidants and facilitating TBK1/NF-κB signaling. These effects
  led to hepatic injury consequently. However, Juglanin treatment showed protective
  effects against PM2.5-induced liver injury by improving Nrf2/SIKE signaling pathway.
