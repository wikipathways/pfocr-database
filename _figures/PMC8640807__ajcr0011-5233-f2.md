caption: SIRT1 pathway overview in chemoresistance cancer. SIRT1 is an NAD+-dependent
  histone deacetylase protein that modulates several pathways of chemoresistance cancer.
  Overexpression of SIRT1 promotes DNA damage repair, antiapoptotic effects, confer
  cell proliferation and increases the function of chemoresistance in cells via protein
  regulators such as activators or inhibitors of SIRT1 have been described. The downstream
  activity targets deacetylation such as cellular metabolism, senescence, DNA repair
  and modulates the tumor microenvironment through multiple cell signaling pathways.
