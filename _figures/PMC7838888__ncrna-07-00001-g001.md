  pathway to facilitate DLBCL cell growth. (C) The lncRNA DBH-AS1 controls the translation
  of fibronectin 1 (FN1) through binding to the RNA-binding protein, BUD13. (D) Many
  lncRNAs have been suggested to function as miRNA sponges to sequester miR-195.
