caption: Figure 5. A model illustrates the involvement of PTP4A3 in multiple steps
  of canonical autophagy pathway. 1) PTP4A3 acts as an activator to promote autophagosome
  formation in a PIK3C3-BECN1-dependent manner; 2) PTP4A3 accelerates LC3-I to LC3-II
  conversion in an ATG5-dependent manner; 3) PTP4A3 enhances the degradation of SQSTM1,
  a key autophagy substrate; 4) PTP4A3 itself is a novel autophagic substrate. Sites
  of action of drug treatments (rapamycin, pepstatin and E64D, CQ, Baf A1) are indicated
  at where appropriate.
