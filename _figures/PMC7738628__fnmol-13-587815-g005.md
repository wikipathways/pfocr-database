  numbers of genes affected in the pathway at P5 (orange circle) and/or P10 (blue

  at P5 and P10 (B; P38-MAP signaling) or P10 only (C; B-lymphocyte signaling). The
  genes detected to enrich the pathways are indicated on age dependent colored background.
