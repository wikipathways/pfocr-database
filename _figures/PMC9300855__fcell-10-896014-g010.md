caption: Schematic diagram depicting the signaling pathway of mitophagy induced by
  clinostat-simulated microgravity and the role of mitophagy in endothelial functional
  changes. Proteotoxic stress activated by microgravity leads to ER stress and subsequently
  Ca2+ transfer from the ER to mitochondria through IP3R, resulting in mitochondria
  Ca2+ overload, increased fission, mitochondrial membrane potential collapse, and
  mitophagy. Mitophagy inhibits permeability and cell migration by ameliorating ROS
  production which induces NLRP3 inflammasome activation.
