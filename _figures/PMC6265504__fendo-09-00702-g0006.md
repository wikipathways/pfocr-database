caption: SIRT1 protects against cerebral ischemic injury in multiple mechanisms. SIRT1
  deacetylate p53 to block the p53-induced apoptotic pathway, thus, promoting neuronal
  survival. SIRT1 deacetylates endothelial nitric oxide synthase (eNOS) to regulate
  vascular tone and maintain brain blood flow. SIRT1protects against white matter
  injury in ischemic injury, possible via promoting the oligodendrocyte regeneration.
  Finally, SIRT1 is required for ischemic preconditioning (IPC) and resveratrol preconditioning
  (RPC) induced ischemic neuroprotection. Arg, L-arginine, NO, nitric oxide.
