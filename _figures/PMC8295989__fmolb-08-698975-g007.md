caption: Schematic diagram of mechanism of TRPC6 in renal IRI. TRPC6 is overexpressed
  during IRI and leads to damaged Ca2+ influx into TECs, which activates ERK and PI3K/AKT
  signaling pathways. Then, these two pathways lead to cell apoptosis by inhibiting
  cytoprotective autophagy which can remove the damaged organelles upon IRI. Moreover,
  the activation of the ERK pathway induces excessive ROS production from the mitochondria
  and results in aggravating apoptosis.
