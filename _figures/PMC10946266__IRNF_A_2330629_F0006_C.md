caption: MgH2 protects against APAP-AKI by inhibiting TXNIP/NLRP3/NF-κB pathway. APAP
  overdose leads to elevated NAPQI level and GSH depletion, resulting in enhanced
  renal ROS production. ROS can lead to mitochondrial dysfunction and activate TXNIP/NLRP3/NF-κB
  signaling pathway, resulting in apoptosis and inflammation. Besides, TXNIP can further
  aggravate accumulation of ROS and oxidative stress. MgH2 may alleviate APAP-AKI
  through improving oxidative stress, inflammation and fibrosis via suppressing TXNIP/NLRP3/NF-κB
  pathway
