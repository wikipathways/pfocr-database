caption: In unstimulated cells, a minimal complex of GSK-3, axin, APC and β-catenin
  is required for GSK-3-mediated phosphorylation of β-catenin which targets it for
  ubiqitination and degradation. Wnt stimulation activates the receptor Frizzled,
  and co-receptor LRP5/6, that then signal through Dishevelled, using an unclear mechanism,
  to inactivate β-catenin phosphorylation. Unphosphorylated β-catenin accumulates
  and translocates to the nucleus where it transactivates genes regulated by TCF/LEF
  transcription factors.
