caption: (A) Nodal binds to its cell surface receptor in the presence of the co-receptor
  EGF-CFC, activating the resulting complex. After phosphorylation, the Smad2/Smad4
  complex translocates to the nucleus. Upon binding of the transcription factor FoxH1,
  transcription of Nodal target genes is initiated. (B) Nodal-independent transcription
  via the same pathway does not require the co-receptor EGF-CFC or the transcription
  factor FoxH1. (C) Lefty antagonises Nodal function by blocking either its co-receptor,
  EGF-CFC, or by directly binding to Nodal. Factors that evolved in the ancestor of
  bilaterians are displayed in red, all other factors evolved in the ancestor of eumetazoans
  or earlier. Figure modified after .
