caption: BMP/TGFβ signaling in vivo is mediated by either the Smad pathway (canonical)
  or the Non-Smad (non-canonical) pathways. Both pathways could mediate important
  functions of BMP/TGFβ in skeletogenesis. The non-Smad pathways also regulate Smad
  proteins by modulating linker region and C-terminal phosphorylation. Moreover, Smad
  proteins also modulate activities of molecules in Non-Smad pathways. The preference
  of intracellular pathways by different receptor complexes, the crosstalk between
  these two pathways, and the exact signaling mechanisms of the Non-Smad pathways
  in skeletal system remain to be investigated.
