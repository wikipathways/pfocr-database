caption: Participation of DDX3 during HCV replication. Two phenomena are described,
  HCV core protein directly interacts with DDX3 and DDX3 binds the HCV RNA. The interaction
  between HCV core and DDX3 results in the translocation of DDX3 to lipid droplets
  and viral replication sites, avoiding type-I IFN pathway activation. On the other
  hand, DDX3 binds the HCV RNA, inducing stress granules assembly and promoting DDX3
  relocalization to lipid droplets to carry out viral assembly and viral replication.
