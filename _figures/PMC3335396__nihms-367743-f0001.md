caption: Ligand-dependent activation of the Hh pathway. In the absence of ligand,
  Ptch1 inhibits SMO activity. This inhibition leads to transcriptional repression
  mediated by Gli3 (GLI3-R). In the presence of ligand, SHH binds to Ptch1, this relieves
  the repression of Ptch1 on SMO. Activation of SMO removes transcriptional repression
  imparted by GLI3-R allowing transcription of Hh target genes (e.g. Ptch1 and Gli1)
  by the transcriptional activators GLI2-A and Gli1.
