  PTEN expression to enhance tamoxifen resistance. DSCAM-AS1 also sponges miR-137

  Eleanors promotes tamoxifen resistance by upregulating ESR1 expression. GAS5: growth-arrest

  homolog; EPS8: epidermal growth factor receptor pathway substrate 8.'
