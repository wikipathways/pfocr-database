caption: Binding of TGFβ to its cognate receptor initiates the signaling pathway.
  In the canonical pathway, activated type I receptors phosphorylates R-Smads, which
  subsequently form a complex with the Co-Smad, Smad4. The resulting R-Smads/Co-Smad
  complex translocates to the nucleus and interacts with distinct transcription factors
  to turn on or off transcription of many TGFβ-responsive genes that regulate cell
  proliferation and differentiation. Additionally, TGFβ activates different non-Smad
  pathways, including PI3K, Ras, Par6, Jnk/p38/MAPK pathways, which cumulatively regulate
  TGFβ-mediated functions.
