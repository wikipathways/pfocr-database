caption: The working model for CARMA3-mediated NF-κB activation in GPCR signal transduction
  pathway. GPCR-induced NF-κB activation involves a signaling cascade of PKC–CARMA3–BCL10–MALT1–TRAF6
  leading to polyubiquitination of the IKK complex, whereas a CARMA3-independent and
  PKC-dependent signal induces IKKβ phosphorylation by an unknown kinase in the GPCR
  signaling pathway. In the absence of CARMA3, GPCR-induced polyubiquitination of
  the IKK complex is defective, thereby resulting in a defective IKK complex.
