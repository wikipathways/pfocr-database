  by insulin receptor tyrosine kinase activity leading to the activation of both the
  PI3K-Akt pathway and the MAPK pathway. The most well characterized function of insulin
  signaling is glucose homeostasis; however insulin stimulates several other cellular
  mechanisms including the biosynthesis of proteins, glycogen, and lipids. Insulin
  receptor substrate (IRS), glycogen synthetase kinase β (GSK3β), extracellular signal-related
  kinase (ERK), Srchomology-2-containing protein (Shc), forkhead box protein O1 (FOXO1),

  p70 ribosomal protein S6 kinase (p70S6K), hormone sensitive lipase (HSL), phosphodiesterase

  receptor-bound protein 2 (Grb2), son of sevenless (SOS).'
