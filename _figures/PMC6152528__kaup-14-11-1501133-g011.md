  autophagy in GBM cells. FKB induces autophagy through the ER stress-dependent ATF4-DDIT3-TRIB3-AKT-MTOR-RPS6KB1

  and ATF4-DDIT3-TRIB3 signaling. Increased activity in these proteins inactivates
  the phosphorylation status of AKT and its critical downstream components, MTOR and
  RPS6KB1, thus leading to increased autophagy flux.
