caption: Biosynthetic pathways for GDP-fucose. The de novo pathway transforms GDP-mannose
  into GDP-fucose via two enzymes, GDP-mannose 4,6-dehydratase (GMD) and GDP-keto-6-deoxymannose
  3,5-epimerase/4-reductase (FX protein). The salvage pathway utilizes free fucose
  in the cytosol to create GDP-fucose by the action of fucose kinase and GDP-fucose
  pyrophosphorylase. The resulting GDP-fucose is used by fucosyltransferases (FucTs)
  in the Golgi apparatus to catalyze transfer onto glycoconjugates.
