caption: Coordinated proteostasis of the stress response and macroautophagy. Accumulation
  of harmful proteins by physiological stresses activates the HSF1-mediated stress
  response pathway, causing an increase in molecular chaperones, co-chaperones, and
  macroautophagy-related genes. Harmful proteins are refolded by molecular chaperones,
  which simultaneously activate the macroautophagy adaptor p62. Protein aggregates
  bind to active p62 and are engulfed into autophagosomes.
