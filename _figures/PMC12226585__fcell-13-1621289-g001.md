caption: 'ADSCs/ADSCs-exosomes mediates a multidimensional network of mechanisms for
  IRI repair. (A) ADSCs/ADSCs-exosomes mainly act on four major pathways: the Wnt/β-catenin
  signaling pathway is a key regulator that promotes cell proliferation and tissue
  regeneration; the NF-κB signaling pathway is a key mediator that inhibits inflammatory
  responses; the STAT3 signaling pathway is a key factor that enhances the ability
  of neovascularization; the NRF2 signaling pathwayis an important target for anti-oxidative
  stress. The four core intervention directions of ADSCs therapy: anti-inflammation,
  anti-oxidation, anti-apoptosis and pro-regeneration. (B) ADSCs/ADSCs-exosomes have
  different core intervention strategies for different organs: heart - macrophage
  polarization, liver - mitochondrial dynamics, brain - iron death blockade, kidney
  - pathological fibrosis process blockade'
