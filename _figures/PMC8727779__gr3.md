caption: Schematic diagram of SHP2 related cytokines and growth factor dependent RAS-RAF-ERK,
  PI3K-AKT and JAK-STAT signaling pathways. SHP2 dephosphorylates negative regulators
  in RAS-RAF-ERK pathway through a variety of mechanisms, such as RAS-GAP, paxillin
  and sprouty, to regulate signal transduction, tumor invasion, cell proliferation,
  differentiation, apoptosis and survival; SHP2 inhibits the activation of PI3K-AKT
  pathway and regulates cell proliferation and apoptosis through dephosphorylation
  of α-catenin, Gab1 and P85 binding sites; SHP2 dually regulates the JAK-STAT signaling
  pathway, which is essential for regulating DNA damage, cell growth, differentiation,
  survival and death.
