caption: 'Metabolic pathway of arachidonic acid via 5- lipoxygenase. Arachidonic acid
  is released from membrane phospholipids by phospholipase A2 and converted to 5-HPETE
  by 5-LOX and 5-LOX activating protein (FLAP). 5-HPETE can then form either 5-HETE
  or LTA4. LTA4 then becomes LTB4 or LTC4. LTC4 can then form LTD4 and subsequently
  LTE4. cPLA2: Cytosolic phospholipase A2; LOX: Lipoxygenase; FLAP: 5-LOX activating
  protein; HPETE: Hydroperoxyeicosatetraenoic acid; HETE: Hydroxyl 6 trans 8, 11,
  14 cis eicosatetraenoic acid; LT: Leukotriene.'
