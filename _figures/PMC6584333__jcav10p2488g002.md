caption: Schematic represents regulatory molecules involved in the ubiquitin-lysosome,
  autophagy-lysosome and caspase-mediated degradation pathway of BCR-ABL protein.
  PTP1B dephosphorylates BCR-ABL residues to prevent the ubiquitination of BCR-ABL
  protein, aggregation formation and lysosome-mediated degradation. In the AS2O3-induced
  BCR-ABL protein autophagy-lysosome degradation, BCR-ABL protein interacts with p62/SQSTM1.
  In the imatinib-induced BCR-ABL protein autophagy-lysosome degradation, BCR-ABL
  protein co-locates with autophagy markers LC3 and GABARAP. Caspase 3 and caspase
  7 can down-regulate BCR-ABL protein level by cleavage.
