  YAP signaling and endothelial cell behavior through direct contact. On the other

  mimicry in glioma. Moreover, TNC-ephrinB2-ephB4 signaling pathway supports GSCs
  differentiation into endothelial cells. YAP, Yes-associated protein; MMP, matrix
