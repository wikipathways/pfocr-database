  treatment. TGF-β, one of the cytokines produced by CCD-18Co myofibroblasts, binds

  or promotion). Berberine can inhibit the TGF-β/Smads signaling to suppress the CCD-18Co-stimulated

  pathway (Yellow lines, Arrows represent activation or promotion, flat lines represent
  inhibition or down-regulation)
