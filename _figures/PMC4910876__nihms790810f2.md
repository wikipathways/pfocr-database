caption: 'Regulation of metabolic processes downstream of mTORC2, with major known
  substrates and metabolic processes highlighted. Protein kinases, including mTORC2,
  are boxed in red. The core proteins of mTORC2 – mTOR, Rictor, mLST8, mSin1, and
  Protor – are depicted as interacting directly with the mTOR kinase, whereas proteins
  with nutrient sensitive or transient interactions with the mTORC2 core – DEPTOR,
  IKK, Sestrin3, Tel2/Tti1, and Xpln – are depicted in a separate, adjacent box. The
  TSC complex – in particular, TSC2 - interacts with and regulates mTORC2 (), but
  it is not clear how or if this interaction is regulated. Dashed Box: Insulin stimulates
  mTORC1 through the AKT-mediated phosphorylation of specific sites on TSC2 and PRAS40
  (; ), but the requirement for mTORC2 in the insulin-mediated activation of mTORC1
  remains unclear. Extracellular signals in addition to insulin, IGF-1, and leptin
  that regulate PI3K or PIP3 may also regulate mTORC2 activity, but these are not
  depicted here.'
