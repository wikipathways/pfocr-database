caption: Downstream network diagrams generated by pairing ligands from light damaged
  NSR and RPE to PPC cell surface receptors listed in . (a) custom network pathway
  for NSR/rod PPC, (b) RPE/rod PPC, (c) NSR/cone PPC and (d) RPE/cone PPC. Signaling
  molecules are compartmentalized into extracellular (ligands), plasma membrane (receptors),
  cytoplasmic and nuclear. Each lines connecting edge (arrow or bar) describes native
  interactions and line colors represent predictions, activation (orange line), inhibition
  (blue line) based on interacting gene expression states, up-regulated (red) or down-regulated
  (green). An inconsistent finding (yellow line) indicates a difference in expression
  level of the downstream molecule between our dataset and the IPA database relative
  to the expression level of the upstream gene. Canonical migratory network hubs in
  rod PPCs include STAT3 and TP53, comparable hubs in cone PPCs are STAT2 and ITCH.
