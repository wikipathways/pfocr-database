caption: Schematic illustration of different signaling pathway mediated by α3(IV)NC1.
  α3(IV)NC1 binds to αVβ3 and α3β1 integrins, and inhibits phosphorylation of FAK.
  Inhibition of FAK activation leads to inhibition of FAK/PI-3K/eIF4E/4E-BP1 cap dependent
  translation. In addition α3(IV)NC1 inhibits NFκB mediated signaling in hypoxic conditions
  leading to inhibition of COX-2/VEGF/bFGF expression, resulting in inhibition of
  hypoxic tumor angiogenesis.
