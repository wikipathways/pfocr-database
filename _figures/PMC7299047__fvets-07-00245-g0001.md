caption: Metabolic pathway of cholecalciferol (vitamin D3) and 1-alpha-hydroxycholecalciferol
  (1α) to 1,25-dihydroxycholecalciferol (1,25-(OH)2-D3). Vitamin D3 is converted to
  25-hydroxycholecalciferol (25-OH-D3) in liver, then 25-OH-D3 is converted to 1,25-(OH)2
  D3 in Kidney. 1α travels to liver to be converted to 1,25-(OH)2-D3. Green circle
  highlights C-25 of D3; orange circle highlights C-25 with hydroxyl group for 25-OH-D3;
  blue circles denote C-1 and C-25 hydroxyl groups of 1,25-(OH)2-D3; purple circle
  highlights C-1 hydroxyl group of 1α.
