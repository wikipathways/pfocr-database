caption: The external and intrinsic mechanisms to mediate immunotherapy resistance
  for CSCs in HCC. External evasion mechanisms are mainly related to HBV/HCV infection,
  alcohol/nonalcoholic steatohepatitis, hypoxia stimulation, abnormal angiogenesis,
  and crosstalk between CSCs and immune cells. Intrinsic evasion mechanisms mainly
  include activation of the Wnt/β-Catenin signaling pathway and TGF-β signaling pathway,
  low-level expression of TAP and/or MHC molecules, and high-level expression of CD47
  and PD-L1.
