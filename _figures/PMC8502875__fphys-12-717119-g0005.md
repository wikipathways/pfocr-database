caption: The mechanism of t-AUCB regulation of ischemic arrhythmia. T-AUCB, a preparation
  of sEHis, regulates microRNA-1 by regulating SRF and the PI3Kα/Akt/GSK3β pathway,
  thereby reducing the size of myocardial infarction and inhibiting ischemic arrhythmias.
  During this process, the PI3Kα/Akt/GSK3β pathway and SRF interact with each other.
  SRF indicates serum response factor; shSRF indicates SRF small hairpin; siSRF indicates
  SRF small interfering RNA; wortmannin is the inhibitor of PI3K; HS173 indicates
  PI3Kα inhibitor; MK2206 is the inhibitor of Akt; TWS119 is the inhibitor of GSK3β.
