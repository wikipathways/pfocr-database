caption: Schematic of proposed mechanisms. (A) Our data show that SCD neutrophils
  produce increased NETs, which are exacerbated upon stimulation (eg, ionomycin),
  leading to increased phosphorylation of NET specific kinases (ERK and Akt), which
  has been shown to result in histone citrullination and inhibition of apoptosis via
  upregulation of antiapoptotic proteins (eg, Mcl-1 [prothrombotic state]). Peptidylarginine
  deiminase 4 (PAD4) forms a complex with intracellular calcium to catalyze histone
  citrullination. NET stimuli activate PKC, PLC, and PI3K, which in turn activate
  ERK and AKT, resulting in calcium-PAD4 complex formation, which catalyzes histone
  citrullination. (B) We found that AnxA1Ac2-26 interacts with Fpr2/ALX, suppressing
  ERK and Akt phosphorylation, preventing histone citrullination, and enabling apoptosis
  by activating caspase-3 (proresolving state). Mcl-1, myeloid cell leukemia protein-1;
  PI3K, phosphatidylinositol-4,5-bisphosphate 3-kinase; PKC, protein kinase C; PLC,
  phospholipase C.
