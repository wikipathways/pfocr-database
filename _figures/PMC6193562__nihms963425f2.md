  and LKB1 use an unusual form of pyrimidine biosynthesis initiated by carbamoylphosphate
  synthetase-1 (CPS1). NSCLC with mutations in KRAS and p53 display glucose-dependent
  glutathione (GSH) biosynthesis. b. KRAS-mutant pancreatic ductal adenocarcinoma
  (PDAC) requires MAPK signaling to regulate glucose flux into the hexosamine biosynthesis
  pathway (HBP) and non-oxidative pentose phosphate pathway (Non-Oxidative PPP). These
  pathways contribute to protein glycosylation and nucleic acid synthesis, respectively.
