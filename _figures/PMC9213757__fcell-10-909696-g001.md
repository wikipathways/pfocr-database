caption: Histone modifications involved in dictating the cell cycle phase specific
  DSB repair pathway choice. (A) Histone mark H4K20me1/2 is enriched in G1 phase of
  the cell cycle. 53BP1 binds to nucleosomes containing both H4K20me1/2 and H2AK15ub,
  mediated by RNF168 upon DSB induction, to promote DSB repair by NHEJ (B) Unmethylated
  H4K20me0 is enriched in S/G2 phases of the cell cycle. BRCA1-BARD1 heterodimer recognizes
  H4K20me0 and H2AK15ub to facilitate HR repair (right). BRCA1 mediated H2AK129ub
  recruits SMARCAD1 to promote DNA end resection and thus HR (left).
