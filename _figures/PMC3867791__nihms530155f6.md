  leads to activation of PKR-like ER-localized kinase (PERK), which phosphorylates
  the eIF2α subunit to promote expression of activating transcription factor 4 (ATF4),
  and elevated REDD1 mRNA transcription. REDD1-mediated inhibition of mTORC1 during
  prolonged serum deprivation facilitates relief of a negative feedback pathway to
  promote activation of Akt in a manner that promotes cell survival.
