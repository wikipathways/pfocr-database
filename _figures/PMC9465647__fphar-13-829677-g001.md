caption: The RA molecular regulation chain surrounding SIRT1. SIRT1 can downregulate
  NF-κB and MMP1/MMP13, both of which promote the invasion of RA. NF-κB participates
  in mir-29a-3p/Keap1 and mir-23a-3p/Cul3. They both regulate downstream Nrf2, mediate
  ARE expression, and produce ROS. On the other hand, BK/B2R/PKCμ Pathway and Nrf2/ARE/ROS
  pathway can act on downstream MAPK, which can mediate AP-1 and NF-κB, thus forming
  a closed loop of complex signaling pathways starting from the SIRT1 pathway.
