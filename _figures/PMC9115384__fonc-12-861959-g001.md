caption: The autophagy pathway and multiple stages can be inhibited. The autophagy
  pathway consists 5 steps. Intracellular membranes are prepared by initiation and
  vesicle nucleation to form AVs through the formation of PI3P on membranes. Next,
  LC3-I is conjugated to PE on emerging AVs. Subsequently, LC3 is docked with the
  cargo adapter so that the cargo can be loaded into the AVs. After AVs matures, it
  fuses with lysosomes to complete the degradation of cargo and the recycling of nutrients.
  Autophagy inhibitors are shown in green boxes.
