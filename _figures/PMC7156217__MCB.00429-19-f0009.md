caption: A possible model for the CD40 signaling mechanisms. Upon ligation, CD40 recruits
  TRAF6, TRAF3, TRAF2, cIAP1, IKK, and MEKK to initiate the immediate phases of IKK
  and JNK activation within 10 min. Thereafter, if TRAF2 is phosphorylated on Ser-11
  by TBK1, the TRAF2-cIAP1-IKK-MEKK complex dissociates from CD40 and translocates
  to the cytoplasm to initiate the secondary/prolonged phases of IKK and JNK activation
  within 45 to 90 min; if TRAF2 is not phosphorylated, the TRAF2-associated complex
  then translocates to lipid rafts where TRAF2 and TRAF3 are degraded, resulting in
  NIK protein accumulation and NIK-dependent activation of the noncanonical NF-κB
  pathway within 2 to 12 h after ligation.
