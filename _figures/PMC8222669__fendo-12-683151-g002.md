caption: Regulation of HN on Keap1/Nrf2 signaling pathway under oxidative stress.
  HN promotes Keap1 degradation and release of Nrf2. Under stress conditions, HN inhibits
  the expression of Keap1, promotes the dissociation of Nrf2 and Keap1, activates
  the expression of antioxidant genes (SOD, CAD, HO-1, NQO1), and protects mitochondria
  from oxidative stress damage. Stress also triggers UPRmt, promotes the expression
  of antioxidant genes and the recovery of mitochondrial function, further promoting
  the formation of HN.
