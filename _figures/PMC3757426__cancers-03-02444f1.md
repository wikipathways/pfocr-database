caption: Epidermal growth factor receptor (EGFR) crosstalk. (A). EGFR physical interactions
  or heterodimerization with other membrane receptors. EGFR is able to bind to other
  receptors including other members of the ErbB family, TKRs, GPCRs and integrins;
  (B). Ligand-independent transactivation of EGFR; (C). Ligand-dependent transactivation
  of EGFR, or triple-membrane-passing signal (TMPS) pathway. Ligand-binding to TLRs,
  TGFpR, TKRs, integrins and GPCRs activates ADAM transmembrane metalloproteases resulting
  in the shedding of EGFR-ligands and the activation of EGFR.
