caption: Overview of Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway. In the absences of Wnt signaling
  degradation and phosphorylation of β-catenin will occur by the destruction complex,
  consisting of Axin, casein kinase 1α (CK1α), glycogen synthase kinase 3β (GSK3β),
  and the tumor suppressor adenomatous polyposis coli (APC) and in the presence of
  Wnt signaling cytosolic β-catenin translocation to the nucleus and transcript the
  genes. Tankyrase stabilized the β-catenin degradation by poly(ADP-ribosyl)ation
  (PARsylation) of Axin. Inhibitors of tankyrase (TNKSi) stabilize the AXIN and inhibit
  the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway
