caption: ErbB receptors and their signaling pathways. Diagram showing functional ErbB
  dimers, neuregulins (NRGs) binding and canonical ErbB signaling pathways. NRGs-induced
  ErbB stimulation activates PI3K-Akt-mTOR and GSK-3 pathways, which foster protein
  synthesis and cell survival, as well as Ras-Raf-MEK-ERK pathway or PLC-PKC pathway,
  mainly involved in gene expression regulation. Other ErbB effectors are kinases
  like Src, Abl, Pyk2, Fyn, JNK, and the signal transducer and activator of transcription
  5 (STAT5), which affect gene expression by regulation of nuclear transcriptional
  factors.
