  in SARS-CoV-induced CCL2 expression. Infection of lung epithelial cells with SARS-CoV

  leading to activation of ERK1/2 and AP-1 and upregulation of CCL2. The elevated
  level of CCL2 protein detected in the sera of SARS-CoV-infected patients may account
