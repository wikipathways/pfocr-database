caption: Proposed mechanisms of FGFRL1-directed signaling via SHP-1. Canonical FGF
  receptors (i.e. FGFR1) activate downstream MAPK signaling events via ligand-induced
  receptor dimerization and trans-autophosphorylation. Conversely, association of
  SHP-1 with FGFRL1 at insulin secretory granules may localize the phosphatase, enabling
  up-regulation of the MAPK pathway (i.e. enhanced ERK1/2 phosphorylation). Alternatively,
  FGFRL1 at the plasma membrane may bind extracellular FGF ligand to elevate ERK1/2
  phosphorylation by means of a MEK-independent signaling cascade.
