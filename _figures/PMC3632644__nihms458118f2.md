caption: GLI1 Isoforms and cancer characteristics. GLI1 is induced by Shh, Ras, TGF-β,
  and PI3-K, leading to induction of PTCH, NANOG, Snail, and other genes. Conversely,
  GLI1 is negatively regulated by PKA and p53. GLI1 expression is linked to proliferation,
  EMT, cancer stemness, and tumorigenesis. Shh can induce tGLI1 activity; however,
  it is unknown whether non-shh pathways can regulate tGLI1. Like GLI1, tGLI1 induces
  PTCH expression. Importantly, tGLI1 has gained the ability to transcriptionally
  upregulate expression of CD24, VEGF-A, MMP-2, and MMP-9 genes, and thereby promotes
  migration, invasion, and angiogenesis.
