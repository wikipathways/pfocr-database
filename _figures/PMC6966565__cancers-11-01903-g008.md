caption: 'A schematic model depicting how WISP-1 regulates EMT functioning in OSCC
  cells. The modeling shows two potential signaling pathways underlying OSCC-derived
  WISP-1 regulation of EMT activation: (1) WISP-1 induces Snail expression via the
  integrin αvβ3/FAK/ILK/Akt signaling pathway, which in turn activates EMT functioning;
  (2) WISP-1 also inhibits miR-153-3p-induced downregulation of Snail mRNA translation
  and thus stimulates Snail protein expression and EMT activation. (RGD peptide: a
  target epitope of integrin αvβ3)'
