caption: Autophagy negatively regulates pyroptosis pathway. PTEN, PI3K/AKT/mTOR, AMPK
  can regulate autophagy through ULK1 complex, and the NLRP3 inflammasome catalyzes
  ULK1 complex assembly by activating RalB nucleotide exchange. The interaction of
  LGALS9 with NLRP3 and P62 facilitates P62-dependent autophagic degradation of NLRP3.
  USP5 recruits E3 ligase MARCHF7 mediating the K48-linked polyubiquitination of NLRP3
  and promoting the autophagy-mediated degradation of NLRP3. IRGM mediates NLRP3-selective
  autophagy by bridging P62 and NLRP3, ASC and forming a complex. PTPN22 promotes
  the dephosphorylation and the activation of NLRP3 inflammasome, avoiding phosphorylated
  NLRP3 interacting with p62 in an ASC-dependent manner and triggers autophagy. TRIM20
  acts as an autophagic receptor that recognizes the inflammasome components and promotes
  the autophagic degradation of NLRP3, pro-caspase-1. USP18 recruits the E3 ubiquitin
  ligase MIB2 catalyzing K33-linked ubiquitination on GSDMD at Lys 168 and mediating
  selective autophagic degradation of GSDMD. The autophagy degradation of GSDMD-N
  and IL-1β can be mediated by P62
