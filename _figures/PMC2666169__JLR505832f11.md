caption: Schematic representation of the oxLDL effects on insulin sensitivity in 3T3-L1
  adipocytes. OxLDL increase serine307-phosphorylation of IRS-1 by IKKβ and JNK. The
  impairment of insulin signaling leads to reduced uptake of glucose via GLUT4. IKKβ
  is also involved in the activation of NF-κB signaling pathway by phosphorylating
  IκBα. NF-κB downregulates GLUT4 translocation and glucose uptake. OxLDL, oxidized
