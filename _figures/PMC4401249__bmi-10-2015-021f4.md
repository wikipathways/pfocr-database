caption: DIANA miRPath analysis of target genes for miRNAs upregulated in AD blood.
  The set of 13 miRNAs upregulated in AD, except for hsa-miR-6513–3p whose information
  is absent in DIANA-microT, was imported into DIANA miRPath. It identified the second-rank
  significant KEGG pathway potentially downregulated in AD, termed long-term potentiation
  (hsa04720), relevant to target gene network for imported miRNAs. The genes targeted
  by more than one miRNA are colored by orange, while the genes targeted by a single
  miRNA are colored by yellow.
