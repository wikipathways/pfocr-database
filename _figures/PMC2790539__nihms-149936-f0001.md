caption: Mitochondria-dependent pathways of apoptosis in cerebral ischemia and reperfusion.

  pathway, c-Jun N-terminal kinase (JNK) pathway, PI3-K pathway, and the MAPK pathway
  are activated. Most signaling pathways induce apoptosis with the help of pro-apoptotic
  proteins, such as cytochrome c, Endo G, AIF and Smac, which are stored in mitochondria.
