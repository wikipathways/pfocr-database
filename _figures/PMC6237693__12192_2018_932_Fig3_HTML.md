  MAPK and NF-kappaB pathways. HSP70 both positively regulates osteoclastic bone resorption
  via the RANKL/RANK pathway and promotes osteogenesis through activation of the ERK
  and Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathways. HSP90 induces the production of osteoclast-associated
  genes such as PU.1 and c-fos to promote the formation of osteoclasts
