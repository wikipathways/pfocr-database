caption: Resorcylic acid natural products produced from Cannabis sativa and from fungi.
  A) C. sativa cannabinoid biosynthetic pathway. The β-resorcylic acid, olivetolic
  acid (OA), is produced from the olivetolic acid synthase (OAS) and olivetolic acid
  cyclase (OAC). OA is processed to the final products THCA and analogues. In the
  absence of OAC, side products (pyrone and olivetol) emerge. B) Tandem fungal iterative
  polyketide synthases produce resorcylic acid lactones.
