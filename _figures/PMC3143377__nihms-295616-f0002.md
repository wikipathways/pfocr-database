caption: PI3 kinases, heterodimers composed of p85 regulatory and p110 catalytic subunits,
  phosphorylate phosphatidylinositol-4,5-bisphosphate (PIP2) to phosphatidylinositol-3,4,5-trisphosphate
  (PIP3), which leads to recruitment and activation of AKT. AKT activation requires
  both phosphorylation at threonine 308(P*) and at serine 473(P**) in order to activate
  mTOR-containing complexes mTORC1 and mTORC2 and HDM2 or inhibit FOXO, GSK3β, and
  BAD. Several small molecules targeting the PI3K/AKT/mTOR pathway at different levels
  are currently in clinical testing.
