caption: 'Targeting TFs in CSC from multiple perspectives A Directly blocking the
  combination of TFs and DNA: mainly through epigenetic mechanisms. B Targeting TFs
  mRNA to regulate the level: miRNAs target the inactivation of TF mRNA, and TFs mediate
  miRNA transcription. LncRNA influences TF mRNA through miRNA and forms ceRNA with
  miRNA. CircRNA targets TF mRNA through miRNA. RBP binds TF mRNA to affect the stability
  and translation. The modification of TF mRNA may play a role. C Promoting TFs degradation
  by ubiquitination. D Modulating various signaling pathways to affect TFs transcription
  and activity by diverse agents: it is effective for the TFs to regulate transcription
  of CSC by acting on ligands, receptors, complexes, and other TFs of various signaling
  pathways. E Inhibiting cell markers that regulate TFs gene expression. F Inducing
  ambient pressure such as metabolism, hypoxia and immune response to cause TFs inactivation'
