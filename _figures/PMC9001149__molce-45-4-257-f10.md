caption: In TLR3/4 activated macrophages, TRIF-mediated RIP1/IRF3 and RIP1/RIP3 signaling
  pathways are enhanced by the caspase inhibitor zVAD, leading to IFN production and
  necrosome formation, respectively. Autocrine IFNR-JAK-STAT1 action further increases
  ROS production, which is a prerequisite for autophagic cell necroptosis. zVAD also
  inhibits LPS- and poly I:C-elicited ERK and Akt activation, both of which are not
  involved in the cell death mechanism.
