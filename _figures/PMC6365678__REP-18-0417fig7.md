caption: CXCL16/CXCR6 interaction promotes endometrial decidualization via PI3K-PDK1-AKT-Cyclin
  D1 pathway. The trophoblast-derived CXCL16 interacts with CXCR6 in ESCs, and thus,
  induces the decidualization of ESCs by activating the PI3K/PDK/AKT/Cyclin D1 signalling
  pathway. Furthermore, the decidualised ESCs (DSCs) autocrine CXCL16 and steroid
  hormones (such as oestrogen and progesterone) have positive feedback on DSCs and
  further improve decidualization during pregnancy.
