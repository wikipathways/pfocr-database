  winter-summer gene expression in pathway members (based on modified KEGG TLR signalling
  pathway), demonstrating winter bias in some cases and summer bias in others. Categorization
  of differential expression is based on overall significance levels in general linear
  models (LMs) with explanatory terms for site, sex and length (less stringent criteria
  than used in initial genome-wide analyses). b Principal co-ordinates (PCO) ordination
  of gene expression in all pathway members, revealing considerable winter-summer
  discrimination; scatter of individual points against the 3 major axes (PCO-1-3)
  and concentration ellipsoids containing 50 % of points
