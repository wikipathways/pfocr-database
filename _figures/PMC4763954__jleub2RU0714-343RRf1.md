caption: (A) Death signaling pathway initiated by Fas:FasL binding in a DC. FADD binds
  the cytoplasmic domain of Fas and recruits caspase-8 (Casp-8) to activate RIP1 and
  initiate apoptosis. (B) Alternative Fas:FasL signaling pathways are controlled by
  c-FLIP, which inhibits signaling from the death-inducing FADD/caspase-8 complex
  and diverts the signals toward MAPK pathways (AKT, JNK, Wnt), the NF-κB pathway,
  or ERK pathways, resulting in survival, cytokine production, and/or proliferation.
  These pathways are not mutually exclusive.
