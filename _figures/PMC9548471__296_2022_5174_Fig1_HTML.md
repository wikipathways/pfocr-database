caption: The central role of JAK/STAT signalling pathway in IL-17 producing T-cells.
  IL-6 and IL-23 signals are transduced via JAK2 and TYK2 to STAT3 which triggers
  production of the effector molecules IL-17 and IL-21. Furthermore, activation of
  NF-κB by IL-23 results in production of IL-6, further amplifying IL-17 and IL-21
  production. Negative feedback is provided by increased expression of SOCS1 and SOCS3
  which acto to inhibit JAK2 and STAT3, respectively, limiting further autocrine and
  paracrine response [–]
