caption: Biosynthetic pathway of C-mannosylated proteins in the endoplasmic reticulum
  (ER). The dolichyl-P-mannose (Dol-P-Man) synthase complex consists of DPM1, 2, and
  3. Dol-P-Man is synthesized on the cytosolic face of the ER from Dol-P and GDP-Man,
  and flips into the ER lumen. The C-mannosyltransferase (i.e., DPY19L1 and DPY19L3)
  transfers an α-mannose to Trp in the W-x-x-W motif of the target proteins. C-Mannosylation
  promotes the formation of appropriate disulfide bonds and secretion of the substrate
  proteins. Man, mannose.
