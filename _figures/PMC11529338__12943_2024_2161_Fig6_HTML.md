caption: The bone microenvironment of prostate cancer with bone metastasis. In the
  bone marrow environment (BME), upregulation of BHLHE22 recruited PRMT5, promoting
  CSF2 expression and increasing levels of immature neutrophils and monocytes. This
  led to exhaustion of CD8+ and CD4+ T cells. In osteoblasts, downregulation of RBM3
  inhibited m6A modification of CTNNB1 mRNA, enhancing CTNNB1 expression and activating
  the Wnt/β-catenin signaling pathway. Enzalutamide treatment increased PTH1R expression
  in osteoblasts, causing interaction with TGFBR2 and promoting bone metastasis
