caption: Role of ncRNAs in activation of NF-κB pathway with TLR signaling as an example.
  Toll-like receptors activate and recruit TIR-containing adaptor molecules, MyD88
  and TRIF, which prime downstream effectors respectively, under the stimulation of
  signals such as LPS. Both of upstream signal paths finally transfer signals to IKKs,
  the protein kinase of IκB, and relieve inhibition of NF-κB. miR-628-5p, miR-21,
  miR-146a, miR-545 and miR-9-5p play an essential role in this process.
