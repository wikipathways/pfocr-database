caption: 'The biotin synthetic pathway is initiated (a) by BioC-catalyzed and S-adenosyl-L-methionine
  (SAM) mediated methylation of malonyl-ACP. The methyl group is red. The malonyl
  ACP methyl ester enters the fatty acid synthetic cycle as the primer. (b) Following
  for two rounds of the fatty acid chain elongation cycle the resulting pimeloyl-ACP
  methyl ester is then (c) hydrolyzed by BioH to form pimeloyl-ACP which is a substrate
  for BioF to begin assembly of the biotin rings (d). Abbreviations: SAH, S-adenosyl-homocysteine;
  AON, 8-amino-7-oxononanoate.'
