caption: Schematic diagram of resveratrol (Res)‐mediated suppression of ALI through
  STAT3/SOCS3 signalling. LPS induces oxidative stress and activates STAT3, subsequently
  induces IL‐6, IL‐1beta and CXCL15 expression, ultimately causes ALI/ARDS. Activation
  of STAT3 up‐regulates expression of SOCS3 that negatively suppresses STAT3 signalling‐mediated
  pro‐inflammatory cytokine expression and maintains a balance between pro‐inflammatory
  and anti‐inflammatory responses. Res increases the expression and activation of
  anti‐inflammatory SIRT1 and SOCS3, subsequently lead to lower expression of IL‐6,
  IL‐1beta and CXCL15
