caption: MiR-424-5p can regulate ovarian cancer progression in 4 different ways. (i)
  MiR-424-5p can inhibit ACSL4 to prevent hypertrophy of primary ovarian cancer. (ii)
  MiR-424-5p decreases the expression of KIF23 to inhibit the proliferation and migration
  of ovarian cancer. (iii) MiR-424-5p targets CCNE1J directly to activate the E2F1-pRb
  signaling pathway, which results in an antitumor effect. (iv) MiR-424-5p increases
  the expression of MIEF2 to facilitate the Warburg effect.
