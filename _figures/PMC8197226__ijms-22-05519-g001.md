caption: 'The JNK pathway in Drosophila. Extrinsic signals such as the ligand Egr/TNFα
  can bind the TNFα receptors (TNFRs) Grnd and Wgn, activating a cascade of kinases:
  Msn/JNKKKK, dTAK1/JNKKK, Hep/JNKK and Bsk/JNK. Hep/JNKK can also be phosphorylated
  by intrinsic factors (outlined by the dashed line), such as Ask1. Bsk/JNK phosphorylates
  the AP-1 transcription factor components Kay/Fos and Jra/Jun. AP-1 target genes
  include puc/DUSP10, which encodes a phosphatase that inactivates Bsk/JNK in a negative
  feedback loop, and upd/IL-6, encoding an inflammatory cytokine. The STRIPAK complex
  that binds to Hep/JNKK, Bsk/JNK and AP-1 and inhibits the pathway.'
