  which in turn activates STAT5 by phosphorylation. Phosphorylated STAT5 goes to the
  nucleus, where it binds to gamma interferon–activated sequence (GAS) site of target
  gene's promoters. It activates transcription of Igf-1 gene., It activates cell cycle
  inhibitors Cdkn2b (cyclin-dependent kinase inhibitor 2B; p15INK4B) and Cdkn1a (p21CIP1).
  It also activates metabolic genes associated with lipid, bile acid, and steroid/drug
  metabolism. STAT5.
