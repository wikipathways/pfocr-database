  destroying the microtubule dynamic and DNA damage and inhibiting translation. (B)

  a type II polyketide []. ACP: acyl carrier protein. KS: ketosynthase. KR: ketoreductase.
  GT: glycosyltransferase. MT: methyl transferase. OA: oxygenase. ME: methyl esterase.

  reticulum stress. OS: oxidative stress. HAT: histone acetylase. HDAC: histone deacetylase.
