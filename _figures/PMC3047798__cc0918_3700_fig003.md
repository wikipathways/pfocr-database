caption: 'Wnt signaling components regulate ubiquitylation. Axin acts as a subunit
  of a larger complex that stimulates SCFFbw7 E3 ligase-mediated degradation of c-Myc.
  Axin binds and coordinates MAP3K1 E3 ligase activity to drive β-catenin/TCF-mediated
  transcription. APC acts as a subunit of a SCF-like E3 ligase that includes Siah-1
  and destabilizes cytosolic β-catenin upon genotoxic stress. In planar cell polarity
  signaling, asymmetric Prickle distribution is facilitated by its local ubiquitin-mediated
  proteolysis, through the action of a Fz-Dvl-Par-Smurf complex. Filled arrowheads
  point out enzyme-substrate; open arrowheads: positive regulation. Dashed lines indicate
  protein movement.'
