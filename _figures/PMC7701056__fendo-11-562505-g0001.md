caption: The PI3K/Akt/mTOR pathway. Following growth factors stimulation and subsequent
  activation of RTKs and GPCRs, PI3K is recruited to the plasma membrane directly
  or through adaptor protein and phosphorylated PIP2 producing PIP3, which recruits
  and activates PDK1. Akt activation is mediated by PDK1 on T308 and by mTORC2 complex
  on S473. Akt controls the activity of mTORC1 inactivating the GTPase activity of
  the TSC1/TSC2 complex toward the mTORC1 activator Rheb. mTORC1 activation induces
  protein synthesis via p70S6K and 4EBP1. The tumor suppressor gene PTEN acts on this
  pathway antagonizing the PI3K action on PIP3.
