caption: Schematic overview of the TLR4 signaling pathway. Activation of the TLR4
  receptor by lipopolysaccharides triggers downstream NF-κB signaling. NF-κB enters
  the nucleus, leading to the expression of miR-146a. Mature miR-146a downregulates
  IRAK1 and TRAF6 levels by targeting their mRNAs and subsequently terminates the
  TLR4 signaling pathway cascade. TLR, Toll-like receptor; miR, microRNA; TRAF6, TNF
  receptor-associated factor 6; IRAK1, interleukin IL-1 receptor-associated kinase

  protein; IKK, inhibitor of nuclear factor-κB kinase; IκB, inhibitor of κB; CD14,
