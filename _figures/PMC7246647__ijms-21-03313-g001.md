caption: 'Fibroblast Growth Factor Receptor (FGFR) structure, ligand binding and signaling.
  Schematic representations of FGF receptor tyrosine kinase structure, composed of
  several domains including three extracellular immunoglobulin-like domains (IgI,
  IgII, and IgIII) and the acid-box, a transmembrane domain, and two intracellular
  tyrosine kinase domains (TK1 and TK2). The basic structure of the FGF/FGFR complex
  includes two receptor molecules, two FGFs, and one heparan sulphate proteoglycan
  (HSPG) chain. The signal transduction network activates four key downstream pathways:
  RAS/RAF/MAPK, PI3K/AKT, STATs and PLCγ.'
