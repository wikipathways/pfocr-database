  and nongenomic actions of BAs are mediated by NRs and GPCRs. The BAs that modulate
  the activity of the receptor are indicated in the arrows and sorted by decreasing
  potency (left: high, right: low). Arrows in dark grey indicate agonistic actions,

  and deoxycholic acid (DCA) are reported to activate the muscarinic receptors. FPR
  = Formyl-peptide receptor. b Simplified overview of the TGR5-signaling pathway leading
  to downstream signaling via cAMP induction. AC = Adenylate cyclase.'
