  differential gene expression at day 1, the middle bar represents differential gene
  expression at day 4 and the lower bar represents differential gene expression at
  day 4 in lung areas where no virus was detected by TaqMan®. Genes were considered
  differentially expressed when showing a fold change greater than two and a p-value
