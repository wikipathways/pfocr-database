caption: In response to SSBs and DSBs, massive PAR is rapidly generated by PARPs and
  jumpstarts DNA damage response. In the NER pathway for SSB repair, PAR mediates
  the recruitment of DDB2 and ALC1. In the BER pathway, PAR targets PNKP, APTX, XRCC1
  and APLF to DNA lesions for the subsequent repair. In NHEJ pathway for DSB repair,
  PAR mediates the recruitment of the Ligase IV -XRCC4 complex. In HR pathway for
  DSB repair, PAR is recognized by several repair machineries, such as the BRCA1-BARD1
  complex, the MRN complex and the hSSB1-INTS complex.
