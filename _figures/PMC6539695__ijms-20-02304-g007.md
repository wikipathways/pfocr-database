  site of PDCD4-protein in Huh7 hepatoma cells. EGF activates PI3K-Akt-mTOR-S6K1 signaling
  pathway and the activated S6K1 phosphorylates PDCD4-protein at S67 and S76. TPA-mediated
  pathway via PKC-δ and/or PKC-ε may phosphorylate S71 and S76. PKC-dependent activation
  of S6K1 was not observed in Huh7 hepatoma cells.
