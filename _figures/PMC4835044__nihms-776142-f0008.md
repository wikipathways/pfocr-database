  2) Gas6 secretion and activation of Axl, 3) association of Shp2 with Gas6/Axl ,
  4) activation of the Ras/PI3K/Akt/ERK/c-Myc pathway, 5) c-Myc binding to the E-box

  c-Myc, 7) feedback regulation of Axl by c-Myc, and 8) feedback inhibition of c-Myc
  and Axl expression by elevated ASS1. De-repression of HIF-1α from the E-box of ASS1
  promoter by ADI is also indicated.
