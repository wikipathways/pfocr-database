  kinase receptors resulting in their autophosphorylation. In the case of insulin,
  this recruits IRS-1 that activates PI3K by binding to the SH2 domain of p85. Active
  PI3K then rapidly converts PI-4,5-P2 to PI-3,4,5-P3, which recruits PDK and AKT
  via their PH domains leading to AKT kinase activation. AKT phosphorylates many downstream
  substrates that regulate cell growth, metabolism, and survival.'
