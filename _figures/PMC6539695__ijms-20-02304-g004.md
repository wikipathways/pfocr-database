caption: Pathways of EGF mediated protein synthesis and PDCD4 degradation. EGF stimulates
  the phosphorylation of the eIF4E inhibitor 4E-BP via activation of PI3K-Akt-mTOR
  pathway and thereby activating protein synthesis. On the other hand, mTOR-activated
  S6K1 phosphorylates PDCD4-protein. The phosphorylated protein is ubiquitinated by
  SCFβTRCP (βTRCP) ubiquitin (Ubi) ligase and degraded in proteasomes stimulating
  protein synthesis.
