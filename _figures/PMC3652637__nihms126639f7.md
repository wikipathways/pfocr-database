caption: Acetycholine (Ach) is released from parasympathic nerves and binds to M3
  muscarinic receptor (M3AChR). This initiates a signaling cascade that involves activation
  of phospholipase Cβ (PLCβ) and protein kinase C (PKC)α, ε, and δ, and the formation
  of diacylglycerol (DAG) and 1,4,5-inositol trisphosphate (IP3). Calcium is released
  from the endoplasmic reticulum (ER) leading to protein secretion. The M3AChR also
  activates non-receptor tyrosine kinases Pyk2 and Src to activate Ras, Raf, mitogen
  activated protein kinase kinase (MEK) and p44/p42 mitogen-activated protein kinase
  (MAPK) that attenuates protein secretion.
