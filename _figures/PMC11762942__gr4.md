  OA pathogenesis.The CH25H-CYP7B1-RORα axis of cholesterol metabolism regulates OA.
  Omega-3 PUFAs inhibit the expression of COX-2, IL-1and TNF-α by inhibiting the NF-κB
  signaling pathway. SFA promotes IL-6 expression by activating the HIF1α pathway.
  Omega-6 PUFA regulates ROS production through the NOX-4 signaling pathway, thereby
  promoting IL-6 expression and exacerbating the progression of OA
