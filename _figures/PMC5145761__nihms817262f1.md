  Analysis (IPA). Genes highlighted in pink represent focus genes. ADCYL [(AC) adenylate
  cyclase)] is involved in biological processes including activation of protein kinase
  A activity and G-protein coupled receptor signaling. PRKACB [(PKA) protein kinase
  cAMP-activated catalytic subunit beta)] is involved in activation of protein kinase
  A and G-protein coupled receptor signaling. TAB1(TGF-beta activated kinase 1/MAP3K7
  binding protein 1) is involved in regulation of MAPK and NFκB activation, signaling
  through MyD88, and TGFβ signaling. IRAK (interleukin 1 receptor associated kinase
  1) is involved in TLR signaling via MyD88, activation of protein kinase A, and G-protein
  coupled receptor signaling.
