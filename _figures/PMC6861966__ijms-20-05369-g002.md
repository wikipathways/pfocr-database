caption: Adaptive feedback signaling in BRAF V600E-mutated mCRC. (A) In BRAF V600E-mutated
  mCRC, activated BRAF V600E monomer activates the MAPK pathway (MEK and ERK), leading
  to cell growth. Activated ERK suppresses the upstream activation of MAPK pathway
  through negative feedback on TRK, such as EGFR. (B) BRAF inhibitors (iBRAF) lead
  to transient inhibition of MAPK pathway and loss of ERK-dependent negative feedback
  on RTK, resulting in paradoxical activation of MAPK pathway.
