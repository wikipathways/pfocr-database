caption: Inflammatory cytokine-associated idiosyncratic drug hepatotoxicity as a “network
  toxicity”. The multipathway modeling approach presented here suggests that an integration
  of multiple intracellular signaling pathway -- namely the MEK–ERK, mTOR–p70 S6K,
  Akt, and p38–HSP27 pathways -- activities is necessary for hepatocytes to specify
  death responses to hepatotoxic drug/cytokine co-treatment conditions. This provides
  motivation of the network-level consideration of multiple survival, stress, and
  apoptosis signaling pathways in evaluating the hepatotoxicity mechanisms of context-dependent
  hepatotoxic drugs. Schematic reproduced from [].
