caption: Simplified schematic of the T cell receptor signaling pathway. TCR engagement
  induces the kinase activity of Lck, which phosphorylates ITAMs in the CD3 zeta chains.
  Zap-70 is recruited to these phosphorylated ITAMs resulting activation of kinase
  activity and phosphorylation of downstream signaling molecules. Multi-protein complexes
  nucleated by the adapter protein LAT are formed. Components of these complexes mediate
  activation of MAPK pathways and generate second messenger molecules. These signals
  activate various transcription factors and induce T cell proliferation and differentiation.
