  cells dependent on GFPT2 for hexosamine synthesis. Uptake of glucose and glutamine
  is elevated by mutant KRAS, establishing the environment favoring hexosamine synthesis.
  LKB1 loss in the context of KRAS mutation, which leads to loss of AMPK activation,
  then enhances HBP through GFPT2. Increased GFPT2 activity may also contribute to
  the glutamate pool for anaplerosis in the mitochondria. α KG, α -ketoglutaric acid.
