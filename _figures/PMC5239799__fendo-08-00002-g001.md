caption: Integration of non-genomic signaling and canonical signaling of androgen
  receptor (AR). In the presence of low androgen levels (picomolar concentrations),
  AR interactions with Src kinase and p85α regulatory subunit of phosphoinositide
  3-kinase activates mitogen-activated protein kinase (MAPK) and Akt pathways to enhance
  cell proliferation and survival in a non-genomic fashion. In the presence of high
  androgen levels (nanomolar concentrations), AR is activated in a canonical pathway
  to regulate the expression of target genes. Activation of MAPK and Akt by non-genomic
  signaling also enhances genomic AR signals by phosphorylating the AR or transcriptional
  coactivators.
