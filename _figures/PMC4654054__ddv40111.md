caption: Graphical representation of activation of the JNK signaling pathway in SMA.
  Two MAPK signaling modules (MAP3K → MAP2K → MAPK) (A) ASK1 → MKK4 → JNK3 and (B)
  MEKK1 → MKK7 → JNK3 found to be activated in the spinal cords of SMA mice and patients.
  In vitro elimination of JNK3 reduces degeneration of SMN-deficient neurons and provides
  neuroprotection. In vivo deletion of Jnk3 in SMA mice results in partial systemic
  rescue and improvement in SMA phenotype.
