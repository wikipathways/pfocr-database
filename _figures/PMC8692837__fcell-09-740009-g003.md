caption: 'Cancer affected by three representative altered environmental conditions
  in space: decompression, radiation, and microgravity. (A) Decompression of oxygen
  activates HIF-1α expression and forms a DNA binding complex with CBP/p300 to VEGF
  gene transcripts, which have pro-angiogenesis and wound-repair functions. (B) Microgravity
  upregulated the expression of cell-to-cell junction proteins, such as IGPR-1 and
  E-cadhrin, which have barrier functions in drug resistance. (C) Radiation in space
  triggers the Wnt signaling pathway, and accumulated β-catenin bind to T-cell factor/lymphoid
  enhancer factor family (TCF/LEF), leading to transcription of EphB/EphrinB, which
  regulates cell migration and cytoskeletal dynamics.'
