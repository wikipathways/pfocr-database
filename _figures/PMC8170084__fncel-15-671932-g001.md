caption: Schematic representation of the m6A pathway and effectors on mRNA. The MACOM
  complex composed of m6A writers (METTL3, METTL14, WTAP, VIRMA, ZC3H13, and RBM15)
  deposits m6A on target RNAs. M6A erasers (FTO and ALKBH5) remove the m6A mark. M6A
  nuclear readers (hnRNPC, hnRNPA2B1, and YTHDC1) facilitate alternative splicing
  or polyadenylation following recognition of m6A-tagged RNA. M6A-tagged RNA can be
  exported to the cytoplasm and bound by cytoplasmic readers (eIf3, ELAVL1, YTHDF1,2,3)
  to modulate stability, translational efficiency or the degradation of RNA. Blue,
  m6A writers; Orange, m6A erasers; Green, m6A readers.
