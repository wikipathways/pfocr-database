caption: Mechanism of preventing DN progression mediated by MSC-exos. MSC-exos with
  miR-let7 and miR-215-5p can inhibit the deposition of the extracellular matrix (ECM)
  and epithelial-mesenchymal transition (EMT) by downregulating the TGF-β signaling
  pathway and Zinc finger E box-binding homeobox 2 (ZEB2); MSC-exos with miR-486 and
  microRNA-26a-5p can repress the apoptosis of podocytes through autophagy activation
  and the downregulation of the TLR4/NF-κB pathway; in addition, they can repress
  proinflammatory cytokine and profibrotic factor levels to prevent early diabetic
  nephropathy (DN).
