caption: Schematic of the proposed mechanism for chronic adrenergic stimulation induced
  heart failure via cAMP-PKA-PDE4D signaling pathway and protective mechanism of PDE4D
  inhibition.Sustained adrenergic activation upregulates PDE4D expression via cAMP-PKA
  signaling, which in turn reduces cAMP-PKA activity, resulting in cardiomyocyte hypertrophy
  and mitochondrial injury via inhibition of CREB-SIRT1 signaling-mediated mitophagy.
  Pharmacological inhibition or knockdown of PDE4D reduced cAMP degradation and activates
  PKA-CREB-SIRT1 signaling, leading to a restoration of PINK1/Parkin-mediated mitophagy
  in cardiomyocytes, which may contribute to improvement of cardiac function and hypertrophy
