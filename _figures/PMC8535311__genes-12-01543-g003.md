caption: The TGFβ signalling pathway. Upon binding of the TGFβ ligand to the TGFβ
  type II receptor, the latter phosphorylates the TGFβ type I receptor which further
  phosphorylates SMAD2/SMAD3. Activated SMAD2/3 binds to SMAD4 and accumulates in
  the nucleus to regulate the transcription of target genes. SMAD7 acts as an inhibitor
  of TGFβ signalling. It recruits phosphatase PP1 or ubiquitin ligases Smurf1, Smurf2,
  to induce dephosphorylation or proteasomal degradation of receptor complexes.
