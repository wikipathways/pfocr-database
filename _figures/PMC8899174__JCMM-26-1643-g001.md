  signalling and inducing further stimulation of osteoblast marker genes for osteogenic
  differentiation. Upon upregulation of Stard3nl, Stard3nl binds with Anxa2, inhibiting
  β‐catenin expression and the nuclear translocation of β‐catenin, thus resulting
