  is activated by multiple types of cell surface receptors including integrin receptors
  upon binding to extracellular matrix (ECM), cytokine receptors, and growth factor
  receptors. GAB2 and FRS2 are primarily activated by growth factor receptors. FRS2

  receptors. All three adaptor proteins lead to Ras/MAPK and PI3K/AKT pathway activation
  that promote cancer initiation and progression.
